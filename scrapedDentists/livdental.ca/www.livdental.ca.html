Accepting New Patients at LivDental – Your Camrose Dental Clinic

At LivDental, your Camrose dental clinic, we are focused on creating a welcoming experience for the whole family. Our dental professionals are here to help you achieve your health and happiness goals with thoughtful customer service and robust dental treatment options.

Providing a Wide Variety of Treatment Options at your Camrose Dental Clinic

Let LivDental be your new Camrose dental clinic! We welcome new patients of all ages at our convenient west-end location. We can care for your family’s dental and oral health needs right here in our offices, offering everything from dental appliances and restorations to orthodontic treatments.

Maintaining your oral health is an essential part of taking care of your whole health. Regular oral examinations and proper oral care habits are essential for promoting a healthy lifestyle and overall well-being.
