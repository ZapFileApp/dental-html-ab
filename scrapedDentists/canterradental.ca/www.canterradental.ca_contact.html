Contact Us

At Canterra Dental we want to hear from you!

Contact us with whatever your question is, and whenever you have it. Simply fill in the form below (as best you can), and we will respond to your inquiry as soon as possible (during regular business hours).

Please do not use this form to cancel or change an existing appointment

    We will reply to your request as soon as possible during business hours.
    If your inquiry is received after business hours we will respond during our next business day.

    Thank you for contacting Canterra Dental! We look forward to meeting you!

    Messages sent using this form are not considered private. Please contact our office by telephone at (403) 237-6611 if you are sending highly confidential or private information. Please review our privacy policy and website terms of use before submitting your request.
