Lac Ste. Anne Denture Clinic

Welcome to Lac Ste. Anne Denture Clinic, we are excited to help you with all your denture care needs, including relines, repairs, implant supported dentures, and more. We are located on Lac St Anne Trail S in Onoway, which means we can conveniently accept patients from all over the region. Serving our community since 2004, our friendly and knowledgeable denture team strives to provide the highest level of denture support possible to ensure our patients achieve optimal use of their dentures.
