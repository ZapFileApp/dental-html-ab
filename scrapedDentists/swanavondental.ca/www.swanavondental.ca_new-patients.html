What to Expect on Your First Visit

Your first appointment is designed to give us a complete understanding of your oral health and your goals.

During your visit, you can expect:

  • A comprehensive dental exam
  • Digital imaging if needed
  • A professional cleaning (when appropriate)
  • A clear, personalized treatment plan

We take the time to explain everything and answer your questions so you feel confident moving forward.
