We Welcome All New Patients

Nova Dental Care is here to help!

Nova Dental Care is a leading SW Calgary Dental Clinic focused on providing the highest quality client care in a safe, modern, and welcoming environment. We are a general dentistry and family dentistry practice offering a range of dental services, from cleaning your toddler’s teeth to crafting dentures.

We are currently accepting new patients for our practice. We realize that starting a new relationship with a dentist can often be nerve-wracking. That is why we provide all of our new patient forms and answers to some of the more frequently asked questions digitally. This way, you can get better acquainted with our practice and, hopefully, we can set your mind at ease, so you enjoy a more relaxing environment when your appointment arrives.

If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact our dental team. We will do our best to respond as quickly as possible during business hours. Thank you again for choosing Nova Care Dental. We look forward to providing you and your family with the best possible dental care. 

Online Patient Forms for Your Convenience

At Nova Dental Care, we want to save you time. That is why we have online forms that you can download right from our website. This allows you to print them off and fill them in at your convenience, in the privacy of your own home.

At Nova Dental Care, we always try to keep your dental visit as efficient as possible, ensuring that it starts and ends on time. We always follow up every visit so that you can provide us with any feedback on how we can further improve to meet ALL of your needs.

Our goal is always to keep you aware of your oral health needs. To accomplish this, we keep in touch with our patients via whatever technology they are most comfortable with, making sure it fits within your schedule. This helps you stay informed and attain all your dental health goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

We have assembled some of the more common questions we receive from new patients and our treasured long-term clients. If you have questions that you do not see answered on this page, please feel free to contact our dental office team, and they will reach out as soon as possible.

Where is Nova Dental Care located?

Nova Dental is a leading SW Calgary Dental Clinic located at 4620 Bow Trail SW, #220, Calgary AB, T3C2G6.

Is there parking available at Nova Dental Care?

Yes, we offer free parking for all of our clients.

Are you currently accepting new patients?

Yes, we are accepting new patients. Please feel free to visit our New Patient’s Forms page for more information.

Do you work with kids?

Yes! We welcome families and children of all ages into our practice.

Do you accept Social Service patients?

Yes! We believe strongly in serving every member of our Wildwood community.

What do I do if I have an emergency when the clinic is closed?

If you have a dental emergency that cannot wait for open clinic hours, please call us at (587) 885-3898, when prompted please leave a message and we will get back to ASAP.

Do you bill my insurance company directly for my visit?

We are pleased to offer Direct Billing (sometimes called Assignment of Benefits), which means we will happily file your insurance claim electronically if allowed. You then pay only the remaining balanced on a case-by-case basis.

What sort of dental amenities do you offer?

We have a wonderfully cozy waiting area with a big-screen TV, coffee and water while you wait; And personalized TV, blanket and pillow in each treatment rooms.

What insurance policies does Nova Dental Care accept?

We currently accept all national plans and can work with most other private insurance providers.

If you have questions that you do not see answered on this page, please feel free to contact our dental office team, and they will reach out as soon as possible.

Are you ready to experience the Nova Dental Care difference?

Reach out to our professional Bow Trail office team today at (587) 885-3898 to schedule a new appointment. We look forward to hearing from you soon!
