Bone Grafting in Dentistry

Whenever there has been bone loss due to an injury or some other form of issue, a bone grafting procedure may be necessary to return the jaw, mouth, and teeth to their proper function. The goal is to add volume and density to any affected area where bone loss may have occurred. This is a specialized procedure our Calgary dental clinic team has experience and tools ready to ensure a successful outcome.

What is Dental Bone Grafting?

Bone grafting is a procedure in which bone volume is rebuilt with different types of bone material. This can be done to rebuild lost bone volume, or it can be used to add volume to areas where there isn’t enough bone. Bone grafting procedures are performed by dentists, oral surgeons, and periodontists (dentists who specialize in gum disease).

Benefits of Bone Grafting Procedure

  • Restores bone mass to your jaw
  • Improves the function of your teeth and mouth
  • Supports your facial structure
