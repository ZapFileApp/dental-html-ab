Your Trusted Family Dentist in Calgary SE

Established in 1989 with Significant Experience and Comprehensive Care

Dentist in Calgary Welcomes New Patients of All Ages

Welcome to Calgary Dental Centers, your home for personalized, family-friendly dental care in Calgary SE. Under the guidance of experienced dentist, Dr. Hanif Asaria, our Calgary SE practice, established in 1989, has developed a strong reputation as a reliable family dentist dedicated to honesty, comfort, and clinical excellence. From routine checkups to complex treatments, we take pride in delivering comprehensive dental care in Calgary SE, including implants, emergencies, and cosmetic dentistry, all under one roof.

Proud Members

Our approach focuses on prevention, comfort, and accessibility. Whether you’re due for a cleaning, managing gum disease, or considering tooth replacement, we ensure every treatment is customized to your needs and budget. We follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide, accept all insurance plans, and offer flexible payment options to make your care transparent.

Convenience and quality care, start to finish.

Calgary Dental Centers have easy access, with free parking, handicapped spaces, and room for buses.

We happily accept referrals from Blue Cross and patients on welfare and social service programs.

We follow the Alberta Blue Cross (ABC) fee guide.
Patients have a familiar co-payment, with no surprises at billing and no overcharging.

Calgary Dental Centers strives to make dental healthcare affordable for every patient. We develop customized treatment plans and accept financing through DentalCard from iFinance Canada.

Patients are never left without a source of contact.
Dr. Asaria is available on weekdays during office hours. After hours, calls are forwarded directly to our dental helpline.

We accept Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)

Your Smile, Our Commitment

At Calgary Dental Centers, we believe that dentistry should never feel rushed or impersonal. Every patient receives direct care from Dr. Asaria himself, ensuring continuity, compassion, and trust built over decades of service to Calgary families. Our patients value our personalized attention, same-day emergency availability, and bilingual support for a comfortable and inclusive experience.

Community and Accessibility

We’re proud to serve Calgary SE with accessible locations, free parking, and a commitment to making good dental care available to everyone. As a family dentist in Calgary SE with a long-standing reputation, our mission is simple: to keep your smile healthy, beautiful, and worry-free through every stage of life.

Focused Pediatric Care and Sedation are Available

Focused pediatric care and sedation are available at our 17th Avenue Dental Center. A full range of procedures from hygiene therapy to fillings, root canals, treatment for gum disease and bad breath, dentures, and extractions are available at our Calgary Dental Care office on MacLeod Trail SE, as well as 17th Avenue. At either location, you are assured of compassionate dentistry for every stage of life.
