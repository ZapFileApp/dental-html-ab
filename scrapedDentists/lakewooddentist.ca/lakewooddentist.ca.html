Why Visit Lakewood Dental Centre in Mill Woods?

Whether you’re visiting us for routine check-ups, advanced treatments, or in need of immediate care, we’re here to help you. Our friendly, experienced team takes the time to understand your individual needs.

Why Choose Lakewood Dental Centre?

  • Affordable, Transparent Pricing: We follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide, offering quality care at competitive rates.
  • Convenient Location: Located in the Kameyosek Shopping Centre with ample parking, making visits easy for Mill Woods residents.
  • Experienced, Caring Team: Our skilled dentists and staff provide expert, patient-centered care in a welcoming environment.
  • Comprehensive Services for All Ages: From children’s check-ups to advanced treatments like implants and crowns, we cater to the whole family’s dental needs.

Flexible Hours for Your Busy Schedule

We know it’s hard to find time for dental visits, so we offer extended hours, including evenings and weekends:

  • Evening Appointments: Available for after-work convenience.
  • Weekend Availability: Open on weekends for even the busiest schedules.

Our flexible scheduling makes dental care easier to fit into your life.

Affordable Dental Care South Edmonton

We believe everyone should have access to excellent dental care without financial stress:

  • Direct Billing to Insurance Providers: To make your experience seamless, we offer direct billing to most insurance providers, so you won’t have to worry about upfront payments.
