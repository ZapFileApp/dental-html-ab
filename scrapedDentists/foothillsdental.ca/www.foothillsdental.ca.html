    FAQ's

    How many teeth do you get with 4 implants?

    With 4 implants, you can get a full arch of teeth—typically 10 to 12 teeth—on either the upper or lower jaw, using a procedure called “All-on-4.”

    Is toothache a dental emergency? How do I get an emergency dentist appointment?

    A toothache can be a dental emergency, especially if it’s severe or accompanied by swelling. Call our dentist in NW Calgary immediately, and we’ll schedule an urgent appointment to address your pain.

    How often should you clean your teeth at the dentist?

    It’s recommended to visit the dentist for a professional cleaning every 6 months to maintain optimal oral health and prevent plaque buildup.

    Why do experts now say not to remove your wisdom teeth?

    Experts suggest leaving wisdom teeth in place if they are healthy, fully erupted, and not causing any problems. Removal is only recommended by our dentist near you if they pose a risk to your dental health.

    Is Foothills Dental Group open on evenings or weekends?

    How long does professional teeth whitening last?

    The professional whitening effect may range between 6 months and two years according to oral hygiene and lifestyles like smoking and drinking coffee.

    How to clean SureSmile aligners?

    Brush your SureSmile aligners thoroughly every day with a gentle toothbrush and lukewarm water. Toothpaste is abrasive and may cause damage to the aligners, so avoid using it.

    How long do dentures last?

    Dentures generally last 5 to 10 years, but they may need adjustments or replacements over time as your gums and jawbone change shape.
