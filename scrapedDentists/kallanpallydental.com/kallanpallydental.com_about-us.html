About Kallanpally Dental Clinic

Kallanpally Dental Clinic provides general dentistry to patients of all ages in Slave Lake. We are conveniently located in a wheelchair-accessible office.

Preventative Dentistry

We have a dedicated team that runs a prevention-oriented practice. Our goal is to help you avoid the leading causes of dental issues.

Exceptional Team

The recruitment of a talented team of dentists and health care professionals recognized for the high quality of their dental procedures.

Languages at our Clinic

Do you prefer to speak another language other than English? We offer dental appointments in French, Hindi, Malayam, Punjabi and Tagalog. Just let us know what you prefer when you book with us.

Technologies

The clinic’s modern accommodations combined with cutting-edge equipment, all at the service of our clients.

Gentle, Compassionate Dental Care for Your Family

We believe high-quality dental care should be accessible to everyone. Our dental office welcomes patients of all ages, needs, and backgrounds. The team at Kallanpally Dental Clinic speaks multiple languages and has created an approachable and welcoming clinic space designed around your comfort.

We value the health of you and your family above all else. In many cases, we provide dental care for multiple generations of families. Our focus on preventative dentistry practices allows us to provide a high level of care so you can continue to share smiles, laughter, and joy with those you love most.
