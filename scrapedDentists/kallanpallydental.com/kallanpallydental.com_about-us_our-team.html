Our Team

Dr. Jeny Antony

Dr. Antony has dedicated over two decades to practicing dentistry in Slave Lake. Her journey from an associate dentist to the ownership of Kallanpally Dental Clinic in 2001, and now as a partner dentist at Kalanpally Dental, reflects her unwavering commitment to providing exceptional dental care.
 
By adopting a ‘need-based approach,’ Dr. Antony prioritizes personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient’s unique needs and goals. Her dedication to staying up-to-date with the latest dental technology and techniques through attending continuing education and seminars ensures top-tier care for every patient.
 
In addition to her work in dentistry, Dr. Antony enjoys dedicating her free time to reading, travelling, going on nature walks, and spending time with her family and friends.

Dr. Harsh Patel

Dr Harsh Patel was born and raised in India where he obtained his Degree in Dentistry in 2010. He moved to Canada in 2019 and completed equivalency process as a General Dentist and now registered with College of dental surgeons of Alberta. He is also a member of Alberta Dental association (ADA) and Canadian Dental association (CDA). Dr Harsh enjoys treating patients of all age group. As a partner in his patients ’oral health, he strives to provide them with the best possible dental care. He puts great emphasis on encouraging his patients to understand and to be involved in their treatment plan. Outside work, Dr Harsh enjoys spending time with his family and engage in various sports activities.
