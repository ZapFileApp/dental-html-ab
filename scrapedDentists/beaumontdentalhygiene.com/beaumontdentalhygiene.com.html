Why choose a dental hygiene only provider?

Registered Dental Hygienists are an integral part in primary health care. Our main focus and objective is preventive oral health care through proper treatment of oral disease, and oral health education, Through client interactions and oral health education Canadian Registered Dental Hygienists hope to empower our clients to achieve and maintain their oral health for better overall health and well-being.
