Who Are We?

GP Periodontics is locally owned and operated by Dr. Michael Piepgrass, a board certified Periodontist.  Procedures performed by Dr. Piepgrass includes the treatment of gum disease, the placement and maintenance of dental implants, extractions - including wisdom teeth, gum and bone grafting, IV sedation, biopsies, and other periodontal treatments.
