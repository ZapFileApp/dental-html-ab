What is Orthodontics?

Orthodontics is a specialized branch of dentistry focused on diagnosing, preventing, and correcting misaligned teeth and jaws. Orthodontists, who are dental professionals with advanced training in this field, address issues like crooked teeth, overbites, underbites, and other alignment problems. Their goal is to improve both oral health and aesthetic appearance. Orthodontic treatment can enhance your smile’s function and overall health.

Invisalign® Clear Aligners

Discreet and Comfortable Teeth Straightening

Invisalign® clear aligners offer a nearly invisible solution to straighten your teeth. Custom-made for your unique smile, these aligners provide a comfortable and flexible option, allowing you to confidently achieve a straighter smile without the need for traditional braces.

Learn More
Bruxism Grinding Appliances

Protect Your Smile from Teeth Grinding

If you grind or clench your teeth, our custom-made bruxism appliances help protect your teeth from damage. These comfortable devices alleviate jaw pain, reduce tooth wear, and protect your dental health while you sleep, ensuring your smile remains intact.

Retainers​

Maintain Your Perfect Smile After Treatment

Retainers are designed to keep your teeth aligned after completing orthodontic treatment. Our comfortable, durable retainers help maintain your newly straightened smile and ensure that your teeth stay in their optimal position for years to come.

Space Maintainers

Preserving Space for Healthy Adult Teeth

Space maintainers help guide your child’s dental development by preserving space for their permanent teeth. These gentle, custom-made devices ensure that adult teeth come in properly and prevent future dental issues, helping maintain healthy, well-aligned smiles.

What Orthodontists Do

Orthodontists are dentists who undergo additional years of training to focus on the alignment of teeth and jaws. They:

  • Diagnose and treat malocclusions (improper bites).
  • Design and implement treatment plans using braces, aligners, and other appliances.
  • Monitor patient progress and make necessary adjustments.
  • Provide guidance on oral hygiene and post-treatment care.
  • Work with pediatric patients to guide proper jaw and tooth development.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many hours a day do I need to wear Invisalign aligners?

For optimal results, wear your aligners for 20-22 hours daily, removing them only for eating and cleaning.

Is Invisalign suitable for all orthodontic cases?

While Invisalign is effective for many cases, complex issues may require traditional braces. We’ll conduct a thorough assessment to determine the best option.

How long does orthodontic treatment typically take?

Treatment duration varies, typically ranging from 6 months to 2 years, depending on individual needs.

Will my dental insurance cover orthodontic treatment?

Many dental insurance plans cover orthodontic treatments. We can help you understand your coverage and explore financing options.

Does Strathmore Smiles Dental offer metal braces?

Yes, we offer traditional metal braces and ceramic braces.

Do you offer pediatric orthodontics for children in Strathmore?

Yes, we provide pediatric orthodontic services, including early intervention.
