What to expect at your first visit

Book appointment

Treatment coordination

At WYE Dental, we work together to deliver top-notch customer service in a calm atmosphere. Your dental care and its administration will be provided with kindness, understanding, and efficiency. Please attend your appointment 5-10 minutes early so that you can complete your Patient Intake Form, or complete it online now.

Patient Intake Form

Hygiene

When you come in for your first appointment, we’ll ensure a comfortable amount of time to get to know each other and to get the fundamentals of your dental care needs and wants. We will spend a bit more time with you at this first appointment than with your regular recare appointments so that we can do a thorough assessment to ensure an accurate treatment plan that’s specific to your smile.

Practice tour

Sometimes patients feel more comfortable having a pre-appointment tour – and if you’re one, we encourage you to call! Meet our doctors and our team. If you’re like many people who are anxious about dentistry, your tour will help you feel more comfortable. Please call us to arrange your visit!

Doctor checkup

Meet Dr. Voravong, Dr Jordan Doyle, Shak Latif or Dr. Silvia Kim to discuss your oral health needs and desires. The findings of a thorough exam will be reviewed with you and a treatment plan will be developed based on what will be best for you. We’ll recommend a personalized recare schedule, and if you’d like, we can even cover some cosmetic options

Diagnostics

Used to create a baseline upon which future care is determined, we will use digital x-rays to see what the eye can’t… and we’ll share our findings with you almost immediately!
