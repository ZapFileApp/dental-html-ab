Welcome to
the website of Westwyn Dental, your home for comprehensive care for your smile!

At Westwyn Dental, Dr. Jonathan Oh and Dr. Connor Schroyer are  dedicated to ensuring patients of the Berwyn, Illinois, community and surrounding cities have access to the care they need to both achieve and maintain healthy smiles.

What services are provided at Westwyn Dental?

We are excited to offer some of the following popular services at our office including:

  • Dental implants. Our Westwyn Dental  Implant team has  placed over 8,000 dental implant  restorations. Our Implant team has placed single teeth, implant supported bridges and over 150 All on 4 hybrid bridges. Implants are used to replace one, several, or sometimes even all the teeth in the smile in a natural and long-lasting manner.
  • Dental crowns. Restorative crowns are used to cap teeth that need restoration and repair or can be placed over the abutment of a placed implant to replace a single tooth. Dental crowns are made of porcelain, a material that looks just like natural tooth enamel and is strong and incredibly durable.
  • Invisalign orthodontics. Many of our patients love using Invisalign orthodontics instead of metal brackets and wires to realign the teeth. This treatment uses clear aligner trays that gently move teeth and reposition them for a more aesthetic appearance and improved functionality.
  • Porcelain veneers. Are your teeth near the front of the smile stained, broken, or spaced? Using porcelain veneers, you can cover these imperfections and achieve a more attractive smile in as little as two appointments.
  • General dentistry. From cleanings to evaluations, cavity fillings to composite resin bonding, we can provide most of your general dentistry needs under one roof, with the care of professionals such as cosmetic and general dentists and a periodontist on staff.

Transform Your Smile with Dental Implants at Westwyn Dental

Join us at Westwyn Dental in Berwyn, IL, as we take you through the dental implant process. Our expert team is dedicated to ensuring your comfort and understanding every step of the way. Discover the benefits of dental implants as a lasting solution for missing teeth and see how we restore smiles with precision and care. Subscribe for more insights and tips on dental health!
Video Transcript

Here at Westwyn Dental, we’re all about offering comprehensive dental care that’s centered around you, our patient. We take special pride in our dental implant procedures. Dental implants are an excellent long-lasting solution for replacing missing teeth, and we’re dedicated to making sure you’re comfortable and understand the process every step of the way. The dental implant procedure usually has several stages. First, we’ll do a complete examination to check your oral health and see if you’re a good fit for dental implants. This might include taking X-rays or 3D images of your mouth and talking about your medical history. Once we’re sure that dental implants are the best option for you, we’ll schedule the first surgical procedure. This involves placing a small titanium post the implant into your jawbone beneath the gum line. We perform this surgery under local anesthesia so you won’t feel any pain during the procedure.
After the implant is placed, there’s a healing period. This can last several months during which the implant bonds with your jawbone In a process known as osseointegration, this forms a solid base for your new artificial tooth. Once the implant has fully bonded with your jawbone, we’ll attach a small connector post known as an abutment to the implant. Lastly, we’ll create a custom-made crown, your new artificial tooth that matches the color and shape of your natural teeth, and attach it to the abutment throughout the whole process. Our team at Westwyn Dental is committed to your comfort and satisfaction. We’ll make sure you’re fully informed about each stage of the procedure, and we’re always here to answer any questions you might have.
