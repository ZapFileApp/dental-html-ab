Meet Our Dentist

Dr. Stuart Gough

Meet Dr. Stuart Gough – a Dedicated Springbank Dentist with a Passion for Oral Health!

Dr Stuart Gough, a third generation Calgarian, is committed to serving the community’s dental needs. Graduating from the University of Alberta with both a BSc and a DDS degree in 1990, he furthered his commitment by serving as a Dental Officer in the Canadian Armed Forces. During his service, Dr Gough deployed in Canada and abroad as part of a Field Surgical Hospital.

While in private practice, Dr Gough served as the executive of the Calgary and District Dental Society and was the Southern Alberta coordinator for Open Wide Alberta, a day when dentists across the province donated free dental care to those less fortunate. He and his family have travelled to many countries such as Tonga, Peru and Guatemala providing volunteer dental treatment in impoverished villages and towns. These experiences have been the highlight of his career.

Dr Gough looks forward to meeting you soon!
