Brooks Restorative (Cosmetic) Dentistry

We Are Your Choice for Brooks Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Struan Vaughan and Dr. Matt Cosman offer Brooks Cosmetic Dentistry and General, Restorative Dental services to enhance or completely redesign the look of your smile. We offer a comprehensive cosmetic dental consultation that involves an assessment of your current oral health to provide you with a treatment plan that best outlines the options required to achieve your ultimate goal for a beautiful new smile.

We know that a straighter, more aligned smile affects oral health issues down the road and can impact more than just your mouth. We will thoroughly examine your overall oral health, to determine the treatment options that best suit your needs.

Parkview Dental’s Cosmetic Dentistry services include:

  • Teeth Whitening (including in-office and take-home options)
  • Dental Crowns
  • Bridges
  • Porcelain Veneers
  • Orthodontics
  • White Fillings
