Canmore's Wildrose Dental Hygiene Centre is located on Bow Valley Trail which is parallel to the CPR railway tracks (immediately south) and the Trans Canada Highway (immediately North). Heading west on Bow Valley trail find us between the east and west turn-offs to downtown Canmore. Look for the blue sign featuring a large white tooth. Off street parking is available at the front of the clinic.
