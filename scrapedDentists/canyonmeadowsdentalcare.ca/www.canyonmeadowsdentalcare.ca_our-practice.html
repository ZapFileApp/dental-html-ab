Our Practice

Welcome to Canyon Meadows Dental Care! We are committed to providing patients with a pleasant dental care experience. Our experienced staff are here to answer any questions you may have regarding your dental treatment, dental insurance plan, or scheduling questions.

We are located in the Canyon Meadows neighbourhood at Elbow Drive and Anderson Road SW. We are accepting new patients and look forward to serving you and your family for all of your dental care needs. New patients are welcome to download and print our new patient intake form prior to coming to see us. Please visit us for a few minutes before your first scheduled appointment with your dental insurance information and Alberta health care card. If you have any questions prior to your first appointment please call us at 1-403-278-3400 and we will be happy to assist you.

At your new patient appointment, you will receive a comprehensive dental exam from one of our Dentists along with required radiographs. We can also provide a dental hygiene appointment at the same time if requested at the time of booking.

Why Choose Canyon Meadows Dental Care?

Health and Safety - At CMDC, the health and safety of our patients and staff is our number one priority. Universal precautions are taken to ensure our office environment is as safe as possible. Any item used in a patient's mouth is either single-use/disposable or sterilized. Each staff member is rigorously trained in contamination prevention and infection protocol and control. Daily maintenance testing is performed on our equipment to ensure that it is functioning at its prime.
