Chestermere Emergency Dentistry

Your Chestermere Choice for All your Emergency Dental Needs!

At Chestermere Dental, we understand that emergencies can happen – often without any warning! We have seen the gamut of dental emergencies and we know the importance of dealing with a dental emergency as quickly as possible. If you are unsure whether a dental problem is an emergency or not, we offer this advice; if it hurts, it is an emergency! This is because even injuries that seem small or superficial can still affect the living tissues inside the teeth. When a dental emergency happens, Chestermere Dental is here to help!

Same Day Appointments for Dental Emergencies!

Quick treatment not only improves the odds of saving injured or damaged teeth, but also keeps the financial costs down. Even if you are not in much pain, any structural damage to a tooth from something like a sports injury or a lost crown or filling should be considered an emergency. Chips or fractures can affect the living tissue inside of the tooth, causing more problems in the future. Furthermore, there may be damage that you are unaware of to the supporting structures, such as the gums and bone. The key to any effective treatment is early intervention to prevent damage from getting worse.

The best time to find a good place for emergency dental treatment is before an emergency happens! Whatever your dental emergency, Chestermere Dental is here for you. We will always do everything we can to see you and treat you as soon as possible. We welcome emergency walk-ins, and we do our best to treat you as soon as possible. We also make every effort to accommodate after hours emergencies. We pride ourselves on being able to treat almost all dental emergencies ranging from broken teeth to oral tissue injuries. To ensure that we will always be able to see you the same day in emergency situations, we always set aside emergency time slots in our day to accommodate for urgent treatment. Dr. James Yue and his team will do their very best to deal with your emergency as quickly as possible and make you as comfortable as possible. We understand that the stress and pain involved when dealing with a dental emergency can be extreme, and we always do our very best to ensure that you are calm, relaxed and comfortable during your entire visit. If you think you have a Dental Emergency, call us immediately at (403) 263-8512

Symptoms of a Dental Emergency

  • A tooth ache that will not go away
  • Severe throbbing tooth pain
  • Tooth pain that is non-responsive to pain killers
  • Any pain that wakes you up at night
  • Pain when biting
  • A cracked tooth
  • A chipped tooth
  • A broken tooth
  • A loose tooth that
  • A restoration that is loose
  • The loss of a crown or filling
  • Sensitivity to cold or hot liquids or sweets
  • Abnormal gum bleeding or swelling
  • Tissue swelling of the face
  • A bad taste or odor in the mouth

If you think you might require emergency dental treatment, or you have any questions about a dental issue or emergency dental treatment, please call the dedicated Chestermere Dental team immediately at (403) 263-8512 and we will be happy to provide you with the care you need!
