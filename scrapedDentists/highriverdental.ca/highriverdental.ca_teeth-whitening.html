Are You Looking to Enhance Your Smile? Then Teeth Whitening May Be The Answer!

The High River Dental Team is pleased to offer Teeth Whitening services to help you achieve the smile of your dreams!

Professional Teeth Whitening

For your convenience, we offer the latest teeth whitening techniques, which include:

  • Professional Take-Home Teeth Whitening: Our take-home teeth whitening kits are a safe, fast, and effective way to professionally remove stains and discoloration from your teeth, while in the comfort of your home. Take-home whitening treatment effectively whitens the enamel surface of the teeth, and our Dental Team will monitor your progress to ensure that any side-effects such as tooth sensitivity are managed quickly.
  • Zoom Teeth Whitening: This in-office teeth whitening procedure can produce results in typically 1 to 2 hours! With your ongoing custom take-home teeth whitening trays and professional strength whitening gel you will continue to see improvements as you continue your home care. Zoom teeth whitening is only performed in our dental office as the hydrogen peroxide gel is applied to the front surface of the teeth and activated by the overhead UV lamp. The UV light helps oxygen enter the enamel and dentin surfaces which bleaches the tooth structure.
  • Restorative Treatments for Improving Your Smile: Teeth whitening may not be ideal for every patient and at High River Dental, we offer alternative restorative dental services to whiten your teeth, such as white fillings, porcelain veneers, and dental crowns.

The dentists at High River Dental are all General Dentists. They are not specialists in Cosmetic Dentistry, Family Dentistry or Orthodontics. Cosmetic Dentistry & Family Dentistry are not specialties recognized by the Alberta Dental Association & College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta (CDSA). As general dentists, they provide cosmetic dental procedures such as porcelain veneers, dental crowns, dental braces, and teeth whitening as part of their General Dentistry license. As a High River Family Dentists, they provide General Dental procedures for all ages within the family.
