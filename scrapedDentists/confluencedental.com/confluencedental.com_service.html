Why A Dentist For Injectable Treatments?

Your dentist knows your facial anatomy better than almost any other healthcare provider. Years of training in jaw mechanics, facial nerves, and oral structures make dentists uniquely qualified to administer injectable treatments with precision.

At Confluence Dental, injectable treatments aren't an add-on. They're a natural extension of the care we already provide. If you're dealing with jaw pain, clenching, or facial tension, we're treating the source of the problem, not just the symptoms. If you're interested in softening fine lines, you're in the hands of someone who understands every muscle and nerve in your face.

No referral needed. No separate clinic. One appointment, one provider who already knows your health history.

TMJ & Jaw Pain Treatment

Chronic jaw clenching and grinding wear down your teeth, cause headaches, and create constant tension in your face and neck. If you wake up with a sore jaw or catch yourself clenching during the day, the muscles responsible are overworking.

Botulinum toxin injections relax the overactive muscles that drive clenching and grinding. The treatment targets the masseter and temporalis muscles directly, reducing their ability to clench with full force. The result: less jaw pain, fewer headaches, and less wear on your teeth.

This isn't a permanent fix. The effects are temporary, and most patients return for follow-up treatments to maintain relief. Your dentist will discuss a treatment schedule based on how your body responds.

What to expect:

The injection takes about 10-15 minutes. A fine needle delivers small amounts of botulinum toxin into the targeted muscles. Most patients describe it as a mild pinch. There's no downtime. You can return to your day immediately.

Results typically develop over 1-2 weeks as the muscles gradually relax. Your dentist will check in to assess your response and adjust your plan if needed.

Cosmetic Treatments & Candidacy

  • Horizontal lines across the forehead develop from years of raising your eyebrows. The injection relaxes the frontalis muscle, softening these lines while keeping your natural expression. Treatment takes under 5 minutes for this area alone. Results develop over 1-2 weeks.

  • The vertical lines between your brows (sometimes called "elevens") come from the corrugator muscles contracting when you concentrate or squint. A few targeted injections relax these muscles and smooth the crease. This is one of the most common treatment areas and one of the most immediately noticeable.

  • The fine lines that fan out from the corners of your eyes when you smile. These are caused by the orbicularis oculi muscle. Treatment softens the lines at rest while preserving your natural smile. Most patients see a visible difference within two weeks.

  • Bunny lines appear on the sides of your nose when you scrunch your face. Lip lines (sometimes called smoker's lines) form around the mouth from repeated pursing. Both respond well to small, precise injections. These are quick additions to a broader treatment session.
