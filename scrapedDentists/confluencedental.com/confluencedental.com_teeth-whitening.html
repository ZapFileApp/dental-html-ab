Why Professional Whitening?

Over-the-counter whitening strips and toothpastes can only do so much. They use low-concentration formulas that take weeks to show subtle results.

Professional whitening uses clinical-grade products that brighten your teeth by 6–8 shades in a single appointment.

The difference isn’t just strength. Your dentist customizes your treatment to your teeth, protects your gums, monitors sensitivity, and ensures even, natural-looking results. No white spots. No uneven patches. Just a noticeably brighter smile.

Book a consultation

Two Approaches to Fit Your Life

  • 60–90 minutes in our chair. Professional-grade whitening gel activated with a special light. Walk out the same day with a visibly brighter smile.

  • Custom-fitted trays made from impressions of your teeth. Apply professional-grade gel at home for 30–60 minutes daily over 1–2 weeks.

  • In-office for an immediate boost, take-home for ongoing maintenance. Maximum results.

What to Expect

  • 4–8 Shade Improvement

    Coffee, tea, and wine drinkers often see the most dramatic change.

  • Temporary Sensitivity

    Resolves within 24–48 hours. We can provide desensitizing treatment if needed.

  • Results Last 1–3 Years

    Depending on diet and habits. Touch-up treatments keep your smile bright between visits.
