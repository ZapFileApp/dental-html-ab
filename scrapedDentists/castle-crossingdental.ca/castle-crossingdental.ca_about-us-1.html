Welcome to castle Crossing Dental

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high quality dental care in a comfortable environment.  We strive to listen to our patients and provide care that is catered to their needs.  We care deeply about our patients and strive to help them maintain their oral health for a lifetime.

Meet Our Team

DR. fraser peterson DDS

Dr. Peterson graduated from the University of Alberta in 2015 with a Doctor of Dental Surgery Degree. He graduated top of his class and received the Alberta Dental Association and College Gold Medal as well as the 3M Clinical Award in Restorative Dentistry by demonstrating excellence in the use of restorative materials.


Dr. Peterson is passionate about providing the best possible care to his patients. He strives to listen to their concerns, find a solution that suits the patients needs, and carry out treatment to the highest standard of care. He enjoys continuing education where he can keep up with the latest techniques.


Outside of work he loves anything outdoors, travelling, and spending time with his wife and 3 children. 

DR. Michael French orthodontist

 

Dr. Michael French’s roots run deep in southern Alberta, he was born and raised in Cardston. Growing up on his family’s ranch near Hill Spring, he developed a strong work ethic and a love for the outdoors. After graduating high school in Cardston, Dr. French pursued his education in the United States, where he earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from Indiana University Dental School, graduating at the top of his class in 2003. He then completed his Orthodontic specialty training at Indiana University in 2005.


For nearly two decades, Dr. French has built a reputation for delivering exceptional orthodontic care in Calgary. Now, he’s thrilled to return to his small-town roots, providing high-quality orthodontic services to the Pincher Creek community and surrounding areas.


Outside the office, Dr. French enjoys spending time with his wife, their 8 children, 3 grandchildren, 9 horses, and 3 dogs. Dr. French looks forward to bringing his expertise and passion for orthodontics to the community

Lori, Dental Administrator

Lori is a dedicated and experienced dental professional, having embarked on her journey in the field with the successful completion of her education as a Registered Dental Assistant (RDA II) in 2005 from Columbia College. Lori is excited to support our team as a dental administrator. 


 She believes in fostering a compassionate and understanding environment, recognizing the importance of building trust with patients. Keeping pace with the dynamic landscape of dental trends is another aspect of her professional life that she values, as she is dedicated to staying current with the latest advancements in dental technology and practices.


Outside of the dental office, Lori's adventurous spirit comes to the forefront. She finds solace and excitement in activities such as hiking, backcountry camping, and cross-country skiing. Nature serves as her playground, and she often explores the great outdoors with her loyal companion – her dog. Lori's commitment to both professional excellence and a fulfilling personal life reflects her holistic approach to well-rounded living.
