How often should I schedule dental check-ups?

We suggest that you see a dentist near you once every six months for a routine dental check-up. This keeps your teeth clean, detects problems early, and ensures your overall oral health is in good shape.

Do you offer Myofunctional Therapy?

Yes, we provide myofunctional therapy in Fairview. Myofunctional therapy corrects abnormal tongue, jaw, and facial muscle habits that may impact breathing, speech, sleep, and bite alignment. It’s a useful treatment for both children and adults.
