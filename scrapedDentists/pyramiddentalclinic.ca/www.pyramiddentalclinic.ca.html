FAQ

How do I find a dentist in my area?

It is very important to find the right dentist for your comfort, confidence, and long-term health. In addition to a competent dentist, also consider a provider who offers a wide variety of services also has modern technology, transparent communication, and patient comfort in mind. Researching practice reviews, years in practice and availability are also helpful when selecting a dentist in your area.

What dental services do you provide?

We offer complete family and general dentistry, including preventive care, dental check-ups, dental cleanings, fillings, crowns, bridges, root canals, extractions, cosmetic dentistry, Invisalign, and restorative options, including implants. Regardless of the service, our team will provide a comfortable and low-stress experience for any level of dental care.

Are new patients welcome?

Yes, absolutely! We welcome all new patients, of any age. Whether you are new to the area or you want to switch to a new dentist who offers comfort and caring service in Fort McMurray, our team will assist you in making every patient feel at home from their very first visit.

Can I expect to receive immediate help if I have a dental emergency?

Yes! We provide quick and prompt emergency dental service for sore/aching tooth pain, infections, broken tooth, lost or displaced parts or any other urgent concern.

How do I set up a visit?

Booking an appointment is straightforward! You can call our clinic directly or you can complete our appointment request form online to select a time that is best for you. Our team will follow up to confirm the appointment and answer any questions.

Do you accept insurance in your clinic?

Yes, we accept most plans and also help our patients to understand their insurance coverage. We also offer direct billing whenever possible to create a positive visit for you without any hassle. We aim to provide access to quality care at our dental clinic in Fort McMurray.
