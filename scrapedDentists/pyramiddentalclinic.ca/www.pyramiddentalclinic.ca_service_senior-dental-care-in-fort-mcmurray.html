Senior Dental Care in Fort McMurray

Why Senior Dental Care Matters?

As we age, teeth and gums can become more susceptible to decay, infection, and other issues. Routine dental checkups help detect problems early, while preventive care like cleanings and fluoride treatments keep your mouth healthy. Proper dental care also supports better nutrition, speech, and self-confidence. We focus on helping seniors enjoy their golden years with strong, healthy smiles.

  • Prevents tooth loss & maintains a natural smile
  • Reduces the risk of gum disease & infections
  • Improves overall health & well-being
  • Enhances chewing, digestion & speech
  • Boosts confidence with a healthy, bright smile

Frequently Asked Questions

Dentists in Fort McMurray
What dental care do you provide for seniors?

We offer complete care with cleanings, exams, gum disease treatment, cavity fillings, denture care, and oral cancer screenings specific to senior needs.

Why do seniors need specialized dental care?
Dry mouth, gum recession, and tooth wear from age require the gentle, special care necessary to maintain oral and overall health.
Are you seeing patients with dentures or partials?
Yes. We do full and partial denture adjustments, repairs, and check-ups to ensure proper fit and function.
How frequently should elderly patients be visited by the dentist?
We suggest visiting the dentist every 6 months or more frequently if a senior has persistent conditions such as diabetes or gum disease.
Is insurance coverage available for senior dental care?
Most policies cover preventive services. We’re happy to help verify your benefits & guide you through your options.
