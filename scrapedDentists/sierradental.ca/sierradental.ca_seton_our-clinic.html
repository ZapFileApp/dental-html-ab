Modern Dental Excellence in South Calgary

Sierra Dental Seton has been a proud addition to the South Calgary community since 2022. As part of the established Sierra Dental Group, our Seton location combines decades of clinical heritage with a fresh, contemporary approach to oral health.

Leadership Driven by Care

Dr. Poorvi Brahmania leads our Seton team with a commitment to clinical excellence and patient comfort. Under her guidance, the clinic has become a cornerstone for families in the area, offering a high standard of personalized care in an environment designed to put patients at ease.

We Follow The ADA Fee Guide

At Sierra Dental in Seton, we are excited to welcome new patients and referrals. Our goal is to help you maintain a healthy smile for life. We take a personalized approach, getting to know you and your dental healthcare goals. When you visit our office, you will feel our passion and dedication to dentistry.

Our team works together to provide the best care possible, even after your appointment. We will follow up with you after your procedure to answer any questions you may have. We understand that dental visits can be inconvenient, which is why we offer flexible hours to fit your schedule. You can easily request an appointment online or give us a call.

We would love to meet you and provide you with an excellent dental experience. Contact us today to schedule your appointment.

Our Difference Makers

Open Late On Week Nights and Weekends.

We’re open until 8:30 pm on Tuesday and Wednesday, plus every other Saturday from 8 am to 3 pm, so dental visits don’t have to cost you a workday or pull your kids from school.

One Dental Home for Every Age.

We treat patients as young as one alongside their parents and grandparents, so your whole family sees the same trusted Seton team, year after year.

We Direct Billing to Insurance.

We submit claims to most major insurance providers on your behalf, so you only pay what isn’t covered. No waiting weeks for reimbursement cheques.

Anxiety-Free Dentistry.

From laughing gas to IV sedation, we offer the full range of sedation options, so even nervous patients and complex procedures feel calm from start to finish.
