Trusted Family Dentistry in Airdrie Since 2017

Sierra Dental has called Airdrie home for over eight years, building the kind of long-term patient relationships that come from taking the time to listen. Our Airdrie clinic pairs the clinical depth of the broader Sierra Dental Group with a local team that builds treatment plans around your needs, not a stopwatch.

Local Roots, Patient-First Care

Our Airdrie clinic is led by Dr. Vinay Sohal, born and raised in Calgary. Dr. Sohal completed his Bachelor’s degree in Biological Sciences at the University of Calgary before earning his dental degree at NYU College of Dentistry in New York.

Under his leadership, the Airdrie team delivers comprehensive care under one roof, general dentistry and hygiene, cosmetic dentistry, dental implants, periodontal procedures, root canal therapy, crowns and bridges, braces, and Invisalign, so Airdrie patients aren’t driving into Calgary for treatment their local clinic should already provide.

ADA Fee Guide Pricing is Standard at Our Clinic

At our Airdrie clinic, you shouldn’t need to guess what a procedure will cost before you commit to it. That’s why our pricing follows the Alberta Dental Association (ADA) Fee Guide, a publicly available document that sets a standard price for every recognized dental procedure in the province.

Working from a published fee guide means you can look up the cost of a service before your appointment, compare what we charge against any other Airdrie or Calgary clinic, and know exactly what you’re paying for. From a routine cleaning or set of X-rays to bigger work like dental implants or full-mouth rehabilitation, the numbers aren’t a mystery, they’re on a guide anyone can read.
