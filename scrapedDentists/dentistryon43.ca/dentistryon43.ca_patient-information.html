Welcome to Dentistry on 43!

New patients are always welcome

What to expect on your first visit

We know visiting a new dentist can feel a little nerve-wracking. That’s why we’ve designed your first visit to be comfortable, thorough, and stress-free.

Warm Welcome

From the moment you walk in, we greet you with a smile and make sure you’re comfortable while handling the paperwork. Grab a coffee, settle in, and know that you’re in good hands—this is the start of something great for your smile.

Comprehensive Exam

We take the time with you to learn about your dental history, concerns, and goals—because your smile journey is unique to you. We’ll do a thorough but gentle exam, explain everything we’re seeing in plain language, and answer any questions you have along the way.

Clear Next Steps

No surprises, no pressure—just honest conversation about what we found and what options make sense for you. Together, we’ll create a personalized treatment plan that fits your needs, timeline, and budget, so you can move forward with confidence.

New Patient Forms

Save time at your appointment by filling out your forms ahead of time. Complete them online, or feel free to arrive 15 minutes early and fill them out in our comfortable waiting area.

Insurance & Financing Made Easy

Insurance Billing Support

We’re happy to assist with insurance billing to help make your visit as smooth as possible. While we can submit claims on your behalf, every insurance plan is different, and coverage is determined entirely by your individual policy.

Because benefits vary from person to person, we can’t guarantee how your provider will process or reimburse your claim. You’re responsible for any portion not covered by your plan.

Just bring your insurance card to your first appointment, and we’ll help with the paperwork. If you’d like us to look into your coverage ahead of time, feel free to give us a call—our team can provide an estimate based on the information your insurance company shares with us.

Flexible Financing with Lendcare

We offer third-party financing through Lendcare with convenient monthly payments designed to fit your budget. Getting approved is quick and easy!

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is a first appointment?

Your first appointment typically takes about 60-90 minutes. This gives us plenty of time for your comprehensive exam, X-rays if needed, a discussion about your dental health and goals, and answering all your questions. We never rush—we want you to feel heard and comfortable.

Do you accept my insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers and offer direct billing to make things easy for you. Give us a call with your insurance information and we can check your coverage before your appointment. Even if you don’t have insurance, we have flexible payment options available.

What if I'm nervous about the dentist?

You’re not alone! Many of our patients have dental anxiety, and we’re well equipped to make nervous patients feel comfortable. We offer sedation options including nitrous oxide (laughing gas) and IV sedation. Our team takes things at your pace, explains everything before we do it, and creates a calming environment. Just let us know you’re nervous—we’ve got you!

Do you treat children?

Absolutely! We’re a family-friendly practice and love seeing kids of all ages. From your child’s first dental visit (usually around age 1-2) through their teen years and beyond, we provide gentle, age-appropriate care. We make dental visits fun and stress-free for little ones so they develop positive associations with the dentist.

Do you handle dental emergencies?

Yes! We prioritize emergency cases and will get you in as quickly as possible if you’re experiencing pain or have a dental emergency. Call our office right away and we’ll do everything we can to see you the same day. We’re here to help when you need us most.

How do I book my first appointment?

Booking is easy! You can call us at (780) 500-1001, book online through our website, or even send us a text. Our friendly team will find a time that works for your schedule and answer any questions you have. We can’t wait to meet you!
