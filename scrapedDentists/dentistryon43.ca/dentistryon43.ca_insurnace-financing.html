Questions? We've Got Answers

Here are the most common questions we hear about insurance and financing

Do you accept my insurance?

We work with most major insurance providers in Alberta and across Canada. Give us a call with your insurance info and we’ll verify your coverage before your appointment—usually within minutes!

What if I don't have insurance?

No insurance? No problem! We offer flexible financing through Lendcare with monthly payment plans that fit your budget. We can also provide treatment options at different price points so you can get the care you need.

How does direct billing work?

Direct billing means we submit your claim directly to your insurance company, and they pay their portion straight to us. You only pay your part at checkout—no need to pay upfront and wait for reimbursement!

Can LendCare be used for any dental procedure?

Yes, LendCare financing can be used for a wide range of dental procedures offered at Dentistry On 43, including but not limited to dental implants, cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and routine dental care. This flexibility allows you to access the dental care you need, when you need it.

How long do I have to pay back the loan?

The repayment term for a loan can vary depending on your specific agreement and your financial situation. Typically, loan terms can range from a few months up to 60 months, allowing you to choose a plan that best fits your budget.

How much will the loan cost?

LendCare offers healthcare loans of between $500 up to a maximum of $15000. The interest rate offered is between 14.99% and 29.99% depending on your creditworthiness, and your loan will be amortized over a 12 to 60-month period. You will receive a clear breakdown of costs from LendCare before accepting the offer.

Do I need to make a downpayment?

No, a down payment is not necessary to be approved for finance. However, if you’ve got a “rainy day” fund you’d like to use towards your treatment, this can help you reduce your overall repayment costs.

What if I have a less-than-perfect credit score?

LendCare considers various factors, not just your credit score, when assessing your loan application. We encourage you to apply even if your credit history is not perfect, as you may still qualify for financing.

Can I pay my loan off early?

Yes, you can pay off your loan early without any penalty in most cases. This flexibility allows you to manage your finances in a way that suits you best.
