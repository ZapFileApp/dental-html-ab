I was born and raised just outside of Beaverlodge, and I’m proud to be a small-town girl at heart. Growing up in a close-knit community taught me the importance of kindness, hard work, and supporting those around me.

Outside of work, I love camping, sewing, and spending quality time with my family. I also have a big love for Chinese food and never turn down a good meal shared with the people I care about.

I enjoy volunteering in my community and giving back whenever I can. I’m naturally cheerful and positive, and I love bringing that energy into the workplace and sharing it with both my team and our patients.
