Children’s Dentistry in Blackfalds

Your Friendly Neighbourhood Choice for Children’s Dentistry in Blackfalds, AB!

At Neighbourhood Dental, we are pleased to provide reliable, effective and affordable dental care for children of ALL ages and life stages. From infants all the way through the golden years, our Blackfalds clinic is committed to providing reliable dental care tailored to each patient’s specific needs. We understand that dental visits can be intimidating for little ones, and we try to address their concerns with understanding and empathy, always taking extra care to ensure that each child feels safe, comfortable and relaxed during their appointment. Our friendly team is dedicated to addressing your child’s specific dental needs while prioritizing a positive experience that helps instill enthusiasm and excitement, encouraging children to confidently and enthusiastically continue their oral health journey and build a lifetime of healthy habits.

Early Dental Visits Equal a Lifetime of Healthy Smiles!

At Neighbourhood Dental, we believe in the importance of starting children on their oral health journey early on. This helps to instill good dental care habits that last a lifetime. We follow the guidelines of the Canadian Dental Association, which recommends that children have their first dental visit within six months of their first tooth coming in, or by their first birthday, whichever happens first. We believe that introducing your child to dental care in a fun, supportive and welcoming environment will help alleviate any concerns or anxieties that children may have, and help to create a lifetime of healthy smiles and positive dental experiences. As parents ourselves, we are committed to doing everything we can to ensure that young patients feel as relaxed and at ease from the moment they walk through our doors. Whether it is your child’s first visit or they are seasoned patients, we are here to lay the foundation for a lifetime of good oral health, and we are here to support you and your family every step of the way.

Neighbourhood Dental is excited to provide dental care to children of all ages, and we always take special care to ensure that our youngest visitors feel comfortable and safe throughout their entire visit with us. If you have any questions about your child’s dental health or you would like to schedule an appointment with one of our dedicated team members, please call us (825) 221-1300 and we will be happy to help!
