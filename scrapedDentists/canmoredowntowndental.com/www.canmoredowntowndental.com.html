We're Your Dentist in Canmore

Welcome to Canmore Downtown Dental

Our team has been dedicated to providing personalized dental care since 1978, helping patients in Canmore reach their oral health goals.

We offer a variety of restorative, preventive, and cosmetic dental services and personalized treatment planning to help you achieve your healthiest, brightest smile.

Our office is conveniently located in the Canmore Professional Centre and offers free on-site parking. With same-day bookings, emergency appointments, and dental sedation, we strive to make every visit as comfortable and convenient as possible.
