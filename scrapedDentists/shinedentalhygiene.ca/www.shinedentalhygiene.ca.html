The Shine Difference #3

Touchless Airflow Cleaning

No scraping. No gritty polish.

Using a gentle stream of warm water, air and a fine cleaning powder, we remove stain, plaque and the sticky biofilm that makes your teeth feel fuzzy.

It’s comfortable, effective and reaches areas traditional polishing often misses. By removing surface stain, many patients leave with a noticeably brighter smile-almost like a mini whitening treatment.

The Shine Difference #4

Bubbles. Yep…lots of them.

Tiny Bubbles. Big Difference.

Our cleaning tips create thousands of tiny bubbles that gently loosen the more stubborn deposits on your teeth while making the cleaning process comfortable and efficient. Many patients are surprised by how smooth and fresh their teeth feel afterward.
