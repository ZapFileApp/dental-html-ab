General Anesthetic Near You

Do you dread a dental visit or worry that the treatment will be painful? Have you ever thought that it would have been better if you were completely asleep during your dental treatment and only woke up when it was finished? We at Gateway Dentistry Group provide general anesthetic in Grande Prairie to those patients who need it to help them feel totally calm and at ease during their dental care.

If you need a general anesthetic near you, we are a team that will be with you all the way to ensure you have a safe and stress-free experience.

What Is a General Anesthetic and How Does It Work?

A general anesthetic near you is a kind of sedation that, when administered for a dental treatment, puts the patient in a very deep sleep. The patient will be without pain, will not hear any sound, and will not have a memory of the treatment. It is a practice that is common for longer or more complicated surgeries or for those patients who have severe anxiety or special medical conditions.

The dentist in Grande Prairie works hand in hand with anesthesiologists who are very proficient in their area in order to ensure that the patient is safe during the whole treatment. We will do a check on your medical history and give a detailed explanation so that you feel comfortable and ready for the procedure.

Who Can Benefit from a General Anesthetic

General anesthetic in Grande Prairie may be recommended for:

  • Patients with severe dental anxiety or fear
  • Children who cannot sit still for long procedures
  • Individuals with special needs or medical conditions
  • Complex surgeries like wisdom tooth removal or full-mouth restoration
  • Anyone who prefers to be fully asleep during treatment

We’ll help you decide if a general anesthetic is the right choice based on your comfort level and dental needs.

What to Expect Before, During, and After

Here is the step-by-step process at Gateway Dentistry Group:

Before your visit
We will schedule a time to review your medical history and explain the general anaesthetic procedure.

During Treatment
A qualified professional will carefully administer your sedation. Our team will carry out your dental treatment while you are asleep.

Aftercare and Recovery
You will be in a less stressful area for the awakening process. Most patients are drowsy for a few hours and are able to return to their normal routine the following day.

FAQ's

Is general anesthetic safe for dental procedures?

Yes, when a general anesthetic is given by a well-trained and experienced group of professionals, it is a very safe procedure for the patient. We also keep an eye on your vital signs all the way through the treatment to make sure that you are safe. A comprehensive health check is done before the intervention.

How long does it take to recover from general anesthetic?

Some patients get really sleepy and feel drowsy for a couple of hours after the anesthesia effects wear off. You’ll have to ask for a ride home and rest for the day. Normally, everyone gets back to their usual state within 24 hours.

Will I feel anything during the procedure?

No, you’ll be completely asleep and won’t feel pain or discomfort. He/she will not be able to recall the treatment later as well. The whole procedure is designed in a way that it gives you a calm and peaceful experience.

Can children receive general anesthetic for dental work?

Yes, general anesthesia is always the option that is considered for pediatric patients when they are extremely scared or when the treatment is very complicated. Our team is very meticulous in ensuring their safety and a good experience during the treatment. We’ll get in touch with the parents before the treatment to give them a complete briefing.
