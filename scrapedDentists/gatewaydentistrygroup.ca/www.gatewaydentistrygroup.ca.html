Dr. Jeff

Falk

Dr. Jeff Falk was born and raised in Innisfail, Alberta. In 2002, he completed a Bachelor of Arts degree in General Studies after studying at Point Loma Nazarene University in San Diego, California and Nazarene University College (now Ambrose University College) in Calgary. He was accepted into the Doctor of Dental Surgery program at the University of Alberta and graduated in 2006. Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Gateway Dentistry Group provide?

Gateway Dentistry Group is a full-service dental clinic that offers preventive care, cosmetic dentistry, restorative dentistry, orthodontics, and much more to keep your smile looking beautiful and healthy.

Do you provide emergency dentistry?

Yes, we provide comprehensive emergency dental services for toothaches, broken teeth, and other urgent dental issues.

Is Gateway Dentistry Group a family-friendly clinic?

Absolutely! We accept patients of all ages and offer gentle care for children, parents, and seniors!

Where is Gateway Dentistry Group located?

Gateway Dentistry Group is located at 11002 104 Ave., Unit 201, Grande Prairie, AB T8V 7W5, Canada, which is conveniently located and easily accessible for you and your family.

Do you accept walk-in patients?

Yes, we take walk-in patients unless we are unable to do so due to appointments scheduled for that time. However, we do always recommend and appoint regular visits when you are able.

Can I get dental implants at Gateway Dentistry Group?

Yes, we offer dental implants to replace missing teeth to help you have a beautiful, healthy, functional smile!
