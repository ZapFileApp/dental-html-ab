Meet your Calgary Dentists at Redstone Family Dental

Dr. Thrilok Channabasappa

Dr. Thrilok Channabasappa is a gentle and skilled dentist who prioritizes patient comfort. Originally from India, he continued his dental education in Canada, bringing a global perspective to his practice. He is certified in advanced sedation methods, including oral sedation and laughing gas, to ensure a calm experience. Dr. Channabasappa believes in clear communication, taking time to listen and explain procedures so patients feel confident in their care. His advanced training includes dental implants, porcelain veneers, and oral surgery, such as the extraction of impacted wisdom teeth. Outside of dentistry, he enjoys cricket, golf, and spending time with his family.

Schedule Appointment

Dr. Gurshant Grewal

Dr. Gurshant Grewal is a registered dentist who is dedicated to his practice. In 2013, he graduated from Boston University’s Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine. Prior to completing his Doctorate, he obtained a Bachelor of Science with a major in Microbiology at the University of California in Berkeley. As a successful entrepreneur, he is the co-founder of Peak Dental Group, a corporation committed to building new practices and partnering with other medical professionals through Alberta and Ontario.

Schedule Appointment

Dr. Sameet Gill

Dr. Sammy Gill was born and raised in Edmonton, Alberta. She earned her Bachelor of Physical Science from the University of Alberta before completing her Doctor of Dental Medicine at Boston University’s Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine. It was the heart of Albertans that brought her back. Dr. Gill built her practice on a simple belief: every patient deserves care that is thorough, compassionate, and worth looking forward to. She has researched and implemented advanced dental technology across her clinics, with a focus on pediatric and restorative dentistry, helping kids feel at ease in the chair and reconstructing beautiful smiles for patients of all ages.
