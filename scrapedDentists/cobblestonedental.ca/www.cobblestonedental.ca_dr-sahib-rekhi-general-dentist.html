Dr. Sahib Rekhi

Originally from Toronto, Ontario, Dr. Sahib Rekhi completed his Bachelor of Science at Western University. He then moved to Sydney, Australia to complete his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree at the University of Sydney.

Growing up around family and friends in the dental field led Dr. Rekhi to join the field, with an interest in Oral Surgery and Implant Dentistry. Some of his hobbies include snowboarding, motorsport, and going to the gym. Dr. Rekhi strives to provide high-quality and caring dental services to the patients of Grande Prairie, and he looks forward to seeing you at your next visit!
