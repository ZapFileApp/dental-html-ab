Understanding Dental Fillings

Dental fillings are used to repair teeth that have been damaged by cavities, fractures, or wear. The process involves removing the decayed or damaged portion of the tooth and filling it with a suitable material to restore its shape, function, and integrity. Fillings are essential for preventing further decay and maintaining overall oral health.

Benefits of Dental Fillings

  • Restored Tooth Function: Fillings repair cavities and restore the normal function of your teeth, allowing you to chew and speak properly.
  • Natural Appearance: Modern filling materials can be matched to the color of your natural teeth, providing a seamless look.
  • Durable and Long-Lasting: High-quality fillings are designed to be strong and long-lasting, withstanding the forces of chewing.
  • Prevent Further Decay: By sealing off cavities, fillings prevent bacteria from entering and causing further damage to your teeth.

Efficient and Comfortable Filling Procedure

Our filling procedures are designed to be efficient and comfortable, ensuring a positive experience for our patients in High River. The steps include:

  1. Anesthesia Administration: We administer local anesthesia to ensure the area is completely numb for a pain-free experience.
  2. Decay Removal: The decayed portion of the tooth is carefully removed, and the cavity is cleaned and prepared for the filling.
