Welcome to Yankee Valley Dental

At Yankee Valley Dental, we care about more than just your teeth – we care about your peace of mind. Our Airdrie dentists balance exceptional skills with a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you are new to the area, looking for a long-term dental clinic, or visiting us for a routine check-up, our mission is to help you feel comfortable.

Conveniently located at #108, 960 Yankee Valley Blvd SE, our state-of-the-art dental clinic in Airdrie is structured around your busy schedule. We proudly provide top-tier care to patients of all ages, ensuring your family feels comfortable, heard, and completely at ease from the moment you walk through our doors.

Why Families Trust Our Dental Clinic in Airdrie

We’ve built our practice around the needs of our local community. Here is how we ensure an exceptional patient experience:

  • Convenient Evening & Weekend Hours: Your family shouldn’t have to miss work or school for dental health. We offer extended hours, including Saturdays, to fit your busy life.
  • Direct Insurance Billing: We take the stress out of paperwork by directly billing your insurance provider. We even communicate with them beforehand, so you know exactly what is covered.
  • Transparent Pricing: Financial transparency matters. Yankee Valley Dental proudly follows the current Alberta Dental Association Fee Guide to keep quality dental care accessible.
  • The Extra Touch Care: Dr. Parsa provides her personal phone number after major treatments like root canals, extractions, or implants, so you’re never left without direct guidance over the weekend.
  • Comfort for All Ages: From an enthusiastic, cheerful first visit for your little ones to gentle, tailored care for adults and seniors, we cater to all generations under one roof.
  • Modern Technology & Anxiety-Free Care: Our clinic utilizes up-to-date diagnostic tools and patient comforts to deliver precise treatments while alleviating dental anxiety.
