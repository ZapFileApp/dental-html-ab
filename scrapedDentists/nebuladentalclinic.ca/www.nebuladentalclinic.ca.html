Welcome to Nebula Dental - Your South Edmonton Dental Clinic

Our Friendly Team is Happy to Speak to You in English, Arabic, Tagalog, Punjabi, Hindi, Urdu or Korean.

Now Welcoming new Patients!

We’re excited to welcome new patients to our dental family. When you choose us, you’re choosing a team of experienced professionals who are passionate about oral health. We offer comprehensive dental services, the latest technology, and a warm, welcoming environment.

Nebula Dental Clinic is located in the beautiful neighborhood of Summerside. We also cater to the neighbourhoods of Charlesworth, Ellerslie, the Orchards and the surrounding areas. Your smile is our top priority and our entire team is dedicated to providing you with the personalized, quality dental care that you deserve. We’re accepting new patients and look forward to serving you, so please give us a call or request an appointment online; we will see you soon!

Become A Patient Now!

We take your comfort as seriously as your smile!

Looking for a new dental clinic that values your smile as much as you do? Look no further. Our clinic is the perfect destination for discerning patients like you, who prioritize quality care, cutting-edge technology, and a wide range of services.

We also offer flexible scheduling, personalized care, direct billing to most insurance providers, and 0% interest payment options!

Comfort Focused Facilities and Equipment

From the moment you step into our clinic, you’ll notice the difference. Our modern, clean, and comfortable environment is designed with you in mind.

We’ve equipped our clinic with the latest dental technologies, including digital X-ray machines, 3D imaging systems, and dental lasers. This means faster diagnoses, more precise treatments, and less time in the dental chair.

Comprehensive Dental Services

Whether you need a routine check-up, a complex restorative procedure, or want to enhance your smile with cosmetic dentistry, we’ve got you covered.

Our team of highly skilled professionals is committed to providing comprehensive dental care under one roof. We offer everything from preventive care and oral health education to advanced dental procedures and emergency services.

Personalized Care for Every Patient

At our clinic, we believe that every patient is unique. That’s why we take the time to get to know you, understand your dental goals, and develop a personalized treatment plan that meets your needs. We’re not just about fixing teeth but about building relationships based on trust and mutual respect.

If you’re ready for a dental experience that puts you at the center, we invite you to join our dental family. Schedule an appointment today and discover why we’re the preferred choice for discerning patients. We can’t wait to help you achieve the healthy, radiant smile you deserve. Welcome to your new dental home!

Comfort Dentistry

Nebula Dental Clinic is focused on COMFORT DENTISTRY, which means we are dedicated to ensuring your visit is as comfortable as possible from the moment you walk in until you leave. Experience a welcoming reception area with complimentary water, Netflix and Nintendo switch for the kids to stay entertained, low-noise equipment, and more — we do everything we can to make your dental visit comfortable.

We offer direct billing to most insurance providers. Ask us about our 0% interest payment options!
