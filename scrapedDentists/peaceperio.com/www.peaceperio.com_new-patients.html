New Patient Information

All New Patients Welcome!

Thank you for considering Peace Periodontics for your periodontal and family dental care requirements. We are pleased to welcome new patients and look forward to meeting you – no referral is required!

To make your first visit run as smoothly as possible, we ask all new patients to complete the following intake forms before your visit with us. You can save yourself time at your first visit by downloading and completing the form below.

Patientform

When You Visit Us

It is important to us that your visit feels friendly and welcoming. That is why when you visit Peace Periodontics, we want you to feel as if you are part of our extended family. From the moment you enter our office and are greeted by our friendly dental administrators to the time you are seen by our Periodontal Team, your comfort and patient experience are our top priority.

We will always try to keep your visit as efficient as possible, ensuring that it starts on time and ends on time. We always follow up every visit so that you can provide us with your feedback on how we can further improve on meeting the needs of ALL our patients. We will follow-up with you after every visit to obtain your feedback on how we can further improve your experience at our office.

At Peace Periodontics, we are always welcoming new patients. Contact us to book your consultation appointment today!
