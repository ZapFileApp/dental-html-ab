Welcome to Peace Periodontics

Your Grande Prairie Periodontist!

Peace Periodontics is committed to providing professional periodontal care to Grande Prairie patients and their families. Our office is located on the main floor of the Nordic Court building on 99 Street in Grande Prairie.

Peace Periodontics has been practicing in Grande Prairie since 1986.
Dr. Ward Piepgrass and Dr. Michael Piepgrass are Board Certified Periodontists who is pleased to offer complete periodontal, surgical and dental implant services to both referred and non-referred patients.

Doctors Piepgrass and their Grande Prairie Periodontal team want to
help provide you and your family with the detailed periodontal care and treatment education that is needed for a lifetime of dental health.

Most patients are referred to our Grande Prairie Periodontal clinic by their general dentist. However, a referral is not necessary. Simply contact us and to set up your periodontal consultation.

Meet Dr. Ward Piepgrass

DDS, MSC, DIP PERIO, FRCD(C)

Dr. Ward Piepgrass was born in Lethbridge, Alberta and spent most of his childhood on a farm near Rich Valley Alberta. Dr. Piepgrass graduated from the University of Alberta with Bachelor of Science and Doctor Of Dental Surgery degrees.

Dr. Piepgrass opened a dental practice in 1986 in Grande Prairie, Alberta. After practicing general dentistry for 18 years, Dr. Piepgrass relocated to Lexington, Kentucky for three years of specialty training in Periodontics and Dental Implant Surgery. He graduated earning a Masters Of Science Degree and Certificate in Periodontology. He is a Diplomate of the American Board of Periodontology and a Fellow of the Royal College of Dentists Of Canada.
