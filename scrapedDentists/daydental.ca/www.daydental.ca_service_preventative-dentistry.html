Preventative
Dentistry

Protecting Oral Wellness
Through Preventative Dentistry

Preventative dentistry involves a proactive approach to maintaining oral health by focusing on preventive measures to avoid dental issues before they arise. Through early protection and intervention, we aim to preserve your natural teeth, promote overall oral wellness, and minimize the need for more extensive treatments in the future.

NIGHT
GUARDS

Night guards are custom designed oral appliances that provide protection and relief for individuals who clench or grind their teeth during sleep. By creating a cushioning barrier between the upper and lower teeth, these guards prevent teeth grinding, minimize wear, and alleviate associated discomfort. Night guards promote restful sleep and safeguard dental health, ensuring a refreshed and pain-free morning.

SPORTS
MOUTH GUARDS

Mouth guards are custom designed protective devices that protect you during physical activities. These guards shield teeth, gums, and jaws from potential impacts, promoting dental well-being and enabling worry-free participation in sports.

Don’t wait a moment longer, protect your teeth by booking an appointment with Day Dental today.
