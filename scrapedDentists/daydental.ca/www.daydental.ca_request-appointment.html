Scam Alert

We’ve been made aware of misleading online ads and messages falsely claiming that Day Dental offers free or grant-funded dental implants. These promotions are not affiliated with our clinic in any way. We do not provide dental implant grants, free treatments, or payments to patients for participating in any implant program.

While we do offer dental implants at Day Dental, all treatment is paid for by the patient or their insurance/financing options.

If you’ve seen one of these ads or were directed to contact us under false pretenses, please report it and reach out to our office at 403-227-0228.
