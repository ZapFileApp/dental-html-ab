Meet Our Team

Meet the Team Behind Your Healthy Smile

At TruSmile Dental, our team is here to support your oral health at every visit. We believe good dental care starts with trust, clear communication, and gentle treatment. Each member of our team is trained to provide safe, professional care using simple steps that are easy to understand.

We take time to explain your treatment, answer your questions, and help you feel comfortable in the dental chair. Our goal is to help you keep your teeth and gums healthy for life.

Clinical Team

Our clinical team works closely with patients to provide preventive care, early diagnosis, and appropriate dental treatment. We focus on maintaining oral hygiene, preventing gum disease, and protecting your natural teeth.

Meet The Doctor

Dr. Gurmukh Singh Sahi

Dentist & Founder

Dr. Gurmukh Singh Sahi is the founder of TruSmile Dental and brings years of experience in comprehensive dental care. He has trained under some of North America’s leading dentists and orthodontic specialists, making him a trusted choice for families looking for personalized, high-quality dental and orthodontic care. Before any treatment, Dr. Sahi carefully examines your teeth, gums, and bite to ensure you receive optimal care. He also emphasizes gum health and proper alignment to help keep your mouth comfortable and healthy. He speaks Punjabi, English, Hindi, Urdu, Spanish, and Tagalog, helping patients from all backgrounds feel at ease. If you are looking for a dentist near you, Dr. Sahi is ready to provide professional care with a personal touch. He believes every patient deserves a smile that is both healthy and confident.

Why Choose Our Team

A strong dental team helps you get better results. At TruSmile Dental, we work together to:

Keep your teeth clean and healthy

Detect dental problems early

Provide safe and simple treatments

Make every visit comfortable

Regular visits and asking questions during your appointment can help you better understand your oral health.

We Are Here for You

We understand that visiting the dentist can feel stressful for some people. That’s why our team focuses on clear communication, gentle care, and patient comfort.

Whether you need a routine cleaning, dental exam, or treatment, our team is ready to help you every step of the way.

At TruSmile Dental, your smile is in safe hands.
