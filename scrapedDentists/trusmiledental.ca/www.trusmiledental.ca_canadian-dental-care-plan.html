Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to file my taxes to stay on the plan?

Yes. To renew your coverage during the April 15 – June 1 window, you must have filed your 2025 tax return. The government uses your Notice of Assessment to confirm you still meet the income requirements.

Can I use my CDCP benefits on a Sunday?

Absolutely. Because TruSmile Dental is open 7 days a week, you can consult us when looking to book appointments for Canadian Dental Care Plan Near You on weekends to fit your schedule.

What if I have a child who needs "Early Orthodontic Treatment"?

We believe in early intervention. While the CDCP is restrictive with orthodontics, we can perform a diagnostic exam and, if the case meets the government’s “medical necessity” criteria, we will submit a request for your coverage through the pre-authorization process.

Why is "Guided Biofilm Therapy" better for CDCP patients?

Since many CDCP patients may have gone years without dental cleaning, traditional scaling can be uncomfortable. GBT uses advanced technology to remove plaque gently, making it the perfect “re-entry” into regular dental care.
