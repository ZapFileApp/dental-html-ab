What Is Invisalign?

Invisalign is a type of orthodontic treatment that uses clear plastic aligners to move your teeth into the right position. These aligners are custom-made to fit your teeth. They apply gentle pressure to shift your teeth gradually over time.

Unlike traditional braces, Invisalign does not use metal brackets or wires. The aligners are smooth, which many patients find more comfortable in the mouth. You can also remove your Invisalign aligners when eating, brushing, and flossing. This helps you maintain good oral hygiene during treatment.

Each set of aligners is typically worn for about one to two weeks. After that, you switch to the next set. Step by step, your teeth move into a better position. Our dental team will check your progress and guide you at every stage.

Who Is an Ideal Candidate for Invisalign?

Invisalign is a good option for many patients. You may be a suitable candidate if you have:

Crooked or crowded teeth

Gaps between teeth

Overbite, underbite, or crossbite

Mild to moderate alignment issues

Invisalign works best for patients who follow instructions carefully. Wear your aligners throughout the day and remove them only during meals and oral hygiene. You should wear the aligners for 20 to 22 hours each day. This helps keep your treatment on track and may support more efficient results. If you often forget or remove them too frequently, treatment may take longer.

Our dentist will examine your teeth and gums before recommending Invisalign in Huntington Hills. Healthy gums and teeth are important for safe orthodontic treatment.

Benefits of Invisalign

Our Invisalign in Huntington Hills can provide many benefits, including:

Clear Appearance

The aligners are transparent, so they are hard to see.

Comfortable Fit

Smooth plastic may help reduce irritation to cheeks and gums.

Removable

You can take them out while eating or brushing.

Better Oral Hygiene

It is easier to clean your teeth compared to braces.

Fewer Dental Visits

There may be less need for frequent adjustments.

Predictable Results

Digital planning helps show how your teeth may move.

Many patients find it easy to fit Invisalign into their daily routine. You can eat your regular meals without worrying about damaging wires or brackets.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will Invisalign affect my speech?

You may notice a small change in speech for a few days. Most patients adjust quickly and resume speaking normally.

Can I drink while wearing Invisalign?

It is recommended to remove aligners when drinking anything other than water. Beverages can stain or damage the aligners.

What happens if I lose an aligner?

Contact our dental clinic as soon as possible. Our team will guide you on whether to use a previous or next aligner.

Do I need to visit the dentist often during treatment?

Yes, you will have regular check-ups, but typically fewer visits than with traditional braces.
