Dentistry built on family, learning, and long term care

We believe the feel of a dental office matters. It shapes first impressions, affects comfort, and helps set the tone for the entire visit. At Montrose Dental Care, we have worked to create an environment that feels friendly, organized, and supportive, a place where families can come for routine care, more complex treatment, and long term relationships with a team they trust.

Take a virtual tour

Our office is here to serve Beaumont and surrounding communities with comprehensive dental care in one convenient location. Whether you are visiting for a checkup, bringing in your child, exploring treatment options, or coming in with an urgent concern, our goal is to make the experience feel clear and welcoming from start to finish. Montrose presents itself as a Beaumont practice offering a wide range of general dental services, with a team focused on assessing patient needs and providing care for families over time.
