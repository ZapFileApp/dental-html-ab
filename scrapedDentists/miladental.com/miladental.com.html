Comprehensive Dental Care at Mila Dental

Mila Dental at Cochrane provides high-quality comprehensive dental care in a modern atmosphere, using advanced technology and techniques.

OUR SERVICES

General Dentistry

Comprehensive care covering routine exams, fillings, and essential treatments to maintain your overall oral health.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with personalized treatments like teeth whitening, veneers, and other aesthetic enhancements.

Cleaning and Prevention

Regular cleanings, exams, and preventive care fo your healthy smile

Endodontics

Specialized care to save and protect damaged teeth with root canals.

Orthodontics

Enhance your smile and bite with personalized orthodontic solutions. We offer clear aligners and other treatments tailored to your specific needs.

Oral Surgery

Expert surgical procedures, including wisdom tooth extractions, to ensure optimal oral health and function.
