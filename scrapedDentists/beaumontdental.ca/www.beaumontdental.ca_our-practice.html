Our Practice

At Beaumont Dental Centre, we are committed to providing our patients with compassionate and professional dental care of the highest standards. In our comfortable and relaxed environment we utilize the most current dental technologies and techniques to help you achieve and maintain a smile that you can be proud of.

On behalf of Beaumont Dental Centre, we welcome you to our practice and look forward to helping you and your family preserve a lifetime of healthy teeth and bright smiles! Take a minute to meet our team of dental professionals.

Our Team

Dr. Roger Eamer

Dr. Roger Eamer received his doctor of dental surgery (D.D.S) degree from the University of Alberta. Following graduation, he served as a clinical officer at the Edmonton Board of Health providing dental care for children from families-in-need. Dr. Eamer was also an assistant clinical professor in the Department of Restorative Dentistry at the University of Alberta. He taught part-time for eleven years while building a private practice in both Edmonton and Beaumont, where he currently practices.

Dr. Marjorie Eamer

Dr. Marjorie Eamer received her doctor of dental surgery (D.D.S) degree from the University of Alberta. After completion of dental school, she held several associateships in Edmonton until starting her practice in Beaumont. With a strong focus on prevention, she wants to ensure lifelong oral health. Dr. Eamer is committed to quality patient care and enjoys spending time getting to know each of her patients. Her goal is to provide a positive dental experience using proven up-to-date techniques and technologies and to make sure her patients are comfortable at every visit.
