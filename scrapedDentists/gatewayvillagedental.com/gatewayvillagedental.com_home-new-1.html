At Gateway Village Dental, we offer a wide range of services to take care of every dental health need. From kids
to grandparents, preventive care to emergency treatments, we offer Alberta families everything you’ll need to
maintain and achieve optimal dental health.

preventive dentistry

Brushing and flossing are your best defense against dental health issues, but preventive care adds another layer of protection. Routine cleanings, fluoride treatments, and oral cancer screenings keep your smile healthy and strong.

don’t skip routine visits

cosmetic dentistry

If you find yourself in front of the mirror wishing that your smile was whiter, straighter, or more esthetically pleasing in any way… It can be! Our cosmetic dentistry options will enhance your smile and restore your confidence.

spruce up your smile
