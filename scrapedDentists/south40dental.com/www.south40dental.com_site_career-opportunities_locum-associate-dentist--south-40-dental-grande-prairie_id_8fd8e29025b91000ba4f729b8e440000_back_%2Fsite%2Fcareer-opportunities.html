Locum Associate Dentist - South 40 Dental Grande Prairie

Locum Associate Dentist – South 40 Dental (Grande Prairie, AB)

Coverage Dates: July 9th and 10th

High-demand clinic • Excellent team culture • Modern technology

About the Opportunity

South 40 Dental is seeking an experienced locum Associate Dentist to join a high‑volume, well‑established practice in Grande Prairie. The clinic is known for its long‑term team, excellent culture, strong patient loyalty, and demand that consistently fills schedules weeks in advance.

This position offers an already‑established patient base and full clinical schedule. The practice features strong technological investment, a large and efficient workspace, and a highly supportive team including experienced RDAs and senior dentists.

Schedule Needing Coverage

  • Thursday: 8:00am – 5:00pm
  • Friday: 8:00am – 4:00pm

Additional Details:

  • 2 DDS chairs allocated to the associate
  • No independent work required — clinical support available
  • RDA support at all times

Required Clinical Skills

  • Bread‑and‑butter dentistry
  • Basic endodontics
  • Basic oral surgery
  • Fixed prosthodontics (crown and bridge)

Additional Skills Not Required but Beneficial

  • Openness to advanced technologies
  • Comfort with new workflows and systems

Sedation Offered

The practice provides a full range of sedation options:

  • Nitrous sedation
  • Oral sedation
  • IV sedation

Technology & Software

  • Dentrix PMS
  • CBCT
  • iOS
  • Panoramic and ceph imaging
  • Soft tissue laser
  • Digital X‑ray
  • Paperless charting
  • Fotona laser

This is a modern and well‑equipped clinic with significant investment in technology and digital workflows.

What Makes South 40 Dental Unique

  • Busy, high‑demand practice with a steady patient base
  • Long‑term, highly experienced support staff
  • Strong team culture built on collaboration and patient‑first care
  • Modern facility with a large footprint and updated equipment
  • Three dentists onsite, fostering a supportive environment
  • Convenient Grande Prairie location with high visibility

Ideal Candidate

  • Experienced dentist
  • Strong in bread‑and‑butter dentistry
  • Excellent communicator and team collaborator
  • Patient‑centric and dedicated to high‑quality care
  • Open to feedback and new clinical technologies
  • Professional, reliable, and committed to long‑term practice success


About You:

  • Graduate from an accredited DDS/DMD Program or successful completion of the National Dental Examining Board of Canada (NDEB) Equivalency Process

  • Registered and in good standing with provincial licensing requirements

  • Valid CPR certification

All applicants must be domiciled in Canada. We thank all applicants, but only suitable applicants will be contacted. Applicants with a disability who require reasonable accommodation for any part of the application process and thereafter can contact our Talent Team at 416-558-8338. Reasonable accommodations will be determined on a case-by-case basis and your request will be responded to as soon as possible.
