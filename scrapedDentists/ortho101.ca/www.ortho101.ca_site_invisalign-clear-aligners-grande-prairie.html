The Invisalign Process

The team at Ortho 101 in Grande Prairie will guide you through the Invisalign treatment process.

Step 1

Discuss Your Goals

One of our orthodontists will discuss how Invisalign can help your smile with you.

Step 2

Get Your 3D Treatment Plan

We'll take a 3D scan of your teeth and share a preview of your new smile with you.

Step 3

Your orthodontist will contact you when your aligners are ready so you can pick them up and get started.
