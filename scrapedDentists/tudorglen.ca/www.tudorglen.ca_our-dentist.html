Dr. Brian Croutze, General Dentist

Dr. Brian Croutze is a graduate of the University of Alberta and a long time resident of St. Albert. In recent years, Dr. Croutze has volunteered with the non-profit Kindness in Action to provided dental services in remote areas of Peru and Guatemala. Apart from dentistry Dr. Croutze enjoys travel, the outdoors, and relaxing with family and friends.
