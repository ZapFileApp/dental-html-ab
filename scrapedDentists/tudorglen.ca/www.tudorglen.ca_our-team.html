our team

Our dental team consists of a number of full time and part time staff, many of whom have been with us for over a decade. Family is important to our whole team, and some of Dr. Brian Croutze’s family members even work at our clinics. Brian’s wife Stephanie works in our clinic as a dental assistant and his sister Cassandra is the practice’s business manager.

We feel very fortunate to work with such wonderful people as well as to serve the families in our community.

Cassandra – Business Manager

Joining our team in October 2015, Cassandra supports the daily operations at both clinics. She assists with the bookkeeping and general administrative tasks around the offices. A graduate of the U of A with a degree in Economics, she enjoys working with numbers and is eager to learn more about the family business.

Outside of work, Cassandra can be found playing piano or enjoying a round of golf.

Saffa, Administrator

Saffa, our full time Administrator, has been in the dental field for over 10 years. As an advocate for patient care, she is here to welcome you to our clinic, schedule appointments, clarify and optimize insurance coverage and answer any questions you may have.

Saffa has been married for 17 years and has five beautiful children. In her free time, she loves to bake with her children and enjoys family movie nights.

Adala – Dental Assistant

Adala, a graduate of the SAIT Dental Assisting program, has been working in the dental field as a Registered Dental Assistant for 16 years. She joined our clinic in 2014.

She is a very busy mom with three lovely daughters and two dogs. When she has time to have some fun, you will find her at the arena playing hockey or cheering on her girls on the ice or at the ball diamond. Her favorite thing to do is spend time with her family at her cabin relaxing!

Stephanie – Dental Assistant

Stephanie received her BA from University of Alberta as well as her Dental Assisting Certificate at NAIT. Recently, Stephanie went with Dr. Brian on a Kindness in Action trip to Guatemala, where they provided dental care for children in rural, undeveloped areas.

Outside of dentistry, Stephanie enjoys a variety of outdoor activities, in addition to spending lots of time with her family and friends.

Elena – Dental Assistant

Elena is an RDA Level II with Orthodontic Module from the University of Alberta. She has been working at our clinic as a Registered Dental Assistant since 2009.

When she is not at work, Elena enjoys being outdoors, swimming, reading, and spending time with her family and friends.

Janet – Hygienist

Janet graduated from the George Brown College Dental Hygiene program, and has worked as a Registered Dental Hygienist in our clinic since 2008. She is energetic and passionate about her work.

Janet is a lifelong athlete with a passion for tennis, golf and fitness training. She is also an avid gardener and enjoys relaxing and recharging with her family in her backyard oasis.

Yana – Hygienist

Yana graduated with a B.Sc in Biological Science at the University of Alberta, where she went on to complete her Dental Hygiene Diploma. She has worked as a Registered Dental Hygienist at our clinic since 2018. She enjoys getting to know her patients and is dedicated to providing client-centered care to ensure her patients leave with a positive dental experience.

When she is not in the office, Yana enjoys spending time with her sons and her family, along with traveling the world and escaping to the mountains.

Tracie – Hygienist

Tracie graduated from Cambrian College in Sudbury, Ontario and moved to Alberta upon graduation in 2006. She enjoys building meaningful relationships with her patients and has a passion for providing gentle care and making a difference in people’s lives. Tracie has been at our clinic since 2022.

In her free time, Tracie loves heading to the mountains and visiting Vancouver Island. She enjoys spending time with her husband and cat and cheering on the Oilers!
