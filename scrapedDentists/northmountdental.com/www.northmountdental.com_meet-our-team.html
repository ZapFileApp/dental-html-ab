Nashila

Registered Dental Assistant II, Preventive Dentistry Module

Nashila was born in Dar Es Salaam, Tazania. She moved to Toronto in 1987 and completed her Grade 13, OAC at Coxwell High School in Toronto before attending Ryerson University. In 1992 she said goodbye to the East and hello to the mountains when she moved to Calgary. Here she attended Columbia College and completed her RDA II in 1993.

In 2008, Nashila completed the PDA (Preventive Dental Module), adding more weight to her resumé. She believes in working with you as a patient, and strives to be a part of improving your health, and this belief has made her a valuable asset at Northmount Dental Care. She also worked with the previous owners of the practice for 20 years and has built a wonderful long-term relationship with many of the clinic's patients.

"I don't think a lot of us are aware how much our mouths contribute to our overall health."

Bring your dental concerns to Nashila and benefit from her wealth of knowledge and experience. Nashila is married and has two children and in her free time she enjoys reading and spending time with her family.

Sarah

Registered Dental Hygienist

Sarah graduated Dental Hygiene with honours from Niagara College in 2002. Brining close to 15 years of experience to the team at Northmount; she has had the opportunity to practice Dental Hygiene in Ontario, British Columbia and even Switzerland before she arrived in Alberta ten years ago. (She can even speak a little bit of German in a pinch!)

She is passionate about providing the best care possible to every patient she meets, and prides herself on gentle comprehensive care and putting everyone at ease in her chair.

"I feel that the foundations of good dental hygiene care begin with communication and education, I am happy to take the time to help our patients really understand the importance of optimal oral health and how it can impact our lives systemically and esthetically."

Sarah grew up on a horse farm near Barrie, Ontario. Sarah has a wonderful, loving husband and 2 amazing kids who bring her joy everyday. She loves spending time with her family, gardening, camping, travelling and listening to all kinds of music.

Whitney

Registered Dental Assistant II 

Whitney graduated from the Dental Assisting program at CDI College in 2021 and joined Northmount Dental Care as a temp shortly after, which then led to securing a full time position in 2023. Whitney was born and raised in Saskatchewan, and moved to Calgary in 2012 and previously worked as a Medical Office Assistant before deciding she wanted to pursue a career in dentistry.

Whitney has worked in the customer service industry since she was 14, and loves meeting and engaging in getting to know her customers and patients. Whitney finds that there is always a lesson or a story to learn from anyone and every she meets, that is one of her main reasons she continues to work with the general public.The dental treatment she had received over the years makes it easy to help bring ease and comfort into a field where most are anxious.

Whitney is excited to work not only with the amazing and supporting team at Northmount Dental, but to be able to learn and continue growing her knowledge in the dental field alongside knowledgeable dentists and team members.
