Calgary Dentists with a Comprehensive Service Offering

From a small child that requires teeth cleaning to an adult with missing teeth, in need of dental implants, we welcome every one of our patients with a warm smile – and we make sure they leave with one too!

Northmount Dental Care offers a comprehensive range of family dental care services, provided by our general dentists Dr. Sonya Verma, Dr. Rahul Kulshrestha and Dr. Shawn Verma.

In addition to family dental care, the dentists at our Northwest Calgary clinic also offer cosmetic dentistry with the aim of giving you a more attractive, healthy smile.

New Patients are Always Welcome

We welcome new patients and will strive to make you feel immediately at ease. And since we understand that you may be hesitant about changing dentists, we offer two complimentary appointment types for new patients.

“Providing general, Cosmetic and family dentistry in NW Calgary. Dentist near Ranchlands, Dalhousie, Edgemont, and Brentwood (Calgary, Alberta)”.

Dr. Sonya Verma and Dr. Rahul Kulshrestha, a husband-and-wife team, are the Calgary dentists (general dentists) at Northmount Dental Care. They all believe that a personal and friendly connection with you, set in a comfortable environment, will establish a strong relationship that will lead to improved oral care.

Your Patient Care Team

Each member of our team is looking forward to seeing you at your next appointment. Whether you've been a patient for years or are booking your first appointment or complimentary consultation, here is an opportunity for you to learn more about the staff of Northmount Dental Care.

The environment that we've created at Northmount Dental Care is one that's stress-free, welcoming and built upon high standards in customer care. Every member of our team strives to maintain this environment with each interaction we have with you, from when you first step in the door to when you leave with a smile.

Our team of dentists and hygienists pride themselves on offering this exceptional level of care, and cultivating a real personal connection with you at every appointment. That's why we always ensure enough time is booked for us to patiently discuss your needs, allow the dentists to see you (even for routine hygiene appointments), and for you to ask questions.

These experiences are important to you, and to us as well.
