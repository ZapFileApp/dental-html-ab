Oral Exams &
Professional Cleanings

Stay comfortable while we care for your teeth

We love our clients and if we can help to reduce your time spent in the dentist chair with us then that’s a good thing, right?

Not everyone wants to spend their free time visiting the dentist! However, maintaining a regular routine of oral exams and cleanings can be the best way to prevent any further reasons to spend time with us at Northmount Dental Care!  Visiting regularly for your routine check ups allows us to make sure your dental health is at its very best and allows us to see early on if any issues are arising, allowing us to deal with any potential issues before they become more of a concern.

Perhaps you haven’t been to the dentist in a while and feel anxious about getting back on track? Rest assured that we will take care of you the best we can and will address your dental health concerns in the most manageable ways possible.

We evaluate your overall health, not just your dental health

With our regular check ups, not only are we able to clean your teeth, remove stains and examine your teeth and gums for signs of decay, gingivitis or periodontal disease, we are also able to see many signs that may be indicators of problems with your general health.

At your regular check up we will do the following to maintain your dental health as well as observe for any signs of issues with your overall health.

  • Clean stains and remove deposits from teeth

  • Display efficient techniques for brushing and flossing

  • Take digital x-rays, if needed

  • Assess your bite and jaw line

  • Examine teeth and gums for signs of tooth and root decay or gingivitis and periodontal disease

Remember that your time here with us is all about YOU. During your appointment it is our priority and pleasure to take the time to answer your questions and address your concerns to make sure that not only your dental health is taken care of at the clinic, but you also have the knowledge and ability to maintain your oral health care routine to the best possible standards at home.

How we make cleanings more comfortable

Don’t avoid the dentist and risk further issues due to the fear of pain. At Northmount Dental Care, we prioritize your comfort and our goal is to make the experience as comfortable as possible and that most definitely means pain free! The advancements in technology such as ultrasonic scalers, as well as the use of sedation dentistry and local anesthetics means your visit with us does not need to cause stress or anxiety.

Prevention is the key to healthy teeth

When you receive regular professional cleanings, your dental health can improve. But there are simple things you can do in between cleanings to prevent problems, like brushing twice per day and flossing once per day. Our hygienists can advise you on the optimal ways to prevent future oral health problems or minimize the impact of current ones. As always, we welcome your questions and our goal is to help you get the most from every visit with us at Northmount Dental Care.

Most dental insurance plans cover oral exams

Your dental insurance will likely cover oral exams and professional cleanings to promote preventative health. If you aren't certain whether your insurance will cover an exam, or how much is covered, call us today or schedule a complimentary consultation to discuss this with us.
