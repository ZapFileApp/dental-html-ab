Sedation Dentistry
in NW Calgary

← Back to All Services

Alleviate your anxiety at the dentist's office

We understand you may be nervous when you come in to our Calgary NW dental clinic. Whether you're anxious about a particular treatment or you're frightened about going to the dentist altogether, you're not alone in how you feel! Sedation dentistry is becoming more common among patients of all ages and for all levels of treatment, allowing you to relax while we care for your teeth.

Minimal to moderate sedation is often enough to alleviate any anxiety associated with dentistry, including recollection of your dental visit, and any of the sights, sounds, smells and feelings experienced during a dental procedure.

If you've been putting off coming to the dentist because of fear or anxiety, oral conscious sedation is an excellent option. Don't let your fears prevent you from caring for your teeth. Our team is here for you and we understand your concerns. We aim to make your visit as comfortable as possible and welcome any questions you may have about sedation dentistry at Northmount Dental Care.

Benefits of sedation dentistry

When you visit our caring and professional team at NW Calgary dental clinic, you will quickly see that our priority is you overall health. Our experienced hygienists will meet with you to discuss your current health status, and get an overall feel for where your general health is at, and once the process of sedation dentistry begins, you can expect to experience the following:

  • Relaxation before and during treatment

  • Minimal memory of the treatment

  • Relaxed gag reflex

  • Treatment seems like it only takes a few minutes

  • In some cases, more work can be done per appointment, reducing repeat visits

Safe, routine sedation options

At Northmount Dental Care, we offer safe, routine sedation options for all treatments.

Sedation dentistry is a safe method of keeping you comfortable during your dental treatment. In our NW Calgary Dental Clinic, We offer this option because our dentists care about your comfort, not just your teeth. Sedation is a simple, routine method of relaxing patients that is reliable and easy to administer.

Sedation dentistry is covered by most dental health plans

If you're not sure whether your dental plan will cover sedation, schedule a complimentary consultation and we will review what is covered and fully disclose and discuss all fees with you prior to your appointment.
