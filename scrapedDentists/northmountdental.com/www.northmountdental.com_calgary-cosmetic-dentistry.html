Cosmetic Dentistry in NW Calgary

← Back to All Services

Helping you look and feel your best

Cosmetic dentistry is an elective branch of dentistry that aims to enhance your appearance through a variety of procedures. Cosmetics in dentistry can range from whitening your teeth to the removal of excess gum to create a more even smile. While traditional dentistry seeks to prevent, diagnose and treat oral problems, dental cosmetics is optional and has the objective of improving your appearance and self-confidence.

Cosmetic dentistry services

Teeth Whitening

Popular teeth whitening procedures will restore your teeth to their original white colour.

Dental Bonding

Restore chipped teeth by bonding a durable resin material to the affected area.

Porcelain Veneers

Ideal if you suffer from gaps in your teeth, misshapen, chipped, uneven or discoloured teeth.

Invisalign

Popularly known as "invisible braces", Invisalign is a customized teeth aligner that's not only transparent, but also removable when eating, brushing or flossing. Straighten your teeth without anyone knowing!

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with a dental prosthetic, giving you a fuller, healthier and more functional smile.

Gum Contouring and Gum Grafting

Gum contouring can be performed to even out your gum line, for a more natural appearance. Gum grafting is a procedure that treats receded and exposed root surfaces.

Orthodontics

Treatment for crowding, underbite, overbite and other conditions of misaligned teeth. Not only can orthodontics enhance your appearance, it plays an important role in dental health.

Dentures

Dentures are removable, false teeth that can replace your missing teeth. Dentures can be natural-looking and comfortable.

Dental Bridges

Placement of fabricated fixed teeth can help "bridge" the gap between two natural teeth, creating a fuller, healthier smile.
