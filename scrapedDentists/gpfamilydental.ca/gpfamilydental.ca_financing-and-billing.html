Financing and Billing

Canadian Dental Care Plan

Apply for Canadian Dental Care Plan

Since your oral health is important to Grande Prairie Family Dental Clinic, we are here to help you with all aspects of your dental care, including making insurance claims, obtaining government assistance, and arranging dental financing.

At Grande Prairie Family Dental Clinic, we do direct billing to the dental insurance providers, what does that mean?

Many insurance companies will instantly respond electronically with their coverage details for your dental work. This means no waiting, and you won’t have to send in the claim yourself!

No need to organize and scan paperwork to send to your company, as we do that part for you.

We can check with your insurance provider to know how much your insurance covers for the procedures and also assist you in maintaining a record of the insurance you have utilized.

We also accept different forms of payment such as cash, debit cards, credit cards, and E- Transfers.

Grande Prairie Family Dental Clinic also accepts financing options from third parties.  we accept ifinance dental cards.

We recognize that there is a big group of our clients who have to postpone/ abandon their required or desired dental treatments due to financial reasons

You can apply for dental financing with ifinance Dental online using https://apply.ifinancecanada.com/20112 or scan

ifinance will notify you of the application outcome within 24 hours.

After signing your documents ifinance will pay for your services directly to us.

Grande Prairie Family Dental Clinic openly welcomes everyone irrespective of their gender, race, or financial background. If you are looking for a dental clinic in Grande Prairie that accepts Non – Insured Health Benefits (NIHB) program or the Alberta Dental Service Corporation (ADSC)program, be confident to contact Grande Prairie Family Dental Clinic. We accept NIHB and ADSC programs.
