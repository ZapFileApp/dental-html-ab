    Patient Information

    1. Do you offer insurance direct billing?

    Yes, we offer direct billing to help make payment options manageable. You will be notified if there are any issues with billing your insurance company directly.

    2. What are your fees?

    We follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide.

    3. What do I do if I have a dental emergency in Calgary?

    Please contact our office. We will prioritize and shift appointments as necessary to cater to your immediate needs.

    4. Is it safe to whiten my teeth at the dentists?

    Yes, it is safe! Our Calgary family dentists will always take precautionary measures custom to your condition. There are many teeth whitening methods available, and treatment will depend on the type of discoloration you have, your age, and your dental history.

    5. I’m nervous about seeing a dentist. Will I be ok?

    We want to make sure that your dental experience is comfortable and pain-free.  Our team members are very gentle and will provide distractions such as; Netflix, blankets, stress balls, and talking patients through the procedure to help eliminate or reduce anxiety.  Nitrous and oral sedation are also available upon request.
