Why Consider Riverbend Dental Health as your Calgary Dentist?

Our mission is to help people take good care of their teeth and gums – we are Calgary dentists that want you to enjoy the benefits of great oral health. Dr. Stringham has over 20 years of dental care experience and is passionate about the more technical aspects of dentistry. Come by and ask him about the latest dental advancements and he will be sure to provide suggestions and insight.

Dr. Speidel has been practicing since 2016 and prides himself on taking the time to understand your needs and provide the best dental care for your situation. He has dedicated himself to perfecting his craft so you can have an enjoyable and pain-free experience.

Our team does not push cosmetic services or any services that you don’t need. Natural health is important. We work hard to help our patients and understand different oral health options and direct patients to the solutions they actually need. We also provide a few ancillary services we believe will benefit patients. Examples include sleep apnea solutions and TMJ pain relief. Whether you are looking for a family dentist, a local dentist or for a trusted source of rejuvenating dentistry, we hope you will consider Riverbend Dental Health in Calgary.

5 Important Things to Know about Riverbend Dental Health

  1. Proven Knowledge: We train hard and are always evaluating new technologies to understand which ones are worthy of our patients’ trust and which ones don’t meet our expectations. This ensures that you receive the most advanced, precise and longest-lasting treatments available. Our dental team consistently takes part in continuous education programs and Dr. Stringham is passionate about his profession.
  2. Personalized Care: We understand that every patient is unique, which is why we take the time to listen. Our goal is to make you feel comfortable while understanding that dentistry makes most of us anxious. We have experience with children, adults and seniors and provide a few calming aids that make a difference. We also discuss treatment plans and associated costs with patients before moving forward, whenever possible. In short, we treat others like we would want to be treated.
  3. Natural Healing Approach: We believe that your body has an innate ability to heal itself. That’s why we promote minimally invasive procedures and natural alternatives whenever possible. For example, we provide pinhole gum surgery to cover receding gums which does not require scalpels and sutures.
  4. Perfect Location: Riverbend Dental Health focuses on serving the communities in the SE and SW that are within easy reach of IKEA. We understand that having a family dentist located in a convenient place makes a world of difference. Free parking a few feet away from the door (and a great big private bathroom when you get here) top off the perfect location.
  5. Financial Peace of Mind: We follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide and assist you with managing your health insurance plan to minimize surprises. Please rest assured that we always seek treatments and associated pricing that build trust.
