Children's Dental Services

Routine dental care is just as important for kids as it is for adults. At Cityscape Square Dental in Cityscape, NE Calgary, our services can be tailored to meet your child's needs and keep their growing smile happy and healthy. 

Early Positive Dental Experiences

At Cityscape Square Dental, we're committed to helping children feel at ease during their dental appointments so they can develop and maintain good oral health habits they can carry with them into adulthood. 

We offer a complete range of dental services for kids and their developing smiles and are dedicated to providing your child with the most positive and stress-free dental experience possible.

We realize that going to the dentist can be an intimidating experience, so we strive to keep the environment calm, friendly and inviting. We will work at your child's pace, answer their questions, and take breaks if they get overwhelmed. 

Your Child's First Visit

Your child's first visit to the dentist should be after their first tooth erupts or by their first birthday. This early visit allows your child to begin getting familiar with the dentist and gives us the opportunity to spot any emerging oral health issues. 

Dental Services for Kids

We tailor our services to meet your child's oral health needs and keep their smile healthy.

Preventive Dental Hygiene

Visiting the dentist for routine appointments when your child is young will help preserve their primary teeth until they are ready to fall out and be replaced by their permanent teeth.

At each visit, we will review proper oral hygiene techniques to ensure they are getting the at-home dental care they need. 

Restorative Dental Services

If your child is at risk of decay, develops a cavity, or is experiencing misalignment issues, your dentist may also recommend restorative treatments such as dental fillings and mouth guards.

Restore Your Smile

Root canals, fillings, crowns and more to repair your smile and restore your oral health.
