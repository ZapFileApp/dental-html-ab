Get Complimentary iTero Scanner Service

We're excited to announce a new addition to our clinic's offerings: complimentary iTero Scanner service for all patients

At Cityscape Square Dental, we're committed to providing the latest in dental technology to enhance your experience and ensure the quality of care.

The iTero Scanner gives us higher resolution scans of your mouth. This allows us to get a highly detailed 3D model of your teeth which allows for faster, more accurate diagnoses and treatment planning, resulting in superior outcomes for our patients.

To take advantage of this complimentary service, simply schedule your appointment with us today and experience the convenience and precision of iTero scanning for yourself.
