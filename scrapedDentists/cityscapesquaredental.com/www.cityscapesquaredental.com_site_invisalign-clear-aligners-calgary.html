Straighten Your Smile With Invisalign at Cityscape Square Dental

The Clear Alternative to Braces

Invisalign clear aligners offer a discreet orthodontic treatment option that works to straighten your smile without the appearance of traditional braces. Plus, with our digital scanner, you can view your virtual results before even starting treatment.

  • No wires or discomfort
  • Available for both teens and adults
  • Clear and removable to fit your lifestyle
  • Fewer appointments may be required
  • Financing Options

The Invisalign Process

The team at Cityscape Square Dental will guide you through the Invisalign treatment process.

Step 1

Discuss Your Goals

One of our dentists will talk to you about how Invisalign can help correct your issue.

Step 2

Get Your 3D Treatment Plan

We’ll do a 3D scan of your teeth and show you a preview of your straighter smile.

Step 3

Pick Up Your Aligners

Your dentist will contact you when your aligners are ready so you can get started.
