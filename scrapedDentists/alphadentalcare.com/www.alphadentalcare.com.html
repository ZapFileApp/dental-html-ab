Your Neighbourhood Calgary Dentist

You will find us located in the Chinook Centre Professional Tower on the 5th floor!

Our team is passionate about providing expert dental care for our patients by restoring your smile and by changing the way you think about visiting the dentist.

We are happy and able to assist with all of your dental concerns and with any treatments that you may require. At Alpha Dental Care we offer routine hygiene, teeth whitening, sleep apnea testing and treatment options, dental implants and other cosmetic dental services. Whether you are new to Calgary or switching dentists, we are excited to hear from you!
