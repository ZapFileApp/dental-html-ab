Dentures in SE Edmonton

Dentures Near You

Missing one or more teeth is more common than one may think. Having missing teeth can have a big impact on your everyday life. Not only can missing teeth affect your appearance, but it can also create challenges performing simple tasks such as speaking or chewing. If you are thinking about replacing missing teeth, dentures in SE Edmonton may be the perfect solution for you.

What Are the Benefits of Dentures?

Dentures are artificial gums and teeth that are customized to fit your mouth and restore your ability to speak, eat and smile. It is important to note that dentures are not as strong as natural teeth, an option to enhance your smile and boost confidence. At The Meadows Dental Clinic, we offer quality dentures near you.

Well-made dentures look natural and fit comfortably in your mouth. If you would like to learn more about dentures, visit our team for a consultation.

Types of Dentures

There are several types of dentures in SE Edmonton, and the type that is best for you depends on your specific circumstances. The three main types of dentures are as follows:

  • Conventional dentures: if you are missing the majority of your teeth, you may opt to remove your few remaining teeth to be a viable candidate for these dentures. Once your gums have had time to heal, our dentist will design your conventional dentures and have them fitted to your mouth. This process can take anywhere from eight to twelve weeks.
  • Immediate dentures: these are just like conventional dentures, but instead of being made after your teeth are extracted, these dentures are manufactured before your extractions. This way, your dentures can be placed in your mouth immediately after your tooth extraction procedure, and you will not have to worry about going without teeth for any length of time.
  • Partial dentures: If you are only missing some teeth but still have a healthy number of natural teeth in your mouth, our dentist may recommend that you get partial dentures. This type of denture works to fill in the gaps and prevent your remaining teeth from shifting. These dentures use small metal clamps that attach to the neighbouring natural teeth.

Interested in Dentures Near You?

If you have missing teeth, then partial or full dentures may be the right choice for you. Dentures are an affordable and simple way to replace missing teeth, and our team at The Meadows Dental Clinic is happy to provide you with dentures in SE Edmonton. Please get in touch with our team to book an appointment for dentures near you today.
