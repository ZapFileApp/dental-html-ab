Our Services

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening significantly improves the overall appearance of a stained, discoloured smile, which is a very common cosmetic dental problem.

Composite Fillings

Composite fillings have a more natural appearance than other options. This type of filling uses a composite resin material to restore your oral health.

Dental Cleanings & Exams

We offer dental exams and cleanings which will promote dental and overall health and help you maintain strong teeth.

Tooth Extractions

During the tooth extraction procedure, a dentist will surgically remove the tooth to eliminate pain or the development of oral health issues.

Dental Crowns

Dental crowns are caps placed on top of damaged teeth. Crowns are used to protect, cover and restore the shape of your teeth.

Root Canal Therapy

Root canal treatment is an effective and safe treatment for teeth whose pulp has become inflamed or infected.

Does cannabis use impact my dental treatment?

Yes. Whether you smoke it, vape, or use edibles, cannabis (marijuana) can negatively impact your dental appointment. Please avoid using cannabis before your appointment, and always talk to your dentist so they can ensure your safety. Click to read more on this topic.

What if a patient doesn't speak English?

If English isn’t your first language, or you feel more comfortable communicating in another language, we can put your mind at ease. Besides English, members of our staff speak Filipino, Gujarati, Hindi and Punjabi, and we would be happy to communicate in whichever language you prefer.
