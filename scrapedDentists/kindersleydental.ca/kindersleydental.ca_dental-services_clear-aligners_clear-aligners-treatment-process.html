SureSmile Treatment Process in Kindersley

5 Steps to a Straighter Smile

At Kindersley Dental Clinic, our SureSmile offer a simple way to straighten your teeth. They're see-through and easy to remove, making them a practical choice.

SEE SureSmile FAQS >

1
STEP 1

Schedule Your First Appointment

We'll check if SureSmile are right for you by looking at your smile. We use a special scanner to take a 3D picture of your teeth to see how they might look after using SureSmile.

2
STEP 2

Plan Made Just for You

After checking your teeth, we create a plan just for you. It shows how your teeth will move and when you'll get each set of aligners.

3
STEP 3

Start Your Treatment Plan

We'll create a set of personalized SureSmile just for you. These aligners are meant to be worn every day and can be taken out when you eat, drink, brush, and floss. While you wear them, these aligners will gently move your teeth to where they should be. Throughout the treatment, your teeth will gradually shift into their intended positions.

4
STEP 4

Switch Aligners as Directed

Every two weeks, switch to the next set of aligners. Also, visit your dentist for quick check-ups every six weeks to track how your teeth are moving.

5
STEP 5

Keep Your New Smile

Finishing your treatment is exciting! At your last visit, we'll talk about how to keep your teeth in their new places. We'll give you retainers to help with that.
