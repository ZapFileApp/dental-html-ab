Suresmile Clear Aligners in SE Calgary

Suresmile Clear Aligners Near You

Tired of hiding your smile behind your lips because of misaligned teeth? Braces might seem like the only option, but what if there was a more discreet and comfortable way to achieve a straighter smile? Enter SureSmile clear aligners, available at Concept Dentistry Calgary!

SureSmile aligners are a revolutionary alternative to traditional braces. They are virtually invisible, making them perfect for adults and teens who want to straighten their teeth without the world knowing. We’re proud to offer Suresmile clear aligners in Calgary as a convenient and effective solution for a variety of teeth straightening needs.

What are Suresmile Clear Aligners?

Suresmile clear aligners are molded individual trays that bend your teeth into the desired position over time. Clear, like metal orthodontic braces are not; that is what makes them nearly invisible when you wear them. This feature alone makes it a great option for those who want to enhance their smile without the negative appearance of having braces.

Each set of aligners is designed to move your teeth in small steps. You wear each set for about two weeks before moving on to the next one in the series. The result? A straighter smile, with less discomfort and fewer visits to the dentist.

Who Can Benefit from Suresmile Clear Aligners?

The Suresmile clear aligners are suitable for any alignment problems that may be moderately complex. Do you suffer from overcrowding, gaps, overbite, underbite, or crossbite? Then Suresmile might be the perfect product for you.

Clear aligners primarily benefit adults and teenagers, who need a system that is discreet for straightening their teeth. In any case, if you have had braces before and your teeth have shifted, then it is worth going through Suresmile treatment to regain a perfect smile.

How Does the Suresmile Treatment Work?

At Concept Dentistry, we begin the process with a consultation to assess your oral health and determine if Suresmile aligners are the best option for your needs. Once our dentist in SE Calgary decide to move forward, here’s what you can expect:

  • Digital Scans: We take pictures of your teeth to make your custom aligners.
  • Custom Plan: We use special technology to create a plan showing how your teeth will move.
  • Receiving Aligners: When your aligners are ready, you’ll get the first set. You should wear each one for 20-22 hours a day, taking them out only for eating and brushing your teeth.
  • Checkups: You’ll come in for checkups to make sure your teeth are moving as planned.
  • Completion: After you finish wearing all your aligners, you’ll have the straight smile you wanted. You might need to wear a retainer to keep your smile.

Why Get Suresmile Aligners?

Many patients prefer Suresmile over traditional braces because of the flexibility and comfort they offer. Here are some key reasons why getting Suresmile clear aligners near you may be the right choice for you:

  • Virtually Invisible: Since the aligners are clear, most people won’t even notice you’re wearing them. You can feel confident in social and professional settings.
  • Removable: You can take the aligners out to eat, drink, brush, and floss, making them more convenient and less restrictive than braces.
  • Comfortable: Suresmile aligners are smooth and don’t have wires or brackets that can irritate your mouth.
  • Tailored Treatment: Each aligner is custom-made for your teeth, ensuring a precise and personalized fit.

Frequently asked questions

What are SureSmile clear aligners?

SureSmile clear aligners are custom-made, removable trays that gently straighten your teeth without traditional braces. They are nearly invisible, making treatment discreet and comfortable.

How long does treatment take with SureSmile?

Treatment time varies depending on your dental needs but typically ranges from a few months to about a year. Your dentist will create a personalized plan for predictable results.

Are SureSmile aligners comfortable to wear?

Yes, they are smooth, custom-fitted, and designed to minimize irritation to your gums and cheeks. Most patients find them more comfortable than traditional braces.

Can I eat and drink normally during treatment?

You should remove your aligners while eating or drinking anything other than water. This allows you to enjoy meals freely and keep your aligners clean.
