Why Choose Concept Dentistry as
Your Dental Clinic in SE Calgary?

At Concept Dentistry, our dental clinic in SE Calgary provides personalized, patient-focused care designed to support your comfort, dignity, and confidence at every visit. Our dentists in SE Calgary take the time to understand your needs, ease dental anxiety, and offer reliable treatment solutions—so you and your family can feel confident and at ease every time you visit.

Emergency Dentistry You Can Trust

If you or a family member are experiencing severe pain, sensitivity, bleeding, or are missing a tooth, don’t wait; contact our dentist right away. We offer same-day emergency dental appointments whenever possible to make you feel better in no time. Our expert team will assess the issue and recommend the most appropriate solution.

Sedation Dentistry for Anxiety-Free Care

Nervous about visiting the dentist? Firstly, such feelings are completely normal. Second, don’t be! At Concept Dentistry, we are pleased to offer oral and nitrous sedation to help put you at ease and make you feel relaxed during your appointment. If you’re nervous about a specific procedure or feel anxious sitting in the chair, we’ll ensure your session is as stress-free as possible here at our Calgary dental clinic.

State-of-the-Art Technology for Accurate Treatment

Our dental clinic is equipped with the latest tools and technology to give you the best care possible. From digital x-rays and intraoral scanners to our CBCT, we integrate this equipment into each treatment to ensure your results are accurate and precise. Such technology also allows our team to detect issues early on, saving you time and money later on.

Independently Owned & Operated for Reliable Service

As a private dental clinic, our SE Calgary dentists are committed to knowing our patients personally and offering care that fits their needs but doesn’t empty their pockets. You’ll have smiling faces each time you come in, and you won’t feel rushed or overlooked. We believe in building long-term relationships founded on trust, comfort, and open communication.

      Frequently asked questions

      What makes Concept Dentistry different from other dental clinics in SE Calgary?

      Concept Dentistry stands out because of our experienced and friendly team. We use sedation dentistry to make visits anxiety-free, state-of-the-art technology for precise care, and focus on building trust with every patient.

      Do you offer Saturday appointments?

      Yes! Our dentist offers Saturday appointments twice a month from 8:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m., making it easier to fit dental care into your busy schedule

      Can you help if I feel anxious about visiting a dentist?

      Yes, as a trusted dentist, we absolutely help patients overcome dental anxiety. We offer sedation dentistry to ensure you feel calm, comfortable, and stress-free throughout your treatment.

      Are you open to new patients?

      Yes, we accept new patients of all ages. Our team takes the time to get to know each patient and formulate an individualized plan for each patient to have a healthy smile.

      How often should I get a regular check-up?

      If you normally healthy, you may simply require periodic preventive checkups based on your age. General adult physical schedule: For those aged 19 to 21, once every 2-3 years. For ages 22 to 64, once every 1-3 years.

      Is Concept Dentistry independently owned?

      Yes! As a trusted Calgary dental clinic, we are independently owned, giving us the freedom to focus on personalized, high-quality patient care.
