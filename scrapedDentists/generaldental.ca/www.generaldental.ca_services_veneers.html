About

Something to smile about.

Porcelain veneers are thin, ceramic shells that are bonded to the front of your teeth to improve their shape, colour, and alignment to give you the best smile possible.

What to Expect

01

Multiple visits.

Treatment involves planning, preparation, and final placement. Generally, the whole process takes two to three appointments, each lasting between 60 and 90 minutes.

02

Lasting results.

With good oral hygiene, porcelain veneers have impressive longevity.
