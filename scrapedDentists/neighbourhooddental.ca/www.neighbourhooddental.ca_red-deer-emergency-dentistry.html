Red Deer Emergency Dentistry

We Welcome Red Deer & Area Patients Experiencing any Kind of Dental Emergency!

At Neighbourhood Dental, we are dedicated to our patients, and we want to make sure that we are always available during a dental emergency. We offer same-day emergency appointments for patients and non-patients during regular business hours. If you are unsure whether an issue is a dental emergency or not, we offer this advice…if it hurts, it is an emergency!

This is because even injuries that seem small or superficial, can affect the living tissue inside of the teeth. Swift treatment greatly improves the chances of saving damaged or injured teeth, and it also helps keep the final costs to you down, by helping avoid more serious treatment later on.

Same Day Appointments for Dental Emergencies!

If you are experiencing any kind of dental emergency that is causing you pain, we encourage you to contact our office as soon as possible. Our trained staff will review your symptoms, and someone will get back to you as soon as possible with an appointment time. We will do our best to accommodate dental emergencies on the same day, since time is of the essence. We offer an after hours care line for patients of record in case a dental emergency occurs outside of regular business hours.

Symptoms of a Dental Emergency

  • A knocked out or lost tooth
  • A cracked, broken or chipped tooth where the root of the tooth is exposed
  • A bitten tongue or lip
  • Infection or abscessed tooth or gums
  • A toothache that cannot be managed with over the counter medicine
  • A loose restoration, such as a dental crown, filling, or bridge
  • Facial swelling
  • Bleeding from the mouth
  • A loss of a filling or crown

If you require emergency dental services, Neighbourhood Dental is here for you!
