Dental Services Available for Urgent Situations

Emergency care can involve many types of dental treatments depending on the problem. The following services are commonly provided to help restore comfort and protect your smile.

Tooth Extractions
When a tooth is badly damaged or infected, removing it may be the safest option. This treatment relieves pain and prevents the infection from spreading.

Root Canal Treatment
Severe toothaches often occur when the pulp inside the tooth becomes infected. Root canal treatment removes the infection and helps save the natural tooth.

Dental Crowns
A crown restores the shape and strength of a cracked or weakened tooth. It also protects the tooth from further damage.

Treatment for Dental Infections
Swelling and infection in the gums or tooth can be painful and dangerous if ignored. Prompt care helps control the infection and protect surrounding teeth.

Broken or Chipped Tooth Repair
Accidents can cause sudden damage to teeth. Repairing chips or fractures helps restore both comfort and appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a dental emergency?

A dental emergency includes severe tooth pain, swelling, a knocked-out tooth, broken teeth, or bleeding that does not stop. These situations require immediate dental care.

Can I visit without an appointment for urgent dental care?

Patients in need of same-day care are often welcomed. Reaching out to them prior to your arrival will allow them to assist you faster.

What steps should I take if my tooth is knocked out?

Keep the tooth clean, put it in milk or saliva, and get dental treatment immediately. Quick care for the tooth saves it from extraction.

How can I reduce pain before reaching the dentist?

Rinse your mouth with warm salt water, apply a cold compress to the cheek, and avoid chewing on the painful side until you receive treatment.
