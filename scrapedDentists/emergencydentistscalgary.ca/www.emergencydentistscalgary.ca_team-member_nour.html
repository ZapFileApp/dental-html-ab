Nour

Nour is a dedicated dental hygienist committed to helping patients maintain healthy, confident smiles. She specializes in preventive care, including professional cleanings, periodontal maintenance, and patient education on proper oral hygiene practices.

Nour’s gentle, approachable nature helps every patient feel at ease. In her free time, she enjoys reading and spending time outdoors, highlighting her caring and energetic personality.
