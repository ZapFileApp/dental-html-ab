Senior Dental Care in NE Calgary 

Seniors encounter a variety of new oral health challenges with age, including increased tooth cavities and decay, dry mouth caused by certain medications, denture maintenance, and aging dental work, such as old fillings or crowns that need to be replaced.

At Marlborough Dental Centre, our team of Calgary dentists have over 20 years of experience working in family dentistry, including working with senior patients to help them achieve and maintain healthy teeth and gums. Our advanced equipment and restorative techniques mean that you can take great care of your teeth quickly and easily.

Dental Care for Seniors

  • Dental Exams: Keep your teeth and gums healthy with regular dental exams. During your dental exam, Dr. Popp and the team will review any health changes that may affect your teeth and then we’ll examine your mouth for cracked or damaged fillings, cavities, gum disease, and evidence of oral cancer. This exam is essential for the early detection and treatment of any potential dental issues. 
  • Replacement Fillings: Repair old fillings that have loosened or cracked with replacement fillings. This quick and easy procedure involves removing your old filling, cleaning out any decay, and then inserting the new filling. Your refilled tooth will be as good as new.
  • Dentures: Replace a few or many missing teeth with our custom-built full or partial dentures. We design your dentures by taking an impression of your teeth to ensure that your final replacement teeth fit as comfortably, naturally, and accurately as possible.
  • Dental Bridges: Permanently fill in the gaps for any missing teeth with a dental bridge. One of our Calgary dentists will take an impression of your teeth to design a bridge that fits the exact shape of your teeth, affix a temporary bridge, and then securely anchor the permanent custom bridge using adjacent teeth or dental implants. Your final bridge will be a natural-looking set of replacement teeth, restoring your bite and confidence. 
  • Dental Crowns: Cover broken, chipped, or discoloured teeth with dental crowns. We shape your tooth and then take an impression to develop a custom-made crown for your unique tooth shape. Once we’ve affixed your permanent, custom-made crown, you’ll find that it’s virtually indistinguishable from your adjacent teeth. 
  • Dental Implants: Install a permanent replacement tooth for teeth that are severely decayed or missing. During the dental implant procedure, one of our Calgary dentists will insert a special titanium metal implant designed to mimic the root of a tooth. Once your implant has properly healed into your jawbone, we install a post or abutment and add your new crown. Your dental implant will look and function just like your natural tooth. 


Benefits of Great Dental Health for Seniors

  • Maintains your ability to eat and speak comfortably
  • Improves your overall health, avoiding oral conditions that can trigger systemic disease
  • Increases your self-confidence with a beautiful, full and healthy smile
  • Detects or prevents serious conditions early on, such as oral cancer
  • Enhances your facial muscle tone and appearance, especially if you have multiple missing teeth
