Why Choose The Ranch Dental Centre?

At The Ranch Dental Centre, we’re proud to serve Strathmore and surrounding communities with comprehensive, professional general practice family dentistry. Our goal is to earn your trust by making every visit comfortable, stress-free, and tailored to your needs.

Comprehensive General Care

From preventive cleanings to cosmetic procedures, restorative care, and implants—all under one roof.

Convenient Hours & Location

Located in Ranch Market with easy parking, plus Saturday appointments to fit your lifestyle.

Peace of Mind Pricing

Following the Alberta Dental Fee Guide and accepting the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP).

Comfort-Focused and Patient Centered

IV sedation, proven techniques, and quiet handpieces to keep you at ease.
