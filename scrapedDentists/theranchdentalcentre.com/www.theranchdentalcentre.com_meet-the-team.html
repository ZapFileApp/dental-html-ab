Dr. Ash (Ashkan) Hamzehi, DDS

Dr. Ash (Ashkan) Hamzehi, DDS

Doctor of Dental Surgery

Dr. Ash Hamzehi is a General Practitioner Dentist. He Holds a Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from Dalhousie University, obtained in 2008.

Dr. Ash is a member of Canadian Dental Association, Alberta Dental Association, and College of Dental Surgeons Of Alberta.

He also maintains a strong emphasis on getting to know each of his patient’s needs. He is an avid believer in providing his patients with evidence-based dentistry, due to his strong research background at University of Toronto.

Aside from dentistry, Dr. Ash enjoys playing guitar and hiking. He believes it is truly a joy to show you how regular dentistry can change your life for the better.

Dr. Zara Liaquat, BDS

Bachelor of Dental Surgery

Dr. Zara is a general practitioner dentist who is a member of Canadian Dental Association, Alberta Dental Association, and College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta.

Her professional focus is to improve her patients’ oral health and well being by providing them with optimal dental care in a stress free environment.

Aside from dentistry, she enjoys cooking, traveling, and spending time with her husband and beautiful children.

Dr. Yatin Arora, DDS

Doctor of Dental Surgery

Dr. Arora studied at Buffalo School of Dental Medicine in New York and obtained his Doctor of Dental Surgery Degree after successful completion of the program.

Licensed to practice in Canada and the United States, he has practiced in Ontario, Michigan, as well as Alberta with a strong emphasis on dental public health.

He recently relocated to Alberta in order to be close to his family and loved ones in Calgary. Dr. Arora enjoys serving patients with diverse dental needs, and he is committed to making every dental visit a truly patient centered experience.

Aside from dentistry, he enjoys exploring new cuisines, traveling, and spending quality time with his family.
