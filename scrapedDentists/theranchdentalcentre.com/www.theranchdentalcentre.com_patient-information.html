First Visit

First Visit at The Ranch Dental Centre Strathmore

Your first visit sets the tone for your dental journey.

At The Ranch Dental Centre, we go above and beyond to ensure you feel comfortable, informed, and confident in your care.

We use proven techniques, quiet instruments designed to reduce discomfort and offer sedation options for patients who need extra support in order to feel at ease. Our goal is to make every appointment to your satisfaction.

What to Bring During Your First Dental Visit

To make your first visit smooth and efficient, please bring:

  • Your most recent dental insurance information
  • A list of any current medications you are taking

What to Expect During Your Visit

Your first appointment at The Ranch Dental Centre in Strathmore is all about getting to know you and creating a personalized plan for your oral health.

During this visit, our team will:

  • Review your medical and dental history
  • Capture any necessary digital radiographs (x-rays)
  • Perform a comprehensive dental examination
  • Discuss your oral health goals and concerns
  • Develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs

In many cases, your first visit may also include a professional dental hygiene appointment.

Our goal is for you to leave with clarity, confidence, and a clear plan that supports your long-term oral health.

CDCP

Canadian Dental Care Plan at The Ranch Dental Centre Strathmore

The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) is a government initiative designed to reduce financial barriers and ensure more Canadians can access essential oral health services.

At The Ranch Dental Centre in Strathmore, we are proud to support this program and help patients receive the care they deserve without unnecessary stress over costs.

For residents of Strathmore, the Canadian Dental Care Plan is especially important. By easing financial pressure, it ensures that more families and individuals in our community can prioritize their oral health and receive timely treatment from local offices close to their residence.

Why Choose The Ranch Dental Centre for CDCP?

At The Ranch Dental Centre, we are experienced with the Canadian Dental Care Plan and understand how to make the process as smooth as possible for patients. Our team will:

  • Guide you through the CDCP process and answer your questions
  • Provide care that is fully aligned with CDCP coverage
  • Support you every step of the way with a focus on comfort and community care

By choosing our Strathmore dentist team, you benefit from a clinic committed to both your oral health and the well-being of our local community.

Who Is Eligible for the Canadian Dental Care Plan?

The Canadian Dental Care Plan is available to patients who meet the following criteria:

  • Do not have access to private or employer dental insurance
  • Have an adjusted family net income of less than $90,000 per year
  • Are a Canadian resident for tax purposes
  • Have filed a tax return for the previous year

For more information or to apply directly, visit the official Government of Canada website.

If you are eligible for the Canadian Dental Care Plan and already have your coverage card, you are just one step away from accessing dental care. The team at The Ranch Dental Centre in Strathmore is here to welcome you and provide care tailored to your needs.

Oral Surgery & Extractions in Strathmore

Sometimes saving a tooth isn’t possible, and removal or surgical treatment is the best option for protecting your overall health and comfort.

At The Ranch Dental Centre, we provide a range of oral surgery services, including extractions, wisdom tooth removal, and root canal therapy, with a focus on patient safety, comfort, and long-term oral health.

Our Strathmore dental team takes time to review your medical history, discuss treatment options, and ensure you feel informed and relaxed at every stage of your procedure.

Dental Extraction in Strathmore

Tooth extractions are sometimes necessary to relieve pain, prevent further complications, and maintain your oral health. A tooth may need to be removed if:

  • It is severely decayed and cannot be restored.
  • It has been badly damaged in an accident.
  • It is an impacted wisdom tooth.
  • It contributes to overcrowding or severe misalignment.
  • It is loose or untreatable due to gum disease.

Before every extraction, our dentists carefully review your medical history and any medications to minimize risks of bleeding or infection. Depending on your needs, we use different surgical techniques to ensure the safest, most comfortable procedure possible.

If you feel anxious about extractions, please let us know—we are here to provide reassurance and make your visit as stress-free as possible. Contact The Ranch Dental Centre to learn more about whether extraction may be the right solution for you.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction in Strathmore

Wisdom teeth can sometimes cause pain, infection, or crowding if they do not erupt properly. In these cases, removal may be the best option to protect your oral health. Wisdom tooth extractions are performed with the same care and focus on safety as other tooth removals.

If you are experiencing discomfort or swelling around your wisdom teeth, reach out to our Strathmore dental team to discuss whether removal is recommended in your situation.

Root Canal Therapy in Strathmore

Root canal therapy allows us to save a tooth that would otherwise need to be removed. The pulp inside the tooth—which contains nerves and blood vessels—can become damaged by deep cavities, cracks, or trauma. If left untreated, this can lead to infection, swelling, and severe pain.

Root canal treatment involves:

  1. Gently numbing the area to keep you comfortable.
  2. Accessing and removing the infected or damaged pulp.
  3. Cleaning and disinfecting the inside of the tooth.
  4. Filling and sealing the tooth to restore function and strength.

This routine procedure helps relieve pain while preserving your natural tooth. If you have questions about whether a root canal is right for you, connect with The Ranch Dental Centre team today.
