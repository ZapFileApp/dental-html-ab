Need Wisdom Teeth Removal in Calgary?

Wisdom teeth can cause issues if they don't come in correctly. Dr. Palka Sawhney Sharma at Lake Chaparral Dental Care offers wisdom teeth extractions to keep your smile healthy and pain-free.

Why Remove Wisdom Teeth?

Wisdom teeth often crowd your existing teeth, leading to misalignment, pain, and cleaning difficulties. Removing them prevents overlapping, helps maintain oral hygiene, and stops potential complications like tooth decay or gum disease.

Signs You May Need Extractions

Impacted wisdom teeth can cause:

Headaches

Food traps

Mouth pain

These issues can worsen, leading to bone damage or oral cysts. Early extraction can prevent long-term problems.

Preparing for Your Wisdom Teeth Extraction

Dr. Palka Sawhney Sharma conducts a thorough examination, using X-rays to assess your teeth. If the situation is complex, we do have the option of performing the wisdom teeth extraction under IV sedation at Lake Chaparral Dental Care.

What to Expect During the Procedure

The area is numbed, and if necessary, an incision is made. The tooth is then removed in sections. Afterward, gauze is applied to control bleeding, and stitches are placed to promote healing.

Post-Procedure Care

Follow your dentist's post-extraction instructions for a smooth recovery. Start with a soft diet and gradually return to normal eating.
