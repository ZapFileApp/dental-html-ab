Who Benefits from a Night Guard?

If you wake up with jaw pain or headaches, you might be grinding your teeth. Night guards can help alleviate these symptoms and protect your teeth from damage.

How Night Guards Protect Your Teeth

Night guards cushion your teeth, preventing the wear and tear caused by grinding. They also help align your bite, reducing stress on your jaw muscles.

Custom vs. Over-the-Counter Guards

Custom night guards, made by your dentist, offer a perfect fit and better protection than over-the-counter options. They are tailored to your unique dental structure for the best results.
