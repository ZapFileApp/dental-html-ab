How Invisalign works

It uses a series of aligners to straighten crooked, misaligned teeth.

We take precise impressions of your mouth.

Software transforms that data into 3D printed aligners that move teeth with low force. (A computer generated image lets you see how your future smile looks.)

You will get several aligner sets to wear for a certain period before moving on to the next.

The complete procedure may take a year or less.
