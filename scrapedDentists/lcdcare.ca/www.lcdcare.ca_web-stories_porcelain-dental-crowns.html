How a Single Porcelain Crown Saves a Damaged Tooth

When a tooth is badly decayed, cracked, or broken, and after root canal treatment, a porcelain crown can cover and protect the part above the gum line while the root stays in place. In Calgary, this helps you avoid extraction and keep your natural bite.

Filling the Gap: Crowns Used in Dental Bridges

Missing a tooth from an injury or extraction? A dental bridge uses crowns on the teeth beside the gap to support a natural-looking replacement tooth, restoring your smile and chewing comfort for everyday life in Calgary.

Crowns on Dental Implants: A Strong, Lasting Solution

Dental implants replace the root of a missing tooth. Once the implant bonds with your jawbone, Dr. Palka Sawhney Sharma places a porcelain crown on top, recreating a complete, sturdy tooth that feels secure when you eat and speak.
