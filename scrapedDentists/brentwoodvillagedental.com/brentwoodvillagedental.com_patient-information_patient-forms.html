Patient Forms

Access important patient information and release forms before your visit.

Be Prepared Before Your Next Visit!

Download Patient Forms

Patients are always welcome to come early to their appointments and fill out forms in the office. If you have questions we are always happy to help. Please ensure you arrived 15 mins early for your appointment if you prefer to fill out the forms in the office to allow for your appointment to start on-time.

General Registration Form

Please print and fill out this form if you are a new patient to our office and are NOT using student coverage for your dental benefits. Please remember to provide us with all active insurance policies; it is our pleasure to direct bill your dental benefits!

Download Form

Student Registration Form

Please print and fill out this form if you are a new patient to our office and are covered under one of the following student insurance dental plans:

U of C (Graduate Students only),SAIT, MRU, ACAD, Bow Valley other school plans listed on ihaveaplan.ca

Download Form

Update Form

Our attentive reception staff will ask you to fill out an update form if:

Your name has changed
Your address has changed
Your medical history has changed
it’s been over 1 year since your last appointment

Download Form

X-Ray Release Form

Please print and fill out this form if you wish to have your x-rays transferred from another dental clinic to our office. You may then forward the paperwork to us via e-mail (info@brentwoodvillagedental.ca) or by fax (403-210-5010). If it’s more convenient for you stop by our office, we are happy to provide you with a release form to fill out here.
