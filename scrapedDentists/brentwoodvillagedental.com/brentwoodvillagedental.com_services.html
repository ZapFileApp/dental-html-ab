General Dentistry In NW Calgary

Find The Right Dental Service For Your Needs!

Brentwood Village Dental Clinic is NW Calgary’s first choice for Dental Care.

We offer a range of Calgary NW Dental Services such as: IV Sedation, Dental Implants, Teeth Whitening, Bridges, Dental Fillings, Dental Crowns, Root Canal Treatments, Night Guards and Oral Hygiene.

Dental Fillings

Quickly repair and restore teeth damaged by cavities or minor cracks with tooth-coloured, durable fillings. We eliminate the decay and seamlessly fill the space to preserve your tooth’s integrity and prevent further harm.

View Service

Dental Night Guards

Stop painful jaw clenching and grinding while you sleep with a custom-fitted night guard. This protective appliance preserves your enamel, relieves headaches, and reduces tension from bruxism.

View Service

Invisalign®

Straighten your teeth discreetly and comfortably using a series of virtually invisible, custom-made aligners. Get the beautiful, confident smile you deserve without the look or hassle of traditional metal braces.

View Service

Iv Sedation

For anxious patients or complex procedures, IV sedation offers the opportunity to fully relax and feel completely at ease. Safely drift through your treatment and wake up with a healthier smile, often having significant work completed in a single visit.

View Service

Root Canal Treatment

We save your natural tooth from extraction by treating infection or inflammation deep inside the root. This common procedure removes the damaged pulp, relieves pain, and protects your tooth for years to come.

View Service

Emergency Dental

Our compassionate dental team is available to provide urgent care when you’re facing a dental emergency, such as severe pain, bleeding, tooth loss, or infection, to ensure your issues can be resolved quickly.

View Service

Dental Cleaning

Protect your long-term oral health and keep your smile shining bright with routine cleanings. This essential preventive care removes buildup that brushing misses, helping to ward off serious problems like decay and gum disease.

View Service

Dental Implants

Experience the most durable and permanent solution for missing teeth with secure, natural-feeling dental implants. These state-of-the-art replacements mimic a natural tooth root, supporting a custom crown for a lifetime.

View Service

Dental Bridges

Replace missing teeth with a fixed, natural-looking solution that restores your confident bite and smile. Bridges anchor securely to adjacent teeth, literally bridging the gap for renewed function and aesthetics.

View Service

Dental Crowns

When a tooth is weak, broken, or heavily restored, a custom crown acts like a protective cap. This restoration strengthens your natural tooth structure and dramatically improves its appearance and durability.

View Service

Wisdom Teeth

Ensure your comfort and prevent future crowding or infection with safe and expert wisdom tooth evaluations and removals. Our team makes the process as smooth and comfortable as possible, prioritizing a swift recovery.

View Service

Teeth Whitening

Brighten your smile dramatically and safely with professional-grade teeth whitening treatments. Eliminate years of stains and discolouration to reveal a radiant, more youthful-looking smile.

View Service

Family Dentist

Enjoy the simplicity and continuity of having one trusted Calgary team care for every smile in your household. We offer comprehensive, gentle dental care for patients of all ages, from the youngest to the oldest.

View Service

Dentures

Restore your ability to speak, chew, and smile with confidence using comfortable, custom-crafted dentures. We offer full and partial options that look remarkably natural and provide reliable facial support.

View Service

Dental Care That Puts You First

Customized Dental Treatment Plans

To help you achieve the smile you’ve always wanted, we provide preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dental services.

Every patient is different and we customize treatment plans based on the needs of each individual. We also recognize that those needs change over time, which is why it’s so important to maintain open and honest communication. While we can recommend a treatment option, we also respect your choices and comfort level.

Regardless of where you are in your oral care journey or the challenges you face, we have the skills, experience, and state-of-the-art equipment to provide the treatment you need. We aim to establish trust so we can continue working with you on maintaining strong teeth and a beautiful smile for life. If you’re a new patient, we look forward to meeting you. Helping our patients enjoy great oral health is the cornerstone of our practice.
