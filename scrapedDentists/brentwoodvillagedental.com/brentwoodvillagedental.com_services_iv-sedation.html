I.V. Sedation

Experience a deeper level of relaxation and complete peace of mind during your dental treatments.

Safe And Compassionate Patient Care

Calgary Sedation Dentistry Services

If dental anxiety prevents you from seeking care, IV sedation offers a path to complete relaxation. Often called “sleep dentistry,” it allows our team to precisely manage your comfort level, ensuring an stress-free experience from start to finish. Don’t let fear lead to advanced decay or more complex issues; IV sedation makes it possible to prioritize your oral health without the emotional toll.

Beyond managing anxiety, IV sedation is an ideal tool for efficiently performing multiple or complex treatments in a single session. Most patients experience a dream-like state and have little memory of the procedure afterward, leaving only a sense of relief. Our specialized team follows rigorous safety protocols, tailoring every sedation plan to your unique medical history and comfort needs.

Sedation Dentistry Options

Is I.V. Sedation Right For Me?

Not every patient has the same comfort level in the dentist’s chair, which is why we offer IV sedation as a compassionate solution for those with higher anxiety or complex needs. Understanding when to choose sedation can help you take the first step toward a healthier smile with total peace of mind.

Why I.V. Sedation?

If the thought of a dental visit causes significant stress or leads you to cancel appointments, IV sedation provides a deep level of calm that allows you to receive care without fear. It is also an excellent choice for patients with a sensitive gag reflex or a low pain threshold, ensuring a smooth and gentle procedure.

For patients requiring extensive restorative work, such as multiple extractions or dental implants, sedation allows us to perform several treatments in one efficient visit. This reduces the number of appointments needed and ensures you remain in a relaxed, dream-like state throughout the entire session.

Professional I.V. Sedation

What To Expect During Your Appointment

At Brentwood Village Dental Clinic, our highly trained team follows strict safety standards to provide a peaceful experience. From your initial health screening to your post-treatment recovery, we ensure every aspect of your sedation is managed with the utmost care and professionalism.

The I.V. Sedation Process:

  • A Pre-Procedure Health Review And Safety Consultation
  • Administering The Sedative Via A Small, Gentle I.V. Line
  • Continuous Monitoring Of Your Vitals Throughout The Procedure
  • A Brief Recovery Period In Our Clinic As The Effects Begin To Fade
  • Being Released To Your Pre-Arranged Driver For A Restful Day At Home

Most patients find the experience to be incredibly easy, often waking up feeling as though no time has passed and with very little memory of the dental work itself.

Dental Fees Explained

What Does I.V. Sedation Cost?

At Brentwood Village Dental Clinic, we provide detailed billing so you can plan for your treatment with confidence and peace of mind.

Our clinic follows the current Alberta Dental Fee Guidefor sedation services, which are typically billed based on the time required for your procedure. Because the total cost depends on the length and complexity of your appointment, we provide a customized estimate during your consultation. We are always happy to help you understand your insurance coverage and explore options to make your comfortable dental experience as accessible as possible.
