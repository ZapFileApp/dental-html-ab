Dental Night Guards

Safeguard your smile from the effects of nighttime grinding with a custom-fitted night guard from our expert Calgary dental clinic.

Preventive Care For Lasting Health

Calgary Dental Night Guard Services

Do you wake up with a sore jaw or frequent headaches? A custom dental night guard is one of the most effective, non-invasive ways to protect your teeth from the physical toll of nighttime grinding and clenching. The process is simple and usually requires just two short visits to ensure a perfect, comfortable fit that won’t disrupt your sleep. If you suspect you are grinding your teeth, it is important not to delay treatment, as this can lead to premature enamel wear. Untreated bruxism can lead to cracked teeth, receding gums, and, eventually, the need for more complex restorative treatments like crowns or veneers.

Although night guards are primarily a preventive tool, they are also a critical solution for maintaining the structural integrity of your jaw joints (TMJ). Quality custom guards help prevent tooth flattened surfaces and keep your bite aligned by providing a durable, cushioned barrier between your upper and lower teeth. There are different designs available depending on the severity of your grinding, and our dentists will provide a customized recommendation based on your unique wear patterns and comfort preferences.

Tailored Oral Protection

Which Type Of Night Guard Is Right For Me?

Not every smile requires the same level of protection, which is why we customize your appliance to address your specific symptoms or activity levels. Understanding the difference between a therapeutic night guard and a high-impact sports guard can help you preserve your natural teeth and prevent painful jaw issues.

Your Night Guard Options:

If you experience morning jaw pain or show signs of enamel wear, a custom night guard is designed to absorb the pressure of clenching. Unlike bulky store-bought versions, our custom-fitted guards are slim and comfortable, keeping your airway clear and your teeth separated while you sleep.

For athletes of all ages, a custom-moulded sports guard offers superior protection against impact compared to “boil-and-bite” alternatives. These guards are engineered to distribute force evenly across your smile, significantly reducing the risk of knocked-out teeth, lip lacerations, and even jaw fractures during physical activity.

Your Night Guard Fitting

What To Expect During Your Appointment

Getting a custom dental night guard at Brentwood Village Dental Clinic is a straightforward process that typically requires two short appointments. Our goal is to provide you with a perfectly fitted appliance that protects your teeth and helps you achieve a more restful, pain-free night’s sleep.

Your Night Guard Appointment:

  • A Comprehensive Evaluation Of Your Teeth And Jaw To Identify Specific Wear Patterns Caused By Grinding.
  • Taking Highly Accurate Digital Or Traditional Impressions To Create A 3D Model Of Your Unique Smile.
  • Selecting The Ideal Material And Thickness For Your Guard Based On The Severity Of Your Clenching.
  • A Professional Fitting Of Your Custom-Crafted Guard To Ensure There Are No Tight Spots Or Discomfort.
  • Final Bite Adjustments To Make Sure Your Jaw Remains In A Relaxed, Neutral Position.

Once your guard is ready, we’ll provide you with simple care instructions to keep your appliance clean and ensure it provides lasting protection for years to come.

What Does A Night Guard Cost?

At Brentwood Village Dental Clinic, we provide detailed, upfront billing so you can understand the long-term value of a custom-fitted guard compared to the high cost of repairing cracked or worn-down teeth.
