Meet Our Team

  • Dr. Percy Tomita, DDS BSc - General Dentist

    Dr. Percy Tomita has been practicing dentistry in the Morinville community since July of 1980. He graduated from the Faculty of Dentistry at the University of Toronto, BSc. Degree (1973-1976) and University of Toronto, DDS Degree (1976-1980). He moved to Alberta in 1980 where he opened up his practice in July. Dr. Tomita enjoys outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, fishing and golfing. He also enjoys the laughter and family time with his wife, two sons and two grandchildren. His passion for continued interests in all fields of health and wellness continues to grow to better his life. Dr. Tomita feels that dentistry has taught him so much about life and has been the most rewarding profession.


    Dr. EJ Ha, DMD BSc - General Dentist

    Dr. E J Ha has been practicing dentistry at the Morinville Dental Clinic since August 1999. She earned her BSc, specializing in Pharmacology, from the University of Alberta in 1994 and graduated with a Doctor of Medical Dentistry (DMD) from the University of Manitoba in 1998. Dr. Ha completed her DMD-General Dental internship at the University of Manitoba in 1999. She enjoys reading, gardening and visiting with her family in the United States.
