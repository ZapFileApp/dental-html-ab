Welcome to Victoria Trail Dental, Your Trusted Northeast Edmonton Dentist

Your Smile. Our Craft.

Welcome to Victoria Trail Dental, a community-focused dental clinic providing comprehensive care for patients of all ages in Northeast Edmonton. Our clinic is conveniently located at Unit 156, 550 Clareview Rd, near Belmont Park, with free on-site parking and flexible appointment options.

We are committed to delivering professional dental care in a calm, welcoming environment where patients feel informed, respected, and supported at every visit.

Learn More About Victoria Trail Dental

Your Northeast Edmonton Dental Clinic for Patients of All Ages

At Victoria Trail Dental, we believe everyone deserves access to high-quality dental care that supports long-term oral health. Our experienced dental team provides a full range of general, preventive, and restorative dental services for patients at every stage of life.

Guided by our philosophy, “Your Smile. Our Craft.,” we approach each appointment with skill, precision, and attention to detail. We use modern techniques and digital technology to support accurate diagnosis, efficient treatment planning, and patient comfort.

We welcome:

  • New patients
  • Dental emergencies
  • Patients of all ages
  • Individuals seeking clear communication and personalized care

Comprehensive Dental Services

Victoria Trail Dental offers a complete range of dental services, including:

  • Routine exams and professional cleanings
  • Invisalign® and cosmetic dentistry
  • Crowns, bridges, dental implants, and other restorative solutions
  • Children’s dentistry
  • Sedation dentistry
  • Emergency dental care
  • Preventive and long-term oral health support
