What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit to Park Mall Dental Centre, we’ll take the time to get to know you and your dental history. You’ll undergo a thorough oral exam, including digital X-rays, to assess your current oral health. We’ll also discuss any concerns or goals you have for your smile, and our team will craft a customized treatment plan to meet your needs. Our goal is to ensure you feel comfortable, informed, and confident in your care.

Do you accept insurance and offer payment plans?

Yes, we strive to make high-quality dental care accessible for everyone. We accept most major dental insurance plans and will help you navigate your coverage. For those without insurance or seeking additional flexibility, we offer convenient payment plans to ensure you receive the care you deserve without financial stress. Our team is here to assist with billing and financing options tailored to your needs.

Are dental implants right for me?

Dental implants are an excellent option for patients with missing or severely damaged teeth. After a comprehensive consultation, we’ll evaluate your oral health and bone structure to determine if implants are the right solution for you. Implants provide a long-lasting, natural-looking solution, and our team will guide you through the process, ensuring your comfort and satisfaction every step of the way.

Is teeth whitening safe and effective?

Teeth whitening at Park Mall Dental Centre is both safe and highly effective, offering noticeable improvements with minimal risk of sensitivity. We use professional-grade whitening products that are tailored to your smile, ensuring both safety and stunning results. Whether you’re looking to brighten your smile before a special occasion or as part of regular care, our team will guide you through the best options for achieving a beautiful, white smile.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

In the event of a dental emergency, it’s important to act quickly. Call us immediately, and our team will prioritize your care. We provide same-day appointments for issues such as severe tooth pain, broken or chipped teeth, and other urgent dental concerns. Our goal is to relieve your discomfort as quickly as possible and prevent further damage. We’re here to ensure you get the urgent care you need when it matters most.
