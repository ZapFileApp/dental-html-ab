ABOUT US

Accessible, Affordable, and Convenient.

Welcome to Park Mall Dental Centre, where your smile is our priority. Located in the heart of Sherwood Park, we are committed to providing exceptional dental care for patients of all ages in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Our team of experienced dental professionals is here to ensure that your visit is stress-free, whether you’re coming in for a routine check-up, a cosmetic procedure, or emergency dental care.

COMMITTED TO EXCELLENCE

Our Foundation of Care

At Park Mall Dental Centre, we are committed to delivering dental care that goes beyond expectations. Rooted in compassion and driven by excellence, our goal is to make every patient feel cared for, confident, and healthy.
