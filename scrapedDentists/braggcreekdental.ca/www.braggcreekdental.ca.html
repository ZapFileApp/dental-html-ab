A Wide Range of Dental Services in Bragg Creek

Trusted Dental Care in Bragg Creek

Thank you for visiting the Bragg Creek Dental Centre website! We’re located in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains, just a short drive from Calgary. Our dental clinic is welcoming, and we offer dental treatments for new patients of all ages. Our cozy yet contemporary dental facility is family-owned and has been in business since 2000. Dr. Clancy and the Bragg Creek Dental Centre team offer comprehensive dental care for the families of Bragg Creek.

Welcome to
Bragg Creek Dental Centre

Our clinic offers some of the latest innovations in digital dental technology. Using computerized CEREC® 3D technology, we create natural-looking crowns that match the colour of your teeth right away.

Not only do we strive to improve the health and appearance of your teeth, but we also offer Dysport® treatments for patients looking to soften the effects of frown lines.

Our dental clinic accepts most dental insurance plans for added convenience, saving you from paying for services upfront and out of pocket. Some conditions may apply.

Testimonials

Two Decades of Experience

For over 20 years, our dental team has been caring for patients and helping them keep their teeth and gums healthy. We offer many types of dental treatments and work hard to provide care that is comfortable, professional and focused on your oral needs.

Family-oriented Practice

We are proud to care for families at every stage of life. Whether you are bringing your child for a first dental visit or need care for yourself or an older family member, our team is here to provide friendly and personalized support.

Complete Dental Services

Our clinic offers a full range of dental services under one roof. From regular checkups and teeth cleanings to fillings, crowns and cosmetic treatments, we make it easier to get the dental care you need in one place.

Dental Specialists

In some cases, you might need some complex dental procedures. In such situations, we can refer you to qualified dental specialists, including orthodontists and oral surgeons. We will be glad to contribute to the preservation of your dental health.

We like to promote dental health actively, and we help to do so by providing our clients with top-quality products at cost.

Areas We Serve

If you’re searching for a dentist near you, Bragg Creek Dental is here to make quality dental care easy and accessible. Conveniently located at 35 White Ave, Bragg Creek, AB T0L 0C5, Canada, we proudly serve patients from Bragg Creek and nearby communities. Our team is committed to creating a welcoming experience for every visit, whether you’re coming in for a routine checkup, preventive care, or more advanced dental treatment. We focus on personalized care to help you and your family enjoy healthy smiles for years to come.
