Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I schedule a dental checkup?
Most patients benefit from routine dental checkups every six months. However, your dental team may recommend a different schedule based on your oral health needs.
What happens during a dental checkup?
During a dental checkup, your dental team will examine your teeth, gums, and overall oral health. Depending on your needs, diagnostic imaging may also be recommended.
Should I have dental checkups even if nothing feels wrong?
Yes. Many dental issues can develop quietly without causing pain or noticeable symptoms. Regular checkups help monitor your oral health and identify concerns early.
Will I need dental X-rays during my checkup?
Not every visit requires X-rays. Your dental team may recommend them when needed to gain a clearer view of areas that are not visible during an examination.
How much time should I expect for a dental checkup?
The length of your appointment can vary based on your oral health needs. Most routine dental checkups are typically completed within 30 to 60 minutes.
How do I find out the cost of a dental checkup?

The cost can vary depending on your individual needs and any services recommended during your visit. Contact Bragg Creek Dental Centre for personalized pricing information.
