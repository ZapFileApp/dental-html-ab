    Why do I need regular dental checkups and cleanings?

    We’ve heard our dentists say, time and time again, that it’s important for us to come in and have our teeth examined and cleaned at least every 6 months. But why?

    Frequent checkups help you and your dentist stay on top of things. Potential issues that can develop into something more serious can be avoided, including cavities, gum disease, and a root canal.

    What’s the best way to whiten my teeth?

    You can improve the aesthetic appearance of your smile in one of two ways: professional teeth whitening or DIY methods.

    In-office whitening can be completed in a single appointment. Your dentist ensures that the right amount of bleach is applied to your teeth so that your enamel isn’t harmed. The bleach will be applied, wiped off, then applied again as many times as it takes to brighten your smile to the shade of white that you want.

    The major perk of DIY whitening is that it can be done in the comfort of your own home and is much more affordable. Whitening strips, mouthwash, and toothpaste are just a few of the options available to you.
