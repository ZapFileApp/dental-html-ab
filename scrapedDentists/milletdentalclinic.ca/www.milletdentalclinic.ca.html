Welcome to Millet Dental Clinic

Discover comprehensive and exceptional dental care, right in the heart of Millet, Alberta, just 15 minutes from Leduc and Wetaskiwin. Say goodbye to city commutes, and say hello to modern dentistry at your doorstep.

Whether you need a check-up, cleaning, cosmetic service, or emergency care, our board-certified dentist and experienced dental team are here for you. Our practice is guided by a no-judgment mentality and we are dedicated to providing the most comfortable experience for our patients.

At Millet Dental Clinic, you’re not just a patient, you’re a part of our dental family. Together, we strive to achieve your brightest, and healthiest smile.

We look forward to seeing you soon!

Dr. Dennis Nguyen & Millet Dental Team

What we offer

Family Dentistry

We offer exams, cleanings, and a full range of dental services to ensure long-term oral health. We see everyone as young as 6 months old to patients in their sunset years.

Emergency Dentistry

We accept emergency patients and aim to accommodate same-day treatment. Our priority is to make sure you're taken care of ASAP.

Cosmetic Dentistry

We offer cosmetic and restorative services to help enhances your smile from veneers, crowns, bridges, teeth whitening, to teeth straightening. We want you to show off that smile!

Direct Billing

We offer direct billing to your insurance company for convenience. Who doesn't love convenience?
