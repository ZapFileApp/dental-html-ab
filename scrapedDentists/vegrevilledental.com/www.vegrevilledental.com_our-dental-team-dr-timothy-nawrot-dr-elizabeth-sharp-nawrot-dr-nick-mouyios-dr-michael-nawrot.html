Our Dental Team

Dr. Nick Mouyios

Dentist

Dr. Nick Mouyios has been practicing dentistry for over 20 years. Dr. Mouyios earned a Bachelor of Science degree and Doctorate of Dental Medicine at the University of Saskatchewan. To stay on the cutting-edge of the latest advancements in dentistry, he maintains memberships in the Canadian Dental Association, American Dental Association and the Alberta Dental Association.

Dr. Timothy Nawrot

Dentist

Dr. Timothy Nawrot earned his dental degree from the University of Alberta and has been providing exceptional dental care for over 30 years. He is a Fellow of the International Congress of Oral Implantology and the Academy of General Dentistry and maintains memberships in the Alberta Dental Association, Canadian Dental Association, American Dental Association, and the International Team of Implantology.


In his spare time, Dr. Nawrot volunteers on the board with Kindness in Action, an organization providing dental health services to underprivileged countries around the world.

Our Team

Our dentists are dedicated to providing compassionate, patient-focused care while staying current with the latest advancements in dentistry.


﻿Our staff is highly skilled, uniquely trained and strives to provide professional, energetic and fun-loving service to each patient! Continuing education is strongly encouraged and supported by Vegreville Dental Centre. We stay abreast of the very latest in technology and advancements in dentistry by attending educational programs provincially, nationally and internationally. As a team, we are especially interested in making all our patients feel right at home! We put your needs first to achieve efficient and comprehensive treatment in a supportive, nurturing environment. We promise to speak candidly with you about possible treatments as well as any risks and associated costs of treatment, and how treatment can address your individual needs as a patient. 

We know that our office is only as effective and welcoming as the people who work in it, which is why we take pride in our friendly, well-trained team members! Most of our staff has been with Vegreville Dental Centre for over a decade! Our dentists and team work together to create a comfortable, supportive experience for every patient.
