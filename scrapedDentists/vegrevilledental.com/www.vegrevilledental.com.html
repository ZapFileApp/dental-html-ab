Meet the CBCT 3D x-ray machine

What are the benefits to the CBCT 3D scans?
3D scans offer the ability to visualize intricate structures inside the mouth, such as: root canals, nerves, and sinuses (air-filled spaces) in the jaw. The imaging is done in 3D & without surgery. It can reduce the need for invasive procedures, shorten treatment time, and offer the chance for a better outcome. It is like the difference between a photo and a sculpture, which provides the ability to see fine anatomical structures and has proven invaluable in treating conditions in many areas of dentistry. While similar to a medical CT scan, the CBCT machine delivers a much lower dose of radiation, subjecting the patient to 10x less exposure than a CT scan.

When would a scan be needed?
The use of the 3D scan has proven invaluable in treating conditions in many areas of dentistry such as Orthodontics, Dental Implants, Orthognathic Jaw Surgery, TMJ Disorder, Oral Surgery, and Sleep Apnea. Each patient’s situation is different, and must be carefully considered by a clinical professional before any test or procedure is performed.
