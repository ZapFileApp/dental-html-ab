Welcome to Caring, at High-quality Dentistry Dental Care Airdrie

Creekside Dental Care is a high-tech, friendly dental office located at the heart of Airdrie, Alberta, although we serve the numerous surrounding locations. Our goal is to treat you, our patients, as we’d treat ourselves.

Dr. Andres Marquez Guzman and the team offer individualized and patient-focused services in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

With dental solutions for all ages, we are sure you’ll love the comprehensive and integrated services we offer. 

Achieve your dental care goals with our dental services

From wellness cleanings to implant services and anything in between, our office covers all your oral care needs. Our experienced and caring team is here to ensure you achieve a healthy, beautiful smile regardless of your starting point.

These are some services you can enjoy in our office.

  • Preventive dentistry: We understand that preventive dental care is the foundation of healthy smiles that last a lifetime. Our services include routine exams and check-ups to prevent, detect, and treat issues before they progress. The earlier we find dental problems, the easier and less costly it is to fix them. 
  • Cosmetic dental care: Are you bothered by the chips, stains, and gaps in your teeth? You don’t have to live with a smile you don’t like. Cosmetic dentistry addresses multiple dental flaws to transform your smile from ordinary to stunning. Our aesthetic dental care services include teeth whitening, dental veneers, crowns, etc. 
  • Restorative dentistry: Do you have missing or damaged teeth? We offer therapeutic treatments to ensure your smile looks and functions as it should. Some treatments we use to rebuild smiles include extractions, crowns, and tooth fillings. Furthermore, we offer dental implants and bridges to replace missing teeth. If you need root canal services, we can either manage your case in-house or refer you to a reputable endodontist.
  • Orthodontics: Do you have crooked teeth or a bad bite? You can achieve the healthy, straight smile you’ve always wanted. Instead of traditional braces, we offer Invisalign treatment—a revolutionary treatment that uses clear, removable aligners to straighten teeth.

Our team has invested in the latest dental technologies to ensure you enjoy fast, comfortable, and precise dentistry. We have integrated intraoral cameras, digital scans, Invisalign, digital radiographs, etc., into our services to ensure you enjoy a positive experience in diagnostics and treatments. 

Why choose Creekside Dental Care?

This is what you can expect when you entrust our smile to our team.

  • Comprehensive dental care

Dr. Andrés Guzmán

Dr. Guzman was born in Colombia, and at age 13 migrated to the U.S. with his parents and brother. In 2008, he graduated from the University of Minnesota with a Chemical Engineering degree, and in 2017 completed his D.D.S. at New York University. In 2018, Dr. Guzman and his wife, Dr. Jazmin, moved to Calgary to start a life together close to the mountains. They are both avid cyclists, love to travel and explore the outdoors, and enjoy sharing time and food with family and friends.
