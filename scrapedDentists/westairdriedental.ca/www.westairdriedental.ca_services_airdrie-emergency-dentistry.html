West Airdrie Emergency Dentistry

Your Choice for West Airdrie Emergency Dentistry!

At West Airdrie Dental, we understand that accidents happen, and we are here for you when they do! Regardless of the need for Emergency Dentistry, be it a chipped, broken, or knocked out tooth, we recommend you seek dental treatment as soon as possible. If you are unsure whether something is a dental emergency or not, we offer this advice; if it hurts, it is an emergency! This is because even injuries that seem small or superficial can still affect the living tissue inside of the tooth. When a dental emergency arises, West Airdrie Dental is here to help make you smile again. Call our West Airdrie office as soon as possible and we will do our best to see the same day.

What Constitutes a Dental Emergency?

  • Sudden and severe pain that will not go away
  • Swelling and fever
  • A loose tooth
  • A broken, chipped, or cracked tooth
  • A tooth knocked out of its socket
  • Fillings that are causing discomfort or pain, or any dental device malfunction (a retainer, bridge, or filling that has become loose or has fallen out)
  • An infection or an abscessed tooth
  • Significant bleeding from the oral tissues
  • Trauma to the oral tissues causing discomfort or pain

Same Day Appointments for Dental Emergencies!

Whatever the dental emergency, West Airdrie Dental will always make every effort to see you and treat you as soon as possible. No injury is too big or too small! We keep appointment times available for emergency cases so that you are able to access a dentist and get seen as quickly as possible, even if you are not a regular patient of ours. We recognize that the pain and stress involved in dealing with a dental emergency can be extreme, and we will always do our best to ensure that you are relaxed, calm, and comfortable during your visit. If you think you require emergency treatment, call us immediately to schedule your appointment today!
