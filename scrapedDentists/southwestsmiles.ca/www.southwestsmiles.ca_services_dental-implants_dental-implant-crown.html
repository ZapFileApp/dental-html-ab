Dental Implant Crown

The Finishing Touch for a Complete, Natural Smile

A dental implant crown is what transforms an implant into a fully functional, natural-looking tooth. At Southwest Smiles Dental Clinic, implant crowns are custom-designed to match your smile—restoring comfort, appearance, and confidence while completing your implant treatment.

What Is a Dental Implant Crown?

A dental implant crown is the visible portion of a dental implant—the part that looks and functions like a natural tooth. Once the implant has fully healed and integrated with the jawbone, a custom crown is attached to the implant post.

Each crown is carefully designed to match the colour, shape, and alignment of your surrounding teeth for a seamless result.

Why Choose a Dental Implant Crown?

  • Restores the appearance of a missing tooth

  • Designed to match your natural smile

  • Strong, durable, and stable

  • Functions like a natural tooth for eating and speaking

  • Custom-crafted for comfort and aesthetics

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

These FAQs address common concerns and provide quick, informative answers that reassure and educate patients.

01
Will my implant crown look natural?

Yes. Implant crowns are custom-made to closely match the appearance of your natural teeth.

02
Is an implant crown removable?

No. Implant crowns are securely attached and function like a permanent tooth.

03
How long does an implant crown last?

With proper care and regular dental visits, implant crowns are designed to last many years.
