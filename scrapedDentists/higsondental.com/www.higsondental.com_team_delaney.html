Meet Delaney

Member of the Higson Dental Group Team

Delaney was raised in Grimshaw and has called Grande Prairie home for nearly five years. She began her career in healthcare during the pandemic, working in a Long Term Care facility while simultaneously pursuing her Office Administration Certificate. Upon completing her studies, she joined the team at Higson Dental Group, where she enjoys connecting with patients and being part of a supportive and dedicated team.

In addition to her role at Higson Dental, Delaney continues to work casually at the local Long Term Care facility. Outside of work, she enjoys spending time at the lake during the summer, practicing yoga, snowboarding in the winter months, and relaxing with her boyfriend and their two dogs—Jersey, a yorkie-poodle, and Rickie, a golden retriever.
