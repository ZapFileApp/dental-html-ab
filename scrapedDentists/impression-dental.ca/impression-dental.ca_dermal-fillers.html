Dermal Fillers Near You

If you’re looking for a simple way to refresh your look and feel more confident, dermal fillers in Edmonton might be a good option for you. At Impression Dental, we are pleased to provide this non-invasive, highly effective treatment for smoothing fine lines and bringing volume back to the face. We strive to make our patients feel at ease every step along the way.

What Are Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers are injected under the skin and are gel-like substances. They are applied to lessen wrinkles, add fullness to cheeks and lips, and eliminate lines from the face. The majority of dermal fillers include a material called hyaluronic acid. It is a naturally occurring substance found within the body that softens your skin and keeps your skin well-hydrated.

How Do Dermal Fillers Work?

As we age, our skin dehydrates and loses collagen and fat. This will cause the skin to sag or wrinkle. Dermal fillers replace lost volume. It is injected, and it lifts the skin and plumps it, giving a smoother and younger look.

The treatment is quick and done right in our dental clinic by a trained dentist near you. It usually takes about 15 to 30 minutes. You’ll see results right away, and there’s very little downtime. Most people can go back to their normal routine right after the treatment.

Who Can Get Dermal Fillers?

Dermal fillers near you are a good choice for adults who want to improve the look of:

  • Smile lines (also called nasolabial folds)
  • Marionette lines (lines near the mouth)
  • Thin lips
  • Hollow cheeks
  • Scars or indentations

During your visit, our dental team will talk with you about your health, goals, and concerns. We’ll help you decide if dermal fillers are right for you.

Why Choose Impression Dental?

At Impression Dental, we believe in treating every patient with care and respect. Our trained dental professionals understand the face’s structure and know how to safely perform facial injections. Because dentists study oral and facial anatomy, they are skilled at delivering dermal fillers with precision.

Here’s what you can expect from us:

  • A clean and safe clinic environment
  • A gentle and trained team
  • Step-by-step explanations before and during the procedure
  • Answers to all your questions

We always make sure you understand the process and feel confident in your choice.

Are Dermal Fillers Safe?

Yes, dermal fillers in Edmonton are considered safe when given by a trained professional. At Impression Dental, we use approved products and follow strict safety steps. You might notice slight swelling or redness after the injection, but this usually goes away within a few hours or days.

How Long Do Dermal Fillers Last?

The results of dermal fillers usually last between 6 to 12 months. This can depend on the type of filler used and how your body responds. When the effects begin to fade, you can come back in for a touch-up.

If you’re thinking about dermal fillers near you, we invite you to schedule a consultation at Impression Dental. We’ll listen to your needs and help create a treatment plan that fits you. It’s a simple and effective way to feel great about your smile and appearance.
