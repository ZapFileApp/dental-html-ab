OUR CORE VALUES

At Urban Centre Dental, this year will mark our 61st anniversary as a family-run business! The practice began in the historic downtown Johnstone building and relocated to Urban Centre Plaza in 1985. This clinic has been a community landmark for many years. With such a long history, we now see four generations of families between these walls!

Our mission is to enrich the quality of life of those in our Fort Saskatchewan community, and we do this by providing world-class care.

To provide the highest standard of care, our dental service group is dedicated to delivering patient-centered services. We unite people with our core values of excellence in patient experience, evidence-based dentistry, gratitude, accountability and integrity, and lifelong learning.

Excellence in Patient Experience

We are in the business of wellness and taking care of others – our day-to-day simply happens to involve teeth. Excellence in the patient experience is of paramount importance to our team, and our attention to detail enriches this experience.

Evidence-Based Dentistry

When it comes to the care we provide, there are reasons for doing things the way we do. Founded in science, we strive for predictability in clinical outcomes, which plays a central role in our patient experience. To provide world-class care, we stay current with the rapidly evolving dental world. From Guided Biofilm Therapy and CEREC technology to our evolving product recommendations, we strive to provide the highest level and quality of care with evidence-based dentistry.

Gratitude

We are thankful for our team and the excellent service they provide. We are also incredibly thankful for our patient base because, without their continued support and trust in us, we could not exist. Gratitude is a cornerstone that lays the foundation for all else and provides meaning in the work we do.

Accountability & Integrity

Our team takes ownership of our service. When called to action, we respond with “yes,” and we make it happen to the best of our potential and abilities.

Lifelong Learning

Lastly, in line with providing evidence-based dentistry, we are committed to lifelong learning. We know the skills that currently provide success may not offer the same level of success as time goes on. A yearning for continual growth and constant improvement is what sets us apart and is an investment unto itself. This aligns with our pursuit of excellence in patient experience and evidence-based dentistry.

MEET OUR DOCTORS

With skill, compassion, and expertise, our general dentists – Dr. Tania Doblanko, Dr. Nicholas Doblanko, and Dr. Paul Fraughton – are here to care for your smile.

Delivering high-quality dentistry that prioritizes your needs and goals, our doctors will carefully evaluate your smile, explain your treatment options, and empower you to make informed decisions about your oral health. From preventive, restorative, cosmetic, periodontal, and emergency services to the latest smile-optimizing dental technology, you’ll receive the tailored dental care you want and deserve at Urban Centre Dental.
