Calgary Implant Placement Surgery

Your Choice for Implant Placement Surgery in SE Calgary!

If you are looking for a safe and effective solution for one or more missing teeth, dental implants might be right for you! Evershine Dental Care is pleased to offer implant placement surgery, as a foundation for replacement teeth that feel, look, and function just like your natural teeth. Let Evershine Dental Care help you achieve the smile you have always wanted.

What is a Dental Implant?

A dental implant is a small titanium screw-like post that is placed permanently in your jawbone to replace the roots where teeth are missing. The post is fixed into the jaw, bonds to your bone, and creates a strong foundation on which artificial teeth may be mounted. During implant placement in Calgary, your jawbone is accessed through your gums. Your dentist may provide removable dental prosthetics to help with stability and the appearance of your bite during recovery. It takes approximately six weeks to heal from implant placement.

Dental implant surgery offers an excellent alternative to bridgework or dentures that do not fit well and can offer an option for people who lack the natural tooth roots to allow building bridgework or dentures.

Benefits of Dental Implants

  • Natural appearance
  • Preserves facial structure
  • Preserves surrounding structure of teeth
  • Allows you the confidence and comfort to speak, eat, laugh and smile
  • Easy to care for
  • Eliminates the inconvenience or embarrassment caused when removing dentures
  • Helps prevent cavities – since implants are cavity proof, your smile is no longer as vulnerable to tooth decay

Evershine Dental Care knows the importance of replacing missing teeth with a high-functioning and natural-looking restoration. Calgary Dental implants can help you achieve your goal of a healthy and beautiful smile.
