New Patients

We Welcome All New Patients! No Referral Necessary!

At Forest Lane Dental Clinic we welcome all new patients! If you are looking for the right dentist for you and your entire family, consider Forest Lane Dental! We are pleased to offer a comprehensive range of dental services to meet all of your dental and oral health goals. We are dedicated to providing you and your entire family with the highest-quality dental care possible in a friendly, relaxed, and comfortable environment.

WE FOLLOW THE LATEST DENTAL FEE GUIDE!

Your First Visit to Forest Lane Dental

At Forest Lane Dental, we are committed to making new patients first visit with us as pleasant as possible. When you visit our clinic, you will be immediately greeted by our friendly and professional Administrative Team, who always do their very best to make you feel welcome and comfortable. Your appointment time will be a dedicated one-on-one time with your Forest Lane Dental team, who will spend as much time as needed to discuss all your dental health needs and goals.

If you cannot remember the last time you went to the dentist, let the Forest Lane Dental team show you how SIMPLE and COMFORTABLE it can be!

During your visit with us, we will make sure that you can easily understand what is going on in your mouth. We will also use pictures and videos if it is helpful for you to understand your dental and oral health needs. All options will be thoroughly explained to you and any questions you have will be answered. By using technology such as X-rays, we can image your entire mouth in just seconds. This helps us understand your oral health and how we can help improve or maintain it. We will never pressure you to proceed with any procedure unless you are completely comfortable and understand the need for it.

We look forward to hearing from you soon!
