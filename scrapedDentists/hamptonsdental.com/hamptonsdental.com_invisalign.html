Invisalign

Invisalign is the most popular clear aligner treatment that helps patients achieve straighter teeth with comfort. Our dental office is equipped to provide Invisalign treatment along with diagnosis and planning using the iTero scanner system. With technology and professional support, Invisalign works well for mild to complex alignment and bite issues.

How Does Invisalign Work?

Individuals wear an Invisalign tray over their teeth to slowly shift them into the intended alignment. A series of trays is required over a 1-2 year period, with each new tray guiding the teeth closer to the goal position. These clear trays are convenient to wear without drawing attention to your teeth or mouth, making them perfect for kids, teenagers, and adults.

The Benefits of Invisalign

Patients love Invisalign because they can be removed while eating, removing dietary restrictions associated with traditional orthodontic treatments. The easily removable trays also make it simpler to clean them and maintain proper oral hygiene throughout the treatment. Ask our dentists if Invisalign treatment is right for you or your family.

Steps to a healthy smile

It’s as easy as 1-2-3

Smile Step #1

Sign up for your free, no obligation, Dental Scan & Treatment Plan Session

Our dental team will take a 3D image of your teeth in minutes using our iTero digital scanner. Then, we’ll show you what your smile and bite could look like using an AI-powered, Outcome Simulator. Our dental team will take the time to review the results of your 3D scan and the Outcome Simulator. If Invisalign® treatment is right for you, we will review the process and share the financing options that are available.

Smile Step #2

Start your Smile Journey

If Invisalign clear aligner treatment is right for you, we will provide you with a series of trays customized to your individual needs. You will need to wear the trays for 20 to 22 hours every day except for when you eat, brush and floss. Each week, you’ll switch your aligners. And, every 3 months or so, you’ll visit the office to check in with our dental team to ensure your treatment is on track and your teeth and gums are staying healthy. We’ll monitor your dental health throughout the journey.

Smile Step #3

Celebrate your healthy smile

Congratulations. You made it. Your teeth are straight and healthy and your smile is beautiful. We will make sure you have completed all the requirements of the treatment and may recommend retainers to ensure you maintain your beautiful smile.

What are the signs of misaligned teeth?

If you’re experiencing cold sensitivity, jaw or muscle pain or headaches, teeth chipping or wearing, difficulty flossing, bad breath, dry mouth or receding gums, you could have abnormal tooth alignment. If you are presenting any of these conditions, we can help get your teeth out of trauma and we can help treat the problem and the symptoms related to these conditions:
