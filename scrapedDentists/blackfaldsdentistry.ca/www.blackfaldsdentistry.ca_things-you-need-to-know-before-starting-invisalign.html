Things You Need To Know Before Starting Invisalign

Invisalign is one of the most popular orthodontic treatments available today. If you’re looking for a discreet and effective way to straighten your teeth, Invisalign might be the perfect solution. In this post, we’ll explore why Invisalign has become so popular, including its discreet appearance, comfortable fit, and convenience. Whether you’re a teenager or an adult, Invisalign could be the key to achieving the beautiful, healthy smile you’ve always wanted. So, let’s dive in and discover what makes Invisalign such a popular choice for orthodontic treatment in Blackfalds near Red Deer.

At Blackfalds Dental Centre, we strive to provide our patients with cutting-edge technology dental treatments. If you are looking to straighten your teeth near Red Deer, contact us today to learn more about Invisalign.

Invisalign in Blackfalds Near Red Deer

Invisalign’s popularity is increasingly ascending; here are a few facts you should know about Invisalign before considering it:

    1. Invisalign is not suitable for everyone: Invisalign is an effective orthodontic treatment offered in Blackfalds Dental Center for misaligned teeth, but it may not be suitable for everyone. A dentist will advise patients with severe dental or complex orthodontic issues to opt for traditional braces, which may better solve those extensive dental problems.
    2. Treatment time may vary: Invisalign treatment time will vary from patient to patient, depending on the severity of each specific case. On average, our dental team in Blackfalds may suggest Invisalign treatment takes from six to 18 months.
    3. You need to wear the aligners for at least 22 hours a day: For Invisalign to be effective, you need to wear the aligners for at least 22 hours a day. You can remove them when eating or brushing your teeth, but they should be worn for most of the day to achieve faster results.
    4. You may experience some discomfort: As with any orthodontic treatment, you may experience some discomfort during the first few days of wearing your clear aligners. This is normal due to the shift of your teeth to the desired position and should subside within a few days.
    5. You will need to change your aligners every 1-2 weeks: To keep your teeth moving in the right direction, you will need to change your aligners every 1-2 weeks as directed by our dentist near Red Deer.
    6. You must maintain good oral hygiene: Invisalign clear aligners are removable, making it easier to maintain good oral hygiene during your treatment. Make sure to brush and floss your teeth regularly to avoid oral health issues.
    7. You may need to wear retainers after the treatment: Once your Invisalign treatment is complete, our dentists may recommend that you wear retainers to maintain the position of your teeth. This will help prevent them from shifting back to their original position.
    8. You can whiten your teeth simultaneously: it is possible to whiten your teeth simultaneously while undergoing an Invisalign treatment near you. While Invisalign clear aligners are designed to fit snugly over your teeth, you can follow a whitening treatment at home or in-clinic to help you achieve a brighter and straighter smile at the same time.
    9. You are Never Too Old For Invisalign! Invisalign is a popular orthodontic treatment for adults who want to improve the alignment of their teeth. One of the advantages of Invisalign is that the clear aligners are virtually invisible, making them a discreet option for adults who may feel self-conscious about wearing traditional metal braces. In addition, Invisalign aligners are removable, which means that they won’t interfere with your daily activities or dietary choices.

By knowing these important factors before starting Invisalign, you can decide whether this treatment is right for you with our dentist in Blackfalds near Red Deer. Make sure to discuss any concerns or questions you may have with our Blackfalds dedicated team before beginning your treatment.
