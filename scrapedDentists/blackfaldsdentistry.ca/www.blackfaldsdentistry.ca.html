Welcome to Blackfalds Dental Centre
Your Dentist in Blackfalds

Keeping your smile healthy and happy is the main priority of the team at the Blackfalds Dental Centre. Our family dental practice serving Blackfalds, Lacombe, Red Deer and the surrounding area provides comprehensive dental services and uncompromising care. Our clinic is wheelchair-friendly, and we are home to a number of benefits, including:

  • New patients are always welcome to become part of our dental family
  • Evening appointments are available to accommodate your busy schedule
  • Direct billing is happily accepted at our dental clinic near you

Our Ethos

At Blackfalds Dental Centre, valuing relationships is what sets us apart.

Positive relationships with our community, with our patients, and with our team, is our core value.

This translates into warm and comfortable service in your care with us. Combined with a wide-ranging suite of services performed by general dentists with the most modern advanced training, ensures exceptionally performed procedures in a small town and familiar atmosphere.

We accept dental insurance with direct billing, as well as offer dental financing to provide flexible and affordable options for our patients.

Technology

We adopt a digital philosophy to enhance patient comfort and accuracy in their care. This includes digital x-rays and digital scanning to provide valuable insights for our patients allowing them to be a part of their own journey by seeing what we see.
