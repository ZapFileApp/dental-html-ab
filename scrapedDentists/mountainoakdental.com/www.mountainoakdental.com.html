COSMETIC DENTISTRY

There are many reasons people choose cosmetic dental procedures to enhance their smile; from repairing defects such as crooked teeth or a malformed bite to treating dental injuries, missing teeth, cracked teeth and more.

PREVENTATIVE DENTISTRY

The goal of preventive dentistry is to maintain the health of the mouth and avoid future dental complications such as enamel wear, cavities, and disease. Avoiding these dental problems will prevent the need for extensive and emergency dental care in the future.

What you can expect from your first visit as a new patient at Mountain Oak Dental?

You can expect to be greeted politely and warmly by our reception staff.

We respect your time and offer convenient appointment times and we make every effort to be on time and available for you during your scheduled appointment time. Our welcoming reception staff will help you fill out forms for your medical and dental history, which is used to design a thorough treatment plan. We are here to listen to your concerns regarding your dental health.

Our experienced team will take a detailed record of your current dental condition using high quality low-dose digital X-rays, including intra-oral photos of your teeth and gums. This allows our clinical team to establish a baseline measurement for your oral health. We develop a comprehensive treatment plan to address your dental concerns which is directed by you and formed based on your unique needs and desires.

After a thorough review of your dental concerns, we can design an in-depth treatment plan to help you achieve your desires for your dental health. Our clinical team and treatment coordinators work together and will help answer any questions you may have. We will also help submit any necessary treatment to your insurance provider on your behalf.
