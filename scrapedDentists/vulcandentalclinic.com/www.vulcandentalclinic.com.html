Your Smile, Our Mission.

We are a full service general dentistry clinic located in the town of Vulcan, AB.

We are patient focused and want you to feel relaxed and comfortable in our office while we treat your specific needs. Our professional and friendly team will put you first and treat you with the utmost respect and care.

Active prevention is the best way to ensure your optimal oral health so book an exam and cleaning with us today! We look forward to seeing you!

Checkup Time!

For most people, it is recommended to visit the dentist once every 6 months for oral health maintenance.  During a maintenance visit a cleaning is performed along with a check up to aid in preventing or minimizing problems that may develop in or around the mouth.

About our clinic

Dr. McKee began working Tuesdays at the Vulcan Dental Clinic in January of 2014.  After having a very positive and welcoming experience working in the community he decided to purchase the practice and officially took over as owner November, 2014.  In April of 2019 he built a new practice at 95 Centre St to meet the dental needs in the area.  He hopes to continue to improve what the Vulcan Dental Clinic can offer and has goals to be a full 5 day clinic for patient convenience in the future.  In the meantime, on off days he has been able to treat patients in Lethbridge when event urgent care or availability is needed.

Thank you Vulcan and area for your support!
