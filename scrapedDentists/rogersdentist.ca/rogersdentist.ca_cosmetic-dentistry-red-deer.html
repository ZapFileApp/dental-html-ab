Cosmetic Dentistry - Red Deer

About Cosmetic Dentistry

A beautiful, bright, full smile can make a better first impression. Dr. Derek Rogers provides Cosmetic Dentistry in Red Deer and is pleased to offer effective cosmetic dental options for those who seek better looking teeth and the benefits of a brilliant smile.

If you want to improve the look of your teeth in a very short space of time, cosmetic dentistry may be the answer. There are a wide range of options to fix and repair broken, crooked, protruding, gapped, discoloured or worn teeth.

Consultation for a Beautiful Smile

The price for cosmetic dental work really depends on a number of factors including the amount of work and treatment required. An initial consultation will be set up so that you can be informed of the services that may be of benefit to achieve your desired results. You can be assured that you will be well informed of all the options available to you to as the range of services for cosmetic dentistry is extensive.

Dr. Derek Rogers' offers Invisalign® teeth straightening, dental implants, porcelain veneers, professional teeth whitening, crowns and bridgework.

Latest Technology

Dr. Rogers' employs the highest of standards and uses state-of-the-art technology. New safe and painless treatment techniques are available that will help preserve your natural tooth structure and create amazing natural transformations. Our goal at Dr. Rogers’ clinic is to deliver beautiful, confident smiles for life. Every step of the treatment is undertaken with the utmost care, skill and attention to detail.

A great smile has a number of common elements including:

 
  • Teeth are straight and properly aligned
  • Teeth are evenly spaced without noticeable gaps
  • Teeth are white without stains
  • The bottom edges of the top teeth follow the curve created by the lower lip
  • The top teeth are visible when smiling – the gums are not
