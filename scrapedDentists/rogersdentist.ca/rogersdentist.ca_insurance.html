Dental Insurance Information

Our experienced staff will be more than happy to help guide you through the process and make sure you get the most out of your benefits for the treatment you require. Insurance benefits can be quite a confusing and misunderstood issue with many patients, although it doesn’t have to be. As a courtesy to our patients we will direct bill on your behalf provided your particular Insurance Company allows for electronic direct billing.

Please remember, insurance is there to help with the financial responsibilities of treatment and should not dictate treatment.

Additional information may be requested by your plan administrator to ensure your treatment is covered by your insurance plan. We’ll supply the requested information keeping your health record confidential and to protect your privacy.

Insurance is designed to assist patients paying for dental treatment. Unfortunately not all dental treatments are eligible or fully reimbursable. If a particular treatment is only partially covered, you will have to share in the cost of your dental care. It is important to understand that you are a partner in your oral health. All treatment and care decisions should be made by you, after considering the recommendations made by your dentist, based on your actual needs. Your dental insurance plan is not always your best long-term treatment plan.
