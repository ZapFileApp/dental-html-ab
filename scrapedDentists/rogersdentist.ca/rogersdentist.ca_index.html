Dental Examinations

A dental examination is most important and critical for continued oral and overall health. During a dental exam Dr. Rogers is trained to detect conditions that could negatively impact your oral health including:

If there are any problems relating to oral health – they are best to be treated in the early stages (many times before you may even be aware there are issues) because in most cases they are easier and less expensive to treat.

Dr. Rogers uses digital radiography which use lower levels of radiation compared to conventional x-rays. Because there isn’t a need to develop x-rays it eliminates the disposal of harmful wastes such as lead and other chemicals into the environment.

Dr. Derek Rogers dental examinations services include:

  • Cavity checkup
  • Inspect decaying or damaged teeth
  • Look for developing gum disease which could lead to tooth and/or bone loss
  • Check for any previous dental work (fillings, crowns, implants, etc) in need of attention
  • Assess suspicious cysts or growths
  • Diagnose any early signs of mouth or throat cancer inflammation or bleeding inside your mouth
  • Examine the health and condition of bones in your jaw, mouth and face
