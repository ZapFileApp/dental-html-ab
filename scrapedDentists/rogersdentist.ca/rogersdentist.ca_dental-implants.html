Dental Implants

A beautiful, bright, full smile can help make a better lasting impression. One of the fastest and best ways to improve the cosmetic appearance of broken, missing or worn teeth is to have them replaced with dental implants.

Dr. Derek Rogers in Red Deer has years of experience seeing and working with patients who have had dental implant procedures.

Proper maintenance of dental implants is important to preserve the implants over the course of time. Dental implants replace the function of missing teeth, and as such, need to be cared for in much the same manner as natural teeth.

Titanium Dental Implants are simply screws that are inserted into the bone of the jaw to replace the root of the missing tooth.

Benefits of Implants

Doctor Derek Rogers is happy to offer cosmetic Dental Implants in Red Deer. Dental Implants can be used to replace missing or badly damaged teeth that would otherwise require extraction.

Dental Implants are an effective replacement for missing teeth as they function and feel the same as teeth.

Dental Implants are not painful; in fact most patients don’t notice the Implants are in place even the day after they were inserted.

Implant Advantages

Besides aesthetic benefits Dental Implants also offer many functional advantages including:

  • The jaw is not large enough causing a tooth to become impacted, unable to break through the gum
  • Impacted teeth can cause serious complications such as infection, cysts or damaging bone or other teeth
  • Wisdom teeth that only partially breakthrough the gum can become infected
  • Wisdom teeth not growing at a correct angle causing the tooth to face forward, backward or sideways
