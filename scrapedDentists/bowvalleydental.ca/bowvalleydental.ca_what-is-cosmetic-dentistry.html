What is Cosmetic Dentistry?

May 17, 2023

Cosmetic dentistry is the branch of oral health care that aims to improve the appearance of your smile. It is beneficial as it preserves long-term health, functionality, and aesthetics. Unlike traditional or general dentistry, which primarily focuses on oral health and hygiene, cosmetic dentistry uses a variety of treatments and procedures to improve a patient’s smile’s aesthetic appeal.

This blog post will cover all the information you need to know about cosmetic dentistry near you, including treatment options and their advantages.

Cosmetic Dentistry in Detail

Cosmetic dentistry is a specialization of dentistry that aims to make someone’s smile, gums, and teeth appear better. It includes a broad range of dental procedures intended to improve teeth’s aesthetic appeal and give patients more self-assurance when smiling. By examining your dental health, your dentist near you can assist in choosing the best course of action to address your cosmetic concerns.

Types of Cosmetic Dentistry Treatments

1. Dental Veneers –

In order to improve the appearance of teeth, thin shells made of porcelain or composite resin called veneers are applied over the fronts of the teeth. They can be used to reshape teeth and cover up stains, chipping, and gaps in teeth.

2. Teeth Whitening –

It entails applying whitening agents to the teeth to get rid of stains and discolorations and it is the most common cosmetic procedure recommended by dentists.

3. Dental Implants –

In order to support a dental crown or bridge, artificial tooth roots called implants are inserted into the jawbone. People who have lost one or more teeth often opt for implants.

4. Orthodontics –

Crooked teeth can be straightened and bite issues can be corrected with orthodontic treatment such as braces or clear aligners.

5. Dental Bonding –

Bonding, a method for improving the appearance of teeth by coating their surfaces with resin that is tooth-colored, can also be used to close gaps between teeth and fix teeth that are cracked, broken, or damaged.

Advantages of Cosmetic Dentistry

Improved Self-Confidence

Having a smile that you’re not happy with can cause a lack of confidence. Cosmetic dentistry can help improve the appearance of your teeth, leading to a more confident smile.

Correct Dental Imperfections

Numerous dental flaws, including discolored or stained teeth, broken or chipped teeth, and misaligned teeth, can be fixed with cosmetic dentistry.

Boost Career Prospects

You can feel more confident in job interviews and other professional settings by receiving cosmetic dentistry to help you make your smile look better.

Look and Feel Younger

You can feel and look younger thanks to cosmetic dentistry! As we age, our teeth may become discolored, worn down, or chipped. Veneers, bonding, and teeth whitening are examples of cosmetic dental treatments that can enhance the appearance of your teeth and give you a more appealing and youthful smile.

Enhance Oral Health

Some cosmetic dental procedures, like braces, can straighten teeth and guard against oral health issues including tooth decay and gum disease.

FAQs

1. Why should I see a cosmetic dentist?

You may wish to see a cosmetic dentist in Downtown Calgary if you want to improve the appearance of your teeth and brighten your smile. Aesthetic dental processes can improve your oral health and self-confidence by addressing dental flaws.

2. How long will each cosmetic treatment take?

Depending on the specific technique and the patient’s needs, the length of any cosmetic dental service can change. Simple processes like teeth whitening may just take an hour or two to complete compared to more complex treatments like dental implants or orthodontics. Your cosmetic dentist will be able to give you a more detailed understanding of how long your particular treatment will take.

3. Am I a good candidate for teeth whitening?

Whether you are a good candidate for teeth whitening depends on the cause of your teeth discoloration. Those whose discoloration is brought on by aging, tobacco use, or specific foods and beverages make the most suitable candidates for teeth whitening. If dental decay or medication use is the underlying cause of your tooth discoloration, teeth whitening may not be effective. Consulting a cosmetic dentist is the best way to determine if teeth whitening is an effective choice for you.
