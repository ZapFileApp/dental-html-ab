    Meet Dr. Salimah Dhanji

    Dr. Salimah Dhanji, from Vancouver, BC, developed an appreciation for dentistry at an early age when a car accident required her father to undergo extensive dental care. She watched in admiration as the doctor attentively treated her father, alleviating his pain, and gave him a new smile. It was then, that Dr. Dhanji’s desire to become a dentist was inspired.

    Dr. Dhanji graduated with honors from the University of Waterloo, where she majored in Biomedical Sciences and minored in Biochemistry. Her aspiration for dentistry led her to the rich and historical city of Boston, where she successfully earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine from the prestigious Boston University’s Goldman School of Dental Medicine. During her study, Dr. Dhanji participated in an externship program where she worked with underprivileged patients at a community health center. This experience enabled her to gain a profound insight into the intricacies and precision of the dental profession

    When you visit Acora Dental, you can expect the following:

    1. We will understand the reason why you are visiting us and address any immediate concerns you may have. We will also discover what your oral health goals are.
    2. We will then take the time to complete a comprehensive dental exam so you can understand your dental health profile.
    3. Next, we will develop an oral health plan to help you achieve your goals and review any recommendations we might have.
    4. Finally, we will get to work!

    We strive to meet your end-to-end dental needs:

    • We offer all the services of a General Dentist, including the usual day-to-day service you are most likely familiar with for you and your children.
    • Emergency Dental Services include broken and chipped teeth, toothaches, etc.
    • Hygiene and Preventative Care, including cleaning, scaling, polishing, fluoridation, and more.
    • Restorative Dentistry, including fillings, dental crowns, dental bridges, and more
    • Root Canal Therapy
    • TMJ/TMD Treatment
    • Oral Surgery, including tooth extractions and wisdom teeth extractions.
