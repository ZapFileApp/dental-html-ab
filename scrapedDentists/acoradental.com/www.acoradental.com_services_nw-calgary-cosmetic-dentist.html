CALGARY COSMETIC DENTISTRY

Your Choice for all Your Cosmetic Dentistry Needs in NW Calgary!

Acora Dental is pleased to offer a wide variety of cosmetic dentistry options to enhance the look of your smile! We offer services to improve the appearance of your teeth, gums, and/or bite. By addressing tooth shape, color, position, size, gaps, alignment, and overall smile appearance we can help you achieve the smile of your dreams!

What is Cosmetic Dentistry?

Cosmetic Dentistry refers to any dental procedure that aims to improve the appearance of a patient’s smile (including teeth, gums, and/or bite), by addressing issues like shape, colour, position, size, gaps, alignment, or the overall smile appearance, without necessarily addressing the health or functionality. Cosmetic dentistry procedures are typically done for aesthetic purposes, rather than to improve your dental health. While dentists are always concerned with the overall health of your mouth, having a cosmetic procedure done may not necessarily improve how well your mouth functions as much as how it looks.
