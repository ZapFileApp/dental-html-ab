Frequently Asked Dental Questions

What treatments are included in cosmetic dentistry?

Cosmetic dentistry includes teeth whitening, veneers, bonding, crowns, and smile makeovers — all designed to improve the look of your smile.

What are dental implants?

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots made of titanium that support replacement teeth or bridges. They look and function like natural teeth.

What counts as a dental emergency?

A dental emergency includes severe pain, knocked-out teeth, broken teeth, bleeding, or swelling that won’t go away.

Do you see patients of all ages?

Yes! We offer complete dental care for children, teens, adults, and seniors.

How does Invisalign® work?

Invisalign® uses a series of clear aligners that gently move your teeth into the desired position over time.

Do you offer direct billing to insurance?

Yes, we offer direct billing to most major insurance providers for your convenience.
