Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to see a dentist in Sherwood Park?

Pricing depends on the service you need. We follow the Alberta dental fee guide and offer direct billing to make payment easier.

What treatments do you offer?

We provide general, family, cosmetic, and emergency dental care, including cleanings, fillings, crowns, whitening, and more.

Is emergency dental care available at your clinic?

Yes, we offer same-day emergency dental services for problems like toothaches, broken teeth, or lost fillings.

What can I expect during my first visit?

Your first appointment includes a full exam, x-rays if needed, and a conversation about your dental health goals.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we welcome most insurance plans and provide billing for added convenience.
