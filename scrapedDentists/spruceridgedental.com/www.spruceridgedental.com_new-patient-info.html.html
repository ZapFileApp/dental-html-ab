New Dental Patient Information Spruce Grove

Welcome to Your New Dental Home

At Spruce Ridge Dental, we want you to feel like a part of our family! That’s why we focus on providing personalized, helpful, and compassionate dentistry in Spruce Grove that makes you feel heard, understood, and taken care of. To make your first visit with us as positive and streamlined as possible, we’ve included some helpful information on what to expect. If you have any questions or would like assistance filling out forms, please don’t hesitate to give us a call!

Your First Visit

At your first dental exam and cleaning, you will receive X-rays, iTero intraoral scans of your mouth, a thorough professional cleaning, and free whitening trays! We understand that a first visit with a new dentist can cause patients to feel anxious, no matter their age, which is why we take our time, get to know you, and help you feel relaxed and comfortable. We also make sure to personalized the education we provide you with, ensuring that you’re being met where you are. This tailored approach is much more helpful instead of the standard cookie cutter instructions that you may receive elsewhere.

Our Financial Options

We don’t want to make paying for dental care a frustrating process for our patients, which is why we offer several ways to pay. Depending on your unique situation and the resources available to you, we may help you maximize your dental insurance or CDCP benefits or apply for financing.

Dental Insurance & CDCP Welcome

Our team is happy to accept insurance plans and the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP), as well as we follow the Alberta Fee Guide. We work with your provider to ensure you receive the maximum coverage for your treatment. Our team also fills out necessary paperwork for your convenience.
