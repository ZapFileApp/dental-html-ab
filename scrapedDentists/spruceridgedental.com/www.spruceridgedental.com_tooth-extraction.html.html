Tooth Extractions Spruce Grove

Creating a Healthier Foundation for Your Smile

In some cases, the best option for our team to improve and protect your oral health may be to extract a damaged or failing tooth. Don’t worry, though! Our team offers several tooth replacement solutions to restore your smile, and we’ll make sure you remain as comfortable as possible throughout. If you believe you could need a tooth extraction in Spruce Grove, give our office a call today to schedule an examination.

Why Choose Spruce Ridge Dental for Tooth Extractions?

  • Same-Day Emergency Dental Appointments
  • We Accept Dental Insurance & CDCP
  • Multiple Tooth Replacement Options Available

Reasons You May Need a Tooth Extraction

While all of our treatment recommendations are based on your unique situation, we typically recommend a tooth extraction in the following cases:

  • A tooth is damaged beyond repair
  • A tooth is causing persistent pain
  • Decay has eroded most of the tooth’s structure
  • An infection has spread past the roots and cannot be treated with a root canal

We will discuss our findings with you, as well as why we are recommending a tooth extraction, once we’ve had a chance to examine your mouth. That way, you can also ask us any questions you may have!

Socket Bone Preservation

After we remove a tooth, the roots will no longer be stimulating the jawbone in that area. This will cause the body to divert bloodflow and resources to other areas of the body that need it, causing the bone to deteriorate over time. To prevent this from happening, we can place a graft in the area to preserve the socket, allowing you to still be eligible for a dental implant down the road if you so choose.
