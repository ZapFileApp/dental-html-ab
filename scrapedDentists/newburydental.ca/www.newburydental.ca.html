Now Accepting the Canadian Dental Care Plan

The new government plan provides coverage for Canadians earning less than $90,000 a year who are not already covered by an employer or other private provider. Coverage generally includes basic treatments like checkups, cleanings, fillings, x-rays, and extractions. To apply, click here.
NOTE: You must receive your coverage card in the mail prior to seeing a dentist.
