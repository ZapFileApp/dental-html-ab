Cosmetic Dentistry

We care about your smile, and offer a variety of cosmetic options for our patients to choose from. We will work with you to develop a customized treatment plan that fits your lifestyle and budget to help you achieve your best possible smile.

Injectables

Want to look younger? Botox® and Dysport® smooth out wrinkles by relaxing the muscles in your face to restore a fresher, more youthful appearance. They can also relieve TMJ symptoms and jaw tension to alleviate headaches, migraines, and facial pain. Schedule a consultation so we can help you choose the right solution for you.

Benefits of Injectables

  • Medium invasiveness.
  • Non-permanent, lasting effects 3 to 6 months.
  • Natural-looking results take effect gradually over two weeks following your treatment and can last up to 3 or 6 months.
  • Expect peak results between 2 to 4 weeks, so keep this in mind when planning for a special event.
  • Procedure takes about 10 to 20 minutes with no downtime. Slight localized bruising may occur.

Ready to rejuvenate your look with Botox®/Dysport®? Embrace the beauty of smoother, more youthful-looking skin at Newbury Dental. Contact us today to schedule your consultation and take the first step towards the confident, radiant appearance you deserve.

Invisalign®

Invisalign is a great option for patients who want to straighten their teeth and improve their smile, but want to avoid the discomfort and esthetics of traditional metal braces. Invisalign uses a series of clear, custom-made aligners that strategically move your teeth into the desired alignment you and your dentist planned together at the start of treatment.

We often get asked if adults are too old to get Invisalign. It's never too old to want an improved smile and gain confidence! In fact, the majority of our Invisalign patients are adults so don't be afraid to ask if you've ever been curious about it. Learn more

Invisalign First

Invisalign First is clear aligner orthodontic treatment for growing children, usually between the ages of 7 to 10. It aligns teeth and expands their upper and lower jaws to make sure there is room for all their adult teeth to grow in. The trays are expertly designed to catch erupting teeth and bring them in line with the dental arch. Learn more

Invisalign Teen

With Invisalign Teen, you don’t need to change what you eat or drink and still do all your favorite things, without your life being interrupted. It is a great orthodontic treatment option for teens who want to achieve a healthy and straight smile without wires and brackets. Learn more

Itero

‍The Itero is an intraoral scanning unit that captures incredible accurate digital impressions of your teeth. Using this data, it creates a 3D digital model of your teeth and mouth. This dental wellness scan can be used to diagnose cavities, gum recession, visualize and measure tooth wear and even plan orthodontic treatment. Learn more

Porcelain Veneers

Veneers is one of the quicker ways to improve your smile and a treatment that can close gaps and address misaligned teeth. They are permanently bonded to the front of your teeth and can change the shape, colour and length of your teeth.

Veneers can be ultra-thin to minimize the impact on your existing teeth and are custom-designed with our dental lab to create the smile you desire. If you have teeth that are small, discoloured or uneven, veneers may be a great option to improve your smile. Learn more

Professional Teeth Whitening

Maintaining white teeth is difficult nowadays with all the coffee, tea, wine, and other foods that stain our teeth on a daily basis. Professional teeth whitening is an excellent way to enhance the brightness of your teeth and is much more effective than over-the-counter options.  We offer both in house Zoom whitening, as well as take home whitening solutions.

Porcelain Crown

A dental crown is a cap that permanently covers the entire tooth and helps maintain the integrity of a decayed, damaged, or cracked tooth.  By getting a dental crown, you will save your tooth by protecting its remaining structure and bringing back its original shape, size, and colour.

At Newbury Dental, our porcelain crowns are custom designed to fit your mouth by using an intraoral scanner rather than the goop from messy impression materials! Learn more

Dental Bridge

A dental bridge may be a great option for you if you are not a good candidate for dental implants and you don't want to settle for a denture.  A dental bridge is permanent and you won't be able to remove it from your mouth like with a denture. It looks just like your natural teeth and restores your smile to optimum health and function.  We will work with you to decide on the best treatment option for replacing the missing gaps in your teeth.

Composite Bonding

Composite bonding can beautifully restore chipped, discoloured, worn or slightly misaligned teeth that could benefit from cosmetic treatment. This method of smile enhancement can be minimally invasive and considered to be a more affordable method of improving your smile.

Composite resin is first applied to your teeth, to blend with your tooth’s colour. The bonding is then artistically sculpted and polished to create the desired tooth shape in just one visit.

Cosmetic Smile Makeover

If you've ever desired a smile that will allow you to look better and improve your confidence, tell us what changes you'd like to make to your smile.  Do you want to change the shape, colour, alignment, or evenness of your teeth? We will work with you to develop a customized treatment plan that doesn't compromise on esthetics or your oral health!

Full Mouth Restoration

A full mouth restoration may be needed for a variety of reasons. Some of the most common reasons may be trauma, severely worn teeth, missing or damaged teeth.  With a full mouth reconstruction, you are able to restore teeth that may not have been functioning harmoniously while also improving your smile and esthetics.  Two birds with one stone! Don't let your health and confidence suffer any longer. Learn more
