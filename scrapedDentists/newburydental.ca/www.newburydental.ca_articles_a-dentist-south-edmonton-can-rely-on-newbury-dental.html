A Dentist South Edmonton Can Rely On

South Edmonton is a warm and welcoming area of the city that offers vibrant communities and lots of activities to do indoors and out. It’s a family first home to many.

In order to make sure that families have everything they need, the staff of Newbury Dental, conveniently located on Chappelle Way, is excited to be the South Edmonton dentist many patients choose to go to.

With several options across multiple disciplines of dentistry for professional and comfortable care, alongside a warm atmosphere, our staff is ready to serve the community.

Welcome to Newbury Dental

Newbury Dental, located in South Edmonton, is a family dental clinic based on a love story, both personally and professionally. Our dedicated South Edmonton dentists and dedicated team members rely on strong core values and modern standards of safety and comfort to ensure that your family – from the youngest members to the oldest – receives the highest quality dental care possible.

Your oral health is our priority. That's why we are proud to offer high-quality care with patient comfort in mind. With blankets, TVs on the ceiling, and headphones, we put the focus on you and aim to be as transparent as possible by offering open conversation and patient-involved planning in all of our services.

Meet the Dentists

When we say that Newbury Dental is a love-built practice, we’re not being metaphorical. Our dental clinic really does start with a love story! It begins in Boston…

Dr. Linda Lee, originally a Toronto native, had just completed her Honours Bachelor of Science in Human Biology from the University of Toronto. She had come to the Boston University Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine in order to earn her Doctorate in Dental Medicine.

Dr. Marcus Ngai, born in Edmonton and raised there, had completed his Bachelor of Science at the University of Alberta and was also attending the Boston University Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine for his Doctorate in Dental Medicine.

Dr. Lee and Dr. Ngai met on Newbury street, and over the course of their schooling, fell in love. When they had earned their degrees, they moved back to Dr. Ngai’s home and used their passion for oral health to set up a dental practice in South Edmonton. They named it after the place where they had shared so many happy memories.

South Edmonton Dental Clinic Services

The full service South Edmonton dental office at Newbury Dental is proud to provide a broad range of dental services for family members of all ages. Our dentists and staff love working with kids and adults both and are glad to help.

Our staff will work with you to create an appropriate and personalized treatment plan for your needs so that we can provide high quality dental care that matches your unique situation, no matter what it is. To see our full range of services, you can visit our website.

General Dental Services

For the basics of oral health care and upkeep, we have you covered with general dental treatments. We offer:

  • Annual dental cleanings and exams for a good oral hygiene routine, and to check for any problems and detect them as early as possible.
  • Tooth-colored fillings to treat cavities.
  • Dental sealants to prevent decay and cavities from happening in the first place. These are a great option for kids!
  • Full-service denture care for partial and full dentures.
  • TMJ treatment to reduce and manage pain and discomfort.
  • Root canals to eliminate pain and infection and save teeth that would otherwise need to be extracted.

Cosmetic Dental Services

Want to make sure your smile looks its best? We’re happy to help with confidence-boosting cosmetic dentistry services! We offer:

  • Invisalign clear aligners to help straighten and restructure your smile for both beauty and health.
  • Porcelain veneers custom-fitted to your teeth to reshape and recolor your smile.
  • Professional teeth whitening services to remove deep stains.
  • Composite bonding to bring back the health and cosmetic appeal of chipped or worn teeth with minimal invasiveness.
  • Smile design, which combines some of our best cosmetic dentistry services to help improve and achieve the smile you want.
  • Full mouth restoration, which combines restorative and cosmetic dental treatments to rebuild a healthy smile after serious disease, decay, or injury.

Dental Implants and Surgery

Have more intense or complicated dental health needs? The dental professionals at Newbury Dental want to make sure you’re as informed and comfortable as possible throughout the procedure. We offer:

  • Tooth extractions for severely damaged or diseased teeth to avoid pain and infection.
  • Wisdom teeth treatment and removal to prevent pain and dangerous infection.
  • Dental implants made of high-quality materials and restored with custom-fitted porcelain crowns or bridges to replace a missing tooth (whether it's one tooth or many!) and improve bone health.
  • Gum contouring and crown lengthening to adjust the size and shape of gums for functional and cosmetic reasons.

We’re also happy to offer oral sedation dentistry to assist our patients who feel anxious about dentistry in general. We want you to feel relaxed and have a comfortable experience.

Emergency Dental Services

Life is unexpected. Injuries happen, pain strikes without warning, and sometimes, you just need assistance as soon as possible. Newbury Dental is happy to offer emergency dentistry to our patients, for quick and reliable care when you need it most.

Unsure How to Pay or When to Come In?

The team at Newbury Dental understands that cost can be an obstacle to receiving comprehensive care and may cause delays that prevent you from receiving sorely-needed treatment.

This is why we offer financing options, including accepting most major insurance plans and offering direct billing. If you're worried about your ability to pay for your dentistry, you can always contact our offices for more information.

We also know that scheduling an appointment can be difficult when you’re juggling a full schedule, which is why we’re happy to offer evening appointments on Tuesdays when we are open until 7 pm.

What New Patients Need to Know

Ready to make Newbury Dental your go-to family dentist? We’re always excited to meet a new patient and their family. Here's what you need to know for your dental visit.

New patients can find all of the forms they need to fill out for our records on our website and are encouraged to fill them out in the comfort of their own homes before coming to their appointments. You will need to bring a valid photo ID and current insurance card to your appointment, as well as any dentistry questions or dental concerns you may have for us!

For all patients, we want to offer gentle care and a welcoming environment where you feel safe. This is why, during the COVID 19 pandemic, we are being careful to follow strict safety protocols so that you are exposed to as little risk as possible, and can visit our offices without worrying.
