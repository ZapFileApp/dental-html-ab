How Invisalign® treatment Works: Three Simple Steps

  1. Schedule a free consultation at Crowsnest Dental

    Set up your free consultation with our team. They will speak with you, scan your mouth with the latest technology, and will advise you whether or not you are a good candidate for Invisalign; This includes simulating for you what your teeth and smile can look like after-Invisalign with our 3D imaging technology.

  2. Get acquainted with the customized treatment plan

    If you decide to proceed, our team will then let you know how long you will want to be using Invisalign treatment in order to get the results you are looking for. They will also walk you through the cost, as well as easy ways to get started, and payment plans to ensure the treatment fits into your budget.

  3. Show-off your smile!

    If you decide to move ahead, we will go ahead and get started building your aligner trays and will setup a schedule for you (based on your treatment plan) to switch your trays as you progress through our professionally managed teeth straightening process. Along the way, don’t forget to show off that smile!
