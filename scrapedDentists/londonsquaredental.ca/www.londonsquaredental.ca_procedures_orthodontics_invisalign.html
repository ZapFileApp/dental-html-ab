What Are Invisalign Clear Aligners?

Invisalign is a revolutionary orthodontic system that uses custom-made, clear plastic aligners to gradually straighten your teeth. As a leading Invisalign dentist in Calgary, London Square Dental has helped thousands of patients achieve straighter smiles without traditional braces. The aligners are virtually invisible, removable, and comfortable to wear, making them a popular choice for teens and adults across Calgary. With clear aligners starting from $50/week and 0% financing, a straighter smile is more affordable than you think.

How Does Invisalign Work?

1
Customized Treatment PlanDay 1

Your journey begins with a consultation with our experienced Invisalign providers. We'll take a 3D scan of your teeth and create a personalized treatment plan that outlines the step-by-step movement of your teeth.

2
Series of AlignersEvery 2 weeks

You'll receive a series of custom-made aligners, each designed to be worn for about two weeks. These aligners gently and gradually shift your teeth into their desired positions.

3
Regular CheckupsEvery 6-8 weeks

You'll visit our office every 6-8 weeks for checkups, allowing us to monitor your progress and provide you with new sets of aligners.

Why Choose Clear Aligners?

Nearly Invisible — Invisalign aligners are virtually undetectable, allowing you to straighten your teeth without anyone noticing.
Removable for Easy Cleaning and Eating — Unlike traditional braces, you can remove your aligners to eat, brush, and floss, making it easier to maintain good oral hygiene.
Comfortable and Convenient — Invisalign aligners are made from smooth, comfortable plastic that won't irritate your cheeks or gums. Plus, you'll have fewer appointments and less time spent in the dental chair.
Effective for a Wide Range of Cases — Invisalign can effectively treat various orthodontic issues, including crowding, spacing, overbites, underbites, and crossbites.

FAQ

How long does Invisalign treatment take?+
Treatment time varies depending on the complexity of your case, but most patients complete treatment within 12-18 months.
Does Invisalign hurt?+
You may experience some minor discomfort as your teeth adjust to each new set of aligners, but this is usually mild and temporary.
How do I care for my Invisalign aligners?+
Simply brush and rinse your aligners with lukewarm water after each meal and before putting them back in your mouth.
What are the benefits of Invisalign over traditional braces?+
Invisalign is discreet, comfortable, removable, and allows for easier cleaning of your teeth.
Does insurance cover Invisalign?+
Some insurance plans cover a portion of Invisalign treatment. We can help you determine your coverage and explore financing options.
