Best Dentist in Calgary — Your Complete Guide to Choosing the Right One

What 4,800+ Calgary patients looked for before choosing London Square Dental Centre

Finding the best dentist in Calgary is one of the most important health decisions you can make for your family. The right dental practice combines experienced clinicians, flexible hours, transparent pricing, and genuine patient care. This guide covers exactly what to look for — and the questions to ask — so you can choose a Calgary dentist you will trust for years to come.

What Makes the Best Dentist in Calgary? 6 Key Factors

Qualifications & Experience

Look for dentists with accredited degrees, ongoing continuing education, and years of clinical experience. Check for memberships in the Alberta Dental Association and Canadian Dental Association.

Range of Services

A practice that offers comprehensive services, from general check-ups to cosmetic, orthodontic, and emergency care, means your whole family can be cared for under one roof without referrals.

Technology & Equipment

Modern dental technology like digital X-rays, 3D imaging, and laser dentistry improves diagnostic accuracy, reduces treatment time, and enhances patient comfort.

Patient Reviews & Reputation

Check Google reviews, testimonials, and ratings. Look for consistent themes: gentle care, clear communication, minimal wait times, and positive outcomes.

Convenience & Accessibility

Consider location, office hours (especially evenings and weekends), emergency availability, and parking. A practice that fits your schedule makes consistent dental care easier.

Insurance & Financing

Direct insurance billing saves you time and upfront costs. Flexible financing options like 0% interest plans make treatments accessible regardless of budget.

10 Questions to Ask Before Choosing a Calgary Dentist

  1. 1Are you accepting new patients?
  2. 2What are your office hours, including evenings and weekends?
  3. 3Do you offer emergency/same-day appointments?
  4. 4Do you direct bill to my insurance provider?
  5. 5What financing options do you offer?
  6. 6What sedation options are available for anxious patients?
  7. 7How do you handle dental emergencies outside office hours?
  8. 8What continuing education do your dentists pursue?
  9. 9Can I see before-and-after photos of your work?
  10. 10Do you offer a full range of services or will I need referrals?
