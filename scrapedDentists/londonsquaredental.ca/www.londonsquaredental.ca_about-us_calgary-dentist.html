Sterilization & Safety

Your safety is non-negotiable. Every instrument that enters your mouth has been through our strict, multi-step sterilization process.

Ultrasonic Baths

All instruments are first cleaned in ultrasonic baths to remove every trace of debris before sterilization.

STATIM Autoclaves

Hospital-grade STATIM steam autoclaves sterilize every tool, with monthly spore tests to verify performance.

Surface Disinfection

Every operatory surface, equipment piece and countertop is disinfected between every patient.
