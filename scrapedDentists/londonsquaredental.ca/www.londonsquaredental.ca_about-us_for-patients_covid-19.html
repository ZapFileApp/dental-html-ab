COVID-19 Patient Information

Your safety is our top priority. Here is what to expect during your visit.

At London Square Dental Centre, we maintain enhanced safety protocols to protect our patients, staff, and community. Our infection control measures meet or exceed all guidelines set by Alberta Health Services and the Alberta Dental Association.

Our Safety Protocols

  • Pre-appointment health screening questionnaire
  • Temperature checks upon arrival
  • Hand sanitization stations at all entry points
  • Medical-grade HEPA air filtration in treatment rooms
  • Full PPE for all clinical staff
  • Enhanced disinfection between every patient
  • Reduced waiting room occupancy for physical distancing
  • Contactless payment and check-in options

What to Expect at Your Visit

  • You may receive a screening questionnaire by email or text before your visit
  • Please arrive no more than 5 minutes before your appointment time
  • Use the hand sanitizer provided when entering the clinic
  • A mask is recommended but not required in common areas
  • If you are feeling unwell, please call us to reschedule
  • Accompanying persons should wait outside when possible

Questions or Concerns?

If you have any questions about our COVID-19 safety protocols or need to reschedule your appointment due to illness, please contact us at (403) 291-4945. We are here to help and want to ensure every patient feels safe and comfortable.

Dental care is essential healthcare. We encourage you to continue attending your regular appointments, as delaying treatment can lead to more serious and costly problems.
