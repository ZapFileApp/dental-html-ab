How London Square Dental gives back to Calgary

About London Square Dental

London Square Dental Centre has been a cornerstone of the NE Calgary community since 2005. We provide comprehensive dental care for over 4,800 patients across the Calgary region. Our commitment extends beyond clinical care, and we actively support local organizations, schools, and community events that improve the health and well-being of our neighbours.

Community Involvement

School Dental Health Programs

We partner with local schools to provide dental health education, free screenings, and oral hygiene kits to children in NE Calgary.

Youth Sports Sponsorship

London Square Dental proudly sponsors local youth sports teams, promoting active lifestyles and community connection.

Charity Dental Days

We regularly host charity dental days providing free or reduced-cost treatment to underserved members of our community.

Cultural Community Support

As a multilingual practice, we actively engage with Calgary's diverse cultural communities through events, health fairs, and educational workshops.

Disclaimer

All of our dentists at London Square Dental are General Dentists unless specifically listed. As general dentists, they may provide cosmetic dental procedures such as porcelain veneers, dental crowns, dental braces, and teeth whitening as part of their General Dentistry license. General Dentistry, Cosmetic Dentistry and Family Dentistry are not specialties recognized by the Alberta Dental Association & College (ADAC). As NE Calgary Family Dentists, they provide General Dental procedures for all ages within the family.
