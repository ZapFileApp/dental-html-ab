Porcelain Veneers in Calgary

Transform your smile with custom porcelain veneers at London Square Dental in NE Calgary. Our experienced cosmetic team corrects chips, gaps, and discoloration with natural-looking, long-lasting results. Open 7 days with direct insurance billing.

Porcelain veneers are one of the most popular cosmetic dental treatments for good reason. At London Square Dental, our skilled cosmetic team designs each veneer to complement your facial features, skin tone, and personal goals, delivering a smile that looks completely natural and uniquely yours.

What Are Porcelain Veneers in Calgary?

Porcelain veneers are thin, custom-made shells crafted from durable porcelain material that bond to the front of your teeth. They are designed to improve the appearance of your smile by addressing issues such as uneven teeth, stains, or minor alignment concerns. This minimally invasive procedure enhances both the function and aesthetics of your teeth. We customize each veneer for a natural and seamless fit.

The Veneer Process

  1. Initial Consultation: During your first visit, we'll assess your smile goals and determine if veneers are right for you.
  2. Tooth Preparation: A thin enamel layer is removed to ensure a proper fit for the veneers.
  3. Impressions: We take impressions of your teeth to create custom veneers tailored to your unique smile.
  4. Temporary Veneers: While your permanent veneers are being made, temporary ones may be placed.
  5. Veneer Placement: The final veneers are bonded to your teeth, and adjustments are made to ensure a perfect fit.

What to Expect During Your Appointment

During your porcelain veneers appointment at London Square Dental, you'll receive personalized care every step of the way. Thanks to local anesthesia, the procedure is painless, and we prioritize your comfort. After placing the veneers, we'll ensure they align perfectly with your bite and provide detailed aftercare instructions.

Who Is a Good Candidate?

Veneers are ideal for patients with healthy teeth and gums who want to correct cosmetic imperfections such as chipped, stained, worn, or slightly misaligned teeth. If you have significant decay or gum disease, we will recommend treatment before proceeding with veneers. During your consultation, we will evaluate your oral health and discuss whether veneers or an alternative treatment best meets your goals.

Benefits of Porcelain Veneers

  • Instant Smile Transformation — Veneers can dramatically improve your smile in just a few appointments, addressing multiple issues like discoloration, chips, or uneven teeth.
  • Custom-Fitted for Your Smile — Each veneer is tailored to your teeth for a natural, seamless look.
  • Minimally Invasive Procedure — Unlike other treatments, veneers require minimal tooth preparation, preserving most of your natural tooth structure.
  • Long-Lasting Results — With proper care, veneers can maintain their appearance and durability for 10-15 years.
  • Boost in Confidence — A beautiful smile can enhance your self-esteem and leave a lasting impression in personal and professional settings.
  • Strengthened Teeth — Veneers add a layer of protection to teeth that are weakened or prone to chips, making them stronger.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are porcelain veneers permanent?+
Porcelain veneers are a long-term solution, but depending on care, they may need replacement after 10-15 years.
Do veneers damage your natural teeth?+
No, veneers require minimal enamel removal and are designed to protect your teeth while improving their appearance.
How do I know if I'm a good candidate for veneers?+
Veneers might be an excellent option if you have healthy teeth and gums but want to address cosmetic imperfections.
How long does the process take?+
The process usually takes 2-3 appointments over a few weeks.
Are veneers stain-resistant?+
Yes, porcelain veneers resist stains better than natural teeth.
