Dentist in Marlborough, Calgary

London Square Dental Centre is a short drive south of the Marlborough and Marlborough Park communities along 32 Ave NE. Families searching for a dentist near Marlborough, Calgary use us for evening and weekend appointments, same-day emergencies, and direct insurance billing—without crossing the city core.

Marlborough sits in Northeast Calgary between major arterials—Memorial Drive to the south and the 32 Avenue NE retail spine to the north. If you live near Marlborough Mall (now rebranded as Pacific Place), Forest Lawn High School, or the townhouses along Marlborough Drive, you are roughly five to eight minutes by car to our doors in the professional building at 3545 32 Ave NE, Unit 230.

Proximity trigger: From the Pacific Place cinema parking lot, head east on 36 Street NE, turn north onto 32 Ave NE, and continue past the Sunridge commercial cluster—we are on the north side of the avenue with signage for London Square Dental Centre. Many Marlborough patients pair hygiene visits with errands at Sunridge Mall or groceries along the 36 Street corridor.

Text directions (driving): take Memorial Drive eastbound, exit toward 36 Street NE, travel north, then turn east on 32 Ave NE. Watch for the plaza between 36 Street and 52 Street—our unit faces the avenue with reserved patient parking nearby. Cyclists often approach via the NE pathway network near Falconridge and connect through quieter residential streets; lock racks are available at the building entrance.

Marlborough families choose us because pediatric-friendly scheduling aligns with school release times at Roland Michener and Dr. Gordon Higgins catchments, while adults appreciate late evenings after shifts near Industrial Avenue or the Deerfoot Business Centre. We advertise nine languages at reception—important in a diverse NE Calgary neighborhood—and maintain 0% financing for larger treatment plans like orthodontics or implants.
