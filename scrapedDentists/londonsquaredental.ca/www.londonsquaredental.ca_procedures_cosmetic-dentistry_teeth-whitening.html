Professional Teeth Whitening in Calgary

Brighten your smile up to 8 shades with professional teeth whitening at London Square Dental in NE Calgary. Safe in-office and custom take-home options tailored to your goals. Open 7 days a week.

A brighter smile can make a powerful first impression. At London Square Dental, our professional teeth whitening options use clinical-grade products to deliver safe, even, and long-lasting results that far surpass anything available over the counter.

What Is Teeth Whitening and How Does It Work?

Teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that removes stains and discoloration caused by everyday factors like coffee, tea, smoking, and aging. These culprits can dull your teeth's natural brightness over time. Professional teeth whitening safely restores your smile's radiance, delivering faster and more effective results than over-the-counter options.

In-Office Whitening

Our in-office whitening treatment delivers immediate, dramatic results in a single appointment. We apply a high-concentration professional whitening gel to your teeth and activate it with a special light to accelerate the bleaching process. In about an hour, you can walk out with a smile that is up to 8 shades brighter. This option is ideal for patients who want fast results before an event or simply prefer the convenience of one visit.

Take-Home Whitening Kits

For patients who prefer to whiten on their own schedule, we provide professional take-home whitening kits with custom-fitted trays made from impressions of your teeth. The custom trays ensure even gel distribution and reduce gum irritation compared to generic strips. You wear the trays for a prescribed amount of time each day, and within one to two weeks, you will see noticeably brighter results from the comfort of your home.

Why Professional Whitening Beats Store-Bought

Over-the-counter whitening strips and toothpastes use low concentrations of bleaching agents that often produce uneven results and increased sensitivity. Professional whitening is supervised by your dentist, uses clinically tested products at optimal concentrations, and includes custom trays that ensure uniform coverage. The result is a safer, faster, and more predictable outcome with less risk of sensitivity or gum irritation.

Our teeth whitening process is straightforward, stress-free, and tailored to your needs:

1

Frequently Asked Questions

Is teeth whitening safe for my teeth?+
Yes, professional teeth whitening at London Square Dental is safe and monitored by our experienced dental team to protect your enamel and gums.
How long do the results last?+
Results can last 6 months to 2 years, depending on your lifestyle and oral hygiene habits. Regular dental cleanings can help maintain your bright smile.
Will teeth whitening cause sensitivity?+
Some patients may experience mild sensitivity, which typically subsides within a day or two. We can recommend desensitizing toothpaste if needed.
Can I whiten my teeth at home?+
Yes, we offer custom take-home whitening kits designed for safe and effective use under our guidance.
