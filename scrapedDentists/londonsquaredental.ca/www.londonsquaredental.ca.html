Common Questions About Our Calgary Dental Clinic

Answers to what Calgary patients ask us most about pricing, insurance, emergencies, and more.

How much does Invisalign cost in Calgary?+
At London Square Dental, Invisalign starts from just $50 per week with 0% financing. The total cost typically ranges from $3,500 to $6,500 depending on the complexity of your case. We also direct bill your insurance to reduce your out-of-pocket costs. Book a free eligibility assessment to get your personalized quote.
Do you offer same-day emergency dental appointments in Calgary?+
Yes, we see dental emergencies 7 days a week, including evenings. Whether you have a severe toothache, broken tooth, knocked out tooth, or dental abscess, walk in or call (403) 291-4945 and we'll fit you in the same day. No referral is needed.
How much do dental implants cost in Calgary?+
Single dental implants at our Calgary clinic start from $1,500, and All-on-4 implants range from $15,000 to $25,000. We offer 0% financing over 6 to 24 months and direct bill your insurance. Implants have a 98% success rate and are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth permanently.
How often should I visit the dentist for a check-up?+
We recommend professional cleanings and check ups every six months. Regular visits let us catch cavities, gum disease, and oral cancer early when treatment is simplest and most affordable. If you have gum disease or other conditions, we may recommend visits every 3 to 4 months.
Do you direct bill dental insurance in Calgary?+
Yes, we direct bill to all major insurance providers including Blue Cross, Sun Life, Manulife, Great West Life, and more so you pay nothing upfront and never wait for reimbursement. We also accept NIHB coverage for First Nations patients and offer 0% financing on all treatments.
What sedation options do you offer for dental anxiety?+
We offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas) for mild anxiety and oral sedation for patients who need deeper relaxation. Our team is specially trained in gentle, anxiety free dentistry. We explain every step beforehand so you always feel in control. Thousands of nervous patients trust us and you are in good hands.
