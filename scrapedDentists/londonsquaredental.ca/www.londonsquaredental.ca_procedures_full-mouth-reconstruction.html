What Is Full Mouth Reconstruction?

Full mouth reconstruction is a comprehensive treatment plan that combines multiple dental procedures to completely restore the health, function, and beauty of your entire mouth. Unlike a single cosmetic treatment, reconstruction addresses every issue systematically, from gum disease and bone loss to missing teeth and bite misalignment. creating lasting results that transform both your oral health and quality of life.

Common Reasons for Full Mouth Reconstruction

Multiple missing teeth
Extensive tooth decay across many teeth
Severely worn or broken teeth
Chronic jaw pain or bite problems
Advanced gum disease
Teeth damaged by injury or trauma
Long-term neglect requiring comprehensive care
Failed previous dental work

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does full mouth reconstruction take?+
Treatment timelines vary based on complexity, typically ranging from 6 months to 2 years. Some procedures can be completed simultaneously to reduce overall treatment time. We create a phased plan during your consultation so you know exactly what to expect.
Is full mouth reconstruction painful?+
We use local anesthesia for all procedures and offer sedation options for anxious patients. Most patients report manageable discomfort between phases. Our team provides detailed aftercare instructions and pain management guidance for every step.
How much does full mouth reconstruction cost?+
Costs range from $20,000 to $50,000+ depending on the procedures required. We offer 0% financing, direct insurance billing, and phased treatment plans to make comprehensive reconstruction financially manageable.
Who needs full mouth reconstruction?+
Patients with multiple dental issues. extensive decay, missing teeth, worn or broken teeth, bite problems, or gum disease affecting most teeth. benefit most. If individual treatments won't adequately restore function and aesthetics, reconstruction may be the best path.
