Complete & Partial Dentures in Calgary

Custom-crafted dentures that fit securely, look natural, and restore your ability to eat, speak, and smile with confidence.

Missing teeth affect far more than your appearance. They make eating difficult, alter your speech, and can cause your facial structure to change over time. At London Square Dental, we create custom dentures using premium materials and precise fitting techniques to give you a comfortable, natural-looking solution that restores your quality of life.

Complete Dentures

Complete dentures replace an entire arch of missing teeth, either upper, lower, or both. They rest on the gum tissue and are custom-shaped to fit the contours of your mouth for a secure, comfortable fit. Modern complete dentures are lighter, more natural-looking, and more comfortable than ever, allowing you to eat your favourite foods and speak clearly.

Partial Dentures

When you still have some healthy natural teeth remaining, partial dentures fill in the gaps without affecting your existing teeth. They use discreet clasps or precision attachments to anchor securely to your natural teeth, providing stability while allowing easy removal for cleaning. Partial dentures also prevent your remaining teeth from shifting into the empty spaces.

The Denture Fitting Process

Creating dentures that fit perfectly requires precision at every step. We begin with detailed impressions and bite measurements, then create a wax model for you to try in. This allows us to verify the fit, tooth arrangement, and appearance before final fabrication. You have the opportunity to see and approve how your new smile will look before your dentures are completed.

Implant-Supported Dentures

For patients who want maximum stability, implant-supported dentures attach to dental implants placed in the jawbone. This eliminates slipping and rocking, providing a secure fit that feels much closer to natural teeth. Implant-supported dentures also help preserve jawbone density, preventing the facial changes that often occur with traditional dentures over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get dentures?+
The denture process typically takes three to five appointments over several weeks. This includes the initial consultation, impressions, a wax try-in for fit and appearance verification, and the final delivery. We take extra time to ensure your dentures fit precisely and look natural before you take them home.
Will dentures look natural?+
Modern dentures are crafted from premium materials that closely replicate the appearance of natural teeth and gum tissue. We carefully select tooth shape, size, and shade to complement your facial features. Most people will not be able to tell you are wearing dentures.
How do I care for my dentures?+
Remove and rinse your dentures after eating to remove food particles. Brush them daily with a soft denture brush and non-abrasive denture cleaner. Soak them overnight in a denture solution to keep them moist and clean. Handle them carefully over a folded towel to prevent breakage if dropped, and visit us regularly for adjustments.
Do dentures need to be replaced?+
Dentures typically need to be relined or replaced every 5 to 8 years. Over time, your jawbone naturally changes shape, which can affect the fit of your dentures. Regular dental visits allow us to monitor the fit and make adjustments or relines as needed to maintain comfort and function.
What is the difference between complete and partial dentures?+
Complete dentures replace all teeth in the upper or lower jaw and rest directly on the gums. Partial dentures replace one or more missing teeth and attach to your remaining natural teeth with clasps or precision attachments. We recommend the best option based on the number and location of your missing teeth.
