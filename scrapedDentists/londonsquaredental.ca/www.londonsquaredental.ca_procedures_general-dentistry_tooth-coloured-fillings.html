Tooth-Coloured Fillings in NE Calgary

We specialize in high-quality tooth coloured fillings in NE Calgary that blend naturally with your teeth. These fillings not only repair cavities but also improve the strength and appearance of your smile.

Tooth coloured fillings, also known as composite fillings, are made from a blend of resin and fine particles. These fillings match the natural shade of your teeth, providing a virtually invisible restoration for cavities or damaged teeth.

Benefits of Tooth Coloured Fillings

Natural-looking and durable, tooth-coloured fillings seamlessly restore your teeth while enhancing strength and confidence. Perfect for repairing cavities or minor damage, they combine aesthetics, safety, and long-term oral health benefits in one solution.

  • Natural Aesthetic: Matches your tooth's shade for a seamless look, ideal for visible teeth.
  • Strength and Durability: Bonds tightly to your tooth, reinforcing its structure and withstanding daily wear.
  • Minimal Invasiveness: Requires less removal of natural tooth material compared to metal fillings.
  • Safe and Mercury-Free: Composed of resin materials that are safe for everyone, including children.

With these benefits, tooth coloured fillings combine functionality and aesthetics to restore your smile.

What to Expect During Your Appointment

We prioritize your comfort and aim for a smooth, efficient experience. Here's what to expect:

  • Initial Assessment: Our dentist will examine your teeth, take x-rays if needed, and explain the procedure.
  • Preparation and Cleaning: The decayed portion of the tooth is removed, and the area is cleaned.
  • Shaping and Placement: A custom-matched composite resin is applied and molded to fit your tooth perfectly.
  • Curing and Bonding: A special light hardens the filling for a durable bond.
  • Polishing: The filling is smoothed and polished for a natural feel and appearance.

Our process ensures you leave with a healthy, beautiful smile in just one visit.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do tooth coloured fillings last?+
With proper care, they last 10+ years, providing a natural and durable solution for small to medium cavities.
Are composite fillings safe?+
Yes, they are mercury-free and entirely safe for dental use.
Can they be used for large cavities?+
Tooth-coloured fillings are ideal for small to medium cavities. More extensive restorations, like crowns, may require other options.
