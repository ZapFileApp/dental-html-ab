Your Neighbourhood Dentist in NE Calgary

London Square Dental Centre was founded in 2005 with one clear goal: to make quality dental care affordable and accessible for every family in Calgary. Located at 3545 32 Ave NE, we serve patients from Marlborough, Falconridge, Castleridge, Rundle, Pineridge, and communities across Northeast Calgary.

Over the past two decades, we've grown into one of Calgary's highest-rated dental clinics with a 4.8-star Google rating and over 4,800 happy patients. Our team of four experienced dentists provides everything from routine cleanings and dental implants to Invisalign, cosmetic dentistry, and same-day emergency care.

We believe cost should never stop you from getting the care you need. That's why we're open 7 days a week with late evening appointments available, direct bill to all major insurance providers, accept NIHB for First Nations patients, and offer 0% financing on every treatment.

Dental Assistants Appreciation Week

Every year we take the time to celebrate the amazing dental assistants who keep our clinic running smoothly. The steady hands and warm smiles that make every patient visit better.

Team Visit to Al Amal Preschool

Our team visited Al Amal Preschool right here in NE Calgary for an oral health and hygiene education session. RDA Medina Hassoun led an interactive presentation on the importance of brushing, flossing and regular dental visits, tailored for our youngest community members.

Through visual learning and interactive activities, the kids showed us how much they already knew about dental care and asked great questions about their own teeth. A huge thank-you to Director Renata Muminhodzic for inviting us into the classroom.

Healthy smiles start early, and we love getting to be part of that journey for Calgary families.
