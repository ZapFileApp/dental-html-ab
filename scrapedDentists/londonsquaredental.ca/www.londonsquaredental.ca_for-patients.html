New Patients Welcome

Your First Visit to Our Calgary Dental Clinic

Everything you need before your appointment, including patient forms, insurance information, financing options, and what to expect on your first day.

Your first visit starts with a thorough oral examination by one of our experienced Calgary dentists. We’ll check the health of your teeth, gums, and jaw, take any needed digital X-rays, and discuss your goals and concerns. In most cases, our hygienist will also perform a professional cleaning to remove plaque and tartar buildup that brushing alone can’t reach.

We recommend visiting every six months for a professional cleaning and check-up. Regular preventive care catches small problems early, before they become expensive. We direct bill your insurance so you pay nothing upfront, and offer 0% financing if you need additional treatment.

At London Square Dental, we take time to get to know every patient. Our team speaks 9+ languages and specializes in anxiety-free dentistry, so whether it’s your first dental visit in years or you’re switching from another Calgary clinic, you’ll feel comfortable from the moment you walk in.

Financial Options

One of the many reasons people are afraid to get their oral health checked is financial concerns. London Square Dental firmly believes that dental care should be affordable, easy, and accessible. We have made payments simple through our convenient plans and multiple payment options. Quality dental care should be accessible to everyone.

Direct Insurance Billing

We submit claims directly to your insurance company so you pay nothing upfront for covered services. No paperwork, no waiting for reimbursement.

0% Financing Available

Split your treatment cost into affordable monthly payments with zero interest. Available for all procedures including Invisalign, implants, and cosmetic treatments.

NIHB for First Nations & Inuit

We proudly accept Non-Insured Health Benefits (NIHB) and direct bill for First Nations and Inuit patients. All eligible dental services are covered.

Multiple Payment Methods

We accept cash, Visa, Mastercard, Interac, and personal cheques. Flexible payment plans can be arranged for extensive treatments.

NIHB for First Nations & Inuit Patients

London Square Dental proudly accepts Non-Insured Health Benefits (NIHB) for eligible First Nations and Inuit patients. We handle all NIHB claims and direct billing so you can focus on your dental health without worrying about paperwork or upfront costs.
