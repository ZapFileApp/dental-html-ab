Meet the whole team

Dentist

Dr. Sonia Thibault, DMD

Born in Quebec, Dr. Thibault earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine from Laval University in 1998 and has practiced in Alberta since 2003. Fluent in English, French, Spanish and Creole, she is passionate about easing dental anxiety with comprehensive, gentle care.

Dentist

Dr. Jagbir Singh, DDS

A University of Alberta graduate (known to patients as Dr. Jake), he is committed to high-quality dental care in a comfortable, stress-free environment. His approachable demeanor helps patients feel at ease through every step of treatment.

Dentist

Dr. Aaron Fong, DDS

Dr. Fong holds a Doctor of Dental Surgery from the University of Alberta and a Bachelor of Science in Physiology with Honours. Outside the clinic he volunteers with Hope Mission and enjoys volleyball and pickleball.

Dentist

Dr. Julie Kuo, DDS

Born in Taiwan and fluent in Mandarin, Dr. Kuo is passionate about building long-lasting relationships with her patients and the wider community. She delivers gentle general dentistry across every age group.

Prosthodontist

Dr. Robert Lesniak, DDS, MSc

Born and raised in St. Albert, Dr. Lesniak earned his DDS from the University of Alberta in 2012. His training includes a general practice residency, years of private practice, specialty training in prosthodontics, and a Master's degree in Craniofacial Science.
