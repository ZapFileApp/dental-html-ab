Get to Know Your Dentists

Are you a Dentist Dreader?

We get it. Just because we love teeth and think they’re super cool, doesn’t mean everyone loves the dentist. That’s why it is our mission to help you feel as relaxed as possible while visiting our space.

Here’s what we do to help:

  • 60-minute initial consultations to do an assessment, and have a conversation about how to care for you and your needs best.

  • Create comfortable and homey vibes with our living room-like waiting area, cosy decor, and comfy amenities, like blankets, neck pillows and fidget toys.

  • Beautiful visuals to let your mind wander off, and think about other things, or pretend to be somewhere else, entirely.

  • TV’s on the ceiling so you can pop on your favourite comfort show (or your kiddos), and get lost in a whole other world, while we work on your smile.

  • Silly, goofy dentists who want you to feel calm, and will do their best to help you crack a smile.

If you have any questions or concerns, we would love to meet with you and chat, to help you make a plan that puts your mind at ease.
