Our Services

We offer the whole shebang for your entire family.

  • Routine care will always be less expensive than emergent care. Regular visits to the dentist will help you in keeping your teeth strong and cavity-free. When you visit us, we professionally clean your teeth, removing plaque and calculus that cause inflammation in your mouth, and we examine your teeth thoroughly. This allows us to identify underlying issues early and provide lifestyle recommendations and treatment options so that little problems can be fixed before they become big problems!

  • A filling is recommended when you have a cavity. We first remove the part of the tooth broken down by the cavity, and then repair the lesion with bonded filling materials. These bonded fillings are made from different materials like porcelain, metal or composite resin. The colour of composite resin is similar to your original tooth, giving your teeth a natural look.

  • Root canal therapy aims to restore severely infected teeth without tooth extraction. In this treatment, the canals inside the roots are cleaned, shaped and filled to protect against future infection.

  • We are definitely in the business of keeping your teeth in your mouth — this best maintains your smile AND your function. But sometimes, due to trauma, decay, or excessive bone loss around the tooth from periodontal disease, we have to consider removing the tooth in order to preserve the health of its surrounding teeth, as well as your entire mouth.

  • Crowns, also called dental caps, are made of ceramic, porcelain, metal or resin. They are used as a cover to protect weak teeth or injured teeth. A crown helps to reshape your teeth, provide better functionality and strengthen the weak teeth.

  • Missing teeth can be restored with dental implants. Dental implants are titanium screws inserted into the bone where the tooth used to be. After adequate healing time, a crown can be installed onto the implant to restore your bite.

The Basics

  • As Invisalign® Providers, helping adults to improve the long-term functionality of their teeth and confidence in their smiles is one of the best parts of our day.

    Oral Health Scans

    Complimentary oral health scans are routinely performed to document the structure and position of your teeth and gums, and help us better educate on changes we are seeing in your mouth. And the best bonus?! The iTero digital scanning system eliminates the need for messy putty in your mouth by creating a 3D digital scan of your teeth!

  • You deserve to love your smile. If you don’t, we can help you with that. Whether it's minimally invasive composite bonding or porcelain veneers, we can make small changes that make a big impact!

  • Introducing your little one to the world of dentistry, creating positive experiences for them, and building confident dental patients for life is one of the most impactful things we do and will always be a bright spot in our day. With TVs on our ceilings, a loaded prize box, and a few other tricks up our mama sleeves, we will always find a way to go above and beyond for your child.

  • IYKYK. But did you know that in addition to reducing fine lines and wrinkles, botox can also be used to help reduce pain and headaches associated with clenching and grinding of your teeth?

  • We can pair conscious sedation with any dental treatment you require to help ease your dental anxiety. We offer oral sedation with a medication called triazolam that you take in office, and are monitored throughout your dental treatment as you relax, and often sleep.

The Bonus Stuff

  • Clenching & grinding your teeth? Us too. To best protect the structure and function of your teeth, gums, and bone support, you should consider wearing a splint or “night guard” when you sleep. This will help prevent tooth-to-tooth contact and minimize the damage excessive forces of grinding can cause.

  • Play sports? Wear a guard! Protect your teeth and your noggin’. A 3D digital scan will replace the need for the goopy impressions, and we have a variety of colour options so you can personalize your guard.
