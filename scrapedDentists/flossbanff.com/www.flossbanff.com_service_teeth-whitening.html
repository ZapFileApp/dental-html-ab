Teeth Whitening in Banff

We Offer Teeth Whitening Near You

Do you have teeth stains or discolouration? Teeth whitening is a cosmetic treatment designed to improve teeth appearance by removing stains and discolorations. It is one of the most popular cosmetic treatments offered in our Banff hygiene office and can help boost self-confidence by giving patients a brighter, aesthetically pleasing smile.

At Floss, we offer teeth whitening treatments suitable for different age groups and specific smile goals.

Types of Teeth Whitening Treatments

There are several teeth whitening treatments, including in-office treatments, take-home kits, and over-the-counter whitening products. Some of the most common teeth whitening treatments include:

  • In-office teeth whitening: This is a professional teeth whitening treatment performed by Julie, our Banff hygienist. A bleaching solution is applied to the teeth, activated by a special light, which produces rapid results in just one visit to our hygiene clinic.
  • Take-home teeth whitening kits: The kits typically include trays from our dedicated team that fit over your teeth and a whitening gel placed inside them to give you results in a matter of days. Patients are advised to wear the trays for a specified amount of time each day, usually for several weeks, to achieve the desired results.
  • Over-the-counter teeth whitening products: over-the-counter whitening products, such as whitening toothpaste, strips, and gels, can be purchased at a drugstore or online. They contain a lower concentration of bleaching agent than professional whitening treatments and may require longer treatment times to achieve the desired results.
  • Laser teeth whitening: This treatment involves the use of a special laser by our hygiene team in Banff to activate the bleaching agent applied to the teeth to produce quick and dramatic results.

It is important to attend a consultation at a hygiene clinic near you before starting any teeth whitening treatment to determine the best option for your needs and ensure that your teeth and gums are healthy for a whitening treatment.
