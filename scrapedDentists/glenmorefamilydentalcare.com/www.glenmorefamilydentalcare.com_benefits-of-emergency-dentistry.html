Benefits of Emergency Dentistry

Dental emergencies can strike at any moment, and you should have a dentist that you can see immediately. Emergency dental services help to alleviate problems that cannot wait until the regular visit. Most local dentists provide such services, so getting care in the moment when something goes wrong is less difficult. In this article, we are going to discuss why emergency dental care is essential and how having a dentist in SE Calgary can assist you.

What is Emergency Dentistry?

Emergency dentistry refers to that specific branch of dentistry where urgent issues arise that call for immediate dental treatment. Some issues may range from severe toothache to fractured or knocked-out teeth. These specialists are equipped and trained in how to treat emergencies and deal with such problems within the shortest possible time and relieve pain while preventing any damage.

What Would Be Considered a Dental Emergency?

Not all dental problems require emergency treatment, but certain situations are deemed dental emergencies that require immediate care. The following are some examples of what would be considered a dental emergency:

  • Severe Toothache: If your tooth hurts a lot and over-the-counter pain medicine doesn’t help, it could mean an infection. You should see a dentist right away.
  • Broken or Chipped Tooth: A broken tooth can expose the inside of the tooth to bacteria, which can lead to an infection. Emergency treatment can fix it.
  • Knocked-Out Tooth: If your tooth gets knocked out, try to see a dentist within 30 minutes to have a better chance of saving it.
  • Loose or Lost Filling/Crown: If a filling or crown falls out, it can hurt and expose the tooth. A dentist can replace it right away.
  • Abscessed Tooth: An abscess is a pocket of infection at the root of your tooth. It needs immediate care to prevent serious problems.
  • Soft Tissue Injuries: Injuries to your tongue, cheeks, or lips may need urgent care.
  • Broken Jaw: A broken jaw needs quick treatment and possibly a referral to a specialist.

Benefits of Emergency Dentistry

1. Immediate Relief from Pain

One of the advantages of emergency dentistry is immediate relief from toothache. In fact, dental emergencies can be extremely painful; thus, waiting for a regular appointment is unconscionable. Through emergency dentistry, patients can get relief from their pain as soon as possible, and they don’t have to stay in agony the whole day.

2. Prevents Further Damage

In some cases, dental emergencies can lead to further damage if left untreated. For example, a cracked tooth can become infected if not treated promptly, leading to more serious complications. By seeking dental care from a professional emergency dentist in SE Calgary, patients can prevent further damage and potentially save their teeth.

3. Saves Time and Money

Another advantage of emergency dentistry is that it saves time and money in the long run. When dental issues are addressed promptly, the patient will not have to deal with more complex and expensive treatments later on. For instance, a small cavity may be treated by a simple filling if detected early, but if left untreated, it may need a root canal or even extraction.

4. Available After-Hours and on Weekends

Dental emergencies can happen at any time, including after-hours and on weekends when most dental offices are closed. Fortunately, many emergency dentists offer extended hours and are available on weekends to provide care when patients need it the most.

5. Peace of Mind

Knowing that there is an emergency dentist near you who can provide prompt care in case of a dental emergency can give patients peace of mind. They can rest assured that they will receive the best possible treatment when they need it, without having to wait for days or weeks for a regular appointment.
