Camrose Denture Clinic

Your smile. Our craft.

Utilizing state of the art scanning, designing, and fabrication, we are now able to create the most accurate, customized dentures ever. With accuracy of up to 50 microns (half the thickness of a human hair) we can ensure the best possible fit, and tooth alignment. Not to mention that all of your data is preserved digitally so that when you require your next set, you do not have to start the entire process over again. If you lose your dentures, they can be re-fabricated in 1 appointment.
