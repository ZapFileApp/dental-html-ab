Braces for Teens

At Braces+, we offer several orthodontics options for teens to help get a healthy, long-lasting beautiful smile. Orthodontic treatment for teenagers is usually full (comprehensive) and is supposed to address all orthodontic concerns from start-to-finish in one phase.

Many teenagers get orthodontic braces so you’re not alone! At Braces+, we are excited to offer the most comfortable, efficient and technologically advanced types of braces available.

Some common options include:

  • Metal braces
  • Ceramic braces
  • Gold (Champagne) Braces

Invisalign Teen: Designed with the Teenager in Mind

Bummed out about having braces? Invisalign Teen® gives you a whole new way to wear braces for a “clearly” amazing smile! Invisalign Teen aligners are a perfect fit for your lifestyle because aligners are:

  • Easy to use
  • Easy to clean
  • Removeable
  • Discreet

Able to move with you! (Enjoy an active lifestyle)

Invisalign Teen is a clear aligner system for teeth made specifically for teenagers. It provides an almost invisible way of straightening teeth using clear, removable aligners that are custom-made specifically for your teen’s teeth.
