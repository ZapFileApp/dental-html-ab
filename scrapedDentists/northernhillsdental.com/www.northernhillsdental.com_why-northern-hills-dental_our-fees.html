We follow the Latest Alberta Dental Association and College Fee Guide

Northern Hills Dental is pleased to follow the Current Alberta Dental Association Dental Fee Guide. If you have any questions about our dental fees or your dental insurance, our dental administration team will be happy to answer any questions you may have!

At Northern Hills Dental, our goal is always to offer the best service yet remain competitive by offering fair and reasonable dental fees to our patients and their families.

Exceptions to Fee Guide Billing

Please be aware, that not all procedures are listed as professional fees. The cost for these services vary based on the associated lab costs and depends upon the complexity of the treatment required. We encourage you to call our office to arrange a visit with one of our dentists to evaluate your oral health and the condition of your teeth so that we can provide a more accurate estimate.

At Northern Hills Dental, we feel it is important that you understand your own specific dental insurance policy.  Also, we ask that you are aware that not all procedures are necessarily covered based on your own specific dental insurance coverage. Our Dental Administration Team is here to help you understand your dental insurance coverage!
