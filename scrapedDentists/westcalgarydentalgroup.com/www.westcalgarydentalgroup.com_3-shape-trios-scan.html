What Is a 3-Shape Trios Scan?

A 3-Shape Trios Scan is a digital scanning system that captures clear 3D images of your teeth and gums. Instead of using traditional moulds, a small handheld scanner gently moves over your teeth.

No discomfort

Fast and accurate images

This system helps your dental team design treatments with precision. From crowns to aligners, everything begins with a clear digital model.

FAQs

What makes a 3-Shape Trios Scan different from traditional impressions?

A 3-Shape Trios Scan uses digital imaging rather than physical moulds. It is faster, more comfortable, and provides highly accurate 3D images for better diagnosis and treatment planning outcomes.

Is the scanning process safe?

Yes, the scan is completely safe. It uses a handheld device with no radiation. The process is non-invasive and suitable for patients of all ages, including children.

How long does a 3-Shape Trios Scan take?

The scanning process usually takes only a few minutes. It is quick and efficient, allowing your dental team to capture detailed images without long waiting times or discomfort.

Can this scan be used for clear aligners?

Yes, digital scans are often used to design clear aligners. The use of proper 3D images ensures that the aligners being created fit well over time.

Will I see the results right away?

Yes, one of the biggest benefits is real-time results. You can view 3D images on a screen immediately and better understand your dental condition and treatment options.

Do I need a 3-Shape Trios Scan for every treatment?

Not always. Your dental team will recommend it based on your needs. It is commonly used for restorations, aligners, and detailed evaluations requiring precise digital imaging.
