CLINIC NAME: BOREAL DENTAL CENTRE

Dr. Klassen, Dr. Ritch, Dr. Pestova Ritch and the team are dedicated to providing you with personalized, quality dental care.

When you visit our office you will experience a comprehensive list of general, restorative and cosmetic dental services for the whole family. Our goal is to assist each patient in achieving and maintaining long term dental health and a beautiful smile.

Some of the services we provide include:

  • Exams (Consultations)
  • Cleanings
  • Root Canal Therapy
  • Periodontal (Scaling Treatments)
  • Partial Dentures
  • Tooth Colored Fillings
  • Crowns (Caps)
  • Porcelain Veneers
  • Extractions
  • Wisdom Teeth Extractions (Oral & IV Sedation)
