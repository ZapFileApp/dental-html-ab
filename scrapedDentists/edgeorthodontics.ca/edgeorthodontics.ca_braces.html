What Can Orthodontic Braces Fix?

Braces can treat a wide range of orthodontic problems, including:

  • Crooked or crowded teeth
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Overbite, underbite, or crossbite
  • Jaw misalignment

By applying gentle, consistent pressure, braces gradually move your teeth into better alignment, improving your smile and your long-term dental health.

Types of Braces we offer

We’ll help you choose the option that fits your lifestyle, goals, and budget.

Metal Braces

Durable and reliable, metal braces are the most common option. They’re effective for treating a wide range of orthodontic issues.

Ceramic Braces

These work the same way as metal braces but use clear or tooth-colored brackets for a more discreet appearance.

Your Braces Treatment Journey

Here’s what you can expect when you start braces treatment at our Lloydminster clinic

Initial Consultation & Digital Scan

We begin with a full assessment of your teeth and bite, using 3D digital imaging. We’ll discuss your goals and explain your treatment options.

Frequently asked questions about braces

How long does braces treatment take?

Most people wear braces for 12 to 24 months, depending on how complex the case is.

Is it painful to get braces

You may feel some pressure or soreness for a few days after getting braces or during adjustments. This is normal and temporary.

What foods should I avoid with braces?

What foods should I avoid with braces?
Avoid sticky, crunchy, or hard foods that could damage your brackets or wires. We’ll give you a complete food guide during your first visit.

How often do I need to come in for appointments?

Every 6 to 8 weeks for adjustments and progress checks.

Are braces covered by insurance?

Many insurance plans cover part of the cost. Our team will help you understand your coverage and submit claims.

Do you offer braces for adults in Lloydminster?

Yes. We treat patients of all ages and offer discreet ceramic options for adults looking for a more subtle treatment.
