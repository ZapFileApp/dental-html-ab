About Bow Island Dental

Learn More About Bow Island Dental Centre

Dr. Peter MacLean founded Coaldale Dental Centre as a first location. Then three years later, our second office opened in Bow Island. Dr. Jackie Pitstra has now been serving as our general dentist providing Southern Alberta communities with professional dental care.

Meet The Team

Dr. Jackie Pitstra

Dentist

Dr. Jackie Pitstra joined Bow Island Dental Centre in 2001. Born and raised in Calgary, she attended the University of Calgary receiving her Bachelor of Science Degree. Then in 1994, she completed her Doctor of Dental Surgery Degree at the University of Alberta. For 6 years, Dr. Pitstra went on to practice dentistry in Saskatoon before moving to the Bow Island area. She holds a membership in the Canadian Dental Association, the Alberta Dental Association, and the Calgary & District Dental Society.

Dr. Pitstra is married and has two sons. She enjoys volunteering at her church and the Coaldale District Chamber of Commerce. An enthusiastic cyclist, you can find Dr. Pitstra cycling in the summer months on the highway between Coaldale and Bow Island.

Cori

Practice Manager

Armed with 20 plus years of office experience, Cori keeps our Coaldale & Bow Island branches running at peak efficiency. Her adaptability, coupled with her natural listening abilities, help her maintain relationships within the office.

Robin

Receptionist

With a solid foundation in Medical and Dental Administration from Training Inc., Robin has been an invaluable member of our Bow Island Dental team since June 2022. When not at the office, Robin enjoys hiking and cherishing quality time spent with family.

Alyssa

Receptionist

Born and raised in Coaldale, Alyssa values the strong sense of community that comes with working close to home and brings an entrepreneurial spirit to the front desk. In her free time, she runs her own businesses, helps at the family farm, and is a proud (and unapologetic) Diet Coke enthusiast.

Tiana

Dental Hygienist

A University of Alberta graduate, Tiana earned her Bachelor of Science degree in Dental Hygiene in April 2022. Born and raised in Coaldale, Tiana loves seeing so many familiar faces and being able to connect with patients.

Darcie

Dental Assistant

Darcie is a diligent worker who's always very excited to meet each patient in our Coaldale and Bow Island offices. She's a graduate from the College of the Rockies Registered Dental Assistant program.

Tamarin

Dental Assistant

A SAIT graduate from 2020, Tamarin has been a vital part of the Bow Island Dental team since April 2021. She boasts a unique talent for ripping apples in half and shares her name with a species of monkey.

Miranda

Dental Assistant

Miranda joined Coaldale Dental in June 2022 after completing her Kinesiology degree at the University of Lethbridge and dental assistant training at the College of the Rockies. She has spent time teaching kindergarten in China and enjoys travelling and spending time with her family and her dogs.

Belinda

Dental Assistant

Born in Arizona and raised in Barnwell, Belinda is a proud NAIT graduate who brings positive energy and a friendly presence to our Bow Island office. Outside the clinic, she stays active with basketball, volleyball, and snowboarding at Castle Mountain with friends and family.
