Two Dental Locations in Southern Alberta

Bow Island Dental Center, our second location opened in 1983 and serves families in Bow Island and Medicine Hat.

The first location was founded in 1980 to serve Coaldale and Lethbridge residents. We have successfully served the dental needs of Southern Alberta's vibrant communities over the last four decades with these two locations.

Our operating hours at each dental clinic are customized for that service area. For patients with busy schedules, our Bow Island clinic offers easy-to-book dental appointments on Monday and Thursday evenings.

Schedule an appointment at either of our dental locations and join hundreds of families from Medicine Hat, Lethbridge, Coaldale, and Bow Island along the Crowsnest Highway corridor with great-looking teeth and happy smiles!
