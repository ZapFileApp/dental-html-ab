Dr. Teodora Radisic, DDS

Dentist

Dr. Teodora Radisic is a detail-oriented and caring dentist who values the blend of science and artistry in dentistry. She graduated from the University of Alberta with her Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree in 2017, where she met her now husband, Dr. Richard Roque. Since then, she has focused on helping patients feel confident about their smiles in a way that suits their lifestyle and goals.

With a special interest in Invisalign treatment, Dr. Radisic enjoys guiding patients through the process of achieving straighter, healthier smiles with a modern, personalized approach. She believes that oral health is an essential part of overall well-being and takes the time to ensure patients feel informed and supported every step of the way.

Away from the clinic, Dr. Radisic enjoys photography and spending time outdoors with her family. Whether she's behind the camera or out exploring nature with her husband and their two children, she finds inspiration in the everyday moments that bring people together.


Dr. Richard Roque, DDS

Dr. Roque earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from the University of Alberta in 2017 and has been committed to helping people with their dental needs throughout his career. Dr. Roque has a special interest in oral surgery and dental implants, and he enjoys working closely with patients to restore both function and confidence in their smiles.

He strongly believes in the connection between oral health and overall systemic health, and takes the time to educate patients on how good dental care plays a vital role in their overall well-being. Dr. Roque takes pride in building trust with his patients and making sure they feel at ease every step of the way.

Dr. Roque met his wife, Dr. Teodora Radisic, while they were both studying dentistry at the University of Alberta. Outside the clinic, he enjoys spending time with her and their two children. He’s also an avid basketball fan and loves getting on the court whenever he can. 


Dr. Rhiannon MacPhail , DDS

Dentist

Dr. Rhiannon MacPhail grew up in the beautiful northern town of Peace River, Alberta, where her love for wide open spaces, fresh air, and an active lifestyle first began. A lifelong sports enthusiast and former figure skater, Rhiannon has always had a passion for movement, energy, and connection, which naturally carried over into her career as a dentist.

Since 2011, Dr. MacPhail has been a proud part of the Southfort Dental team, where she’s known for her warm approach, gentle touch, and genuine care for each and every patient. Whether it’s a routine checkup or helping someone rediscover their smile, she believes dental visits should be as comfortable and stress-free as possible… and maybe even a little fun!

Outside of the clinic, life is full and joyful. Dr. MacPhail is happily married and a proud mom to two sweet boys, Austin and Elijah, who keep her on her toes and fill her days with laughter. Her family also includes two lovable dogs, Boss and Flossy, who are always up for an adventure. When she’s not caring for patients, you’ll find her enjoying the outdoors, spending time at the lake, biking, hiking, or
just soaking in the natural beauty of the prairies.

Calling Fort Saskatchewan home for the past 15 years, Dr. MacPhail feels truly lucky to live and work in such a welcoming community. She’s passionate about building lasting relationships with her patients and helping them feel confident in their smiles…for her, dentistry isn’t just a job, it’s a privilege.


Dr. Rosalind Ting, DDS

Dentist

Dr. Rosalind Ting was born in Edmonton, but grew up in Fort McMurray, Alberta.  After completing her dental degree at the University of Alberta in 2005, she began working at Fort Dental Centre in the Fort Saskatchewan Mall.  She has enjoyed the development of that clinic into what is now Southfort Dental Centre.  Dr. Ting is grateful for the opportunity to take care of the oral health needs of families in Fort Saskatchewan.
