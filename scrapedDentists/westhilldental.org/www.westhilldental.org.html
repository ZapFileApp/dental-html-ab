General & Cosmetic Dentistry Peace River AB

Peace River AB Dentists Zoe Nath, DMD and Darnell Dickson, DMD, practice a full scope of general and cosmetic dentistry including:

  • Dental Implant placement
  • Dental hygiene
  • Porcelain veneers
  • Crowns
  • Bridges
  • White bonded composite fillings
  • Dental sealants
  • Tooth extractions
  • Bonding
  • Botox
