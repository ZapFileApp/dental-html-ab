What Sets Us Apart in Dental Care?

Affordable care

Quality dental care should be accessible to all. At 9th Avenue Dental, we strive to provide dental treatments in keeping with Alberta's recommended dental fees. We direct bill to insurance and welcome patients under the CDCP plan so patient families living in Downtown Calgary can receive the best care without a burden.

Modern Technology

we utilize up-to-date technology, and advanced software programs to achieve efficient high-quality, precise procedures. Our modern equipment can help to alleviate pain, improve treatment precision, and reduce recovery time. This provides us with the means to protect your oral health by providing you and your loved ones the best treatment possible.

Comfortable Environment

We acknowledge that going to the dentist may feel stressful. Therefore we place a great emphasis on a friendly, calming and welcoming environment. Our supportive staff members do everything they can to make your visit comfortable and easy. Your comfort and well-being is our priority.

Experienced Team

Our team of downtown Calgary dentists are experienced and trained to deliver custom care for all patients, irrespective of their age. With decades of combined experience, our team has the skill to handle complex cases and treatment procedures while modifying your treatments to your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)?
Yes, we do! We know how important it is for dental care to be affordable, and 9th Avenue Dental is proud to be a CDCP provider dental clinic in Downtown Calgary. If you’re not sure how your coverage works or how to get started with the plan at our clinic, just give us a shout—our team is here to help you navigate the paperwork so you can focus on your smile.
What services does 9th Ave Dental offer?
We offer a variety of dental services, such as orthodontics, restorative procedures, cosmetic dentistry, general dentistry, and more. We can handle both standard cleaning and advanced methods.
I’m having a dental emergency—what should I do?
First, take a deep breath—we’ve got you. If you’ve got a broken tooth, a lost filling, or pain that won’t quit, call us right away. We specialize in emergency dentistry and do our absolute best to fit you in for a same-day appointment. Our goal is to get you out of pain and back to your day as quickly as possible.
Do you accept new patients?
Yes! New patients are always welcome. We would be pleased to assist you whether you need an urgent appointment or are searching for a new dentist.
