Dentist Near Lower Mount Royal

Your smile should be treated with genuine care, right near home. If you need a dentist near Lower Mount Royal, then our team at 9th Avenue Dental is here to help.

Simple Care, Complete Care, for Every Smile

Getting dental care shouldn’t feel complicated. Dental appointments may feel scary, but at 9th Avenue Dental, we make oral care simple, comfortable, and personal for almost everyone. With experience, our team provides a full range of services, including:
  • General Dentistry – Regular checkups and cleanings to ensure lifelong management of oral health.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry – From whitening to veneers, we’ll help you smile confidently.
  • Invisalign – Smooth aligners to straighten your teeth, no metal braces.
  • Oral Surgery – Undertaken safely and gently with our experience, while still ensuring precision is taken care of.
  • Sedation Dentistry – Undertaken as a service for patients who wish to remain calm and relaxed during their visit.
Every treatment is handled with attention and care, because your comfort matters just as much as your results.

Why 9th Avenue Dental Patients Trust Us

Living in Lower Mount Royal can make a person busy. We’ve made sure to facilitate every step of your dental experience to be easy. Below are some reasons why patients in Lower Mount Royal choose our dentist in Downtown Calgary:

  • Direct Bill to your Insurance provider – No paperwork or stress; we handle it for you.
  • Convenient Location – Our team is located just down the street from Lower Mount Royal and the surrounding communities.
  • Accept CDCP coverage – This will help eliminate the economic barrier for patients to receive dental care that is budget-friendly.
  • Flexible Availability – Evenings and flexible appointment availability that suits your schedule.
Our team is led by Dr. Nader Emami and Dr. Tanmay Sanvaliya. With personalized care and communication allowing you to experience a confident and painless dental treatment.
