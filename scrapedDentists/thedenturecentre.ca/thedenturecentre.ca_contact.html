Red Deer Contact Info & Details

The Denture and Implant Centre is happy to be serving clients from all over Central Alberta at our Specialized Clinic in Red Deer. You can find us easily on 46th Street just east of 51st Avenue. There is plenty of parking in front of our wheelchair accessible building. The nearest Red Deer public transit is just one block away. We look forward to serving you!
