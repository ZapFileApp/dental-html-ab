Whether you’re visiting for preventive care, cosmetic options, or restorative treatment, each appointment is tailored to your needs in a welcoming and supportive environment. We aim to make every visit clear, comfortable, and easy to fit into your day.

Comprehensive Dental Services in Calgary

Located in Calgary’s downtown core, 5th Ave Dental Centre welcomes patients from nearby areas, including SW Calgary. Our team provides professional, patient-centred care in a friendly and comfortable environment.

Whether you’re visiting for a routine check-up or a more specific concern, we offer personalized treatment options designed to support long-term oral health.

As a Certified Invisalign® Provider, we also offer clear aligners as a discreet option for straightening teeth without traditional braces.

Our Dental Services

At 5th Ave Dental Centre, we offer a full range of dental services to help you maintain, restore, and enhance your oral health. Each treatment is provided with care, clear communication, and a focus on your comfort.

Our services include:

  • Preventive Care: Routine cleanings, oral exams, and digital X-rays.

  • Restorative Treatments: Fillings, crowns, and bridges.

  • Root Canal Therapy: Treatment to address infection and preserve natural teeth.

  • Cosmetic Dentistry Teeth whitening, veneers, and aesthetic treatment options.

  • Dental Implants: Secure, natural-looking tooth replacement solutions.

  • Prosthodontist: Comprehensive care for crowns, bridges, partial dentures, and full dentures.

 

We also offer free parking, extended evening hours, and alternative Saturdays & Sundays appointments to support your schedule.
