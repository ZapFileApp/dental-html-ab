FAQs and Oral Health

Have Questions? We Have Answers

Welcome to the 5th Ave Dental Centre FAQ and Oral Health page! Our goal is to provide you with valuable information to help you maintain a healthy smile. Below, you’ll find answers to common questions about our services and essential oral health tips. Located in SW Calgary, we are dedicated to offering comprehensive dental care tailored to your needs.

Q: What services do you offer at 5th Ave Dental Centre?

A: At 5th Ave Dental Centre, we offer a wide range of dental services, including general checkups, cleanings, fillings, root canals, extractions, cosmetic dentistry, and more. Our experienced team is here to address all your dental needs.

Q: How do I schedule an appointment?

A: We look forward to seeing you soon! You can call us directly at (403) 261-2005 or book an appointment online using Dental Appointment form. You may choose to book an appointment with or without a cleaning.

Q: How often should I schedule a dental checkup?

A: We recommend scheduling a dental checkup and cleaning every six months. However, your dentist may recommend a different frequency based on your individual oral health needs. Book your Dental Appointment or Call Us today (403) 261-2005

Q: How do I fill out a New Patient Form?

A: Our Dental Appointment are quick and easy to fill out. We require basic personal information. All information you provide will remain completely confidential.

Q: Do I need X-rays taken?
A: We take necessary x-rays for proper diagnosis and treatment planning, but if you have recently had x-rays taken, we will work with you to contact the last office where you had then, and get the x-rays from the previous dental provider.
Q: What should I expect during my first visit?

A: During your first visit, we will perform a comprehensive dental examination, review your medical history, and discuss any concerns you may have. Depending on your needs, we may take X-rays and recommend a treatment plan tailored to you.

Q: Do you offer Direct Billing?
A: We are pleased to offer direct billing to most insurance companies. Please bring copies of your current insurance provider to your first visit so we can best assist you.
Q: What is a Pre-treatment estimate?
A: A pre-treatment estimate gives you the approximate cost of treatment. Upon request, we will file all the necessary paperwork with your insurance company before any treatment is done. While this may delay your treatment while you wait for a response from your insurance provider, you will know what (if any) out-of-pocket costs you will be required to cover.
Q: If I have Insurance, do I have to pay for anything?

A: We follow the Alberta fee guide, and we do our very best to provide an accurate estate of your insurance discounts for treatment. Please note that for certain procedures, you will be required to pay for the remaining portion of your treatment.

If you cannot find the answer to your question in our list of FAQs, please do not hesitate
to call us at (403) 261-2005 and we will be happy to help.
Call 5th Ave Dental Centre at (403) 261-2005  or book your dental appointment online now!
