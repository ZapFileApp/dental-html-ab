What Are Dental Implants? 

Dental implants are small titanium posts that are surgically placed into the jawbone where teeth are missing. These posts act as artificial roots and support crowns, bridges, or dentures.

The implant fuses with the bone over time—a process called osseointegration—creating a stable foundation for the replacement tooth. The result? A natural-looking, fully functional tooth that won’t slip, click, or need removal.

Unlike traditional dentures that rest on top of the gums, dental implants in SW Calgary integrate with your bone structure, helping maintain the shape of your face and prevent bone loss.

Why Choose Dental Implants Over Dentures?

Dentures may be a common option, but they come with limitations: they can move around, require adhesives, and may impact speech or chewing. Implants, on the other hand, offer many benefits:

  • Stability and Comfort
  • Natural Appearance
  • Long-Term Solution
  • Jawbone Preservation
  • No Daily Removal

Patients appreciate the convenience and peace of mind that dental implants provide compared to removable dentures.

Who Is a Good Candidate for Dental Implants?

You may be a good candidate for dental implants if you:

  • Are missing one or more teeth
  • Have healthy gums and sufficient jawbone density
  • Are in good general health
  • Don’t smoke, or are willing to quit during the healing process

Even if you’ve been told in the past that you’re not a candidate due to bone loss, newer techniques such as bone grafting may make implants possible. Glenmore Park Dental provides personalized assessments to determine the best solution for your situation.
