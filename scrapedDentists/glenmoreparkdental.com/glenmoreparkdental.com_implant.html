Regain Your Confidence with Permanent Dental Implants

Losing a single tooth affects more than your smile. It can cause surrounding teeth to shift, lead to bone loss over time, and change the way you eat and speak. A single tooth implant at Glenmore Park Dental replaces your missing tooth from the root up, with a titanium post anchored into the jawbone and a custom crown that blends seamlessly with your natural teeth. 

Unlike a traditional bridge, a single implant doesn't require grinding down the healthy teeth on either side. It stands completely on its own, preserving your surrounding tooth structure and long-term oral health. The result looks, feels, and functions just like your original tooth. 

Not sure if a single tooth implant is the right solution for you? Book an appointment, and our Calgary implant team will walk you through your options.

If you're missing multiple teeth in a row, an implant-supported bridge offers a stable, natural-looking restoration without touching the healthy teeth around it. Traditional bridges rely on neighbouring teeth for support, requiring them to be filed down and permanently altered. Implant-supported bridges are different — two implants anchor the bridge directly into the jawbone, eliminating that compromise entirely. 

The result is a fixed, comfortable restoration that restores your bite, supports your facial structure, and looks completely natural, with no slipping, no adhesives, and no unnecessary impact on the teeth you still have. 

Not sure whether a bridge or individual implants are the better fit for your situation? Book an appointment, and we'll help you understand which option gives you the best long-term outcome.

If you've lost most or all of your teeth — or you're struggling with loose, uncomfortable dentures — full-arch implants offer a permanent alternative that traditional dentures simply can't match. Using a small number of strategically placed implants, we anchor a full set of teeth securely into your jawbone, restoring your ability to eat, speak, and smile with complete confidence. 

Unlike removable dentures, full-arch implants don't shift, don't require adhesives, and don't need to be taken out at night. They also help preserve the jawbone, preventing the facial changes that often accompany long-term tooth loss. It's one of the most life-changing procedures we offer at Glenmore Park Dental — and for many patients, the only regret is not doing it sooner. 

Not sure if full-arch implants are right for your situation? Book an appointment, and our experienced Calgary team will assess your needs and explain all available options.
