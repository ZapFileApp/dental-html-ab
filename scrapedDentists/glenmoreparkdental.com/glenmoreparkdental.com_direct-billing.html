Simple, Stress-Free Payments with Direct Billing!

At Glenmore Park Dental, we believe that paying for dental care should be easy. That’s why we offer direct billing to all major insurance providers, so you don’t have to deal with paperwork, out-of-pocket expenses, or reimbursement delays. 

With direct billing, we:

  • Submit your claim directly to your insurance provider
  • Only charge you for any remaining balance, if applicable
  • Handle the paperwork, so you don’t have to

We work with all major insurance companies, including:

  • Alberta Blue Cross
  • Sun Life Financial
  • Canada Life
  • Manulife
  • Green Shield Canada
  • Other major insurers

Everything You Need to Know About Your Dental Visit!

Direct billing allows us to submit your dental insurance claim directly to your provider, so you only pay for any portion not covered by your plan.

We accept most major insurance providers, including Alberta Blue Cross, Sun Life, Manulife, Canada Life, Green Shield, and more. Call us if you don’t see your insurer listed! 

If your plan covers 100% of your treatment, you won’t pay anything. If only part of your treatment is covered, you’ll pay the remaining balance.

We can submit a predetermination request to your insurance provider to find out what’s covered before your appointment.

If your provider doesn’t support direct billing, you will pay upfront and submit a claim for reimbursement.

Yes! We provide direct billing for family members, including children covered under a parent’s insurance plan.
