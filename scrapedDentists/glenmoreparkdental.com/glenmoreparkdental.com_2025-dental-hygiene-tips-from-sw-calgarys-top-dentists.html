5 Simple Steps from Glenmore Park Dental to Improve Oral Hygiene in 2025

Step 1: Master the Basics – Brush and Floss Properly 


It all starts at home. Brushing twice a day and flossing once daily are the cornerstones of a healthy smile. But technique makes all the difference. Use a soft-bristled toothbrush angled toward your gums, brushing in gentle, circular motions. When flossing, hug the sides of each tooth in a “C” shape to clean below the gumline.

At Glenmore Park Dental, we often see patients who brush regularly but still develop gum issues due to ineffective techniques. That’s why our hygienists take the time to provide personalized instruction during every appointment—especially helpful for young families and busy professionals in SW Calgary.

Step 2: Don’t Skip Your Professional Cleanings 


Even the most thorough at-home routine can’t replace a professional dental cleaning. Tartar buildup can only be removed with specialized tools. We recommend visiting Glenmore Park Dental every six months for a full cleaning and exam—or more often if you have a history of gum disease or tartar buildup.

With flexible scheduling and a warm, welcoming environment, we make it easy for residents in Lakeview, Glamorgan, and Elboya to stay on top of their dental health.

Step 3: Try Biofilm Removal from Glenmore Park Dental for a More Comfortable Cleaning 


Looking for a gentler alternative to traditional cleanings? Glenmore Park Dental offers biofilm removal (air polishing)—a modern, minimally invasive method that removes harmful bacteria using air polishing technology. It’s safe, comfortable, and effective for patients of all ages.

air polishing appointments also include fluoride treatment, tartar removal, and customized hygiene advice to help you maintain results at home. It’s no wonder patients from Calgary’s SW choose our clinic for a pain-free and thorough cleaning experience.

Step 4: Drink More Water, Eat Less Sugar 


Calgary’s dry air contributes to dry mouth, which can lead to tooth decay. Staying hydrated is key—especially after meals—to wash away food particles and support saliva production. Avoid sugary drinks and acidic snacks, which can erode enamel and cause cavities.

During your visit, our team provides dietary guidance tailored to your lifestyle—helping families in Acadia and professionals in Garrison Woods make smarter choices every day.

Step 5: Be Proactive with Preventive Treatments 


Prevention is powerful. Glenmore Park Dental offers fluoride varnishes, sealants, and screenings for conditions like sleep apnea, teeth grinding, and early gum disease. These small steps today can prevent larger, costlier issues down the road. Regular checkups ensure your dental hygiene is well maintained for a healthy smile.

Whether you’re booking for yourself or your child, our clinic takes the time to create a preventive plan that fits your needs—and your schedule.
