What Does CDCP Cover?

The Canadian Dental Care Plan covers essential dental services to help you maintain a healthy smile. At our Glenmore Park practice, your CDCP benefits can be used for:

  • Preventive care – Regular exams, cleanings, and X-rays
  • Restorative treatments – Fillings, crowns, and root canal therapy
  • Oral surgery – Tooth extractions (simple and surgical)
  • Denture services – Complete dentures, partial dentures, and repairs
  • Additional treatments – Periodontal care and other services (some may require pre-authorization)

We make coverage simple: Our team verifies your CDCP benefits upfront and explains exactly what's covered before any treatment begins. You'll have complete clarity on costs and coverage—guaranteed.

Everything You Need to Know About CDCP Before your Dental Visit!

The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) is a federal program designed to make dental care more accessible and affordable for eligible Canadians. It covers essential dental services like exams, cleanings, fillings, and extractions for patients who meet income and insurance criteria. Furthermore, through CDCP, our dental office will bill the government directly, so you don’t have to worry. Glenmore Park Dental is proud to accept CDCP patients and help them access quality care with less financial stress. 

Yes! Glenmore Park Dental proudly welcomes patients who are eligible for the Canadian Dental Care Plan. We offer direct billing for CDCP-covered treatments to make accessing dental care in Calgary as easy and convenient as possible. Contact us today to book your hassle free appointment!

Cosmetic services like whitening, veneers, implants, and crown restorations (except prefabricated crowns for children) are not included. At Glenmore Park Dental, we’ll walk you through exactly what’s covered and handle the direct billing so there are no surprises.

The CDCP covers essential dental services such as routine exams, cleanings, x-rays, cavity fillings, and extractions. While cosmetic treatments like teeth whitening and veneers are not covered, Glenmore Park Dental provides a full range of general dental services eligible under the plan.

Eligibility for the CDCP depends on your household income and whether you have private dental insurance. If you meet the criteria, you can use your benefits at participating clinics like Glenmore Park Dental. We encourage you to check the official CDCP guidelines or contact our team for help confirming your eligibility.

Using your CDCP coverage is simple at Glenmore Park Dental. Just bring your eligibility information to your appointment, and our team will handle the direct billing for covered services. We’re happy to assist with any paperwork or questions you may have about your CDCP benefits.

If a needed service isn’t covered by the CDCP, Glenmore Park Dental will discuss your treatment options and associated costs with you clearly and transparently. We also offer flexible payment solutions to help you access any additional care you may need.
