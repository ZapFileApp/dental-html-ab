About Redwater Dental Clinic

A team that listens, explains clearly, and helps you feel comfortable

Since 1980, families across Redwater and surrounding communities have trusted us with their care — and many of those patients have been coming back for decades. That trust doesn't come from technology or credentials alone. It comes from how we treat people.

Led by Dr. AJ Jalota, our team takes the time to listen, explain clearly, and help every patient feel comfortable — whether it's your first visit or your fiftieth. We offer full-service dental care under one roof, including sedation options and comfort amenities for patients who need a little extra support. New patients are always welcome.

We also take a whole-body approach to dentistry — using mercury-free, biocompatible materials and considering how your oral health connects to your overall wellbeing.
