About Our Practice

Whether you're coming in for a routine cleaning, dealing with a toothache, or finally getting the dental work you've been putting off — we're here to make the experience easier than you expect. No judgment. No pressure. Just honest, thoughtful care.

Led by Dr. AJ Jalota and a team that genuinely cares how you feel, we offer everything from general check-ups to implants, same-day crowns, and cosmetic work — all under one roof, with sedation options and a comfort menu for patients who need a little extra support.

We take a whole-body approach to your care — using mercury-free, biocompatible materials and always considering how your oral health connects to your overall wellbeing.
