Our Dental Office in Redwater, Alberta

Take a tour of our modern, welcoming clinic

Welcome to Redwater Dental Clinic, where generations of families have trusted us with their smiles since 1980. Led by Dr. AJ Jalota, our clinic provides personalized, full-service dental care in Redwater.

We emphasize expertise in aesthetic dentistry, implant work, and state-of-the-art technology including 3D Cone Beam imaging. Our modern, bright open-concept clinic features a dedicated kids' area, and every child leaves with their own toy after their appointment — making dental visits something the whole family can look forward to.
