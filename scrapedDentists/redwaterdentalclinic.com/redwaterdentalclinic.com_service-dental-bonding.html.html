Dental Bonding

Dental bonding is a quick and affordable cosmetic procedure that uses tooth-colored resin to repair chips, cracks, gaps, and discoloration. At Redwater Dental Clinic, we can transform your smile in just one visit with this versatile treatment.

Benefits of Dental Bonding

  • Completed in a single visit — no lab work needed
  • Affordable alternative to veneers or crowns
  • Matches your natural tooth color perfectly
  • Minimal tooth preparation required
  • Repairs chips, cracks, and gaps between teeth
  • Can reshape misformed or uneven teeth

The Bonding Process

Dental bonding is one of the simplest cosmetic procedures:

  1. Shade selection to match your natural teeth
  2. Light preparation of the tooth surface
  3. Application and sculpting of the composite resin
  4. Curing with a special light to harden the material
  5. Final shaping and polishing for a natural finish

Dental bonding typically takes 30 to 60 minutes per tooth and usually requires no anesthetic. It is an excellent option for minor cosmetic improvements. For a more dramatic change, consider pairing bonding with professional teeth whitening.
