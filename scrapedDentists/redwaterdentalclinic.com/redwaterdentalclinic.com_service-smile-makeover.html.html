Smile Makeover

A customized plan to transform your smile

What Is a Smile Makeover?

A smile makeover is a personalized treatment plan that combines two or more cosmetic and restorative dental procedures to comprehensively improve the appearance of your smile. Rather than addressing one issue at a time, a smile makeover takes a holistic approach, considering the colour, alignment, shape, size, and overall balance of your teeth to create the smile you have always wanted.

At Redwater Dental Clinic in Redwater, AB, Dr. AJ works closely with each patient to design a smile makeover plan that addresses their specific concerns and goals. Whether you are dealing with stained teeth, gaps, chips, missing teeth, or a combination of issues, we can create a treatment plan that delivers dramatic, natural-looking results. Every smile makeover is unique because every patient's needs and goals are different.

Treatments We Combine

A smile makeover may include any combination of the following treatments, depending on your individual needs:

Porcelain Veneers: Thin shells of porcelain bonded to the front surfaces of your teeth to correct chips, cracks, gaps, uneven teeth, and permanent staining. Veneers provide a dramatic transformation and can completely change the shape, size, and colour of your teeth. At Redwater Dental Clinic, we also offer same-day veneers using our same-day technology for faster results.

Professional Teeth Whitening: In-office whitening can brighten your teeth by several shades in a single visit. This is often one of the first steps in a smile makeover, as it establishes the ideal shade of white before other restorations are placed to match. We also offer take-home whitening kits for gradual, convenient whitening at your own pace.

Dental Implants: For patients with missing teeth, dental implants provide a permanent, natural-looking replacement. Implants are anchored directly into the jawbone, functioning and feeling like real teeth. They are often a cornerstone of smile makeovers for patients who have lost teeth due to injury, decay, or gum disease.

Dental Crowns and Bridges: Crowns restore damaged, broken, or severely decayed teeth to their original shape and strength. Bridges replace one or more missing teeth by anchoring to the teeth on either side. Both can be colour-matched to blend seamlessly with your natural teeth or new restorations.

Invisalign Clear Aligners: For patients with crooked or misaligned teeth, Invisalign can straighten your smile discreetly before or alongside other cosmetic treatments. Clear aligners are virtually invisible and can be removed for eating and brushing, making them a convenient option for adults.

Dental Bonding: A quick, affordable option for minor chips, gaps, or discolouration. Tooth-coloured composite resin is applied and sculpted directly onto the tooth, then hardened with a light. Bonding can be an effective component of a smile makeover for smaller imperfections.

Gum Contouring: If your gums are uneven or you have a "gummy smile," gum contouring can reshape the gum line to create a more balanced, symmetrical appearance. This procedure can make a significant difference in the overall aesthetics of your smile.

Who Is a Candidate?

Almost anyone who is unhappy with the appearance of their smile can benefit from a smile makeover. You may be a candidate if you have:

  • Stained, yellowed, or discoloured teeth that do not respond to regular whitening
  • Chipped, cracked, or worn teeth
  • Gaps between teeth
  • Crooked or misaligned teeth
  • Missing teeth
  • Uneven or short teeth
  • An uneven gum line or excessive gum tissue
  • Old, discoloured fillings or restorations that need replacing
  • A smile that you feel self-conscious about

Before beginning any cosmetic work, we ensure your oral health foundation is solid. This means addressing any cavities, gum disease, or other health issues first. A healthy mouth is the best starting point for a beautiful smile.

The Smile Makeover Process

At Redwater Dental Clinic, a smile makeover begins with a thorough consultation. Here is what you can expect:

  1. Consultation and Assessment: We discuss your goals, examine your teeth, take X-rays and photographs, and evaluate your overall oral health. This is your chance to tell us exactly what you would like to change about your smile.
  2. Treatment Planning: Dr. AJ creates a customized treatment plan that outlines which procedures are recommended, the sequence of treatments, the expected timeline, and the estimated cost. We may use digital imaging to show you a preview of your potential results.
  3. Foundation Work: If needed, we address any underlying oral health issues first, such as cavities, gum disease, or infections. This ensures long-lasting results.
  4. Cosmetic and Restorative Treatments: We proceed with the planned treatments in the optimal order. Some procedures may be done in a single visit, while others may require multiple appointments spread over weeks or months.
  5. Final Results and Maintenance: Once all treatments are complete, we review your results and provide a maintenance plan to help you keep your new smile looking its best for years to come.

Smile Makeover Results

The results of a smile makeover can be truly life-changing. Patients often report increased confidence, a willingness to smile more freely in social and professional situations, and an overall improvement in their quality of life. At Redwater Dental Clinic, we take pride in delivering results that look natural and feel comfortable.

The longevity of your results depends on the specific treatments involved and how well you maintain your oral health. Porcelain veneers can last 10 to 15 years or more. Dental implants can last a lifetime with proper care. Professional whitening results can be maintained with touch-up treatments and good oral hygiene.

To keep your smile makeover looking its best, we recommend regular checkups and cleanings at Redwater Dental Clinic, consistent brushing and flossing, avoiding habits that can damage restorations (like chewing ice or biting your nails), and wearing a night guard if you grind your teeth.
