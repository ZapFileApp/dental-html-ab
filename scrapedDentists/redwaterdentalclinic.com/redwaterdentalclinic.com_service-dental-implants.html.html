Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement solution that looks and feels natural

Quick answer: A dental implant is a titanium post surgically placed in the jawbone to replace the root of a missing tooth, topped with a custom crown that looks and functions like a natural tooth. At Redwater Dental Clinic, the full implant process — from initial 3D scan to final crown placement — typically takes three to six months, with healing time between stages.

What Are Dental Implants?

Dental implants are the gold standard for replacing missing teeth. They consist of a titanium post surgically placed in your jawbone, topped with a custom crown that matches your natural teeth perfectly.

If the idea of implant surgery feels daunting, that's completely normal. Many of our implant patients felt the same way before their procedure — and most say it was easier than they expected. Dr. AJ will walk you through the entire process at your own pace. For comfort, we offer oral sedation, and IV sedation is also available at the clinic when needed.

Benefits of Dental Implants

  • Look, feel, and function like natural teeth
  • Prevent bone loss in the jaw
  • Last a lifetime with proper care
  • Don't require altering adjacent teeth (unlike dental bridges)
  • Restore full chewing power
  • Maintain facial structure

The Implant Process

Our experienced team uses advanced 3D Cone Beam imaging to plan your implant placement with precision. The process typically involves:

  1. Initial consultation and 3D imaging
  2. Surgical placement of the titanium post
  3. Healing period (3-6 months)
  4. Placement of the custom crown

Am I a Candidate?

Most adults with good general health and adequate jawbone are candidates for dental implants. If implants are not suitable, alternatives such as dentures may be recommended. We'll perform a thorough evaluation to determine if implants are right for you.

How Long Do Dental Implants Last?

Dental implants have a success rate of over 95% according to the American Dental Association and can last a lifetime with proper care. The titanium post is biocompatible — it fuses naturally with your jawbone through a process called osseointegration. The crown on top is made from ceramic or zirconia, giving you a metal-free, natural-looking restoration. Simply brush and floss as you would your natural teeth and keep up with regular dental checkups. Learn more about our whole-body approach to dental care.

Recovery & Timeline

The full implant process takes three to six months from placement to final crown. After the titanium post is placed, a healing period of three to four months allows the implant to fuse with the jawbone (osseointegration). Most patients manage post-surgery discomfort with over-the-counter pain relief and return to normal activities within a day or two. A temporary restoration keeps your smile complete during the healing period.

Cost & Insurance

Dental implant costs vary depending on the number of implants needed, whether bone grafting is required, and the type of restoration (crown, bridge, or denture). At Redwater Dental Clinic, we provide a detailed cost estimate during your consultation so there are no surprises.

  • We direct bill most major insurance providers including Sun Life, Manulife, Great-West Life, Blue Cross, and Canada Life
  • Many insurance plans cover a portion of implant treatment
  • Flexible payment plans available to make treatment affordable
  • CDCP (Canadian Dental Care Plan) accepted
