Welcome to our office.
Your home, workplace or care facility.

We pride ourselves on providing accessible and flexible dental hygiene care to those who need it the most.
Whether you are a busy executive, a senior, or a family with multiple children, we give you the comfort of knowing your oral health is being taken care of.

Sit back, relax, and leave the rest to us!

What is mobile dental hygiene services?

We provide the convenience of professional oral dental care in the comfort of your own environment.
On the day of your appointment, we bring a fully mobile dental hygiene kit that includes everything we need.

We ensure that essential dental cleanings and preventive treatments are easily accessible to you and your family.

About Us

My Mobile Dental Hygienist was founded by Kristy Gerber in 2019 after countless conversations with friends and family about the inconveniences of getting to their dental hygiene appointments.
We take the work out of maintaining your oral hygiene by bringing a full-service mobile dental hygiene clinic to you.

Whether it is because life is just too busy, mobility issues make it difficult to leave your home or bringing multiple kids to the dentist's office is not your version of fun, we provide the professional care that you expect, with the convenience you need.
