For Corporate Employees

  • What if you could provide an added benefit for your staff and save your business time and money?

    Traditional dental appointments often require your staff to set aside hours for a check-up or treatment. In contrast, in-office dental care offers quick and efficient appointments!

    We work with your HR department to set up a timeframe that will work for your employees. This could be one day, or over multiple days.

    Our team will set up our portable dental cleaning station in a meeting or boardroom and will see each employee at their appointment time.

    We then submit any insurance forms for each patient and inform their dentist that they have had a routine cleaning. That's it!

  • Many companies spend a lot of money on dental benefits and/or health spending accounts that do not get used.

    We make it easy for your employees to make their health a priority.

    We provide a unique benefit to your employees by giving them convenient access to the dental care they need without having to leave the office!

    There are no waiting rooms and we offer fast appointments for the most time-squeezed staff.

  • In-office dental care is the perfect solution for busy office professionals looking to prioritize their oral health without compromising their demanding schedules.

    Your team’s overall health is an important factor directly linked to their performance at work.
    Providing them with a convenient way to access the dental health care they need will help them feel great and decrease missed days.

    Our team is dedicated to providing high-quality, professional dental care to your employees.

    Providing them with a unique and amazing experience they will tell their friends about!

Seniors and Care Facilities

  • With over two decades of experience as dental hygienists, we specialize in serving patients with mobility, cognitive impairments, and other special needs.

    Our dedicated professional staff prioritizes the oral health of seniors, ensuring the highest standard of dental care.

    Our goals are to provide access to oral care for those who have challenges getting to a regular dental office, to prevent and treat oral diseases and improve patient quality of life due to dental pain or issue.

  • We take pride in offering comprehensive dental hygiene care to residents of care facilities.
    Our dedicated team understands the unique needs of elderly residents and patients with mobility challenges.
    Eliminating the logistical challenges of traveling to a traditional dental office is a major advantage. Providing a higher quality of life by promoting comfort, convenience, and personalized care.

    We bring everything we need to your care facility, making it convenient and stress-free for caregivers, removing the need for your residents to leave the facility.

    Our fully portable dental equipment can accommodate bedside or wheelchair dental care eliminating mobility challenges.

    We provide teeth cleaning, polishing, desensitizing, sealants and fluoride.

    Oral health assessments, oral cancer screenings and periodontal therapy, with local anesthetic, if required are also offered.

Individuals and Families

  • Our mobile dental hygienist service was initiated to cater to the demands of modern, fast-paced lifestyles. By bringing dental hygiene appointments directly to you, we eliminate the need for time-consuming trips to a dental office.

    This convenience ensures that oral health remains a top priority, even amidst hectic work and life schedules.

  • Leave the stress of waiting rooms behind and book your family’s dental cleanings in your home.

    We understand the challenges you face in balancing family commitments. Our mobile service is designed to alleviate the burden of getting to dental cleaning appointments with little ones in tow. We make it easier for families to prioritize their oral health when they book a family appointment with us.

    By offering dental hygiene care in your home, we aim to support your family in maintaining a healthy and happy lifestyle.
