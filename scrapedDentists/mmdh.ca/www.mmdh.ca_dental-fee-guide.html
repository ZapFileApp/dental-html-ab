Our Dental Fee Guide

Understanding your Dental Costs

Making dental wellness more accessible—both in cost and convenience.
Our office follows the current Alberta Dental Fee Guide to provide transparent and consistent pricing.

Clear, Affordable, Professional

At My Mobile Dental Hygienist, we’re committed to making high-quality dental hygiene care accessible, transparent, and convenient.

Our office follows the current Alberta Dental Fee Guide to help provide fair and consistent pricing, so you can manage your oral health with ease and confidence.

Why Experience Matters:

Our team brings over 21 years of clinical experience to every appointment. Each of our hygienists holds a 4-year university degree, which means we offer care grounded in advanced education, evidence-based practice, and patient-centered service.

We take pride in our collaborative approach with local dentists to ensure continuity of care—whether you’re due for a cleaning or need follow-up treatment.

What is the Alberta Dental Fee Guide?

The Alberta Dental Fee Guide is a recommended pricing schedule developed by the Alberta Dental Association.

It provides guidance on the typical costs of dental services across the province, and many insurance providers use this guide to determine coverage and reimbursement.

Our office follows the current Alberta Dental Fee Guide.

This approach helps provide:

● Transparent pricing for patients

● Consistent reimbursement from insurers

● Accessible care without unexpected costs

Dental Insurance

No Insurance?

We’re here to help you make the most of your dental care budget. A dental hygiene appointment is often the ideal starting point—allowing us to assess your oral health and guide you on the timing of your next visit to the dental office for an exam or X-rays.

Have Insurance?

We offer direct billing and are happy to help you understand your benefits. Not all plans cover every procedure, so our team will work with you to review your coverage and explain any out-of-pocket costs clearly.
