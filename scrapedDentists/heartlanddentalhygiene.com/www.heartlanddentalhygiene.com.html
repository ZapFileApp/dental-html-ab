About

Corinne Story, the owner of the practice, has been a Registered Dental Hygienist (RDH) for over 30 years. Her Diploma in Dental Hygiene is from the University of Alberta, and her Degree in Dental Science is from the University of British Columbia. Her education also includes a Master of Arts in Physical Education and Recreation (Kinesiology) from the University of Alberta, and all required coursework for a PhD in Nursing.

Heartland Dental Hygiene Services

Heartland Dental Hygiene promotes biological/holistic dental hygiene. Clients are offered services such as, dental hygiene exams, teeth cleanings using traditional methods (hand scaling and power scaling) and the newest technology using guided biofilm therapy (GBT). Heartland also provides periodontal therapy, myofunctional therapy, Zoom teeth whitening, implant care, biofilm testing, and overall oral health education.
