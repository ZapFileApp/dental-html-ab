Dental Services in Edmonton

We Offer A Full Range Of Dental Services

Bonnie Doon Dental Associates provides dental services in Edmonton and we are located south side Edmonton, just north of Whyte Avenue on 91st.  We accept your insurance for your dental services and will direct bill for you and your families convenience.  We are open Saturdays from 9am to 4pm to accommodate those who can’t get away from work during the week.  Give us a call at 780-466-2161 for your dental services in Edmonton today!  All services provided by a general dentist.
