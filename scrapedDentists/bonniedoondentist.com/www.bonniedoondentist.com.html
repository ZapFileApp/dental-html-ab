IV Sedation - Are You a Potential Candidate?

Bonnie Doon Dental Associates is thrilled to announce that we now offer IV sedation. Ideal candidates for IV sedation dentistry include individuals with moderate to severe dental anxiety, patients in need of extensive or particularly painful dental work, and those with a strong gag reflex or who require longer or multiple dental procedures. The suitability for IV sedation is determined through a comprehensive health evaluation and consideration of the patient’s medical history.

IV sedation dentistry provides deep conscious sedation through intravenous (IV) administration of sedatives during dental procedures. This approach falls under the category of conscious sedation, meaning that while patients remain awake, they become significantly less aware of their surroundings. This state allows them to respond to verbal commands from their dental team.

Dental Tips For Oral Health

What you can do to maintain a healthy smile!

Eat Your Way to Whiter Teeth
Did you know? Eating crunchy vegetables like broccoli, carrots and cauliflower can actually help clean your teeth. As you bite off and chew your crisp veggies, the vegetable fibres are naturally scrubbing your teeth and gums which can help prevent buildup of plaque, tartar, and bacteria. Follow up your healthy veggies with a glass of fresh water for a post snack rinse and, SAY CHEESE!!
Music For The Soul
If you experience some anxiety about seeing the dentist or certain dental procedures, let your dentist know and they will be happy to explore options that can help address your fears. For some, listening to music during dental procedures can help redirect their thoughts from anxiety to a favorite song or band. Listen to an audio book or podcasts while waiting for your appointment and before you know it, you are in and out with a healthy smile.
Brushing Tips for Kids
To help your kids build healthy brushing habits, here are a few tricks that can make the twice daily routine a little less of a struggle. Try encouraging your kids to brush their teeth while they listen to a favorite song, or have them use a 2 minute timer. Brush your teeth alongside them and let them see an expert in action! Using colored disclosing tablets, available from your dentist, can help kids see where they are missing the mark.

Let Us Help You

General Dentistry

Even when you take good care of your teeth, problems can still happen. Your teeth are prone to decay and breakage as you age, and your gums are susceptible to infections or diseases. Ensure your mouth stays as healthy as possible by visiting your family dentist when you experience pain with your teeth or jaw while chewing, have tender or bleeding gums, or feel any loose teeth.
Preventative Plans

Consistent appointments with your family dentist from Edmonton allow us to track your teeth over time and predict any potential issues you may have. Early detection is always important, so please don’t delay your dentist appointments! Our thorough teeth cleanings remove the primary causes of many dental problems, including plaque, tartar and bacteria. During your appointment, we’ll also polish your teeth and review your brushing and flossing techniques together.
Regular Visits

Your dental health is closely tied to your general well-being, which is why you should regularly visit your family dentist for a check-up. When you take proper care of your teeth and gums, you will smile with more confidence and be healthier overall. Once you get in the habit of brushing and flossing twice a day, you’ll be on the right path for excellent oral health and hygiene!

Complete Dental Care

We are a full service dental clinic offering a comprehensive range of dental services provided by professional staff. From infants to seniors, we can take care of the whole family and their changing dental needs with a wide range of treatments from routine to complex. We are pleased to offer an array of professional services to maintain or restore your oral health.
Flexible Payment Options

Dental care costs vary according to the extent of treatment needed. We support patient choice by providing transparent information about treatment options and costs. Please make an appointment to discuss your individual circumstance and dental needs. We accept most insurance plans and can direct bill your insurance provider for prompt and accurate claims processing.  We have options for our patients who do not have dental insurance coverage, come see us to discuss your dental care needs.
Patient Experience

Just like you do, the staff at Bonnie Doon Dental Associates have busy lives. We understand the challenge of fitting dental visits in around work and family and other activities.  Our dental team prides itself on providing friendly and prompt service to all our patients, in the office and over the phone.  Keeping appointments on track each day and helping our patients meet their dental care goals is important to us.  When you arrive for your appointment, you will be greeted with a smile and we’ll work hard to have you in and out of your appointment as promptly as we can.
Meeting Your Needs

In order to offer you top quality care and dental services, we take the time to learn your dental care history and goals before building a personalized treatment plan with you.  Each visit begins with a short conversation with you to determine any changes since your last checkup and the reason for this visit.  From there, together we can determine the best dental care options to meet your specific needs.  Our staff is dedicated to providing our patients with education and options to work toward and achieve expected outcomes for lifelong dental health.
