Commonly Asked Questions

Q Do you take my insurance?

A

Your dental insurance is most likely accepted at our office. We work with many different insurance companies so you can get the dental care you need. We also offer flexible payment plans to help make treatment affordable. Plus, our Skyview Membership Club offers a great way to get care without insurance. You can get coverage on preventive services and a 20 percent discount. Whether it's using your insurance or one of our payment options, we'll make sure you can afford to get the care you need.

Q Are you accepting new patients? 

A

Yes, we are accepting new patients across all age groups. From toddlers to teenagers, adults to seniors - we offer personalized dental care solutions tailored to meet your unique needs and stage of life. Whether you're due for a routine checkup or require more extensive treatment, our warm and knowledgeable dental team has you covered. Be sure you take advantage of our new patient offers!

Q What are some signs I should see a dentist right away?

A

If you experience a toothache, sudden inflammation, continuous bleeding, or a dislodged tooth, seek dental help as soon as possible. These symptoms can indicate urgent dental issues requiring immediate attention to relieve discomfort and avoid additional problems. It is crucial to get immediate care to protect your oral health and prevent complications.
