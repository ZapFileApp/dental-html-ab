Meet The Doctors

At Parkstone Dental, we believe you are in control of your oral health and should have a say in your dental care. Our friendly and caring team is by your side to answer all your questions, address your concerns, and help you preserve your beautiful smile through patient education and regular checkups. As your dentist in Tofield, we want you and your family to feel great about every visit you have with us! Check out our new facility – we’re confident you’ll be impressed! If you live in Sherwood Park, we have a second location to serve you.
