Dr. Douglas Campbell

Dr. Campbell grew up on a farm in rural Saskatchewan and obtained his dental degree in Saskatoon in 1975. He started his career by working in northern Saskatchewan providing pediatric dental services for the first 3 years. Dr Campbell then went to complete his specialty in Pediatric dentistry at the University of Oregon in 1979, he has been practicing in Red Deer since 1980.

In his spare time, Dr. Campbell loves spending time with his wife of 47 years, Fran, their three sons and four grandsons. His interests are with horses, cows and Harleys.

Dr. Samneet K. Mangat

Dr. Samneet K. Mangat, a.k.a Dr. Sam, is the newest member of the Cool Smiles team! She is a board certified Pediatric Dentist and is a fellow of the Royal College of Dentists of Canada.

Dr.Sam was born and raised in Calgary, AB and is excited to move back after being away for 6 years! She attended the University of Calgary where she graduated with her Bachelor’s of Science (with distinction). Following that, Dr. Sam moved to Detroit where she completed her dental school. It was then Dr. Sam found her passion in working with kids, which led to her specializing in Pediatric Dentistry. After completing a two year residency at the Children’s Hospital Colorado, Dr. Sam decided it was time to move back home, and she couldn’t be more excited!

In her spare time, Dr. Sam loves to try different cuisines, travel with her husband, and of course spend time with her nieces and nephews!
