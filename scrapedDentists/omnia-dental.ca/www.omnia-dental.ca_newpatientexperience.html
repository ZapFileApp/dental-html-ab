    NEW PATIENT EXPERIENCE

    At Omnia Dental, our commitment to exceptional patient care begins the moment you step into our practice. Our warm, welcoming ambiance is designed to put you at ease. We prioritize understanding your unique needs through advanced dental health scans, thorough cleanings, comprehensive exams, and personalized treatment plans—fostering a strong partnership between patient and practitioner.

    WHAT TO EXPECT

    • Comprehensive Dental Examination
      Our exams include digital X-rays, a 3D wellness scan of your head and neck, and detailed intra-oral photos and scans, providing a full understanding of your dental health.
       
    • Customized Treatment Plan
      Based on our clinical and radiographic findings, we create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs.

    BEFORE YOUR FIRST APPOINTMENT

    • New Patient Form
      Please arrive 10 minutes early to complete our new patient form.
       
    • Automated Text Reminders
      You’ll receive a reminder text before your appointment. Please reply to confirm your attendance.
       
    • Dental Insurance Information
      We accept all major insurance providers, including Canada Care Plan. As an added convenience, we offer complimentary pre-authorization of required treatments upon request, once your treatment plan has been established.
