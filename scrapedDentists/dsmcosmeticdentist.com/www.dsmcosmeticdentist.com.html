Cosmetic Dentistry in Des Moines, Iowa

World Class Smiles by a Cosmetic Dentist in Des Moines, Iowa!

 

Stephen Forrest, DDS at Des Moines Cosmetic Dentistry Center provides the highest quality cosmetic, family, Invisalign, and implant dentistry in the greater Des Moines area. Dr. Forrest is a general dentist who specializes in all aspects of cosmetic dentistry. He believes your smile is one of the best assets you have to look your best and feel confident with your appearance and overall health. His dental practice hosts state-of-the-art dental diagnostic and treatment equipment and materials in a comfortable and professional office. Dr. Forrest is dedicated to ensuring your dentistry is the best for your individual needs.

Our Cosmetic Dental Services

Des Moines Cosmetic Dentistry Center offers a complete range of cosmetic and restorative treatments designed to fit your goals and lifestyle.

Learn More

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with permanent, natural-looking dental implants designed to restore both function and confidence. Dr. Forrest provides single-tooth, multiple-tooth, and full-arch implant solutions for patients across the Des Moines metro.

Learn more about dental implants

Porcelain Veneers

Transform chipped, stained, or uneven teeth with custom porcelain veneers crafted to match your smile naturally. Whether you’re preparing for a special event or finally addressing a long-standing concern, veneers deliver lasting results.

Learn more about porcelain veneers

Smile Makeover

Combine multiple cosmetic treatments into one comprehensive plan tailored to your goals, timeline, and budget. Smile makeovers often blend veneers, whitening, implants, and Invisalign to create a complete smile transformation

Learn more about smile makeovers

Invisalign

Straighten your teeth without traditional metal braces using Invisalign clear aligners. A discreet, comfortable option for adults who want a confident smile without the look or hassle of conventional orthodontics.

Learn more about Invisalign

Teeth Whitening

Brighten your smile safely with professional teeth whitening that delivers dramatic results far beyond what over-the-counter options can achieve. Both in-office and take-home whitening options are available based on your goals and timeline.

Learn More

Meet Dr. Stephen Forrest

Stephen Forrest, D.D.S. has been practicing cosmetic and restorative dentistry in the Des Moines metro for more than 30 years. He earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery from the University of Iowa College of Dentistry in 1985 and holds a Fellowship with the Academy of General Dentistry. Dr. Forrest is also a member of the Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, the American Dental Association, and the Iowa Dental Association.

Patients describe Dr. Forrest as a careful listener who takes time to understand their goals before recommending treatment. His approach combines decades of clinical experience with modern cosmetic dental techniques to deliver results that look natural and last.
