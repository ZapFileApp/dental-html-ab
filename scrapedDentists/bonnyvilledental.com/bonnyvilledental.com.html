Bonnyville Dental Clinic

Our dentists in Bonnyville are dedicated to providing excellence and comprehensive dental care in the most comfortable and relaxing atmosphere possible. Our goal is to ensure all of our patients feel comfortable during their visit and receive all of the support, services, and resources needed to achieve a beautiful and healthy smile. Dr. David Skuba

Dr. David Skuba is one of the primary dentists at Bonnyville Dental Clinic. Dr. Skuba joined the practice in 1982 after graduating from University of Alberta and has been practicing here for over 39 years. He is a member of the Alberta Dental Association and College and the Alberta College of Paramedics. The part of dentistry he most enjoys is all forms of surgery, general dentistry, periodontics, biopsies and sedation dentistry.
