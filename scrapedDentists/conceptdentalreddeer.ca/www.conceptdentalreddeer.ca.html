The Concept Dental Difference

We are a general dental practice, devoted to family dental care, implant dentistry and sedation dentistry. We want to help maintain, restore and enhance the natural beauty of your smile using conservative procedures that will result in a beautiful, long-lasting smile.
Education & Prevention

As a practice, we are true believers that preventative care and education are the keys to optimal dental health. We strive to provide “dental healthcare” VS “disease care”. That’s why we focus on thorough exams – checking the overall health of your teeth and gums, performing oral cancer exams, and taking x-rays, when necessary. We know that routine cleanings, flossing, sealants, and fluoride are all helpful in preventing dental disease. We’re focused on the beauty of your smile and also concerned about your health. A review of your medical history can help us stay informed of your overall health, any new medications, or illnesses that may impact your dental health.

Uncompromising Safety

Infection control is crucial in our office. To protect our patients and ourselves, we maintain strict sterilization and cross-contamination protocols using standards recommended by the Alberta Dental Association & College (ADA & C) and the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA).

Training & Expertise

As your dental health professionals, we want you to be confident knowing that we are a team of highly trained and skilled clinicians. We pride ourselves in providing the care you need to keep your smile healthy. To give you the best possible service and results, we are committed to continual education and learning. We attend dental lectures, meetings, and dental conventions to stay informed of new techniques, the latest products, and newest equipment available to the modern dental office. Dr Appalraju strongly believes in continuing dental education. Each year, he exceeds the requirements for continuing education in order to provide you with the best possible dental care. Also, membership in various professional dental associations helps us to stay abreast of changes and recommendations for our profession.

A positive Experience

Treating our patients as unique individuals and building a foundation of trust is vital to our success. We understand how anxious some patients may feel about their dental visits, and how we can make a positive difference by delivering a relaxing and stress-free experience. Our entire team is dedicated to providing you with excellent, personalized care and service to ensure your visits as comfortable and pleasant as possible.We thank you for allowing us to take care of your dental requirements and look forward to serving you. Make an appointment today and we will give you a reason to smile!

High Standards

A consistently high standard of excellence in personalized dental care enables us to provide the quality dental services patients deserve. We offer comprehensive treatment planning and use restorative and cosmetic dentistry to achieve your optimal dental health. Should a dental emergency occur, we’ll make every effort to see and care for you as soon as possible.

Transparent Pricing

At Concept Dental, we believe getting the dental care you need shouldn’t create financial stress. From your very first visit, we take the time to clearly explain your treatment options, priorities, and associated costs so you can make informed decisions with confidence.Our team will help you maximize your dental benefits and guide you through available payment options, including no-interest financing, to make treatment more manageable and accessible.
