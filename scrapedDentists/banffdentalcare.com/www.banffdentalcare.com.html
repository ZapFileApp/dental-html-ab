Dental Crowns and Bridges

Dental crowns are an excellent option to restore teeth that have been severely damaged. The crown will encase your whole tooth to restore its appearance and functionality. Dental crowns are no longer obvious and silver. Instead, they are made from ceramic, which gives a very natural finish. Our team at Banff Dental Care is happy to offer quality dental crowns near you.
A series of three or more crowns will make a dental bridge. One crown is used to replace your missing tooth. The others will anchor onto your surrounding teeth to provide support. Dental bridges are an excellent dental procedure to restore the functionality of your teeth and to improve the appearance of your smile.

Dental Implants

Are you suffering from missing teeth? The gaps in your teeth can cause you to be conscious of your smile. In addition to embarrassment, it will also cause negative effects on your oral health. Your jaw bone will slowly deteriorate without stimulation from your teeth, and your teeth will gradually shift to fill in the empty space. We provide dental implants in Banff to fill in the missing gaps.

Root Canal Therapy

It’s essential to practice proper oral hygiene. Without thorough cleaning, plaque and tartar begin to build on your teeth. This causes tooth decay, which can lead to cavities if left untreated. The decay will eventually spread towards the nerves and roots of your tooth. At this point, the nerves and roots in your tooth get infected and will begin to die. A root canal can save your tooth from dying and prevent an extraction. We provide root canal therapy in Banff to help preserve your tooth.

Teeth Whitening

Your smile is something you should feel confident about. If your teeth are discolored, consider speaking to your dentist about receiving teeth whitening. It’s a simple procedure that can provide immediate, exceptional results. Are you interested in receiving professional teeth whitening in Banff? Our office provides in-office teeth whitening near you to help you feel confident in your smile.
