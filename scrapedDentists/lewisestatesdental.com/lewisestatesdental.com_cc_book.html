Our Approach

Comprehensive family dentistry in West Edmonton

Your family’s oral health is deeply connected to your overall health, and we treat it that way.

For your initial consultation, we will explore your oral and overall health and the root of your dental care needs. Our expert team will collect comprehensive data, including X-rays and photos, to gain a complete picture of your smile, mouth, and jaws.

Periodically as dictated by your oral health, we will reassess your teeth and tissues for disease. We will complete dental hygiene therapy, help you understand your weaknesses, and assist you in organizing any additional care that is needed.

Your family’s oral treatment plan will manage your basic, foundational treatment needs, including periodontal therapy and basic restorative care, but can also address other concerns from a functional or aesthetic perspective. This may include other treatments such as implants, crowns or dental veneers, bridges, and orthodontics.

If recommended as part of your quality care, this period may also include visits with dental specialists and/or medical providers to keep your dental treatment plan on track.
