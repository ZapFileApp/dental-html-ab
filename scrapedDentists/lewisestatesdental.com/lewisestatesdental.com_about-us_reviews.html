Once they both finished dental school, they opened up a brand new dental practice together on the west end of the city. Ironically, I was in need of a dentist at same time they were looking for new patients! I asked Dr. Bains if she would take me on as a new patient because they had been so loyal to me in the past. In hindsight, that was the best decision I had ever made in my lifetime in regards to my oral health! Not only is she professional, she is kind, empathetic and compassionate!

Dr. Bains is a true magician and a master of her dental craft. She will do everything possible to accommodate her clientele and creates beautiful smiles every day. To her, every patient is treated as if they were family. In fact, most of the dental rooms have pictures of her real family over the years to make you feel at ease as she begins any procedure!

I've spent hundreds of hours under her care and she has done an incredible job every single time! Not only is she a professional at what she does, she also surrounds herself and hires only the best of the best staff!

I have had the opportunity to meet and be under the care of most of them, but I want to make a particular shout out to Ashley and Adrianna! Ashley is both the senior administrator & registered dental assistant of the clinic and is one of the kindest souls you will ever meet. She truly really cares about all the patient's needs and will always accommodate my personal needs every single time without hesitation! A true champion!

Adrianna, on the other hand, is one of the registered dental assistants that works hand in hand with Dr. Bains and is a complete dental rock star! Unfortunately, I now suffer from both balance and mobility issues from early onset Parkinson's disease. In numerous accounts, she has gone out of her way to ensure my safe passage directly from my dental chair to my parked vehicle outside. She even caught me a couple times in the process and stabilized me again preventing potential nasty falls. She must be a superhero on the side! Honestly, Dr. Bains trains and communicates with all her staff so well that it almost doesn't matter who is your registered dental assistant. Everyone steps up to the plate to ensure the patients are treated first class every single time!

On top of everything else, Dr. Bains always has the latest cutting edge technology in terms of dental equipment to ensure the best possible care and results for her patients. She is also always learning new skills by extensive training and branches out to other services that you may need one day!

In summary, I am so appreciative of every dental service that Dr. Bains has provided me over the last 30 years. Dr. Bains, thank you so much for your care and kindness from you & all your staff! Highest recommendation possible!

Hear Directly From Our Patients

Going to Lewis Estates Dental Centre is like a breath of fresh air. It’s so relaxing, and everyone is genuinely friendly. They take the time to explain everything before they start working, which really puts me at ease. Plus, they’re amazing with kids! I can bring my little ones, and they’re comfortable hanging out. It’s such a relief to find a place where I feel like a person, not just a number.

What really sets Dr. Bains apart is how she looks at the whole picture. It’s not just about your teeth; she cares about your overall health. For example, she noticed my son’s dark circles and breathing issues and realized it could be asthma, referring us to an ear, nose, and throat (ENT) specialist. And she referred my husband to an ENT specialist for sleep apnea! She uses the latest technology, like laser dentistry, and during COVID, the clinic was incredibly clean with air purifiers, showing how much she prioritizes patient safety. She even took photos of my teeth for future reference and used that purple dye to show me where I needed to brush better, which was super helpful.

—Mandy

Lewis Estates Dental Centre is just different. You walk in, and it’s clear they’re using the latest tech, like the air purification system. Dr. Bains doesn’t just clean your teeth; she checks for everything, even oral cancer, and keeps detailed records. Plus, she’s big on prevention. For example, she recommended a mouthguard for my teeth grinding and showed me how to use a water pick and Listerine for my implants.

They really care about your comfort. I struggle a bit with dental pain, and they’re always patient, giving the numbing agent extra time to work. And when my hockey injuries required specialists, she connected me with the fantastic oral surgeon Dr. Long for bone grafts and implants, handling all the arrangements. She really personalizes your care.

Dr. Bains is incredibly professional and knowledgeable. She explains everything clearly, and you can tell she’s up to date on the latest practices. She recommended top professionals and really cares about your overall health, not just your teeth. The atmosphere is welcoming and friendly. If you need a dentist who’s both skilled and compassionate, especially if you have complex needs, I’d highly recommend her.

—Bob
