Financial Information

The cost of your treatment will vary depending on your individual needs and treatment plan. We will discuss with you the cost of all possible treatment options so that you can make the best choice for you.

All fees are due in full at the time of service regardless of your insurance coverage. We accept cash, debit, MasterCard, Visa and American Express. We will file your primary dental claim electronically and request that your insurance provider reimburses the payment directly to you. 

Our office staff is always available to help you with insurance claims and paperwork.  Every insurance carrier and policy varies, therefore we will try our best to answer your questions, but it is your responsibility to understand your insurance coverage. 

Please let us know if you have any questions about our financial policies prior to your treatment.
