Dental Bridges or Crowns in Castor and Stettler, AB

Dental Bridges or Crowns Near You

Dental bridges and crowns are fixed dental restorations that are adhered to remaining teeth or dental implants. A dental crown is commonly used to cover or “cap” a damaged tooth or to cover a dental implant. Dental crowns are recommended by your dentist when:

  • You have a large filling and not enough tooth structure remaining.
  • When want to protect a weak tooth from becoming fractured.
  • You want to restore a fractured tooth.
  • Wants a dental bridge attach by you.
  • When you want to cover a dental implant.
  • You want to cover a tooth that is discolored or poorly shaped.
  • You want to cover a tooth that has undergone root canal therapy.

When you have gaps in your smile, it causes your existing teeth to shift out of place and result in a bad bite. Additionally, this can lead to periodontal (gum disease) and TMJ. A bridge is a common dental restoration used when a patient has one or more absent teeth. It covers the space where your teeth are missing and is cemented to natural teeth or dental implants that surround the space.

Dental bridges or crowns in Castor and Stettler can improve a tooth’s appearance, shape, alignment, and your dental occlusion (bite).

How Do I Know If I Need a Crown or Bridge?

Only your dentist can determine if you require dental crowns or dental bridges near you. If you experience any of the following symptoms, consult with a dentist as soon as possible:

  • Teeth that are visibly damaged.
  • Tooth pain that is consistent or sporadic.
  • Sensitivity to temperature.
  • Pain when you brush your teeth.
  • Pain when you chew.
  • Swollen gums, lips or jaw.
  • Bleeding teeth or gums.
  • Unexplained tooth pain.

The Procedures for Dental Bridges and Crowns

The steps involved in the procedures for dental bridges or crowns in Castor and Stettler are similar.

The procedure for dental crowns will involve your dentist:

  • Preparing your tooth.
  • Taking impressions of your tooth.
  • Affixing a temporary crown (until the permanent crown is ready).
  • Cementing the permanent crown.

The procedure for dental bridges near you will involve your dentist:

  • Preparing your abutment teeth.
  • Taking impressions of your teeth.
  • Affixing a temporary dental bridge (until the permanent bridge is ready).
  • Cementing the permanent bridge.
