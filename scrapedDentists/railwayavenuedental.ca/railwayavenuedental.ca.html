DENTAL TREATMENT DESIGNED FOR YOU

What if you could look forward to your next dental visit?

That’s not the case for most people; they either dread going to the dentist, or at the very least, would like to be somewhere else.

Dr. Marilyn Given DDS at Railway Avenue Dental had a different experience growing up. She loved going to the dentist. The dentist she had as a child was an old family friend—gentle, kind and caring. In fact, she enjoyed the times she spent at his dental office so much, she thought it would be a great career. Later, when Dr. Given made the decision to follow her passion and become a dentist, she also decided she wanted to give the same experience to her patients—a caring and kind atmosphere where your questions and concerns are answered. At Railway Avenue Dental, conveniently located in Airdrie, Alberta we offer a full range of dental services for the entire family.

GENERAL DENTISTRY IN AIRDRIE

Taking proper care of your teeth and gums is a lifelong commitment. As well as regular visits to your dentist, it includes a good oral hygiene routine that you follow at home. Your oral health can impact the rest of your health. By practicing good oral hygiene, you are contributing to your overall good health. At Railway Avenue Dental, all treatment plans will be thoroughly explained so our patients have a complete understanding before work begins.

HYGIENE IN AIRDRIE

Good oral hygiene is important, not only for your appearance, but also for your general health. Poor oral hygiene can lead to a number of dental and medical problems including gum disease, infection, bone loss and more.  There has also been shown to be a link between poor oral health and cardiovascular problems. Regular dental visits and hygiene appointments to our Airdrie Dental Studio can help to  prevent these problems.

TEETH WHITENING IN AIRDRIE

For most people the natural colour of teeth falls within a certain range, and teeth naturally darken with age. The color of teeth can also be affected by the build up of surface stains from the consumption of certain foods or drinks such as coffee, tea and red wine.  For individuals who would like to whiten their smiles, Railway Avenue Dental located in Airdrie, offers both in office whitening procedures as well as take home whitening treatments

DENTAL IMPLANTS IN AIRDRIE

Modern dental implants are small titanium screws that replace and function like natural tooth roots. Dental implants are gently placed during a surgical procedure. Once the dental implant has fully healed, your dentist can then screw on an implant crown.

FAMILY ORTHODONTICS AIRDRIE

Whatever your age, if you have teeth that are crooked, crowded, or stick out, you can benefit from orthodontic treatment. Teeth that haven’t grown the way nature intended them to can affect not only your smile, but also your health. If you are not happy with the way your teeth look or work, orthodontic treatment may help.
