At Broadway Family Dental our goal is to treat each patient like an individual and like family. We take the utmost care in making sure your visits are as comfortable as possible. We know that is difficult for some to come to the dentist and that is why we take great pride in treating each patient with care and respect in a non-judgemental way. One of the most rewarding things we do is help someone who has been apprehensive of the dentist regain their smile and their confidence again!

We’ve designed our office and practice philosophy to ensure that all of our patients receive the personalized care they deserve.

We Don’t think you should have to shuttle your family all over to receive treatment. Our comprehensive services include:

  • Routine Cleanings & Exams
  • Preventive Dental Care
  • Porcelain Crowns and Bridges
  • Tooth-Colored Fillings
  • Professional Teeth Whitening
  • Braces
  • Custom Mouthguards
  • Protective Sealants
  • Root Canal Therapy
  • Periodontal Therapy
  • 24 Hour Emergency Care

WELCOME TO OUR DENTAL FAMILY!

Patients of all ages welcome to Broadway Family Dental. Our friendly staff is concerned with total wellness and holistic care. We offer comprehensive dental care. We are insurance friendly and also offering financing options.

AFFORDABLE NEIGHBOURHOOD DENTISTRY

Our office is family-oriented. We have toys, books, and prizes for the kids. We also have free wi-fi and light refreshments. Our treatments rooms have TVs and blankets.

STRESS-FREE DENTISTRY

We love what we do, but we know that most people don’t look forward to dental visits. We’ve equipped our private treatment areas with TV’s and pillows to make you fall more comfortable, but when modern amenities aren’t enough to calm your nerves, ask us about sedation dentistry! We can perform almost any treatment with the help of sedation dentistry, from deep cleanings to oral surgery, and your visit will be over before you know it.

Dr. Harker works hard to understand each of his patients needs and desires so he can treat them with the most relevant and rewarding care. He is committed to staying at the forefront of trends and developments in the dental field.
