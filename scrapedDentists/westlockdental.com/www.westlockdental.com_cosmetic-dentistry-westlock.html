Full Smile Makeovers

Achieve the Smile of Your Dreams

At Westlock Dental Centre we want you to have the smile of your dreams.  We know that confidence starts with a smile you love.  A comprehensive restorative plan is an amazing way to achieve the smile you have always wanted.

Why Choose a Full Smile Makeover?

Our Full Smile Makeovers combine various dental procedures to address multiple concerns, providing you with a cohesive and stunning result. We use advanced techniques and the latest technology to ensure optimal outcomes and a comfortable experience.

Our Process

Your Journey to a Beautiful Smile

1 | Initial Consultation

Your journey begins with a 40-minute consultation. During this appointment, we get to know you, discuss your goals, and explore the possibilities for your new smile.

2 | Treatment Plan Creation

Based on your consultation, we create a customized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and desires.

3 | Digital Smile Design

To ensure that you are completely satisfied with your treatment, we utilize cutting-edge technology, like Digital Smile Design. This innovative method allows you to visualize your new smile in advance, giving you the confidence that your treatment will meet your wishes and expectations.
