About

Welcome to Westlock Dental Centre, where we have been nurturing smiles for over three generations of patients!

At Westlock Dental Centre, we believe that dentistry is more than just a profession - it's a passion for helping our patients achieve their best smiles and optimal oral health. With a commitment to excellence and a dedication to personalized care, we're proud to offer modern dentistry with a personal touch.

Founded on a legacy of providing exceptional dental care to our community, Westlock Dental Centre has earned a reputation for excellence over the years. Our team of experienced professionals combines clinical expertise with a genuine compassion for our patients' well-being, ensuring that every visit to our clinic is a positive and rewarding experience.

Our Services

Personalized Care, Tailored to You

  • Dental cleanings are essential for your overall health. Our experienced team of dental hygienists will work with you to keep your oral health in tip top shape, helping you keep your smile bright and healthy!

  • Restoring your teeth with these procedures can be incredible options to help get the smile you want. Speak to our dentists and we can custom tailor a plan specific to your needs. We can even make some of these restorations in office – meaning the procedure can be completed in one visit!

  • Aligning your teeth can improve so much about your smile! It’s more than just cosmetic benefits – straight teeth are easier to clean, are less at risk of gum disease, are more protected from chipping/wearing, and in some cases can even help prevent things like headaches! At our office, we offer complementary consultations for Invisalign clear aligner therapy – and you’re never too old to start!

  • Missing teeth? We have great fixed solutions for you such as implants and dental bridges.  We want you to feel good about your smile and we will work with you to determine which solution is best for your specific situation!

  • We love to make sure our patients are comfortable during these procedures and work towards restoring your teeth so you can smile with confidence.

  • Our dentists are proficient in safely and comfortably removing teeth when necessary. In many instances, patients can even have their wisdom teeth removed in-office without the need for deep sedation.

  • We offer oral and nitrous (laughing gas) sedation options here. If you want or need dental work done but you find yourself putting it off due to anxiety, ask us about what sedation options could be beneficial to you!
