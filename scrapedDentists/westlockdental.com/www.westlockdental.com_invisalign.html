Invisalign

Love Your Smile with Invisalign

At Westlock Dental Centre, we’re proud to offer Invisalign, the clear and comfortable way to straighten your teeth. Invisalign aligners are a modern alternative to traditional braces, providing an effective solution for a variety of dental issues while being virtually invisible.

Invisalign offers various advantages* for your oral health. With their removable trays, maintaining healthier gums becomes more convenient through improved hygiene during brushing and flossing. Moreover, this treatment minimizes wear and trauma on your teeth, reducing the likelihood of chipping, breaking, and lessening stress on your jawbone and joints.

*Source: American Association of Orthodontics

Less noticeable more comfortable.

Benefits of Invisalign

  • Nearly Invisible: Invisalign aligners are clear and discreet.

  • Removable: Easily take out your aligners for eating, brushing, and flossing.

  • Comfortable: Smooth plastic aligners are more comfortable than metal braces.

  • Convenient: Suitable for both teens and adults, with minimal lifestyle disruption.

It’s as easy as 1, 2, 3!

  • Step 1: Book

    Book online for your Invisalign consult appointment. This is where we take a few photos and a digital intraoral scan of your teeth. If necessary, we can also take any required x-rays at this visit as well. Our dental team will review the results and discuss your smile and your bite. If Invisalign is the right treatment for you, we will discuss overall treatment, length of treatment, and associated costs.

  • Step 2: Treat

    If Invisalign is the right treatment option for you, we will build you a custom treatment plan and provide you with clear trays to help you on your way to a healthier bite and beautiful smile. You will need to wear your trays all the time every day (and at night!) except when brushing or eating. You will be guided to switch your aligners and schedule regular appointments with our dental team to track and manage your alignment and health of your teeth gums.

  • Step 3: Celebrate

    Celebrate your new smile! Once your treatment has been completed, we will finalize our success with lots of beautiful photos and graduate you to retainers to make sure your smile lasts!

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Invisalign has wonderful benefits to help with your occlusion (bite) and improve the look of your smile.  In our consultation process we can establish if you are candidate by taking a quick no charge scan of your teeth. That, along with information from your x-rays, will help us to determine if Invisalign is a safe option for you.

  • Costs can vary depending on treatment level and length of treatment, but the typical cost for Invisalign treatment at our office is $6,400. We offer interest-free monthly payment plans to help ease the burden, and we will help you to find out if your dental benefits plan includes orthodontic coverage!

  • Just like every smile is different, every treatment plan is different. In our free consultation process we will be able to define a length of time for your treatment to be completed. Average treatment times typically range from 6 to 24 months.

  • Rinse your aligners with water every night and throughout the day before putting them back in. Brush your aligners daily with a soft-bristled toothbrush to remove any debris or food particles that have accumulated during the day. Rinse thoroughly after brushing. You can even soak your aligners in a cleaning solution or ultrasonic cleaner (both available on Amazon), but only for as long as it takes for you to finish a meal, then it’s time to put your trays right back in!
