Preventative Treatment

Understanding Dental Hygiene Therapy: Key to Your Overall Health

Dental hygiene therapy, often known as dental cleaning, is a vital component of your comprehensive health and preventive care plan. Regular dental cleanings are essential for monitoring and controlling the effects of oral diseases on your general well-being. Oral diseases typically manifest as gum inflammation and bleeding, though they seldom cause pain. In its early stages, this condition is called gingivitis, but if left untreated, it can develop into periodontal disease.

Periodontal disease is particularly concerning because it increases the risk of systemic conditions such as heart disease. This underscores the importance of early and effective management of gum health. The frequency of your dental hygiene therapy sessions will depend on your oral health status. Those with more advanced periodontal disease may need more frequent cleanings to maintain optimal oral and overall health. Your hygienist will tailor your routine to fit your individual needs.

Dental Exams & Radiographs

The Importance of Regular Dental Examinations

Regular dental examinations, or checkups, are essential for identifying and diagnosing dental issues early. These examinations, often combined with x-rays, photographs, and other oral tests, help detect conditions such as cavities, gum disease, and oral cancer. Your hygienist will also use your x-rays to diagnose and treat your periodontal condition.

At Westlock Dental Centre, we strive to create a safe and comfortable environment for our patients. Our goal is for you to feel welcomed and confident, knowing you are in the experienced and caring hands of our dedicated dental team.

Night Guards & Sports Guards

Protecting your teeth from damage is crucial, whether you are sleeping or active in sports. Night guards are designed to prevent teeth grinding and clenching during sleep, which can lead to dental issues such as worn enamel and jaw pain. Sports guards, on the other hand, provide essential protection during physical activities, reducing the risk of dental injuries. At Westlock Dental Centre, we custom-fit night guards and sports guards to ensure maximum comfort and effectiveness.

Digital Scanning & Diagnostics

Advances in dental technology have revolutionized the way we diagnose and treat oral health issues. Digital scanning and diagnostics enable us to obtain highly detailed images of your teeth and gums, facilitating accurate and early detection of problems such as cavities, gum disease, and other dental concerns. This technology ensures that your treatment plan is based on precise and comprehensive information, enhancing the quality of care you receive at Westlock Dental Centre.

Orthodontics

Orthodontic treatment is not just about improving the appearance of your smile; it's also about enhancing your overall oral health. By correcting misaligned teeth and bite issues, orthodontics can prevent problems such as tooth decay, gum disease, and jaw pain. At Westlock Dental Centre, we offer clear aligner therapy tailored to meet your individual needs, helping you achieve a healthier, more confident smile.
