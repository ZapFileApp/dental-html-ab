What is the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)?

The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) is a government initiative designed to reduce the financial burden of dental care for eligible Canadians. With its comprehensive coverage, the CDCP is designed to support residents in keeping their smiles healthy, free from the worry of steep costs. This program is particularly beneficial for:

  • Seniors: Ensuring that older adults can receive the dental care they need as they age.
  • Children: Supporting the oral health of the younger generation to set the foundation for lifelong dental wellness.
  • Adults with Disabilities: Unlocking the path to vital dental services that enhance your health and improve your quality of life.
