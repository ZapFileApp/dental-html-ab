frequently asked questions

Do you offer direct billing to insurance?

Yes, our dental clinic near you provides direct insurance billing so you can focus on your dental care while we handle the paperwork and make the process simple and stress-free.

Do you accept the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)?

Yes, at Marda Loop Dental Choice, we’re always happy to welcome new patients to our dental clinic in Marda Loop, Calgary. Whether you need a routine check-up, a specific treatment, or a new dental home for you and your family, our friendly team is here to help. To book an appointment, simply give us a call or schedule online. We look forward to caring for your smile!

Do you have a cancellation policy?

Yes, we reserve dedicated time with our dentists and hygienists to focus entirely on your care. If you’re unable to make your appointment, we kindly ask that you give us at least two business days’ notice. Just contact our office, and our team will be happy to assist you with rescheduling.

Do you follow the provincial dental fee guide?

Yes, our dentist in Marda Loop adheres to the Alberta Dental Fee Guide, which is updated annually on January 1st by the Alberta Dental Association and College. This guide is commonly used by dental offices to set service fees and is also a key reference for many insurance providers when calculating coverage.

What should I know before my first appointment?

For your first visit, we recommend arriving 5–10 minutes early to allow time for check-in and any required paperwork. Please bring your health card, insurance details, and any recent dental records or x-rays you may have. This information will help our dentist near you to get a clear picture of your dental history so we can deliver the highest quality care.
