Your Family’s Smiles Are in Good Hands

Quality

A healthy smile is a beautiful smile! You deserve the best, and that’s what we deliver. We believe in doing things right, the first time. There are no guarantees in dentistry, but we’ll provide clear direction and advise if we foresee any potential issues with your chosen treatment plan.

Value

Your dental health needs to fit your goals, priorities AND budget. That’s why we’ll explain your treatment options and can provide pricing upfront to ensure there are no surprises. Not all dental clinics follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide – we do!

Convenience

You have a busy life and a busy family schedule. The last thing you need is yet another thing to stress about. Your visits will be quick and efficient so you can get back to your other responsibilities.

Technology

You’ve probably had some bad experiences in the past. And that’s where they belong – in the past! Digital and 3D technology means today’s dentistry is faster, easier, and more comfortable than ever.

Fun

Whether you’re 15 years old or 70, your dental visits should be comfortable, enjoyable, and FUN! We make sure you LOVE the journey, not just the destination as we work together on your smile.

Our hearts break when we meet people who are suffering from dental problems because they’ve had bad experiences in the past.

We’re here to break the cycle!

1

Your Choice Guarantee

You choose how you want your smile to look and feel, and then you can count on us to help get you from HERE to THERE as easily as possible.

2

One Dentist Guarantee

Frustrated by getting a different dentist every time with your current clinic? At Wapiti Dental you will only deal with one dentist, and we’ve got your back!

3

No Shame Guarantee

You will never feel judged, guilty, or shamed about your situation or your goals. You WILL feel supported as you get back on track with your oral health.

Warmly Welcoming New Patients

Are you suffering from dental or teeth problems?  Do you have dental anxiety?  It’s time to visit us at Wapiti Dental.  We’ve invested in the latest technology, including a Solea Laser that allows us to skip drills and needles for most procedures.
