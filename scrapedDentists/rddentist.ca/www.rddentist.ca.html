Our Services

At Red Deer Dental Clinic, we offer comprehensive dental care.

See All

Endodontics
We provide root canal treatments, the removal of nerve tissue from inside the tooth.

Preventative Care
Regular maintenance of teeth is cost effective, as early detection of problems helps save you money and your teeth.

Restorative Dentistry
This area of dentistry involves repairing or replacing lost or damaged tooth structure, or replacement of teeth.

Sedation
Many people have anxiety about getting dental treatment.

Surgical Procedures
Skilled teeth extractions by experts, ensuring your oral health and comfort.

Teeth Clenching & Grinding
This area of dentistry is complex, and causes and symptoms vary widely between patients.

At Red Deer Dental Clinic,

We understand that a beautiful, healthy-looking smile can only come from regular oral care which is why we offer a range of dental services from dental cleanings, and preventative dentistry to tooth replacement.

At Red Deer Dental Clinic, we are proud to serve patients in Red Deer and its surrounding area. As your dental team in Red Deer, you and your loved ones can expect a wide array of services and procedures. Our family-oriented office is modern, comfortable, and staffed with warm and friendly dentists and professionals who listen to their patients’ questions and concerns. Patients of all ages are welcome.

At Red Deer Dental Clinic, we give our patients plenty of reasons to smile. Let us be your Red Deer dental office of choice.
