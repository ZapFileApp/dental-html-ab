Your Downtown Calgary Choice for Dental Implants!

If you are looking for a safe, reliable and long-lasting solution for one or more missing teeth, dental implants might be the right solution for you! Dental at the Bow is pleased to offer dental implants as an effective and durable foundation for replacement teeth. Our dedicated team works together with only the best specialists in Calgary utilizing the latest technology and techniques, ensuring precise implant placement and the best possible results. Once the titanium post is placed and healed, we then design and craft the tooth portion called the ‘crown’ to finalize the implant procedure. If you’re curious about dental implants start with a consult with Dental at the Bow!

What is a Dental Implant?

A dental implant is a small titanium post that is surgically inserted to in the place of a missing root. Once the implant is placed the surrounding bone heals around it creating a string and stable foundation on which to place the tooth (crown), teeth, or implant supported denture. Dental implants closely replicate the look, feel and functionality of natural teeth.

What are the benefits of Dental Implants?

Dental implants offer a range of benefits;

  • Enhanced Appearance – Implants are designed to match the colour, shape and size of your natural teeth, ensuring a seamless and beautiful smile.
  • Restores Function – Implants provide a stable and secure base for restorations, improving chewing, speaking, and overall oral function compared to traditional dentures or bridges
  • Prevents Bone Loss – By mimicking the function of natural tooth roots, implants stimulate the jawbone and promote bone growth through a process called osseointegration, where the implant fuses with the bone.
  • Long Term Solution – With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime, making them a reliable long-term solution for tooth replacement.

Dental Implants may be a good choice for someone who wants to avoid the discomfort and constant maintenance that comes with something like removable dentures. Dental implants also do not require altering adjacent teeth, preserving your natural tooth structure and promoting better overall oral health.
