Dental Checkups Are Important!

Maintaining a healthy and bright smile involves more than just regular brushing and flossing at home; scheduling regular dental check-ups throughout the year at our dental office is a crucial part of this process. As your trusted dentist in Sherwood Park, we advocate for the importance of these regular check-ups.

Regular checkups are a fundamental part of preventative care. It helps us identify potential dental problems before they escalate into more significant issues that might require complex dental treatment. Remember, a healthy smile is the mirror of a healthy body!

So don’t delay, schedule your regular check-up with us today!

Sherwood Park Dental Clinic FAQs

How Much Do Dental Cleanings Cost At The Clinic?

Our costs are always below the Alberta Dental Fee Guide! Please call us today so you can get a quote based on your specific needs!
What teeth whitening services do you offer in Sherwood Park?

We offer a range of teeth whitening options to suit your needs and budget. Whether you prefer an in-office treatment or a take-home kit, we can help restore the brilliance of your smile.
Do you provide dental implants in Sherwood Park?

Yes, we offer dental implants implants as a long-term and natural-looking solution for missing teeth. Our skilled general dentists use CBCT and intraoral scanning technology to help guide the treatment planning and surgical placement of dental implants.
What should I do in case of dental emergencies?

If you have a dental emergency, contact us immediately. Our team is equipped to handle a wide range of dental emergencies, ensuring you receive prompt and appropriate care.
How often should I schedule a dental cleaning?

Regular dental cleanings are crucial for maintaining good oral health. We recommend scheduling a dental cleaning every six months to prevent cavities and gum disease.
Do you offer financing options for dental treatments?
We understand that managing dental costs can be a challenge, which is why we offer a variety of financing options to make our services more affordable and accessible.
How long does it typically take to get an appointment?

We try to schedule appointments as soon as possible after you contact us. We're usually able to get you in within a few days.
Do you have any multilingual staff?

Yes! At this location, we have dentists who speak English, French, Cantonese, or Mandarin.
Are you currently accepting new patients in Sherwood Park?

Absolutely! We're always happy to welcome new patients to our dental clinics. Contact us to schedule your first appointment today.
