Teeth Whitening in North Edmonton

Teeth Whitening Near You

Are you looking to say goodbye to stains and hello to a brighter, more beautiful smile with teeth whitening in Edmonton? Here at Henday Dental, we offer both in-office and take-home options, so you can choose the treatment that’s best for your needs and lifestyle.

Professional teeth whitening is a great way to enhance your smile and boost your confidence. Stop in for a free consultation on teeth whitening in Edmonton and get a tailored treatment plan that best suits you.

WHEN SHOULD YOU RECIEVE TEETH WHITENING?

Are you hesitating to smile confidently in either social or professional settings due to the appearance of your teeth? It may be worthwhile to discuss teeth whitening with a dentist near you. A bright, white smile can help you feel confident and make /a great first impression.

BENEFITS OF PROFESSIONAL TEETH WHITENING

The advantages of getting your teeth whitened extend beyond just having a brighter and whiter smile in the mirror…

  • Removes deep and stubborn stains that regular brushing and over-the-counter products can’t address
  • Provides faster and more dramatic results than at-home whitening kits
  • Uses safe and effective products that are only available through dental professionals
  • Offers customized treatment plans based on individual needs and goals
  • Improves overall appearance and confidence
  • Enhances the longevity of dental restorations (crowns, bridges, etc.) by keeping them the same color as newly whitened teeth
  • Can be done in a single office visit or through take-home kits for convenience
  • Allows for professional supervision to ensure safety and optimal results.

FIND THE TEETH WHITENING TREATMENT THAT IS BEST FOR YOU

In-office Whitening

Our in-office teeth whitening near you is a quick and effective way to remove deep stains that cannot be removed by regular brushing. During your consultation, we will discuss how well the treatments fit your lifestyle and what you can reasonably expect.

During the teeth whitening treatment, a safe solution containing hydrogen peroxide or hydrogen carbamide is applied to the teeth. This solution triggers a bleaching reaction that targets and eliminates stains that have built up within the enamel’s inner tubules.

To enhance the effectiveness and efficiency of the treatment, a specialized laser light is used, resulting in superior whitening results in a shorter period compared to common store-bought whitening products.

Complimentary Take-home Whitening

After your dental cleaning at our North East Edmonton clinic, we will provide you with a complimentary teeth whitening product and custom-fitted trays for at-home use. However, it is important to have good overall oral health before beginning the teeth whitening process.

These free whitening kits contain powerful formulas used by professionals, allowing you to see the positive results and enjoy their benefits firsthand. Schedule a consultation with our team today to see which teeth whitening option fits your needs best!

Frequently Asked Questions: Teeth Whitening

How does professional teeth whitening in North Edmonton compare to over-the-counter kits? Professional teeth whitening at our McConachie dental clinic is faster, safer, and more effective than drugstore options. We use medical-grade whitening gels that are more concentrated than retail products, and our custom-fitted trays ensure even application while protecting your gums from irritation. You can achieve a noticeably brighter smile in just one visit or through our professional take-home kits.

Is professional teeth whitening safe for my enamel? When performed under the supervision of our North Edmonton dentists, teeth whitening is a very safe procedure. We use specially formulated gels designed to minimize sensitivity and protect your enamel. During your consultation, we evaluate your oral health to ensure you are a good candidate for whitening and to prevent any discomfort.

How long do the results of teeth whitening last? The longevity of professional whitening results varies for every patient, as it depends heavily on your lifestyle and oral hygiene habits. To maintain your bright smile, we recommend avoiding stain-causing foods and drinks (like coffee, red wine, and berries) and maintaining regular dental cleanings at our McConachie location.

Does teeth whitening work on crowns, veneers, or fillings? Whitening treatments are only effective on natural tooth enamel and will not change the colour of dental crowns, bridges, porcelain veneers, or composite fillings. If you have existing dental work, our North Edmonton cosmetic dentists can discuss a personalized plan to ensure your smile looks uniform and bright.

What causes tooth discolouration? Many factors can lead to yellowing or staining, including aging, tobacco use, and frequent consumption of dark-coloured beverages. Our North Edmonton team can help identify the cause of your discolouration and recommend the best whitening solution to restore your confidence and your smile.
