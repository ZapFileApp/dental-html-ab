Our devoted dentists are passionate about helping the whole family feel at ease while chairside. That’s why we proudly provide modern, compassionate care personalized to every stage of life.

What Is Family Dentistry, and Why Does It Matter?

Family dentistry in Red Deer focuses on the unique dental needs of patients at all ages, from toddler teeth to teenagers, young and middle-aged adults, and seniors. These services deliver a full spectrum of care in one familiar setting, unlike pediatric-only care.

Our Family Dentistry Services Include:

  • Dental Hygiene Services:
    We evaluate your oral and overall health and detect any potential problems. As your dentist in Red Deer, Alberta, we help ensure that you keep your teeth in optimal shape for the long term. Every family member receives a personalized exam and cleaning that’s gentle enough for kids, sufficient for adults, and seniors with sensitive gums. Apart from removing stains and plaque from your teeth, our dental hygienists apply dental sealants to prevent cavities. With trusted technology like Guided Biofilm Therapy (GBT), we make hygiene visits comfortable, even for the most anxious or youngest patients.
  • Fluoride Treatments and Dental Sealants:
    Our preventive treatments include effective fluoride applications and protective sealants that shield teeth from decay. These personalized treatments are key for growing smiles, helping kids, teens, and even adults avoid cavities and develop lifelong confidence in their oral health.
  • White Fillings, Crowns, and Bridges:
    Whether a child chips a tooth or an adult needs a crown, our tooth-coloured materials blend seamlessly with your natural smile. We restore function and appearance with customized technique, so your family can eat, speak, and smile comfortably.
  • Orthodontic Assessments (Including Invisalign® for Teens/Adults):
    We monitor bite and alignment at every visit, catching problems early and providing guidance on Invisalign® clear aligners. These personalized, transparent alignment system straightens teeth discreetly, helping you smile confidently more quickly. 
  • Gum Disease Prevention and Therapy:
    Gum health is crucial at every age. We provide periodontal treatments to restore gum disease and identify issues early. Our dentist in Red Deer educates your family on preventing gingivitis and periodontitis. Whether you’re a parent or a senior seeking support, we’re your trusted partner!
  • Tooth Extractions:
    Sometimes, removing a tooth is the healthiest option. At times, there isn’t sufficient room for the wisdom teeth to erupt fully, so they become impacted or trapped and need to be removed. We also offer aftercare tips and solutions to help your mouth heal quickly, whether it’s a child’s stubborn baby tooth or a teen’s wisdom teeth.
  • Emergency Dental Care:
    Dental emergencies don’t follow a schedule. From toothache to knocked-out teeth and broken braces or wires, cut or bleeding tongues or lips, our Red Deer team is ready with same-day care for all ages. We’re here to relieve discomfort and restore health, so you can get back to your daily life with peace of mind.
  • Root Canal Therapy:
    When the nerve of your tooth becomes infected or inflamed, a root canal treatment lets you keep the tooth rather than having to pull it out. Saving your tooth helps to prevent your other teeth from drifting out of line and causing jaw problems.
  • Family Block Appointments and Flexible Scheduling:
    Need to bring in multiple kids or your whole family at once? We offer convenient appointment times, are open everyday with early morning and evening visits, so everyone can be seen together, saving you time and stress. 

What Technology Do We Utilize?

Guided Biofilm Therapy(GBT)

GBT uses modern tools to remove biofilms or bacterial deposits from the surface of teeth. The plaque is located, making removal easier. This technique allows us to evaluate your teeth and gums, locate the biofilm, understand your tooth’s condition, and get rid of remaining calculus. 

AIRFLOW®

AIRFLOW® technology is a dental handpiece used to clean and polish teeth. It removes deposits as deep as 4 mm under the gumline. Our trusted dental hygienists use this technology to perform laser therapy, scaling, and root planning.

Cephalometric X-ray

This specific type of X-ray allows our dentist to capture a complete image of the side of the face. We use it to view the teeth, jawbone, and soft tissues and determine if there are any developmental concerns involving the head, neck, and jaw.

STA Single Tooth Anesthesia®

This computer-assisted system is used for local anesthesia. We utilize the technology to carefully guide us in administering injections. Patients can experience less discomfort and not have unnecessary numbness in collateral areas.

How CDCP Support Your Family’s Dental Health

Navigating dental costs can be a real concern for many families, but with the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP), keeping everyone healthy just got slightly easier. CDCP coverage is designed to make preventive and essential dental care accessible to more Canadians, including children, teens, adults, and seniors. This plan accommodates patients that do not have access to dental insurance, have an adjusted family net income less than $90,000, are a Canadian resident, and have filed tax returns in the country.

Here’s how CDCP coverage can help your family at Image Dental Care:

  • Covers Preventive Visits for Eligible Patients:
    CDCP supports dental exams, cleanings, and routine X-rays, making it easier to keep up with essential care and catch problems early, often before they become bigger or more costly to treat.
  • Includes Care for Children and Teens:
    Services like fluoride treatments and dental sealants are included, helping kids build strong, healthy smiles from their very first tooth through the teen years.
  • Supports Adult and Senior Dental Needs:
    CDCP can help cover dental implants, fillings, wisdom teeth removal, and gum disease management, so parents and grandparents get the support they need to maintain healthy teeth and gums at every stage of life.
  • Reduces Out-of-Pocket Expenses:
    With direct billing and clear guidance from our team, eligible families can focus on their health, not their budgets. We help you understand what’s covered and minimize surprise costs.

Our dentist in Red Deer proudly accepts CDCP coverage so that eligible patients in Red Deer can access essential dental services without financial stress!

Smart Oral Health Tips for Red Deer Families

Caring for your family’s teeth and gums doesn’t end at the dental office. Building healthy habits at home makes a lasting difference for every age. Here are some practical, dentist-approved tips to help your whole family maintain strong, healthy smiles between visits:

  • Brush Together, Twice a Day:
    Set a family routine by brushing in the morning and before bed. For young kids, use a soft-bristle brush and a rice-sized amount of fluoride toothpaste – let them “help” as you guide their hand.
  • Floss Like a Pro:
    Show children how to floss gently between every tooth once a day. For little hands or braces, try floss picks or soft interdental brushes.
  • Snack Smart:
    Limit sticky, sugary snacks and drinks. Choose water, crunchy fruits, veggies, and cheese to keep teeth strong and reduce the risk of cavities.
  • Make Dental Visits a Positive Event:
    Celebrate healthy checkups with small rewards or a fun family outing, not sugary treats. Talk about the dentist as a friendly helper, not a place to fear.
  • Lead by Example:
    Let your children see you caring for your own teeth, and talk openly about why oral health matters for everyone – kids are great copycats!
  • Don’t Skip Regular Checkups:
    Even if teeth look healthy, regular dental visits are essential for prevention and early detection – plus, they help reinforce your homecare routine.
