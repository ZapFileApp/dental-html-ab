Since 1986, our family owned and operated clinic in downtown, chinatown has provided a high standard of friendly, compassionate care to each and every patient.

At Centre Street Dental, we take the time to listen and understand your needs, lifestyle, and oral health goals to create a personalized treatment plan just for you. We offer a wide range of services, from routine checkups and cleanings to full smile transformations, all aimed at boosting your confidence and supporting your overall health.

With fees that follow the Alberta Dental Association fee guide and direct billing, our clinic strives to provide honest and affordable dentistry.

We are proud to be a multilingual office! Our team can speak English, Cantonese, Mandarin, and Vietnamese.

Our Services

Dental Hygiene and Check ups

Regular cleanings and check ups are essential for a healthy smile. Our dentists and hygienists provide thorough, gentle care catered to your personal needs.

General Dentistry

Our experienced dentists are able to provide patients with all aspects of general dentistry including but not limited to pediatric dentistry, fillings, extractions, and root canal therapy.

Emergency Dental Care

We go above and beyond to communicate with our patients to make sure emergency care is available as soon as possible to alleviate pain and treat the root cause.

Replacement of Missing Teeth

Our team will work with you to discuss the best options for tooth replacement. We offer dental implants, bridges, or dentures to help restore your smile.

Cosmetic Dentistry

From laser whitening to veneers, crowns and tooth bonding our team will customize a plan to suit your needs so you can get the smile you deserve.

Invisalign

Invisalign is an alternative treatment to braces by using a series of clear custom fit aligners. If you are unhappy with your tooth alignment book an appointment today to see if Invisalign is right for you.
