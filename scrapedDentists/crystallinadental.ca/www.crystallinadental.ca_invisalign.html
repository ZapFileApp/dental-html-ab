Invisalign Clear Aligners

Straighten your teeth with clear invisible braces

You may have decided not to correct your crooked or misaligned teeth due to the possible inconvenience of wearing traditional metal braces. Traditional metal braces might be a burden or may even feel uncomfortable, and above everything else, they are very prominent and noticeable. With modern dentistry, we have the option of Invisalign clear invisible braces.

Using Invisalign, your dentist can straighten your teeth without using brackets and wires; instead your dentist will employ a series of customized clear removable aligners. Invisalign braces are practically undetectable allowing you to feel confident while speaking, eating and smiling. Invisalign clear braces are also fully removable.

Invisalign braces allows the convenience of:

  • Comfort and custom designed for your unique smile
  • Flexibility of wearing braces without others knowing you have them on
  • Eat your favourite meals whenever you want
  • Easy to maintenance, simply brush and floss your teeth as you usually would


The process of straightening teeth can take several months or even years to complete and traditional metal braces may not be your most desired option.

How Does Invisalign Work?

By selecting the Invisalign teeth straighten system, we are employing advanced 3D computer technology with modern day dentistry. Invisalign aligners are specially produced to progressively adjust your teeth in little steps, ultimately reaching the desired final position without the use of wires and brackets.

Each clear aligner is designed and fabricated to fit your mouth at each phase of treatment created by your dentist. Talk to your dentist to see if Invisalign braces are the correct solution for your dental needs.
