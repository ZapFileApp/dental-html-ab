Dental X-Rays

Digital x-rays are one of the most modern ways of acquiring dental x-rays. This advanced technology reduces exposure to radiation by 80 percent compared to traditional dental films. Digital x-rays can provide a dentist with highly detailed images of oral structures, allowing them to view details not otherwise visible. In addition to improving the quality of your dental care, digital x-rays also help your dentist plan your treatment, allowing them to accurately diagnose any hidden abnormalities.
