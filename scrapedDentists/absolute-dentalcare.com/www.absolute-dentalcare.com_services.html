Our services

From children’s checkups to advanced cosmetic and restorative treatments, we offer a full range of dental services tailored to your unique needs.

Children’s Dentistry

Gentle exams, preventive treatments, and a fun environment to keep little ones feeling comfortable.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Smile makeovers, whitening treatments, and more to give you a confident, radiant smile.

Extractions

Safe removal of problematic teeth, with a focus on comfort and quick recovery.

Snoring & Sleep Apnea Solutions

Custom oral appliances to help you and your loved ones rest easier.

Root Canal Treatment

Advanced care to save infected or damaged teeth and relieve pain.

Teeth Cleaning & Preventive Care

Routine cleanings, check-ups, and personalized hygiene instructions to maintain optimal oral health.

Crowns & Bridges

Restorative solutions to strengthen damaged teeth or replace missing ones with natural-looking results.

Implants

Long-lasting replacements for missing teeth, restoring function and appearance.

Oral Sedation

Alleviate dental anxiety with sedation options that keep you relaxed during procedures.

Zoom Whitening

Philips Zoom is a professional, in-office whitening treatment designed to safely lighten discoloration caused by coffee, tea, wine, aging, and everyday staining.
