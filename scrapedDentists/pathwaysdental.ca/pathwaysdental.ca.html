Some of Our Most Requested NE Calgary Dental Services

EMERGENCY DENTISTRY

You need a Calgary Emergency Dentist ASAP when you have a toothache, broken teeth, or damaged restoration.

IV SEDATION

With IV Sedation (Intravenous Sedation), patients achieve complete relaxation even during the most complex procedures.

DENTAL IMPLANTS

Dental Implants may be an option for people who have lost a tooth or teeth due to periodontal disease, an injury, or some other reason.

ORTHODONTICS

Do you have crooked or misaligned teeth? You too can have a straight beautiful smile that is easier to maintain!

GUM DISEASE

Plaque & bacteria are the primary causes of gum disease. However, other factors can contribute to periodontal disease.

COSMETIC DENTISTRY

Do you want to enhance your smile? Cosmetic services such as porcelain veneers, teeth whitening, or composite bonding may be right for you!

Cosmetic & Therapeutic Injections

Headaches? Clenched muscles? Frown lines? We offer treatments to relax muscles and enhance youthfulness by using neuromodulators.

TEETH WHITENING

Tooth whitening can be a very effective way of lightening the natural color of your teeth without removing any of the tooth surfaces.

    Do You Have Questions? We Have Answers!

    Does Pathways Dental Clinic offer Affordable Calgary Dentistry?

    Yes. We follow the latest ADA&C Dental Fee Guide. Some of our fees are below the Dental Fee Guide, so you can count on predictable and consistent billing for all your dental needs.

    Does Pathways Dental Clinic take all insurance policies? Do you bill directly?

    Yes. We accept all national plans and do direct billing for most insurance companies.

    Is Pathways Dental open evenings or weekends?

    Yes. Pathways Dental is open until 8:00 pm on most days, and we are pleased to offer evening appointments. For more convenience, we are also open every Saturday and some Sundays.

    I think I have a dental emergency. What should I do?

    If you think you are having a dental emergency, please call us right away. We take calls 24/7, 365 days a year. We will get to you ASAP! Do not try to wait it out.

    Does Pathways Dental offer Sedation?

    Yes! Our NE Calgary Dentist’s office provides all modalities of sedation, including IV sedation.

    Does Pathways Dental Clinic treat kids?

    Yes, we treat kids! We have a very kid-friendly office. We have a playroom and also offer sedation services such as nitrous (if needed).

    How can I book my appointment at Pathways Dental Clinic?

    We offer an easy online booking system for you to book online. Click here to schedule your first appointment, or if you are an existing patient wanting to book.

    Or simply call us at (403) 455 5565, we have someone standing by 24/7 to receive your call which will get to us quickly, even after hours.

    What is your cancellation/reschedule policy?

    We have a firm 48-hour cancellation/rescheduling policy for all appointments. We understand that sometimes life happens, and you need to reschedule. Please try to give us ample warning. If you think you can’t make an appointment, please call us right away! We strive to continue to offer the best services to our patients and will enforce any missed fees if it happens repeatedly and can sometimes require a deposit to book large treatments. This is to ensure that all of our patients have a chance to receive the care they need.
