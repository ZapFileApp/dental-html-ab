What does a hygienist do?

Our dental hygienists at Beacon Smiles Dental believe in teaching our patients preventative oral health. We look for signs of gum disease and provide treatment right away. Our goal is to teach you and your family the correct dental hygiene techniques to ensure oral health. Our dental treatment includes professional cleaning of your teeth by removing plaque and tartar.


The role of our hygienists is to show you the best way to keep your teeth plaque-free. Plaque is a sticky coating that can negatively impact your teeth with cavities and other tooth degradation.


We understand that keeping your teeth in pristine shape can be difficult. However, every effort should be made to keep your teeth healthy as possible. Our dental hygienist in Calgary will advise you on simple yet effective treatment plans.

Why you should get your teeth cleaned

  • Help ensure you keep your natural teeth!
  • Your hygienist can remove plaque build up that is in spaces you can't reach
  • Your hygienist can remove some of the stains that discolour your teeth
  • Help prevent gum disease by making sure your teeth are clean!
  • Your hygienist will complete an oral cancer screening
  • Educating patients about their oral health
