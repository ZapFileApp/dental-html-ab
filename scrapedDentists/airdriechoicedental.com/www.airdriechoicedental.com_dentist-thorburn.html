Our Comprehensive Dental Services

We have several options to whiten and keep your smile healthy here at Airdrie Choice Dental. Let’s take a look:

  • Preventive Dentistry: We prevent problems before they happen with routine check-ups, cleanings, fluoride to strengthen your teeth, and sealants for children to avoid cavities.
  • Restorative Dentistry: Need to fill a cavity or repair a tooth? We perform natural-coloured fillings, crowns to cap a broken tooth, bridges to replace missing teeth, and even dental implants to fill an absent tooth.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry: Do you want a whiter smile? We have teeth whitening and veneers (thin coverings on your teeth) to brighten your smile. We even offer Invisalign to straighten your teeth without metal braces.
  • Emergency Dentistry: Toothache? Cracked tooth? We’ll get you in immediately to help you feel better quickly.
  • Oral Health Care: We take advantage of the newest technology, such as digital impressions (bye-bye gloopy moulds!), and look for any indication of trouble, such as oral cancer. We also provide special gum cleanings.
  • Wisdom Teeth & Extractions: If your wisdom teeth are causing problems, we can safely and comfortably remove them.
  • Sedation Dentistry: If you are nervous about visiting the dentist, we can assist you in relaxing during your visit.

We treat each person like family and use the latest technology to make sure that you feel comfortable.
