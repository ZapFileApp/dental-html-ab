Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Naghmeh Farsi

Dr. Farsi earned her degree from the University of Toronto in 2015. After graduating, she taught as a clinical instructor in the Faculty of Dentistry while practicing at various dental clinics in Toronto and Mississauga for five years. Frequently Asked Questions

Can you bill my insurance provider directly?

Yes, we provide direct billing services for the majority of insurance companies.

 
Do you accept CDCP?

Yes, Wolf Willow Orchid Dental is proud to partner with the Canadian Dental Care Plan to serve individuals in SE Calgary and surrounding communities.

 
What services does Wolf Willow Orchid Dental provide?

At Wolf Willow Orchid Dental, a premier dental clinic, we provide a full range of services to meet your needs. Whether you’re searching for a “routine check-up,” fillings, or advanced procedures like crowns and braces, our team is dedicated to keeping your smile healthy and bright. If you’re looking for a trusted dentist near me in South East Calgary, we’re here for you!

Do you offer pediatric dental services?

Yes, we do! We’re passionate about making dental visits fun and stress-free for kids. From their first check-up to cavity prevention, our specialized pediatric care ensures your child’s oral health is in good hands. If you’re searching for Calgary dentists SE who care for the whole family, we’re here to help.

Do you provide teeth whitening?

Absolutely! Brighten your smile with our professional teeth whitening services. Safe, effective, and designed to make your smile radiant, our treatments are a fantastic choice. If you’ve been searching for a dental clinic to help you achieve a dazzling smile, we’ve got you covered.

What if I need an emergency dentist?

If you have a dental emergency, don’t worry. Our emergency dentist is experienced in handling urgent situations like toothaches, broken teeth, or accidents. When you need an emergency dentist, we’re just a call away.

How can I book an appointment?

You can call, book through our website, or use our patient portal. Finding the best time to visit your trusted Calgary dentists SE is simple with our friendly team ready to assist.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept most insurance plans, major credit and debit cards, and offer flexible payment options to accommodate your needs. Let us know what works best for you, and we’ll ensure your experience at our dental clinic is smooth and stress-free.

How often should I visit the dentist?

It is generally recommended to visit the dentist every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, your dentist may suggest more or less frequent visits depending on your oral health needs.

What can I do to prevent cavities?

To prevent cavities, practice good oral hygiene by brushing twice a day with fluoride toothpaste, flossing daily, maintaining a healthy diet low in sugar, and visiting your dentist regularly.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Teeth whitening is safe when performed under the supervision or guidance of a dental professional. Over-the-counter products may not provide the same level of safety or effectiveness as professional treatments.

 
What are the signs of gum disease?

Signs of gum disease include red, swollen, or bleeding gums, persistent bad breath, receding gums, and loose teeth. If you notice any of these symptoms, consult your dentist promptly.
