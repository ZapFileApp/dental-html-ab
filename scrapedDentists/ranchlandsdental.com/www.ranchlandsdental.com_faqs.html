Do you follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide?

Yes! We follow the 2025 Alberta Dental Fee Guide, so you can feel confident knowing our fees align with the recommended rates.

Do you offer appointments on weekends?

We sure do! We’re open on alternating Saturdays to help accommodate your busy schedule. Give us a call or book an appointment online to find a time that works for you!

Do you offer direct insurance billing?

Yes, we do! We’ll take care of the paperwork and bill your insurance provider directly, so you can focus on your smile.

I have an emergency! What should I do?

Don’t worry—we’re here to help! If you’re experiencing a dental emergency, please email us at ranchlandsemerg@trecdental.com or call us right away at 403-239-5212.
