About Us

22nd @ Taylor Dental Care in Red Deer aims to provide you with a comfortable and relaxing environment for your checkups and dental treatments. We will exceed your expectations through professional results and our friendly staff. We promise to listen to your needs and wishes. ​
​
Dental health is not just about your mouth and your teeth. Your dental health is wired to your physical health. We are passionate and driven to improve your physical health by treating your dentistry and oral hygiene needs. 

A Family Dentist in Red Deer for the Whole Family

At 22nd @ Taylor Dental Care, we are dedicated to providing comprehensive care for the oral health needs of your entire family. From routine check-ups and cleanings to specialized treatments, our team of experienced professionals ensures that every family member enjoys a healthy and beautiful smile. We strive to create a comfortable and welcoming environment where you and your loved ones can feel at ease while receiving top-quality dental care. Trust us to be your partner in maintaining optimal oral health for a lifetime.

Modern Laser Dentistry

At 22nd @ Taylor Dental Care, we combine modern technology with a personal touch to deliver top-notch dental services. Our clinic utilizes advanced techniques like laser dentistry for gentler, more effective treatments. This means procedures can be quicker and more comfortable, helping reduce anxiety and promote healing. We stay up-to-date with the latest in dental innovation so you and your family receive the best care possible.

Preventative Dentistry

We believe that excellent dental health starts with prevention and education. Our hygienists provide thorough cleanings and one-on-one guidance on oral hygiene. Regular dental checkups and cleanings are the foundation of a lifetime of healthy teeth and gums. By catching issues early and keeping up good habits, you can avoid more serious problems down the road and save on future costs.

Dental Services with 22nd @ Taylor Dental Care

Emergency Dentistry

Immediate dental care when you need it most. We provide quick, reliable treatment for toothaches, injuries, or any urgent dental issue to relieve pain and protect your smile. If you’re facing a dental emergency in Red Deer, call us right away and we’ll do our best to see you promptly.

Routine Exams

Taking care of your oral health is crucial for maintaining overall well-being. At 22nd@Taylor Dental Care, we offer routine dental checkups to detect early signs of dental problems and prevent them from becoming issues.

Teeth Whitening

Our skilled dentists and staff are passionate about delivering lasting, beautiful smiles. Using the latest dental advances, we remove tough stains caused by everyday foods and drinks, revealing your teeth’s natural brilliance.
