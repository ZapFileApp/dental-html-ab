Frequently Asked Questions

What dental services does Highlands Dental Centre provide?

Highlands Dental Centre offers preventive, restorative, and cosmetic services, including cleanings, Invisalign, dental implants, full mouth restoration, and more for patients of all ages.

How can I book an appointment with a dentist near me at Highlands Dental Centre?

You can easily book an appointment online or call Highlands Dental Centre. Our team will help you find a convenient time at either of our two Edmonton locations.

Do you offer services for children and seniors at Highlands Dental Centre?
Yes! Highlands Dental Centre provides comprehensive dental care for toddlers, children, adults, and seniors, ensuring comfort and quality for every age group.
What is non-surgical gum treatment and who needs it?
Non-surgical gum treatment at Highlands Dental Centre is a gentle procedure to manage and treat gum disease, ideal for patients showing early signs of periodontal issues.
Does Highlands Dental Centre provide cosmetic dentistry?
Yes, Highlands Dental Centre offers cosmetic dentistry services, including teeth whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers, to help you achieve a beautiful, confident smile.
Are dental implants and full mouth restorations available at Highlands Dental Centre?
Absolutely. Highlands Dental Centre provides implant restorations and full mouth restorations to replace missing teeth, restore function, and enhance aesthetics.
What makes Highlands Dental Centre different from other dental clinics in Highlands?
Highlands Dental Centre focuses on patient-centered care, multilingual communication, flexible scheduling, and a gentle, modern approach to dentistry for lasting oral health.
Can I receive orthodontic treatment at Highlands Dental Centre?
Yes, Highlands Dental Centre provides orthodontics and orthopedics to improve teeth and jaw alignment with personalized treatment plans tailored to your needs.
