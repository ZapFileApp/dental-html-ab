From Red Deer, For Red Deer

A place where generations of families feel at home — and where you will too, the moment you walk in.  

For over 45 years, Ocudent Dental has cared for the Red Deer community with honest dentistry and familiar faces you can trust. Founded by Dr. Barry Fleming, our practice was built on a simple vision, to provide personal, high-quality care that lasts. That same commitment continues today through the families we serve and the long-standing team who make every visit feel like home. Now located at 5101, 20 Sharpe Ave, Red Deer, across from the Centrium, our modern, calming space reflects the comfort and care we’re known for. Whether you’re new or returning, we welcome you to connect with a Red Deer family dentist you can rely on for years to come.
