    We’re back!!!

    As part of phase one of Alberta’s COVID-19 relaunch strategy, Spruce Grove Dental Clinic is ramping up our services with enhanced Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) and protocols to keep our valued patients and staff safe. We are now providing all of our regular dental services starting the week of May 19th and we’re so happy to be back and excited to see all of your smiling faces once again.
    You will notice that there are some new changes to enhance patient safety. You will be asked COVID-19 screening questions when we confirm your appointment and again when you arrive at the clinic. We will ask you to wear a mask when you arrive at our clinic and there will be hand sanitizer for you to use when you arrive and before you depart. You’ll also notice a few changes in our waiting room to help facilitate physical distancing and our staff will be using a few additional items of personal protective equipment.
    All of these changes are happening throughout our society during this time so you probably won’t be surprised when you see us, but rest assured, it’s all to enhance your safety and ours.

    We’re all in this together and we’re looking forward to seeing you again.
