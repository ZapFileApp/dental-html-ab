Cardston, Alberta: A Charming Town with a Rich History

Located in the heart of southern Alberta, Cardston is a town steeped in history and natural beauty. At All Smiles Dental, we're proud to call Cardston home and serve our community with exceptional dental care.

Landmarks and Attractions

Cardston Alberta Temple

A historic landmark and sacred site, this stunning temple is a beautiful example of architectural excellence and a popular tourist destination.

Remington Carriage Museum

Showcasing a vast collection of horse-drawn vehicles, offering a unique glimpse into our town's pioneer past.

Courthouse Museum

Featuring local history and artifacts, a must-visit for anyone interested in Cardston's rich heritage.

Waterton Lakes National Park

As a gateway to this majestic national park, Cardston offers easy access to stunning scenery and abundant wildlife.

Community and Culture

Cardston is a close-knit community with a strong sense of pride and heritage. From the annual Cardston Stampede to the Carriage House Theatre, there's always something happening in this vibrant town.

With a strong emphasis on family, community, and outdoor recreation, Cardston is an ideal place to live, work, and raise a family.

  • Family-Oriented Community
  • Rich Cultural Heritage
  • Outdoor Recreation Opportunities
  • Annual Community Events
