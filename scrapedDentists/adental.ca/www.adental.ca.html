Our Family here for Yours

welcome!

 

Aurora Dental Group has been proud to serve beautiful Sylvan Lake and the surrounding areas for over 18 years! We are pleased to provide a wide range of General Dentistry services to all ages, with the aim to restore, improve and maintain your smile. The skill, experience, and commitment of our practice professionals is apparent. We understand how empowering a Beautiful Smile can be, and thus our aim everyday is to help you implement and maintain healthy habits while providing comprehensive and compassionate care. 

Our patients appreciate our prime location in Hewlett Park Landing with the convenience of free parking and wheelchair accessibility. We offer complimentary beverages while you wait in our comfortable patient lounge complete with a kids zone! We understand that a visit to the Dentist can be a source of anxiety for many, so we do our best to alleviate your stress! To help you relax during your time with us, our treatment rooms are equipped with lower back massaging treatment chairs, and ceiling/wall mounted TV’s. We also have our customized Staff Playlist with over 1300 songs from a variety of genres and decades! Our patients often share with us how much they loved the music while with us and we’re confident you will as well!

Which brings us to YOU! We welcome New Patients daily, and are happy to say that most of them are referred by our valued existing patients. If you  have friends or family who attend our clinic, you may want to see for yourself why they have chosen us to care for their dental needs!
