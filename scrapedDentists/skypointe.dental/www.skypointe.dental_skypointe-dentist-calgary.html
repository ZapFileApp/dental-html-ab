What are our favourite things you can find in Skypointe, Calgary NE?

Skypointe is one of Calgary's newest communities located in the city's rich and cultural northeast corner. Defined by a vibrant culinary arts scene, a strong entrepreneurial spirit and a family-focused atmosphere, Skypointe is a welcoming community that we are proud to be a part of!

From local aesthetics and hair studios like Tulip Hair Salon & Spa, to senior's accommodations like AgeCare Skypointe, to beautiful housing by Trico homes and delicious food by Lovely Sweet House & Indian Restaurant, there are many amenities to enjoy!

Plus, you have dental services, a medical centre, optometry services, physiotherapy AND an Anytime Fitness all in short walking distance of each other within Skypointe landing. You can knock all of your regular appointments out in one day!

Oh yes, and we can't forget about the Marble Slab around the corner — makes a sweet treat for you and your kiddos while out running errands!

Watching Skypointe grow has been enriching and rewarding. We can't wait to see how it continues to flourish as time goes on!
