Why Us

Affordable Quality Dentist in Sherwood Park

Our Patients Love Us. Find Your Reason

  • Open 5 Days A Week
  • FREE Teeth Whitening
  • Emergency Walk-ins Welcome
  • Direct Billing To Insurance

Looking For A New Dentist

Choosing the right dentist will ensure a positive experience and be given excellent quality care. It’s what makes us different at Sherwood Park Dental Care Centre and judging from my own patient feedback, it’s why our patients have chosen us.
Changing The Perception
Every treatment is performed with meticulous regard and care for the patient. The result? A pleasant dental experience that will encourage you to go back for your next appointments.
Direct Billing, Insurance Coverage And Easy Payment Plans
We direct bill insurance and accept all major insurance and government plans. We also offer instant financing with easy monthly payment plans.
Kids, Adults & Seniors. We Welcome Them All
Kids: It is very important to stress good hygiene habits from an early age. We use an easygoing non confrontational approach to treating kids. This is why parents entrust us with treating their children. Toys and videos are available for children. Seniors: Keeping one’s teeth and smile in good condition is important at any age. It defines the quality of life. One is never too old for comfort and confidence! Full and partial dentures services are available for those with full or partial tooth loss.
Free Consultation & Second Opinion
We offer free consultation. During the initial consultation appointment, Sherwood Park Dental dentists will thoroughly review the patients’ medical and dental history and overall concerns will be discussed before starting the treatment.
Free Teeth Whitening For Life
A bright, gleaming smile can have a positive effect on your overall appearance, and ultimately help you feel more confident about the way you look. We are excited to offer our patients FREE Teeth Whitening for life with all adult check-ups and cleanings. Stained teeth can cast a shadow over your smile, though it is a natural part of the aging process. Red wine, coffee, tea, and tobacco use can all cause your teeth to stain more quickly.
We Provide Immediate Emergency Dental Care
We recognize dental emergencies can happen anytime. Accidents that lead to broken teeth and severe dental pain cannot be planned. This is one of the primary reasons why we offer emergency walk in services. Whether you are a current patient or someone who needs help, our team will take the time to resolve the situation – most often with same-day appointments. Over the years, we have helped hundreds of your friends and neighbors save their teeth and get out of dental pain. Pick up the phone. We are ready for your call – morning, noon or night.

Dental Care That Will Make You Smile

We offer world-class dental care with treatment that’s tailored to your individual needs and desires. We are dedicated to providing the best possible treatment in a welcoming and comfortable setting where patients are cared for like family. Don’t just take our word for it—check out the glowing testimonials from our patients!

A Wide Range of Dental Services

Our highly skilled dentists provide comprehensive care for kids, adults and seniors. In addition to dental exams, cleanings and fillings, we offer a number of restorative and cosmetic procedures, including crowns, bridges, veneers and teeth whitening. Embark on a journey to a healthier smile.

In Good Hands, With Gentle Care

A refreshing new approach to dental care. Changing the perception. The result? A pleasant dental experience that will encourage you to go back for your next appointments.
