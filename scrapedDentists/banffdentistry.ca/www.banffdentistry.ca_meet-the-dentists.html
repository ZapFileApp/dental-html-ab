Learn About Our Dentist

Dr. Fauzia Sajjad, DDS

Dr Fauzia Sajjad is proud to be serving the bow valley community since 2013. She is a University of Alberta Graduate and member of Alberta Dental Association and Canadian Dental Association.

Her passion for providing excellent oral health care has been her motivation to enhance her skills by continuing education. She prides herself to be able to offer all general dentistry services. She is delighted that both Banff dentistry and Canmore Dentistry teams are a part of her family now and will be managed by her and her husband Dr Shakil amin . Her spare time is spent with her son kaiser and enjoying the natural beauty of this beautiful town .



Dr. Toni Jett Trottier, DDS

Originally from small town Sundre, Alberta, Dr TJ Trottier is thrilled to be back in the Canadian Rockies after nearly 9 years living in Australia. Dr TJ moved to Australia in 2015 where she completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery degree, received with Distinction. 

After graduation, Dr TJ worked at a private practice in Sunshine Coast, Queensland, where she enjoyed serving the close-knit community. Her main philosophies in providing excellent dental care involve honest communication, patient education, and high attention to detail. 

Dr TJ enjoys all aspects of general dentistry and loves the variety each day brings. Although she has a particular interest in implant dentistry and cosmetic cases. Dr TJ also enjoys making the dentist a fun and positive environment for the little ones! 

Swapping out the surfboard for a pair of skis, Dr TJ is keen to make the most of living in the Rockies. Outside of work, you’ll most likely find Dr TJ on a trail run or bike ride with her dog or hitting the slopes. She is an avid mountain biker, runner, and hiker. Most recently completing a 4,300km thru-hike of the Pacific Crest Trail!


Dr. Brayden Fracchia, DDS

Hailing from tropical Queensland Australia, Brayden completed his studies in Cairns as well as completing placements in Tasmania, and Mount Isa and the Gulf. He moved to the Sunshine Coast in early 2020 to begin his career as a dentist where he has worked both in the private sector, and with Queensland Oral Health Services.

Education is something that Brayden holds in high regard and enjoys researching root canal treatments and injection moulding with composite fillings. Brayden also enjoys learning paediatric dentistry and believes that everyone should have a comfortable and enjoyable experience at the dentist. His favourite compliment is when people have a snooze in the chair!

Brayden moved to the Bow Valley to continue to grow his knowledge and skill base to become a well-rounded clinician and looks to grow from the wealth of knowledge that the other clinicians bring to the practice. His passion is maintaining teeth through life and enjoys restoring broken teeth back to something functional and appealing to the eye.

When not in the clinic you can only find Brayden in the mountains. He is a keen outdoorsman and with his dog Olive, enjoys both mountain biking and trail running. Brayden looks forward to meeting you and providing you with a calm and comfortable dental experience. 


Dr. Lester Ikuta , DDS

I was born in Vancouver and moved to Calgary as a child. I later attended the University of Alberta, where I earned my Bachelor of Science, followed by a Doctor of Dental Surgery degree. After working for a year as an associate, I opened my first dental practice in Northeast Calgary and, shortly after, my second practice in South Calgary.

Throughout my career, I have remained committed to continuing education, attending local and international conferences and completing a wide range of advanced courses. I trained at the Kois advanced dental education Center in Seattle and also at DOCS Education, a leading institution in sedation dentistry. I have also completed numerous courses in CEREC 3D CAD/CAM dentistry, a technology I have continually refined and mastered over the years.

My practice philosophy, which has guided me for 40 years, is centered on patient-focused care - educating patients so they can make informed treatment decisions while providing the highest standard of care.

When I’m not working, I enjoy the outdoors and can often be found golfing, skiing both cross-country and downhill, enjoying hiking and biking trails, or walking our two dogs around Canmore.

For the past 20 years, my family have split our time between Calgary and Canmore. As we shift to spending more time in Canmore, I am grateful for the opportunity to join the team at Canmore Dentistry.  


Dr. Yazen Al-Badran, DMD

Meet Dr. Yazen Al-Badran, DMD. Yazen earned his DMD from the University of Sydney before bringing his passion for modern dentistry to the beautiful community of Canmore.

Dedicated to providing evidence-based care, Dr. Al-Badran is currently advancing his expertise through the renowned Kois Center to provide the highest quality care to his patients. He has also completed extensive training at the California Implant Institute and is a Candidate for Fellowship with the ICOI (FICOI). Besides his implant qualifications, Yazen is an Invisalign® Silver Provider; he loves blending clear aligner therapy with high-level dental photography to meticulously design and celebrate his patients' new smiles.

At his core, Dr. Al-Badran is a devoted family man whose personal values are deeply rooted in his relationships. He spends his free time surrounded by his beautiful wife, family, and friends, travelling or simply spending quality time together. He strives to bring those same values of connection, trust, and warmth into his practice every day.
