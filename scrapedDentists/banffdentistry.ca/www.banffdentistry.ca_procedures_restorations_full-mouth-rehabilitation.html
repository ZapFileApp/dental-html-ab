Full Mouth Rehabilitation

Restore Your Smile, Function, and Confidence

If you’re dealing with multiple dental concerns, full mouth rehabilitation offers a comprehensive solution to rebuild your smile from the ground up. Combining advanced restorative and cosmetic techniques, this customized approach restores function, health, and aesthetics—so you can eat, speak, and smile with confidence again.

What Is Full Mouth Rehabilitation?

Full mouth rehabilitation (also known as full mouth reconstruction) is a personalized treatment plan that addresses extensive dental issues affecting most or all of your teeth. It may involve a combination of procedures to repair damage, replace missing teeth, and improve overall oral health.

Each plan is tailored to your unique needs, goals, and long-term health.

Who Is a Candidate?

You may benefit from full mouth rehabilitation if you experience:

  • Multiple missing teeth

  • Severely worn or damaged teeth due to Bruxism

  • Chronic jaw pain or bite issues related to Temporomandibular Joint Disorder (TMJ/TMD)

  • Extensive tooth decay or failing dental work

  • Difficulty chewing or speaking

  • Trauma or injury affecting your teeth and jaw

Treatments Included in Full Mouth Rehabilitation

Your treatment plan may include a combination of:

  • Dental crowns and bridges

  • Dental implants for permanent tooth replacement

  • Implant-supported dentures or hybrid dentures

  • Porcelain veneers for cosmetic enhancement

  • Periodontal (gum) therapy

  • Bite correction and occlusal adjustments

Our goal is to restore both function and aesthetics for long-lasting results.

The Full Mouth Rehabilitation Process

  1. Comprehensive Consultation
    We evaluate your teeth, gums, bite, and overall oral health using advanced diagnostics.

  2. Customized Treatment Plan
    A step-by-step plan is created to address your specific needs and goals.

  3. Phased Treatment Approach
    Procedures are completed in a strategic sequence to ensure optimal healing and results.

  4. Final Restoration & Maintenance
    Once treatment is complete, we provide guidance to maintain your new smile for years to come.

Benefits of Full Mouth Rehabilitation

Improved Oral Health
Addresses underlying issues and prevents future complications.

Restored Function
Eat, speak, and chew comfortably again.

Enhanced Aesthetics
Achieve a natural, beautiful smile.

Boosted Confidence
Feel more confident in both personal and professional settings.

A Life-Changing Transformation

Full mouth rehabilitation is more than just dental treatment—it’s a complete transformation of your oral health and quality of life. With modern technology and personalized care, we can help you achieve a smile that looks great and functions perfectly.
