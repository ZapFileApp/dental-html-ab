Meet Our Team at Banff Dentistry 

At the core of our dental practice is a friendly team of professionals that are dedicated to your utmost comfort and optimal oral health. We view each patient as a unique individual, and tailor each treatment to your specific needs. As part of our commitment to providing close, personalized attention to every patient, we want to encourage patient communication. Please know that we are here to provide outstanding patient care, and that we value the trust you have placed in us.

Mataya Raven

Office Manager

Born and raised in Radium Hot Springs, BC, Mataya moved to Canmore in early 2024 and quickly felt at home in both the community and the practice. Now serving as Office Manager, she brings extensive experience in dental insurance and a genuine passion for helping patients understand and maximize their benefits. Her approachable nature and attention to detail ensure every patient feels supported, informed, and confident about their coverage and care.

Outside the office, Mataya enjoys a good book, cooking alongside her chef partner, and making the most of life in the beautiful mountain community she’s proud to call home.
