Custom Sports Guards

Protect Your Smile During Every Game

Whether you’re a professional athlete or play sports recreationally, protecting your teeth is essential. Custom sports guards are designed to shield your teeth, gums, and jaw from impact—helping prevent serious dental injuries so you can play with confidence.

What Are Sports Guards?

Sports guards (also known as mouthguards) are protective oral appliances worn during physical activity to reduce the risk of dental trauma. They act as a cushion, absorbing shock and minimizing the impact on your teeth and jaw during contact or high-intensity sports.

Why Choose a Custom Sports Guard?

While over-the-counter options are available, a professionally made sports guard offers significant advantages:

Superior Fit & Comfort
Custom guards are made specifically for your teeth, ensuring a secure and comfortable fit without slipping.

Maximum Protection
Designed to absorb and distribute impact forces more effectively than store-bought alternatives.

Better Breathing & Speaking
A precise fit allows for easier breathing and clearer communication during play.

Durability
High-quality materials provide long-lasting protection throughout your season.

Who Should Wear a Sports Guard?

Sports guards are recommended for athletes of all ages who participate in activities with a risk of contact or falls, including:

  • Football

  • Basketball

  • Hockey

  • Soccer

  • Baseball & softball

  • Wrestling & martial arts

  • Skateboarding, biking, and other recreational sports

Even non-contact sports can pose a risk of dental injury.

Prevent Serious Dental Injuries

Wearing a sports guard can help prevent:

  • Broken or chipped teeth

  • Knocked-out teeth

  • Cuts to the lips, cheeks, and tongue

  • Jaw injuries and fractures

  • Damage associated with Temporomandibular Joint Disorder (TMJ/TMD)

A simple preventive step can save you from complex and costly dental treatments down the road.

Our Custom Sports Guard Process

  1. Digital Scan or Impression – We capture an accurate model of your teeth.

  2. Custom Fabrication – Your sports guard is crafted for optimal protection and fit.

  3. Fitting & Adjustments – We ensure maximum comfort and performance before you leave.

Play Hard. Stay Protected.

A custom sports guard gives you the confidence to focus on your performance—not your teeth. With a comfortable fit and reliable protection, you can stay in the game and protect your smile at the same time.
