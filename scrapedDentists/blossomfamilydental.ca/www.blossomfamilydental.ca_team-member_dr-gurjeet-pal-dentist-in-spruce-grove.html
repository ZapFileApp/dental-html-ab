Meet Dr. Gurjeet Pal Ghuman

Dr. Gurjeet Pal Ghuman – Dentist

At Blossom family dental, Dr. Ghuman leads with a simple mission to provide high quality dental care in a space where patient feels truly comfortable, respected, and cared for.

Graduating with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery in 2014, Dr. Ghuman has spent over a decade transforming smiles and improving lives. She is known for her gentle, conservative, and preventative approach, always aiming to preserve natural teeth and promote long term oral health.

Dr. Ghuman has achieved several prestigious milestones in the field of clear aligner treatment, including:

  • Diamond Plus Invisalign provider – placing her in the Top 1% of Invisalign providers in North America.
  • Fellowship from the American Academy of Clear Aligners (AACA) – a distinction that highlights her advanced training and leadership in clear aligner therapy.
  • “Done in One” Invisalign Award – honoring her excellence in delivering high quality, efficient and Invisalign results in a single streamlined treatment plan.

In addition to clinical practice, Dr. Ghuman has also served as an Instructor at the Bredin Institute where she trained and mentored internationally trained dentists preparing for Canadian dental licensing exams – reflecting her leadership and dedication to the profession.

Dr. Ghuman stays committed to excellence by actively pursuing continuing education and staying up to date with the latest advancements in dental care. She’s a proud member in good standing with The College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta (CDSA), The Alberta Dental Association (ADA) and The American Academy of Clear Aligners (AACA).

Outside the office Dr. Ghuman leads a mindful and active lifestyle. She enjoys music, long walks, yoga, meditation and most of all, spending meaningful time with her family.

Whether you’re coming in for a checkup or a complete smile makeover, Dr. Ghuman and her team are here to support you – with kindness, expertise, and a commitment to making your comfort their priority.
