    WHY GUIDED BIOFILM THERAPY?

    GUIDED BIOFILM THERAPY (GBT)

    Based on the available evidence, GBT is an effective way to remove biofilm, plaque and stain from your teeth.

    ​

    Many patients find GBT more comfortable and experience less pain compared to traditional methods, making it a patient friendly option to maintaining your oral health.

    ​

    Independent Published Review

    ​
     

    THE PROCESS

    TEETH WHITENING

    Assess: Create a personalized treatment plan.

    ​

    Disclose: A disclosing solution is applied to the teeth, staining bacterial biofilm making it a visible guide for the hygienist and patient to see where the plaque is accumulating.

    ​

    Airflow®: Using a combination of warm water, air, and erythritol powder to safely remove biofilm, bacteria, stain and plaque from hard and soft surfaces in the mouth.

    ​

    Piezon®: Next, we use an ultrasonic instrument with warm water to remove hard deposits from above and below the gum line.

    ​

    Refining: In some cases, traditional dental instruments may be used to remove any remaining hard deposits.

    ​

    Evaluation: Ensure all biofilm and deposits have been entirely removed.

    ​

    After GBT, you have the option of a fluoride treatment.

    ​

    ​

    ​

    Many patients notice 1-2 shades brighter after each treatment as GBT effectively removes stain from the teeth.
