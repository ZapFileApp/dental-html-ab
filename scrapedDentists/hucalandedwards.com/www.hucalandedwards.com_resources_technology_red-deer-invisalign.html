Invisalign®

Invisalign® and Invisalign Teen® are the 'clear alternatives' to braces for individuals who need minimal to moderate tooth movement.  The Invisalign™ System uses a series of clear aligners to gently move your teeth over time to create a beautiful smile.

    

For more information on Invisalign® in Red Deer, please contact our office.
