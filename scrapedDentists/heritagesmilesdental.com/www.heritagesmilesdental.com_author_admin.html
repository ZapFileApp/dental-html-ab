Dental FAQ’s

How do I begin my search for a dentist near me?

If you are looking for a dentist, there are a few simple ways to start. You can ask family, friends, or coworkers for recommendations. You can also search online for a “dentist” and read reviews. After shortlisting a few clinics, contact them to learn more about their services and ask any questions you may have.

How does a dentist handle dental emergencies?

If you face a dental emergency, visit your dentist as soon as possible. At the clinic, the dentist will check your teeth and gums and recommend an appropriate treatment plan.

What are the benefits of Invisalign?

Invisalign is a clear, comfortable alternative to metal braces. It helps you straighten your teeth, improve oral health, and feel more confident about your smile.

What types of sedation are offered at a dental clinic?

Sedation helps patients feel calm during treatment. Our dental clinic offers nitrous oxide, IV sedation, and oral sedation.

What happens at a dental cleaning and checkup?

Regular dental cleanings and checkups are essential to maintaining a healthy smile. During a cleaning, your hygienist will remove plaque and tartar buildup from your teeth, polish them to remove residual tartar, and provide a fluoride treatment to protect your teeth against cavities.

Once the cleaning is complete, our dentist near you will examine your mouth for cavities, gum disease, and other abnormalities. An oral cancer screening is also typically included in an oral exam

How can you prepare your child for their first dental appointment?

You can help your child feel ready by keeping their teeth clean, explaining the visit in simple words, and telling them what the dentist will do during the appointment.

How can I find an affordable dentist in Calgary to reduce dental care costs?

You can find affordable dental care by looking at dental schools, checking government dental programs, and using insurance coverage where possible.

Does your clinic follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide?

Yes, we follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide. You can learn more here: [ https://www.heritagesmilesdental.com/alberta-dental-fee-guide/]
