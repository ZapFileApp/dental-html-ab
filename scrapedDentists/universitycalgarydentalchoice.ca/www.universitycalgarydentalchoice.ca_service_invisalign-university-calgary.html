What is Invisalign®?

Invisalign Clear Aligners near you are removable, transparent trays that correct misaligned teeth. Unlike metal braces, these clear aligners are almost invisible and can be taken out for eating, brushing or flossing, making them an ideal choice for teens and adults.

Our dentist near you may recommend Invisalign:

You have crowded or crooked teeth.

There are gaps between your teeth.

You have an overbite, underbite, or crossbite.

You want a discreet and removable straightening option.

Your Invisalign Journey at University of Calgary Dental Choice

Consultation and Scan

Our dentist in University Calgary will check your teeth, take scans, and create a plan for your care.

Custom Aligners

The aligners are made to meet your specific requirements and fit you comfortably.

Wear and Progress

You will be required to wear your aligners for 20-22 hours every day during the course of this treatment.

Final Results

Once we are done with your treatment, we will provide you with a retainer to keep your smile in shape.

Benefits of Invisalign® Treatment

Nearly Invisible

Straighten your teeth discreetly with Invisalign Clear Aligners in University Calgary. Most people won’t even notice you’re wearing them.

Comfortable Fit

Your aligners, which apply gentle force to shift your teeth into position, are wire-free and bracket-free. Therefore, the aligners fit over your teeth smoothly without causing discomfort.

Removable and Simple to Clean

You can take out your aligners before meals and easily clean them. It helps you keep your mouth clean and not restrict your diet.

Clean and Easy Aftercare

Caring for your aligners is simple. Clean them daily to keep them clear and odour-free for a fresh and confident smile.
