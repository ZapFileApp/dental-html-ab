Convenient, Trusted Dental Care — Right on Campus

As the preferred dental provider for students at the University of Calgary, Dental Choice makes it easy to prioritize your oral health while balancing classes, exams, and student life.

Conveniently located on the second floor of the McEwan Student Centre, our clinic is just steps from classrooms and campus facilities. We accommodate and accept insurance plans for students, staff, and faculty, making dental care accessible for the entire campus community.

Whether you need a routine check-up, emergency care, or ongoing treatment, our team is here to provide efficient, comprehensive care tailored to your schedule.

Frequently Asked
Questions

Do you accept student insurance plans?

Yes! We accept a variety of student insurance plans, including those offered through the University of Calgary. If you’re unsure whether your plan is covered, our team is happy to verify your benefits before your appointment.

Where are you located on campus?
We are conveniently located on campus on the third floor of MacEwan Students Centre. Look for us one floor above the food court and beside the medical centre.
Do you follow the provincial fee guide?

Yes, at University of Calgary Dental Choice, our clinic adheres to the Alberta Dental Fee Guide, updated each year on January 1st by the Alberta Dental Association and College. This guide sets recommended fees for dental services, ensuring consistency and transparency in care. It is also commonly used by insurance providers to determine coverage, making it a valuable resource for both our patients and their dental benefits.

How should I prepare for my first appointment?

For your first appointment with our NW Calgary dentist, please bring your health card, insurance details, and any recent dental records or X-rays. We also suggest arriving 5–10 minutes early to complete check-in and any necessary forms.
