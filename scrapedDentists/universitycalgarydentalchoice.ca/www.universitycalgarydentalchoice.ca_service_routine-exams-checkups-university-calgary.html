What are Routine Dental Exams & Checkups?

Regular dental checkups and exams near you help in assessing your oral health comprehensively. After evaluating your teeth, mouth, and gums, the dentist may suggest some digital X-rays, an Oral Cancer Screening, and a deep cleaning.

Frequent dental check-ups will recognize the changes and/or issues sooner rather than later and promote healthy, strong teeth.

The Importance of Dental Check-ups and Exams

Regular dental checkups could find cavities and gum disease early on. At University Calgary Dental Choice, our dentist near you will review your risk factors, give customized advice, and suggest preventive care for healthy teeth.

What to Expect at Your Visit

A routine check-up and dental exam will include:

Comprehensive Oral Exam

Your dentist will check your teeth, gums, jaw and bite for problems.

Digital X-rays

They will assist in identifying any cavities, bone loss or other concerns that can’t be seen.

Professional Cleaning

Removal of tartar and plaque helps reduce decay and gum disease.

Oral Health Recommendations

We often provide specific advice for brushing, flossing and keeping your smile healthy.

You will have a safe and comfortable experience. As part of the experience, you will receive feedback from a trusted dentist in University Calgary to help maintain your oral health.

Benefits of Regular Checkups

Detect cavities, gum disease, and oral health issues early.

Prevent serious dental problems and avoid costly treatments.

Maintain fresh breath and healthy gums.

Receive personalised oral care advice.

Having a regular dental exam & checkup is essential preventive care for your smile, whether you are due for a cleaning or want to keep your smile healthy.

frequently asked questions

How often should I schedule a dental exam?

Most patients benefit from going for dental exams and checkups every six months. Although your dentist might recommend a different schedule.

What happens during a dental exam?

Your dentist will look at your gums and the rest of your mouth. They may also take digital X-rays. After that, they will clean your teeth. These exams can also be personalised for specific needs.

Are dental exams painful?

Dental exams are simple and gentle, with any cleaning or minor procedures performed carefully to ensure your comfort.

Can routine checkups prevent serious dental issues?

Yes. Dental checkups help spot dental cavities and gum diseases, as well as other dental or oral problems that can be fixed easily.
