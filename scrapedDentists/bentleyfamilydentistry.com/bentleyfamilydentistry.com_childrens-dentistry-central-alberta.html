Children’s Dentistry for Central Alberta Families

Gentle, friendly dental care—for the smallest (and wiggliest) smiles in your family.

At Bentley Family Dentistry, we understand kids. From toddlers to teens, we help children feel comfortable, confident, and even excited about visiting the dentist. Families from Sylvan Lake, Rimbey, Lacombe, and all across Central Alberta choose our clinic because we offer something special: advanced care with a personal, small-town touch.

We treat your kids the way we’d treat our own—because in a community like ours, that’s just how it’s done.

Positive First Visits = Lifelong Healthy Habits

We believe that early dental visits should be full of smiles—not stress. Our warm, kid-friendly approach helps children build healthy habits and positive associations with dental care from the very start.

From cleanings and fluoride treatments to cavity prevention and education, we provide everything young smiles need to grow strong. Whether you’re from Eckville, Ponoka, Sunbreaker Cove, or Benalto, we’re proud to be the go-to children’s dental office for families across Central Alberta.

Kid-Sized Comfort. Parent-Sized Peace of Mind.

You won’t find white coats and scary tools here. What you will find is a welcoming team, a gentle touch, and a genuine interest in your child’s well-being.

We know that parents from Rosedale Valley, Gilby, Half Moon Bay, and beyond want the best for their kids—and we deliver. Our tech is up to date, our staff is highly trained, and our chairs are comfy (with cartoons on standby).

Just a Short Drive for Care That Feels Close to Home

Whether you’re in Rimbey, Sylvan Lake, Lacombe, or one of the many beautiful communities around Bentley, you’re not far from a dental team that truly gets kids. With our “Small Town Heart – Big City Care” philosophy, we bring advanced children’s dentistry to your backyard.

Call us today to schedule your child’s visit—we promise it’ll be more fun than they expect (and maybe even more fun than you expect too).

A Healthy Start Today Builds a Healthier Smile Tomorrow

Early care isn’t just about baby teeth—it’s about setting your child up for lifelong oral health.

At Bentley Family Dentistry, we go beyond cleanings and checkups. We help children from across Central Alberta—Sylvan Lake, Rimbey, Ponoka, Lacombe, and smaller communities like Eckville and Gilby—start strong with personalized care that grows with them.

Here’s how we help shape healthy habits and healthy mouths from the very beginning:

  • First Dental Visit (Age 1): We recommend that children have their first dental visit by their first birthday. These early visits are quick, comfortable, and focused on education and prevention.

  • Habit Correction: Thumb sucking, pacifier use, and mouth breathing can affect tooth and jaw development. We gently guide families through solutions that protect proper growth.

  • Guided Growth & Jaw Development: We monitor your child’s oral development closely, identifying any red flags early and intervening before they turn into bigger issues.

  • Phase I Orthopedics: In certain cases, early orthodontic care (before all adult teeth arrive) can help create space, prevent crowding, and support balanced facial development.

  • Palatal Expansion: For kids with narrow upper jaws or breathing issues, we offer conservative palatal development strategies that promote a healthy airway and better alignment—often avoiding the need for braces later.

Many families make the trip to Bentley because they know we’re looking at the whole child, not just their teeth. With high-tech tools and a heart-centered approach, we’re helping Central Alberta kids grow into confident, healthy adults—one small smile at a time. Make an appointment today!
