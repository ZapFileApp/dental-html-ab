Treatment & Surgery

Orthodontics

Orthodontics focuses on straightening teeth and improving how your bite fits together. Proper alignment not only enhances your smile but also supports better oral health and function over time.

‍

At McConachie Dental, we offer comfortable and modern orthodontic options to fit your lifestyle. Whether you are considering treatment for your child or yourself, we take the time to explain your options clearly and create a plan that works for you.

‍

Straighter teeth can make everyday tasks like brushing and chewing easier, while also boosting your confidence. Our goal is to help you achieve a balanced, natural-looking smile in a way that feels simple and manageable.

‍

Is Orthodontic Treatment Right for You?

‍

Orthodontic care can help with crowded teeth, gaps, or bite concerns such as overbite or underbite. If you are unsure, we can assess your smile and guide you through your options with clear, honest advice.

Straighten Your Smile with Comfortable Options

Treatments We Offer:

Orthodontics

Clear Aligners

Clear aligners are a discreet way to straighten your teeth using a series of custom-made trays.

More Details

They are nearly invisible and can be removed for eating, brushing, and flossing.

‍

This option is popular for patients who want a flexible and comfortable alternative to traditional braces. Treatment is designed to fit your lifestyle while gradually improving your smile.

Invisalign®

Invisalign® uses clear, removable aligners to gently move your teeth into place over time.

More Details

Each set of aligners is custom-made for a precise and comfortable fit.

‍

Invisalign® is a convenient option for both teens and adults who want a more subtle way to straighten their smile. It allows you to maintain your daily routine with minimal disruption.

Spark®

Spark® is an advanced clear aligner system that uses digital planning for accurate and efficient results.

More Details

Your treatment is carefully mapped out to guide each stage of tooth movement.

‍

This technology allows for a more personalized approach and can help reduce treatment time in some cases. It's a great option for patients looking for a modern and precise orthodontic solution.
