ABOUT US

Welcome to Diamond Smiles!

We are proud to offer comprehensive dentistry to Black Diamond, Turner Valley and surrounding communities to meet all your functional and aesthetic needs. Our team of dental professionals are dedicated to consistently exceptional care in every experience in our clinic.

Your comfort and ease matter to us. We are passionate about providing you with a peaceful environment where you feel genuinely cared for. From our family to yours — we look forward to seeing you in the clinic soon and getting to know you.

MEET THE DOCTOR

Dr. Chase

Dr. Chase Leavitt received his Doctorate of Dental Surgery from The Ohio State University, home of the Buckeyes. Dr. Chase grew up on a dairy farm in a small town in Southern Alberta and has always had a dream of building a clinic in a tight-knit community like Diamond Valley.

Dr. Chase has advanced training in endodontics, implantology, Invisalign and cosmetic dentistry. He strives for high quality work with every patient he sees and utilizes evidence-based dentistry in all the work he does. Chase and his wife Brooke love spending time outside snowboarding hiking and exploring Kananaskis and Banff with their two daughters.
