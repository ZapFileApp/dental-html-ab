Invisalign Clear Aligners

Our dentists at Karo Dental offer clear aligners to address a range of orthodontic issues. Request a 30-minute consultation today to see if Invisalign is right for you.

Request Consultation

Straighten Your Smile With Invisalign

Invisalign is designed to correct orthodontic issues without metal brackets and wires, providing patients with a low-profile orthodontic treatment option. Plus, with our digital scanner and chairside monitors, we can show you virtual results before your treatment begins.

Invisalign treatment includes the following features:

  • No wires rubbing on the soft tissues of your mouth
  • Can be used by teens and adults
  • Clear and removable, they can easily fit your lifestyle
  • Fewer appointments may be required

What To Expect With Invisalign at Karo Dental

For more information, browse our Invisalign FAQs.

Initial Consultation

Request an appointment with our dentists to learn more about Invisalign clear aligners and whether they might be right for you.

Digital Smile Preview

We will use our 3D scanner technology to provide you with a digital preview of what your smile may look like once your treatment is finished.

Custom Treatment Plan

Your dentist will work with you to create a custom Invisalign treatment plan that is suitable to your orthodontic needs and smile goals.

Fewer Appointments 

Because there are no tightening appointments, you may spend less time in our office. However, we will still see you for routine check-ins.

Straighter Smiles, Healthier Sleep.

If you want to straighten your smile but don't like the look of traditional braces, Invisalign clear aligners may be right for you. But did you know that Invisalign does more than straighten your teeth?

With Invisalign, your dentist can help address a number of orthodontic issues. Without the appropriate treatment, these issues may lead to conditions like sleep apnea and teeth grinding (bruxism), which can affect your quality of life and potentially cause complications.

Complete our 30-second Smile Assessment to see if Invisalign might be right for you.

The Invisalign Process

The team at Karo Dental will guide you through the Invisalign treatment process.

Step 1

Discuss Your Goals

One of our dentists will meet with you to review your dental history and discuss how Invisalign can help you achieve your goals.

Step 2

Get Your 3D Treatment Plan

We’ll use an intraoral scanner to produce a detailed 3D image of your teeth and show you a preview of your straighter smile.

Step 3

Fitting Your Aligners

Our dental team will reach out to you as soon as your aligners are ready. We will schedule an appointment to fit your aligners and place any necessary attachments.
