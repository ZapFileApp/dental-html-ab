Creative Smiles

At Creative Smiles Dental Clinic our mission is to empower our patients with the confidence to express their joy through a healthy and radiant smile. Dr. Emmanuel Enguito and Dr. Rekha Parajuli lead our dedicated team in providing exceptional dental care that transforms lives.

We are committed to delivering the highest standard of dental care by staying at the forefront of advancements in dentistry, using state-of-the-art technology, and employing evidence-based practices. We understand that each patient is unique, and we take the time to listen, educate, and tailor treatment plans to meet individual needs and goals, fostering trust and comfort.

Services

What We Do

Explore Our Comprehensive Services: From routine check-ups to advanced treatments, our practice offers a wide range of dental services tailored to meet your unique needs and ensure your oral health is at its best.

All Services

Crowns and Bridges

Renew Your Smile’s Beauty and Function: Our crowns and bridges are expertly designed to restore damaged or missing teeth, enhancing your overall dental well-being.

 Root Canal

Relieve Tooth Pain Safely and Comfortably: Our skilled professionals provide efficient and pain-free root canal treatments to preserve your natural teeth.

Wisdom Teeth

Expert Wisdom Teeth Extraction: Rely on our experienced team to safely remove troublesome wisdom teeth, ensuring minimal discomfort and a smooth recovery.

Kids Dentistry

Gentle Care for Young Smiles: Our dedicated pediatric team creates a warm and friendly environment, making children’s dental visits a positive and educational experience.

Dentures

Restore Your Smile’s Confidence: Our custom-crafted dentures offer a comfortable and natural-looking solution for patients seeking full or partial tooth replacement.

Cleaning and Prevention

Maintain a Healthy Smile: Our preventive care services, including regular cleanings and check-ups, are your first line of defense against dental issues, helping you achieve long-term oral health.
