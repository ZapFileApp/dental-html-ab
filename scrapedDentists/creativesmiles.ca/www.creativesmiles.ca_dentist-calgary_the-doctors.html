The Doctors

Caring and Personalized Dentistry in Calgary

Creative Smiles

At Creative Smiles Dental Clinic our mission is to empower our patients with the confidence to express their joy through a healthy and radiant smile. Dr. Emmanuel Enguito and Dr. Rekha Parajuli leads our dedicated team in providing exceptional dental care that transforms lives.

We are committed to delivering the highest standard of dental care by staying at the forefront of advancements in dentistry, using state-of-the-art technology, and employing evidence-based practices. We understand that each patient is unique, and we take the time to listen, educate, and tailor treatment plans to meet individual needs and goals, fostering trust and comfort.

Our warm and welcoming environment ensures that every patient is treated with kindness, empathy, and respect. We believe in the power of preventive dentistry and are dedicated to educating our patients on oral health, providing the tools and knowledge necessary to maintain a lifetime of healthy smiles. Which is why our patients have continued to trust us with their oral health for many years.

Join us on a journey towards a healthier, brighter, and more confident smile. At Creative Smiles Dental Clinic, your smile is our masterpiece, and your well-being is our priority.

With warm regards, Dr. Emmanuel Enguito and Dr. Rekha Parajuli
