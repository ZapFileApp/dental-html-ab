FAQ

What services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of dental services, including preventive care, cosmetic treatments, restorative dentistry, and orthodontics. Whether you need a routine cleaning or a complete smile makeover, we have you covered. Book an Appointment

How often should I schedule dental check-ups?

We recommend scheduling dental check-ups every six months to maintain optimal oral health. Regular visits allow us to catch potential issues early and keep your smile bright.

Are dental procedures painful?

Our team at cUsmile Dental Care Centre prioritizes your comfort. We use advanced techniques and sedation options to ensure that your experience is as pain-free as possible.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including major credit cards, cash, and insurance plans. Our team can assist you in understanding your options and maximizing your benefits.

How can I get started with cUSmile?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us to schedule your first appointment. We look forward to welcoming you to our practice and helping you achieve a healthy, beautiful smile! If you have more questions or need assistance, please don’t hesitate to contact us at (403)-263-1124 or email us at info@cusmile.ca We’re here to support your dental journey!
