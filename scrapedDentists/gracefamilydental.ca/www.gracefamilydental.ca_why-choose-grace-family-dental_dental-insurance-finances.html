Dental Insurance & Finances

We offer Direct Insurance Billing as well as Financing!

At Grace Family Dental, we understand that dental insurance & finances can be a complex subject. While it is not possible for us to know every detail, limit, or exclusion involved in all the plans we deal with, we will try our best to assist you in navigating your insurance coverage to the best of our ability.

Our office prides itself on providing a very high standard of dental care at a very fair price. If you have any questions about dental insurance & finances, please do not hesitate to contact us at (403) 980-7555

To guarantee accuracy when dealing with your insurance company, it is very important that you bring your dental insurance information with you. Please remember that most dental insurance plans are intended to cover some, but not all, of the costs associated with your dental care. Reimbursement amounts are not and never have been a guideline for quality of care. We are happy to help you understand your insurance plans, but please recognize that it is your responsibility to provide us with up-to-date insurance information.

We will submit your information to your insurance company for you. However, full payment for services rendered is due at the time of service. We offer several payment options for your convenience:

Cash
Credit Card: Visa, or MasterCard
Interac Direct Payment
Direct Billing (Assignment)

Grace Family Dental accepts almost all insurance plans, and we are pleased to offer easy, direct billing to your dental insurance company. We will submit your claim directly to your insurance company (if they support this service), so you have less out of pocket expenses and can be reimbursed faster. Please note that reimbursement amounts are not a guideline for quality of care. Not all dental insurance plans are the same, which makes it vital for you to understand your specific plan details before your appointment. Most dental plans cover some, but not all, of the costs associated with your dental care.

Here at Grace Family Dental, we strive to help our patients maximize their dental insurance benefits. We ask our patients to please bring along a copy of their insurance plan details for us to help you get the most out of your dental insurance plan.

If you have any questions about Direct Billing or your insurance coverage, please contact us today at (403) 980-7555 for more information about our insurance billing policy and procedures.

We Offer Payment Plans & Financing for Major Restorative Work 

Do you need dental treatment but cannot afford it? Grace Family Dental is pleased to offer our in-office payment plans for major treatment work. Please ask our team about this option at your next visit.

Call Grace Family Dental today at (403) 980-7555 if you have any questions about any of our insurance billing policies and procedures
