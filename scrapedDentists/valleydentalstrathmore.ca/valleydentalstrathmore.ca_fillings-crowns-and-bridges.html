Professional Restorative Dental Treatments in Strathmore

As a child, having a smile with missing teeth can be an exciting time as you go through your rite of passage. But that same toothless smile as an adult can bring on feelings of embarrassment and insecurities. To help you smile more confidently, Valley Dental Clinic is pleased to offer restorative dental treatments in Strathmore and surrounding areas. Whether you’ve lost teeth due to an accident or disease, we can provide you with a dental prosthetic that flawlessly mimics the look and function of natural teeth. After evaluating the overall health of your teeth, gums, and jaw bone structure, your dentist might suggest:

  • Fillings
    if any of your teeth are showing signs of decay in the form of cavities, we can provide fillings to prevent the cavities from getting larger and causing more pain.
  • Crowns
    We can outfit you with a cap to help restore the look and function of any teeth that have been damaged or weakened to increase strength and aesthetic.
  • Bridges
    held in place by your teeth or by dental implants, bridges are a form of dental restoration for clients who are missing one or more teeth.

Tooth Fillings

Dental fillings are used to remove tooth decay from cavities. Our team of oral health experts provides your vulnerable teeth with aesthetic fillings that are made from the highest quality and durable materials. We are here with the most up-to-date dental filling technology to help you. You can schedule an appointment with us by calling us or writing to us.


With our fillings, you won’t even notice you ever had any cavities. Moreover, dental fillings will help improve the aesthetic beauty of your teeth. Our dental fillings are made of composite resin that looks more natural yet remains as durable and as strong as ever!3h

Benefits of Tooth Fillings

  • Help with chipped teeth
  • Help with cracked or broken teeth
  • Close space between two teeth
  • Help with decayed teeth3.

Dental Crowns

A dental crown is a cap that is used to restore a damaged tooth. If you have cracked, chipped or broken teeth, crowns can also be used to improve the appearance of your smile. Crowns can be made of different materials; they can be:

  • All-metal
  • Ceramic-on-metal
  • All-ceramic

Your dentist will suggest the crown material to you depending on the location of the crown in the teeth. For example, it is recommended to use all-ceramic crowns for the front teeth as they look more natural. Ceramic-on-metal crowns also look natural and also have the durability that only metal can provide.

It usually takes two sittings to fit a crown. On your first visit, your dentist will remove the decay, shape the tooth, take measurements, and fit it with a temporary crown of either plastic or metal.

On the subsequent visit, your temporary crown will be removed and replaced with the permanent crown. We will cement the crown into place so that you have a new beautiful looking tooth.

Benefits of Dental Crowns

To help keep your teeth and gums as healthy as possible between appointments, we’re pleased to provide the following dentist-recommended products for purchase:

  • Reinforce the strength of the tooth
  • Looks natural
  • Supports misshapen teeth
  • Makes your smile better

Dental Bridges

At Valley Dental Clinic, we offer our clients dental bridges if they want to replace a tooth without wearing dentures or undergoing surgery. The new tooth used can be of ceramic or porcelain material to match your teeth. Our restorative dental services will make sure that your new teeth look and feel amazing.


Dental bridges are durable and last many years. However, you may need replacement or dental services because of normal wear.

Benefits of Dental Bridges

  • Fill the spaces of missing teeth
  • Prevent remaining teeth from drifting out of position
  • Restore your smile
  • Maintain facial shape
  • Restore chewing and speaking ability
  • Upgrade from a removable partial denture to a permanent dental appliance
