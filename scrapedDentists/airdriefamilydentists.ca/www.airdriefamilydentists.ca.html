    FAQs

    Are you accepting new patients?

    Yes! We welcome patients of all ages, from children to seniors. We focus on building long-term relationships with every person who walks through our doors.

    Do you accept emergency patients?

    Yes, we do. Our team is here to provide emergency services to help you when you need urgent dental care.

    How do I prepare for my first appointment?

    To get ready, you can request an appointment through our website. Since we focus on “personalized care,” it’s helpful to think about your dental goals—whether you want a routine checkup or are interested in specific treatments like whitening or veneers.

    What dental services do you offer?

    We offer a wide range of services to cover all your needs, including:

    • General Care: Routine dental checkups and family dentistry.
    • Restorations: Dental crowns, composite (tooth-colored) fillings, and implants.
    • Cosmetic Care: Teeth whitening and veneers to help you get a beautiful smile.
    • Advanced Care: Oral surgery and full mouth rehabilitation.
    How often should I visit the dentist in Airdrie, AB?

    For most people, it is ideal to come in for “routine checkups” to maintain a healthy smile and prevent future problems. (Usually, dentists recommend a visit every six months).

    Can I switch my dentist to your dental clinic easily?

    Absolutely! We strive to make every visit stress-free and positive. Our modern clinic and friendly team make the transition smooth, ensuring your family’s oral health is in good hands from day one.
