Dr. Kuen Chow, Dentist

Since semi-retiring in October of 2024 after 37 years of practicing dentistry my life has evolved into more of a “giving back” philosophy.  Dentistry has been a richly rewarding profession to me. I have had the privilege of meeting so many wonderful patients and staff that have shaped me into the man I am today. Through their loyalty and words of wisdom it has made my life wonderfully fulfilling and I cannot thank my patients and staff enough.
 
My activities now will include continuing short-term missions to utilize the gift God has blessed me with to provide dentistry to the less fortunate in our society.  I will continue, as long as I am able to run a charity called The Children’s Tooth Fairy Foundation that provides toothbrushes to the less fortunate children around the world.  Traveling and exploring the world continues to be one of my passions, as well as a new sport called River surfing. Canadian Adaptive Snowsports Association is a recent addition to my retirement portfolio which helps disabled kids to ski with adaptive equipment like sled skis and quad skis.  

As long as I can continue to provide proficient dental care for specialties such as IV sedation, wisdom teeth extraction and dental implants, I will continue to keep my mind active and strive to help patients with their dental needs.  
 
Thank you all again for your support though out my career.  
Warm regards,  Dr. Kuen Chow D.D.S.

Dr. Vy Le, Dentist

Dr. Le was raised in Winnipeg and obtained her Bachelor of Science and Doctor of Dental Medicine degrees from University of Manitoba. Dr. Le moved to Calgary upon completion of her dental degree in 2016 and now calls Calgary her home. Her bubbly personality and strong communication skills allow her to instantly bond with patients. She is skillful in all aspects of dentistry and always available to answer any questions that you may have. She enjoys attending continuing education courses to ensure that she stays abreast with the latest technologies and techniques.

Dr. Le has many hobbies and maintains a well-balanced lifestyle. She loves hiking, working out, and spending time with her husband and their two boys. Dr. Le also enjoys travelling and learning about different cultures. She has a strong passion for dental mission work and has travelled to rural and remote areas of Vietnam, Uganda, and Tanzania to deliver free dental health to communities in need.

Dr. Kiran Jandu, Dentist

Dr. Kiran Jandu, was born and raised in Calgary, Alberta and completed her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree at the University of Saskatchewan. She has been practicing for over 15 years and enjoys all aspects of dentistry and taking continuing education courses to provide the best care for her patients. She combines corrective dentistry with a holistic approach to oral health, ensuring each patient gains confidence and a healthy and lasting approach to oral care.


Dr. Jandu is a devoted mother and adventurer. She finds joy in spending time with her toddler, cherishing every moment of laughter and discovery. She loves biking, hiking, spending time outdoors and is always up for trying something new.

Dr. Kevin Kotecha, Dentist

Dr. Kotecha or preferably Kevin is an experienced dentist dedicated to providing high-quality care in a comfortable and welcoming environment. Specializing in preventive, restorative, and cosmetic dentistry, Dr. Kotecha takes a personalized
approach to help patients achieve and maintain healthy smiles. Committed to ongoing education, Dr. Kotecha stays up to date with the latest advancements in dental care.

In his free time you can find him out on the trails with his dog or finding recipes to try out.

Dr. Olivia Bolen

Dr Olivia Bolen grew up in Regina SK, where she attended the University of Regina to complete her Bachelor’s of Science while playing for the Cougar Women’s soccer team.

Following that she attended the University of Saskatchewan College of Dentistry, earning her Doctor of Dental Medicine in 2023. Dr Bolen is an enthusiastic and motivated dentist with a passion for creating a positive experience while improving the oral health of her patients. She looks forward to forming relationships with patients in the Sunshine Dental community in order to provide high quality, individualized dental care in all aspects of their oral health needs.

While not in the clinic, Olivia enjoys staying active outdoors, running, playing golf and tennis, reading a good book, and spending quality time with family and friends.
