    Visit Sherwood Park Denture Clinic Ltd. for Custom Denture Solutions

    Are you considering dentures to replace missing teeth? Or perhaps you need custom-fitted mouthguards to protect your teeth against damage from grinding or sports injury. Trust our 30 years of experience at Sherwood Park Denture Clinic Ltd. to provide you with high-quality denture solutions in Sherwood Park, Fort Saskatchewan, Beaumont, and Camrose. We also offer sport guards, night guards, and sleep apnea treatment.


    Connect with us right away if you have a question or concern about your dentures solutions and other services. We’d love to help you. Please call us at (780) 467-9090 during office hours to speak with a member of our team. 

    Convenient Location, Wheelchair Accessible

    Sherwood Park Denture Clinic Ltd. is dedicated to helping our patients feel comfortable and confident in their new dentures. Located in the Broadmoor Business Centre, our clinic offers free parking, and we are wheelchair accessible. We look forward to helping you at our advanced denture facility!
