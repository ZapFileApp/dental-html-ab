Dr. Christopher Leong, M.D., D.D.S.

Dr. Chris Leong is a caring and genuine dentist who values the comfort and education of the patients in his practice. He is known by his patients as gentle and considerate. Many who have been nervous in the past about coming to the dentist have been surprised at how comfortable treatment can be with Dr. Leong. As the father of two young boys, he understands how to work with kids in dentistry, and takes pride in ensuring that children in his care go home with smiles and positive dental experiences. These kinds of patient experiences are what brings excitement about dentistry to Dr. Leong and make it a joy to practice in a family setting.

In his opinion, the best treatment for oral health problems is prevention, however if any concerns arise, Dr. Leong’s philosophy is always to ensure that his patients understand why things are happening and help them come to a decision about the best treatment for their needs. He also values continual learning in order to bring current and comprehensive knowledge of treatment to his patients.

Dr. Leong was born and raised in Calgary, but has been practicing in the Cochrane since 2006. After 3 years of biochemistry at the University of Calgary, he was accepted to medical school at the University of Alberta and completed his Bachelor of Medical Sciences (B.M.Sc.) in 2001 and his Doctor of Medicine (M.D.) with honours in research in 2002. Dr. Leong then went on to complete his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree at the University of Alberta in 2006, earning the Dr. W. Scott Hamilton Scholarship for superior academic achievement and the American Association of Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeons dental student award for outstanding performance in oral surgery.

When not performing dentistry, Dr. Leong values time with his children, wife, and friends. He enjoys playing both the piano and guitar, dabbling in photography, and is an avid foodie. Dr. Leong is also faithfully involved with various ministries at his church as a leader, mentor and teacher.

Toni, RDH

Toni graduated from the University of Alberta Dental Hygiene program with Distinction in 1999 and has practiced in Calgary and Cochrane since moving to Cochrane after graduation. She has taken advanced training in periodontal therapy and has a particular interest in temporomandibular (jaw joint) disorders. Toni is a caring and skilled hygienist who loves to educate her patients about their dental health without giving lectures!! She is thrilled to be working in an environment she loves, with amazing patients and co-workers and tries to maintain a healthy lifestyle by running and competing in triathlons.

Stephanie, RDA II

Stephanie Dyck is a registered dental assistant who graduated with distinction in 2007 from Columbia College in Calgary. She was born and raised in Cochrane and has been lucky enough to work there since graduation. Stephanie always knew she wanted a career in the dental profession since she was a little girl. Her patience, friendliness and knowledge of the profession helps make her a great fit to the office. She loves working with children and has the ability to make them feel comfortable at the dental office. The dedication to continuous learning, caring for the patients best interests and a close team is what attracted Stephanie to Bow View Dental Care. In her free time she loves spending time with her family, friends, her husband and their dog Marley.

Katy, RDA II

Katy is a registered dental assistant who graduated in 2012 from KDM Dental College in Calgary. She was born and raised on a farm outside Lacombe and moved to Cochrane in 2014 with her husband. She really enjoys the small town feel of Cochrane and loves working in town. Katy truly cares about each patient and their specific needs. She is patient, kind, and loves getting to know everyone. She really enjoys working with kids and helping them learn the importance of dental health. In her free time, Katy enjoys motorcycling with her husband, playing volleyball and doing yoga, and hanging out with family and friends.
