Your New Smile in Three Steps

From consultation to your final reveal, the Neer Veneer process at Arbor Dental Group is straightforward, comfortable, and efficient, designed to fit into your life.
Visit 1

Smile Consultation & Digital Scan

We begin with a thorough evaluation of your teeth and a conversation about your smile goals. Using digital imaging, we map your exact tooth structure and give you a preview of your expected outcome before any commitment is made.
No-pressure smile assessment
Digital smile design preview
Custom shade and shape selection
Visit 2

Precision Fabrication

Your measurements and design specifications are sent to a specialist dental lab, where each veneer is individually crafted from high-grade porcelain. The result is a set of veneers that fits your teeth exactly and matches your desired aesthetic.
High-grade, stain-resistant porcelain
Matched to your natural tooth color
Crafted for durability and longevity
Visit 3

Veneer Placement & Reveal

Your custom veneers are bonded to the front surface of your teeth using a strong, tooth-colored adhesive, often with little to no preparation needed. You'll walk out with a smile that looks and feels entirely natural.
Minimal to no enamel reduction
Secure, long-lasting bond
Same-day smile reveal

Are Neer Veneers the Right Smile Solution for You?

Neer Veneers are an excellent option for patients who have healthy teeth and gums and are looking to improve the color, shape, or size of their smile. They're especially well-suited for those seeking a minimally invasive alternative to traditional veneers or lengthy orthodontic treatment.
