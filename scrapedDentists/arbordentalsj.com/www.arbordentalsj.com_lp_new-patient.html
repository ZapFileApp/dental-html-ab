Hassle-Free First Appointment

Your first visit begins with a thorough consultation to discuss your diagnosis and treatment options. A friendly team member will take digital X-rays and intraoral photos as prescribed by Dentists. Next, a comprehensive periodontal (gum) exam will be conducted as part of our detailed evaluation. These images are displayed on a 40-inch TV, allowing our Dentists to review your results with you in real-time. He will then recommend the appropriate dental cleaning and any necessary treatments. If time permits, we can even complete your cleaning during the same visit.

Meet
Dr. Mark Ajlouni

Dr. Mark Ajlouni grew up in San Jose and received his Doctorate at the University of the Pacific School of Dentistry in 1995. He founded Arbor Dental Group with his wife Dr. Connie Kais in 1998. Dr. Ajlouni practices by the principle of minimally invasive dentistry and specializes in cosmetic procedures such as traditional and no-preparation veneers, all porcelain restorations and full-mouth reconstruction. Dr. Ajlouni prides himself in not only offering exceptional dental treatment but also being able to create a professional environment from the moment a patient walks in the door. "Dentistry is not only about providing great dental care, but also offering efficient overall service making your dental visit an easy and pleasant experience."

Meet
Dr. Connie Kais

Dr. Connie Kais was born and raised in San Jose and is a graduate of Presentation High School. She earned a B.S. degree in Biological Sciences from Loyola Marymount University Dr. Connie Kais is a graduate of University of the Pacific School of Dentistry, one of the best in the country. Having practiced dentistry since 1994, she has been through several post graduate courses in different fields of dentistry since receiving her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree.

Meet
Dr. Sara Anwar

Dr. Sara Anwar was born in Egypt and moved to Chicago when she started college. After finishing her education, she decided to move to the golden state to enjoy sunshine and start her career there. She earned her Bachelor's degree in Biology, and Doctorate of Dental Surgery Degree from the University of Illinois at Chicago in 2011 with a recognized award for clinical excellence. As a former Clinical Assistant Professor at UIC-College of dentistry, a clinical instructor at The University of Pacific – School of Dentistry, and a current full-time private practitioner, Dr. Anwar combines her academic background with her clinical skills to provide the best comprehensive dental care to the community.

Meet
Dr. Yerie Nam

Dr. Yerie Nam is a bay area native, born in San Francisco and raised in Hercules, CA. She received her bachelor's in Integrative Biology from the University of California, Berkeley. From there, she went to San Francisco and graduated with honors from the University of the Pacific Arthur A. Dugoni School of Dentistry. She moved to South Bay in 2021 and is excited to learn more about the area! Dr. Nam is dedicated to providing ethical high quality care and takes many continuing education courses to ensure she is up to date with the latest technology and products available in dentistry.
