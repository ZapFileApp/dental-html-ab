Now Hiring Dental Assistant

Apply Today!

Full time RDA! We are looking for another awesome Dental Assistant to join our fantastic clinic. Just check out our reviews online! Salary is $30.00 - $35.00, based on skill and experience. We are a full service dental clinic with a great Hygiene program. We offer all general dentistry as well as surgical treatments (wisdom teeth extractions and dental implants). Surgical experience is also an asset. We offer our team members free parking, extended health care benefits, dental care, uniform allowance and a bonus system. Also, lunch is provided every Saturday!

Hours:

  • Monday and Tuesday: 9a - 4p
  • Wednesday: 9a - 2p
  • Thursday: 2p - 8p
  • Saturday: 9a - 3p (except for all long weekends as the clinic is closed)

This really is a wonderful place to work and we look forward to meeting you!

-Dr. Dennis and Dr. Merry
