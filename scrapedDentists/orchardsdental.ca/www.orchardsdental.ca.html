We Follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide!

The Alberta Dental Fee Guide is an annual suggested fee guide for dentists in Alberta. There are over 1,600 dental codes and code descriptors that are outlined in the guide related to specific elements of dental treatment. The fees and codes used within this guide are what many dental plan carriers will base their plan coverage around. Orchards Dental is proud to be a dental clinic in South Edmonton which follows the Alberta Dental Fee Guide.

Root Canals South Edmonton

When dealing with teeth that are severely damaged or infected a root canal may be the best course of action to save the tooth. Depending on the situation we will try to save a tooth rather than remove it. At Orchards Dental in South Edmonton, we understand patients can feel anxious before a root canal procedure which is why before any procedure our team will make sure you are comfortable. We also offer sedation options like nitrous oxide and oral sedation for those patients who need it. The best way you can avoid having to go through a root canal procedure is by following good dental hygiene and monitoring your oral health.

Dental Implants South Edmonton

When it comes to replacing missing teeth having dental implants is the best as they appear similar to natural teeth and function very similarly to them. Dental implants have other advantages like not being able to get cavities and never needing root canals. With each tooth, you lose the other teeth in your mouth taking additional force with every bite. This results in the rest of your teeth breaking down faster and more susceptible to cracks and fractures. Regardless of if you are missing one tooth or missing many teeth, dental implants help to restore your mouth to full functionality including helping you eat more comfortably and boost your natural smile. Implants have a long-term survivability rate of more than 95%

Invisalign Edmonton
 

Looking for a straighter smile without the hassle of traditional braces? Our Invisalign in Edmonton offers a discreet and comfortable solution to align your teeth effectively. With clear, removable aligners tailored to your unique needs, you can achieve the perfect smile while maintaining your confidence. Contact a local Invisalign provider in Edmonton today to begin your journey to a healthier, more radiant smile!

 Many of our adult patients want to know how they can fix their crooked teeth without the use of traditional metal brackets and wires. At Orchards Dental in South Edmonton, we offer orthodontic treatment with Edmonton Invisalign. Invisalign is a system that uses a series of clear, plastic aligners rather than metal brackets and wires. Invisalign is not just for adults but can be used by teenagers as well. Invisalign helps you keep a positive dental appearance while correcting dental misalignments. Invisalign’s translucent aligners fit into place over your teeth and are worn throughout the day. The benefit of Invisalign is that most people will not even notice them in your mouth.
