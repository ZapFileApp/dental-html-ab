At Orchards Dental, we are dedicated to providing you with the care you need in a comfortable, relaxing atmosphere. The services and treatments we offer at our South Edmonton Dental office allow you to have total dental health – from preventive treatments to restorative and cosmetic care.

Patients of all ages are welcome at Orchards Dental, including children! We love visiting with our patients and watching our youngest patients grow and develop!

Preventive Care

A healthy mouth begins with a foundation of preventive care. Part of this preventive care happens in your home, with your daily hygiene routine. Our dental hygienists are happy to review your care with you and help you find ways to make the most of this important habit.

Every six months, you should return for a professional cleaning and examination. Your cleanings give you a fresh start, and your examinations help us catch problems in their earliest stages so they can be treated promptly. Some patients, for instance those with active gum disease, may need more frequent appointments. We pay close attention to your periodontal health because your gums are a crucial part of both your dental health and your overall health. We’ll help you prevent gum disease or, if it’s already present, bring it under control.

Restorative Treatments

If your smile needs some extra care to be its healthiest, we are happy to help. We offer a range of restorative treatment options, including tooth-coloured fillings, crowns, root canals, and extractions. We also have solutions for missing teeth, including partial dentures, bridges, and dental implants.

Cosmetic Dentistry South Edmonton

Does your smile need a little assistance to look its best? We can help with beautiful porcelain veneers and crowns, cosmetic bonding using the same tooth-coloured composite we use for fillings, and professional teeth whitening treatments.

We also offer orthodontic treatment with the Invisalign system of clear plastic aligners. This system can correct a range of common misalignments. If you’d like to straighten your teeth and improve both the appearance and health of your smile, let us know. Dr. Dennis George Anithottam will examine your teeth and determine whether you are a good candidate for treatment with Invisalign. We can also discuss other minimal tooth movement orthodontic treatments to help improve the alignment and beauty of your smile.
