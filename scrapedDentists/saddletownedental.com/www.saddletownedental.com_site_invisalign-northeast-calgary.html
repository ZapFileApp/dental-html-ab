Invisalign in Calgary NE: A Clear Alternative to Braces

Invisalign works to correct orthodontic issues while providing the appearance of wearing no braces. Plus, with our digital scanner, you can view your virtual results before even starting treatment.

  • No wires or discomfort
  • Available for both teens and adults
  • Clear and removable to fit your lifestyle
  • Fewer appointments

The Invisalign Process

The team at Saddletowne Dental will guide you through the Invisalign treatment process.

Step 1

Discuss Your Goals

One of our dentists will talk to you about how Invisalign can help your smile.

Step 2

Get Your 3D Treatment Plan

We’ll do a 3D scan of your teeth, and show you a preview of your new smile.

Step 3

Pick Up Your Aligners

Your dentist will contact you when your aligners are ready so you can get started.
