The Invisalign Treatment Process

What To Expect During Invisalign Treatment 

Invisalign clear aligners from Saddletowne Dental offer a convenient treatment option for straightening your smile with fewer hassles than traditional braces.

Browse Invisalign FAQS

Step 1

Book Your First
Consultation

We will begin by examining your smile to determine if Invisalign is right for you. Next, we will use a digital scanner to create a 3D image of your smile and what it could like like with Invisalign treatment.

Step 2

Develop a Custom
Treatment Plan

Based on the decisions made in your first appointment, we will map out a precise Invisalign treatment plan with timelines. Your plan will include the exact movement of your teeth that will occur with each set of aligners.

Step 3

Start Your Custom
Treatment Plan

We will produce series of custom Invisalign clear aligners just for you. You will wear them each day, removing them to eat, drink, brush and floss. As your treatment progresses, your aligners will gradually begin to shift your teeth.

Step 4

Switch Your Aligners
Every Two Weeks

Approximately every 2 weeks, you will switch to the next set of clear aligners. You will also have brief checkups with your dental team – every six weeks or so – to review your progress. 

Step 5

Maintain Your
Brand New Smile

It's an exciting day when you reach the end of your treatment! During your final appointment, we will discuss next steps with you, and prescribe retainers to keep your teeth in their new positions.
