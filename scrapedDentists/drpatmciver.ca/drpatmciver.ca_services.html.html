OUR SERVICES INCLUDE

  • Check-Ups & Cleanings - Having regular checkups and a cleaning is an important part of dental health. From toddlers through adulthood.
  • Tooth Coloured Fillings
  • Emergency Dental visits - If you are having a toothache and/or need immediate attention for another dental emergency the staff at City Centre Dental will meet you with compassion and prompt attention.
  • Aesthetic Dental Services - We do crowns, bridges, porcelain veneers and more. When it comes to the importance of your smile we will discuss all options available appropriate for your dental needs. Including composite veneers.
  • Partials and Full Dentures - We perform all procedures for the construction of partial or complete dentures (immediate or standard) along with denture adjustments and/or relines.
  • Root Canal Therapy - We perform this procedure in office and have been for over 30 years.
  • Orthodontics - From braces to retainers we do many different orthodontic procedures that help correct and maintain the positioning of teeth for optimal oral function.
  • Night Guards & Sport Mouth Guards
  • Dental Extractions
