Smile Enhancement in Beltline, Calgary

Smile Enhancement Near You

When you have a bright and beautiful smile, it will make you more confident and make you feel good. At 17th Avenue Dental Choice, we help our patients achieve the smile of their dreams through smile enhancement in Beltline, Calgary. Whether your teeth are stained, crooked, chipped or uneven, we have solutions that can transform your appearance and boost your confidence.

What Is Smile Enhancement?

Smile enhancement is a combination of dental treatments that improve how your teeth look. These treatments are designed to enhance your smile’s shine, straightness and appearance. We use various techniques based on your concern about your smile. Some patients will only need one simple treatment, while others find that combining several procedures will help them achieve their dream smile.

Who Is an Ideal Candidate?

You could be a good candidate for smile enhancement near you, including if you have.

Stained or discoloured teeth that don’t whiten with brushing

Crooked or misaligned teeth that affect your appearance

Chipped, cracked, or broken teeth

Gaps between your teeth

Teeth that look too short or uneven

Old fillings that show when you smile

Most healthy adults and teens can benefit from these treatments. During the consultation, the dentist near you will check your teeth and gums to make sure you’re ready for cosmetic procedures.

What Treatments Are Involved?

We offer several smile enhancement options

Porcelain or Zirconia Veneers: These are thin custom made shells placed on the front of your teeth to cover stains, chips or gaps.

Dental Bonding: A tooth-coloured resin material applied to repair or restore small chips or reshape teeth.

Tooth-Coloured Fillings: These are natural looking fillings that blend with your teeth.

Invisalign Clear Aligner Therapy: Clear plastic trays that straighten your teeth without using metal braces.

Teeth Whitening: A professional treatment that helps in removing the stains or discolorations from teeth and eventually brightening your smile.

Our dentist in Beltline, Calgary, provides or prepares a personalized treatment plan based on the requirements and objectives you have.

Benefits of Smile Enhancement

Improving your smile offers many advantages.

Increased confidence in social and professional situations

Younger, healthier appearance

Straightening your teeth enhances your ability to maintain oral hygiene

Improved bite function with orthodontic treatments

Long-lasting results with proper care

Enhancements with a natural appearance that suit your facial features

The Process

Your smile enhancement in Beltline, Calgary, journey starts with a consultation. We will talk about what you dislike about your current smile and what you would like to change. Our dentist will assess your teeth, take pictures of your teeth and your smile, and may even have you check out some digital imaging of the results.

Next, we’ll create your customized treatment plan. Some procedures can be done in one visit, while others take multiple appointments. We’ll explain each step so you know exactly what to expect.

Once your treatment has been finished, we will provide instructions on how to take care of your new smile and will schedule follow-up visits.

Ready for Your New Smile?

Contact 17th Avenue Dental Choice today at (403) 265-4400 to schedule your smile enhancement near you consultation and start your journey to a confident and beautiful smile.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I eat normally after smile enhancement treatments?

Sure, you can eat them, but you may have to avoid very hard or sticky foods for a few days afterward. To protect your veneers and bonding from damage, you shouldn’t bite anything hard, like ice or pens.

Will my insurance cover smile enhancement?

Your dental insurer considers most cosmetic treatments to be elective procedures and does not cover them. When a treatment improves oral function, partial coverage may apply. For example, if a patient has a broken tooth, fixing that may offer partial coverage. We’ll help you understand your benefits.

Can I get smile enhancement if I have dental issues?

Yes. Before proceeding, we take care of any dental issues.

How do I maintain my smile after enhancement?

To keep your smile healthy and bright, you should brush your teeth at least twice daily and visit the dentist regularly. Avoid intake of staining foods as well as drinks when possible.
