What is Invisalign?

Straighten Your Smile Discreetly
with Invisalign Near You

Invisalign is a modern way to straighten teeth using aligners that are removable and almost invisible as they sit on teeth. Clear aligners are made from a smooth plastic that is specially custom-made and fits over your teeth. The aligners are generally invisible and can be removed while eating, brushing or flossing, unlike the metal brackets.

Invisalign can easily fix lots of common dental problems

Crowded or crooked teeth

Gaps between teeth

Overbites and underbites

Crossbites and open bites

Mild alignment or spacing issues

Benefits of Invisalign Treatment

  • Clear and Hard to See: You can smile with confidence during treatment because a lot of people won’t even notice you’re wearing them. Our dentist near you can make sure the fit is just right.
  • Easy to Remove: These are easily removable for eating, brushing and flossing. Simple to manage daily life
  • Gentle and Comfortable: Not having a wire or bracket means less irritation to the gums and cheeks.
  • Simple to Care For: It is easy to clean your aligners and keep your teeth healthy.
  • Quick and Steady Result: Most people start to see results in 6 to 18 months, depending on the plan.
  • Better for Your Teeth: They are removable, therefore good for your teeth as you maintain oral hygiene during treatment.
