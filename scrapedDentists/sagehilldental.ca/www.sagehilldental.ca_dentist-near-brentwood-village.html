FAQ

How long does a regular dental visit usually take?

Most routine visits, including cleaning and checkups, last between 45 minutes and an hour, depending on your needs.

What if I haven't seen a dentist in a long time?

No worries—many people return after a long break. We start with a gentle checkup and talk through your concerns without judgment.

Are dental X-rays safe?

Yes, modern dental X-rays use very low radiation and help us spot issues early so treatment stays simple.

Will my child be allowed to attend my appointment?

Yes, many parents bring their children along. It helps children feel comfortable and get used to the dental setting.
