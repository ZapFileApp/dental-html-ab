Frequently Asked Questions

What happens when you receive a dental hygiene treatment and checkup at a dental clinic near you?

Frequent dental cleanings and checkups are essential to maintaining your oral health. During your dental cleaning, our hygienist will thoroughly remove the plaque and tartar from your teeth, then polish and floss your teeth. We will provide you with a fluoride treatment to strengthen your teeth. Our dentist will then examine your teeth, gums, tongue, and cheeks for tooth decay, gingivitis, and any other abnormalities.
