Accreditation
Information

Our facility is proudly accredited by both the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Alberta as a Non-Hospital Surgical Facility and the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta as a Dental Surgical Facility—meeting or exceeding all Alberta Health Services standards. We are committed to delivering safe, comfortable, and quality care in a fully certified surgical environment.

Experience Dentistry Differently

At Dental Service Group, our goal is simple: to make dental care comfortable, caring, and stress-free for everyone. Since 1997, we’ve proudly served Edmonton and Northern Alberta, offering personalized care for patients of all ages.

Our experienced team—including dentists, anesthesiologists, nurses, and dental assistants—uses the latest technology to provide safe, high-quality treatment in a welcoming environment.

We understand that dental visits can be stressful or overwhelming, so we offer a full range of sedation options, including IV sedation and general anesthesia, to help you relax and feel at ease. Whether it’s a routine checkup, kids’ care, implants, or surgery, we’re here to make the experience as smooth and supportive as possible.

Our story

Founded on a Vision of Compassionate Care

Founded in 1997 by Dr. Kirk Chambers, Dental Service Group was created with a clear mission: to deliver exceptional dental care in a comfortable, anxiety-free environment. After earning top honors at the University of Alberta and completing a General Practice Residency, Dr. Chambers operated a fly-in dental practice providing dental treatment under general anesthesia for children at High Level Hospital and performed IV sedation dental extractions in multiple northern clinics—a service that continues today.

He later assumed the general anesthetic practice of Dr. Kay Burnham, laying the foundation for Dental Service Group. What began as a five-person team has grown into a skilled group of over 30 dental and medical professionals. Today, our fully accredited facility proudly serves Edmonton and Northern Alberta with the same dedication to patient comfort and clinical excellence that inspired its founding.

Meet Dr. Matthew Cohen

DSG's Medical Director

Dr. Cohen is a board-certified Anesthesiologist through the Royal College of Physician and Surgeons of Canada and joined Dental Service Group in 2001. In 2004 he was appointed DSG Medical Director-overseeing medical staff, developing and implementing policies, and ensuring quality patient care.

In his spare time, he enjoys relaxing with his wife and three children. An avid jiu-jitsu enthusiast, he stays active both on and off the mat. A lifelong music lover, his go-to playlists feature Prince and The Tragically Hip. He’s rarely seen without a book in one hand and a good cup of coffee in the other.

  • Dr. Cohen holds a hospital appointment at the Royal Alexandra Hospital and serves as an Associate Clinical Professor of Anesthesia at the University of Alberta.

  • Graduated University of Saskatchewan Medical School, followed by an Anesthetic Residency at the University of Alberta.
