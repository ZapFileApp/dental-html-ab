Meet Dr. Shahin (Shawn)

Dr. Shahin is a general dentist who received his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from the University of Western Ontario in Canada. Before entering the field of healthcare, Dr. Shahin first earned a Master of Sciences in Geomatics Engineering from the University of Calgary, where he worked as an engineer. However, he eventually pursued his lifelong dream of working in the healthcare industry.

Having Trouble Sleeping?

The Snore Centre is a multidisciplinary clinic offering personalized assessment, diagnosis and treatment for Snoring, Sleep Apnea and a variety of other sleep disordered breathing conditions. With on-site board certified dental physicians and partnerships with ENTs, sleep physicians and other health care professionals, The Snore Centre will provide a truly customized approach to patient care and treatment.
