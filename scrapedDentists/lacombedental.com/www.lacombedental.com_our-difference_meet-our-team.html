Meet Our Team

Home - Our Difference - Meet Our Team

Our knowledgeable and caring dental professionals and administrative staff are happy to meet you and help you get the dental care you need. 

We want to provide exceptional care in a caring environment. So to make you a little more comfortable during your appointment, you can get to know some of our staff below. 

 

Our Dentists

Dr. Paul West, D.D.S.

Dr. West grew up in a small Southern Alberta town. He graduated with a Bachelor of Science from the University of Lethbridge before completing his Dental Degree at the University of Alberta in 2003. After graduating, he and his family moved to Lacombe and he began working at Lacombe Dental Clinic in 2005.

Dr. West is passionate about providing the highest quality care to his patients and treats everyone as he would his family. He enjoys attending courses to keep current with dentistry's latest techniques and materials. He has been a long-time member of the Central Alberta Dental Society. Dr. West is a registered member of the Alberta Dental Association, the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta, and the Canadian Dental Association.

When not at the clinic - he is busy with his 5 kids. He is also a long-time member of the Lacombe Rotary Club. 

His passion over the past few years has been triathlon; he has completed 3 Ironman 70.3 races and other shorter-distance triathlons.  He can be found swimming, biking or running all around Lacombe.
 

Dr. Devon Lynn, D.D.S.

Dr. Devon Lynn was born, raised, and attended college in Red Deer. He completed his first degree at the University of Alberta with a Bachelor of Science. The next adventure would take him and his wife Tiffany across the world to study dentistry in Australia. While living abroad, they welcomed two sons into their lives.

Dr. Lynn values kindness and honesty as well as the delivery of quality dental care. He believes that he can make a difference in patients’ lives by encouraging them to be active in their treatment decision process, and educating them as to how they can maintain a healthy smile for life.

He enjoys spending time with his wife and two sons, rock climbing, snowboarding, and cooking.

Dr. Lynn is a registered member of the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta.

Dr. Travis Lynn

Dr. Travis Lynn grew up in Central Alberta and is proud to return home to serve the Lacombe community. He believes dentistry should feel clear, comfortable, and collaborative. He strives to understand each patient’s goals and concerns to find practical solutions that support both oral health and overall well-being.

Dr. Lynn completed his dental training at the University of Melbourne, graduating with distinction, affording him with a well-rounded foundation in general dentistry. As a member of both the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta and the Alberta Dental Association, he is committed to maintaining high professional standards and practicing evidence-based care. Early in his career, he takes a thoughtful, detail-oriented approach and prioritizes consistency and quality in every patient interaction.

Outside of the clinic you will find him cooking up something new, at the squash courts or out enjoying and photographing nature. 

Dr. Elisabeth Specht, D.D.S.

Dr. Elisabeth Specht is a proud native of Lacombe, growing up on a farm just off Milton Road. She graduated from the University of Alberta with her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree in 2007. She then completed a General Practice Residency at the University of Alberta Hospital. For five years, she happily worked there to treat severely medically compromised patients. Following her residency, she worked in the Northwest Territories for several years. 

Through these experiences, she realized her biggest passion was in dentistry, specifically dental surgery and dental sedation. She also recognized the challenges faced by patients who live in smaller municipalities in accessing dental care; this is why Dr. Specht has partnered with several clinics throughout Alberta to make dental surgery and sedation more accessible.
 
On a more personal level, Dr. Specht loves to spend quality time with her beloved husband, Bernhard, her family and friends. She also enjoys cuddling with her dogs, playing the piano, gardening, and cooking. Travelling is also high on her list of favourite things to do. She has travelled to Colombia, Peru, Nicaragua, Cambodia and Ecuador with the dental charity Kindness in Action to provide dental care to people in remote and under-served areas.
