Family Dentistry

Home - Dentistry - Family Dentistry

Having a dentist your whole family can trust for all your dental needs plays a large part in your oral and overall health. Preventive dental care, both the care you receive at the dentist and the tools you use at home, is important.

We strive to treat everyone as we would our own family. So, whether your children are coming in for their first check-up or you are coming in for a routine cleaning, Lacombe Dental Clinic’s team of friendly and caring dental professionals provides treatment options based on your needs and wants for your smile. We have a complete discussion with you about your concerns, fears, and treatment goals.

We just don’t look at your teeth and bite, we examine your overall oral health to ensure there are no concerns such as airway blockages and oral cancer. And even if you require a more complex dental procedure, we are committed to providing you with the dental services that you need. 

All dentistry performed by a General Dentist.
