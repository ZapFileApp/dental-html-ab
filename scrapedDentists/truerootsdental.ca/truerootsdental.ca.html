Welcome to
True Roots Dental

At True Roots Dental, your top priority is a bright and healthy smile! We are dedicated to providing our patients of all ages with complete, caring, and high-quality dental care. When you require treatment, you can see your dentist regularly to detect problems early and prevent them from becoming bigger problems. It is essential to maintain those visits throughout life as adults.

Our clinic utilizes advanced dental technology and a patient-centered approach to make every dental visit efficient, gentle and tailored to your dental needs. Our solutions safeguard your natural teeth and enhance your smile for overall well-being. From preventive care to full-mouth rehabilitation, we’ve got it all!

Dr. Rahim Ali

Dr. Rahim Ali is a caring dentist with more than 20 years of experience. He got his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Alberta in 2005. Dr. Ali focuses on restorative dentistry like white fillings, crowns, bridges, and implant-retained crowns. He also does root canals and provides Invisalign to straighten teeth.

Dr. James Quon

Dr. James Quon believes that effective communication and empathy are just as important as technical skills in providing excellent dental care. A born and raised Edmontonian, he graduated from the University of Alberta’s School of Dentistry in 2011. Frequently Asked Questions
Should I go to the dentist frequently?

Most patients should have dental check-ups every six months to prevent disease and maintain oral health. Your dentist may tailor a schedule based on your needs.

Are you offering cosmetic treatments?

Absolutely! Our cosmetic services, including teeth whitening, veneers, and smile design, are designed to give you the smile of your dreams.

Can I finance dental work?

We offer flexible payment plans and assist with insurance claims to make your dental care affordable.

Are emergencies being addressed quickly?

Certainly, we prioritize dental emergencies and provide prompt care for pain, injury, or urgent dental issues.

Do you treat children and families?

Yes! We provide gentle, kid-friendly care and family dentistry services to keep every member’s smile healthy.

How can I book an appointment?

Booking is easy online or by phone. Our team will help you find a convenient time for your visit.
