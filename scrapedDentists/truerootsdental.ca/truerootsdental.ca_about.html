About

About Us-
True Roots Dental

At True Roots Dental, every smile has a story behind it. Whether it is your little one’s first visit or you are maintaining a confident smile, we ensure that your experience is comfortable, stress-free, fun, and personalized. Every patient at True Roots Dental is made to feel important from the moment they arrive.

We recognize that visiting the dentist can be intimidating, which is why we strive to create a warm and welcoming environment. Your comfort and satisfaction are our priorities, and we take the time to address your questions and customize our care to your needs.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Gentle Care and Friendly Approach
    We make dental visits comfortable for everyone. Our team treats each patient with kindness, patience, and respect, ensuring even the youngest family members feel at ease.
  • Welcoming Environment
    From the moment you enter, you’ll notice our bright, calm, and inviting atmosphere. We strive to create a space where families can feel relaxed and confident in their care.
  • Family Dentistry for All Ages
    True Roots Dental provides comprehensive care for patients of all ages. From routine checkups to advanced treatments, we make sure your entire family’s dental health is in trusted hands.
  • Accepts the Canadian Dental Care Plan
    We make dental care accessible by accepting the Canadian Dental Care Plan, helping you focus on your health without worrying about coverage.
  • Advanced Technology with Personalized Care
    We use modern dental technology to provide precise care while keeping every treatment personal and tailored to your needs.

Our Comprehensive Dental Services

At True Roots Dental, we offer a full range of dental services to help keep your smile healthy and beautiful:

  • Preventive Care – Maintain oral health with regular checkups and cleanings.
  • Restorative Dentistry – Repair and restore teeth with fillings, crowns, bridges, and dentures.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry – Enhance your smile with whitening, veneers, and smile makeovers.
  • Orthodontics – Straighten teeth comfortably with Invisalign and other options.
  • Implant Dentistry – Replace missing teeth with single implants or implant-supported restorations.
  • Sedation Dentistry – Experience calm, stress-free dental care in a relaxed environment.
  • Pediatric Dentistry – Gentle, caring dentistry tailored for children of all ages.

We focus on treating the whole person—not just the teeth—so you leave with a smile that feels as good as it looks.

Our Patient-Centred Approach

At True Roots Dental, your needs come first. We listen carefully, explain your options clearly, and create personalized treatment plans using the latest technology—all to make your dental care comfortable and stress-free.

Ready to Experience Gentle, Personalized Care?

Your smile starts here. Call True Roots Dental today at (780) 963-4885 to schedule your appointment. We look forward to welcoming you and your family to a new standard of dental care—where every visit leaves you smiling.
