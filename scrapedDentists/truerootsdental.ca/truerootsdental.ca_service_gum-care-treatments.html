Why Gum Health Matters

Gums help hold teeth in place and protect the bone underneath. When gum health is ignored, it may lead to sensitivity, bad breath, or tooth loss over time. Early care helps reduce discomfort and supports long-lasting oral health for children, teens, adults, and seniors.

At True Roots Dental, gum care treatments in Stony Plain, AB, focus on early attention and gentle prevention. Our goal is to help you feel confident about your smile while avoiding future problems through steady, supportive care.

Signs You May Need Gum Care

Some signs are easy to miss, especially when discomfort feels mild at first. Common signs include bleeding gums while brushing, tenderness, swelling, or gums pulling away from the teeth. Ongoing bad breath can also signal gum concerns.

Patients looking for gum care treatments in Stony Plain, AB, often visit us after noticing these changes. Our team listens closely and explains each step clearly, helping you understand what your gums need and why early care matters.

FAQs

How do I know if I need gum care treatment?

You may need gum care if your gums bleed during brushing, feel tender, look swollen, or appear to pull back from your teeth. A dental visit can help confirm what care is needed.

Are gum care treatments painful?

Gum care is designed to feel gentle and comfortable. Our team focuses on calm care and clear communication so patients feel relaxed during treatment.

How often should gum care visits be scheduled?

The timing depends on your gum health and habits. Some patients need regular cleanings, while others require focused care, and we guide you accordingly.

Is gum care important for children?

Yes. Healthy gums support proper tooth growth and long-term oral health. Early care and guidance help children build strong habits as they grow.
