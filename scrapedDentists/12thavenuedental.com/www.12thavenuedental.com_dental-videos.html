New Patients Welcome

"It’s important to us to build caring and understanding relationships with our patients. We want you to feel comfortable and safe so you can ask questions and understand your treatment options. We’re a long-standing family dental practice and we cover all preventative, restorative, and cosmetic needs. We believe in talking about your concerns first, so you feel included in your treatment and confident in the outcome. I look forward to meeting you!" - Dr. Rakochey
