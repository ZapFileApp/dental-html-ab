Meet Our Wetaskiwin Dentist Dr. Rahim Ali, DDS

Dr. Rahim Ali is a dedicated and compassionate dentist with over 20 years of clinical experience. A proud graduate of the University of Alberta, he earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree with honours in 2005. He began his career practicing in British Columbia before returning to Alberta in 2013, where he continues to provide high-quality dental care to patients of all ages.

Dr. Ali has a strong passion for restorative dentistry, including composite (white) fillings, crowns, bridges, and implant-retained crowns. He also enjoys performing root canal treatments and providing Invisalign therapy to help patients achieve healthier, more confident smiles.
