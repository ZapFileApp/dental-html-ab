Dental Services We Offer

General Dentistry:

Includes routine checkups, cleanings, and cavity fillings to maintain oral health. Services also encompass examinations for tooth decay and gum disease, repairs for cracked or broken teeth, and prevention strategies to keep your smile in optimal condition.

Pediatric Dentistry:

Focuses on creating positive, stress-free dental experiences for children from their first tooth onward. Emphasizes early detection of decay, proper hygiene habits, and preventative care to set kids up for a lifetime of healthy oral habits. Learn about our Free Before Three program!

Emergency Dental Care:

Offers prompt treatment for urgent issues such as severe toothaches, swelling, broken teeth, or lost fillings. Extended evening and weekend hours, as well as after-hours contact options, ensure patients receive timely relief and care. Learn more about emergency dental situations.

Dental Implants:

Provides a permanent solution to missing teeth, offering natural-looking replacements that restore appearance and functionality. Dental implants preserve healthy tooth structure and can last for many years with proper maintenance.

Dental Crowns & Caps:

Restores severely damaged or decayed teeth by placing a durable cover (crown or cap) to protect and strengthen the tooth. Common materials include gold, porcelain, and porcelain-fused-to-metal options, chosen based on durability, function, and aesthetics.

Oral Sedation:

Helps patients overcome dental anxiety and discomfort by providing a relaxed, calm experience during treatment. The patient remains awake yet comfortable, making it easier to complete necessary dental work without stress.

Invisalign® & Orthodontics:

Offers a modern, discreet method to straighten teeth using clear, custom-made aligners. Invisalign® helps improve dental alignment without the noticeable appearance or discomfort of traditional braces.

Teeth Whitening:

Addresses tooth discoloration caused by aging, coffee, tobacco, and other factors. Patients receive take-home whitening systems guided by their dentist to achieve a brighter, more confident smile at their own pace.

Wisdom Teeth Removal:

Extracts problematic wisdom teeth that often emerge crooked, crowded, or impacted. Removing these molars prevents damage to adjacent teeth, reduces infection risk, and alleviates pain or discomfort.

Root Canal Treatment (Endodontics):

Removes infected or dead pulp from the tooth root to save the tooth and prevent further infection. Typically a pain-free procedure, it preserves the natural tooth while restoring oral health and function.

Mouth Guards & Night Guards:

Custom-fit devices protect teeth and gums during contact sports or prevent damage from nighttime grinding (bruxism). They offer a comfortable, personalized fit that reduces the risk of injury and related discomfort.

Facial Esthetic Injectables (TMJ/TMD Treatment):

Uses injectables to alleviate TMJ/TMD pain and reduce tension-related issues in the jaw and facial muscles. Patients may also benefit from improved facial aesthetics, helping them achieve a more comfortable and confident smile.
