Meet The Dentist

Dr. Gurshant Grewal

Dr. Gurshant Grewal is a registered dentist who is dedicated to his practice. In 2013, he graduated from Boston University’s Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine. Prior to completing his Doctorate, he obtained a Bachelor of Science with a major in Microbiology at the University of California in Berkeley. As a successful entrepreneur, he is the co-founder of Peak Dental Group, a corporation committed to building new practices and partnering with other medical professionals through Alberta and Ontario.
Learn more about Dr. Gurshant Grewal

Dr. Komal Preet Sandhu

Dr. Komal Preet Sandhu is a dedicated dentist born and raised in India. She completed her Bachelor of Dental Surgery at BJS Dental College and Hospital in Ludhiana, Punjab. After moving to Canada, she pursued further dental education at the University of New England in Maine, USA, where she graduated with a Doctor of Dental Medicine in May 2022, earning a Letter of Commendation for Patient Care.

Learn more about Dr. Sandhu

Dr. Sameet Gill

Dr. Sameet Gill has spent her career asking one question: what does it actually take for a patient to feel genuinely cared for in a dental chair? The answer she has arrived at, over more than a decade of practice across Alberta, has less to do with technology alone and more to do with the person behind it. She practices at Pro Grace Dentistry in Brentwood NW, Calgary, where she brings her DMD from Boston University, her MBA from the University of Toronto’s Rotman School of Management, and a commitment to care that starts the moment a patient walks in.

Learn more about Dr. Sameet Gill
