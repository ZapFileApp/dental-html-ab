Welcome to RockyView Dental Care - Healthy Smiles Start Here!

At Rockyview Dental Care, we provide modern, compassionate dentistry for patients of all ages. Conveniently located at #115 – 1011 Glenmore Trail SW, Calgary, our experienced team of Dr. Lilian Mui, Dr. Genevieve Rosca, Dr. Keelan O'Malley and Dr. Zion Lee has been caring for Calgary families for more than two decades. We focus on creating lasting relationships, offering high-quality care, and helping each patient feel comfortable and confident in their smile.

When you visit our clinic, you will always be welcomed by a friendly team who takes time to listen and make sure you feel supported. Whether you are bringing your child for their first appointment or coming in for ongoing care, we are here to help your family enjoy healthy smiles for life.

A Community-Focused Approach to Family Care

At Rockyview Dental Care, our connection to the community runs deep. For more than 20 years, we have cared for generations of Calgary families, helping children grow up with healthy smiles and supporting parents and grandparents through every stage of care. We believe dentistry is about more than healthy teeth; it is about building trust, forming relationships, and being part of our patients’ lives.

Our team takes time to get to know each person who visits our clinic, understanding their goals, comfort level, and unique needs. Whether you are bringing in your child for their first appointment or visiting for your own care, you can always expect kindness, patience, and thoughtful guidance from a team that treats you like family.
