Welcome to Fort Dental Health Group, located in Fort Saskatchewan, AB

We are a friendly, family dental clinic located in Fort Saskatchewan, happily catering to patients of all ages, offering a wide array of family dental services including comprehensive exams and hygiene appointments, full-mouth restorations, cosmetic dentistry, dentistry for children, preventive care treatments, and everything in between. Meeting the needs of several generations within a family is a unique opportunity that we love – it enables us to really get to know each and every one of our patients.
