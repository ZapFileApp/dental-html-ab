Learn More About Aulona Dental

Welcome to Aulona Dental, a locally owned and independently operated dental clinic proudly serving Edson, Alberta, and nearby communities. As a neighbourhood dental practice with deep roots in the area, we are honoured to care for patients of all ages and to be part of the community we call home.

You can find us at #5, 5601 5th Ave in Edson, conveniently located with free on-site parking, making quality dental care easy to access for individuals and families alike.

A Community-Focused Dental Clinic in Edson

At Aulona Dental, we believe great dental care starts with strong relationships. Having lived and worked in the Edson community for many years, our team understands the importance of trust, consistency, and personalized care.

We take the time to truly get to know our patients, building relationships that support long-term oral health and consistent, personalized care. Whether you are visiting us for a routine checkup or more comprehensive treatment, you can expect a welcoming environment where your comfort, concerns, and long-term oral health are always our priority.

Personalized Dental Care for Every Stage of Life

We offer general and family dentistry for children, adults, and seniors, with services designed to support your oral health at every stage. Our approach is centred on listening carefully, explaining your options clearly, and working with you to create a treatment plan that fits your needs and goals.

From preventive cleanings and examinations to restorative and cosmetic treatments, our experienced team is here to guide you through your care with clarity and compassion.

Modern Dentistry in a Comfortable, Welcoming Space

Our clinic is designed to feel calm, modern, and comfortable. It is a place where you can feel at ease from the moment you arrive. We respect your time, offer flexible scheduling, and are currently accepting new patients.

No referral is required, direct insurance billing is available, and dental emergencies are always welcome.

Proudly Serving Edson and Surrounding Communities

In addition to Edson, we are pleased to serve patients from Ansel, Wolf Creek, Yates, and Bickerdike. We are grateful for the trust our community places in us and look forward to building long-term relationships with our patients.

At Aulona Dental, we are proud to be your neighbourhood dentist. A place where quality care, community connection, and comfort come together.

Your home for lasting smiles.
