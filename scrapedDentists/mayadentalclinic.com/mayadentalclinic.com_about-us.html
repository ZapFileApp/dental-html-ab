What Makes us Different

At Maya Dental, it’s all about you. Our dentists take the time to listen because we care about what you have to say.

Our team helps you understand the treatment plan options available for your case, to allow you to choose the option that works best for you, without judgement or pressure.

Our equipment and facilities are top of the line and the best in the market. We are proud that we only use the latest Sirona dental chair which has an integrated disinfection system and built-in sanitization adapters that guarantees there is no water backflow, cross-contamination and infection.

We are completely transparent in everything. Our open concept design of the clinic allows you to see our tools, disinfecting methods and storage, while at the same time provides you privacy during treatment.

Our Mission

To bring quality, personalized dental care to our patients & free them with a beautiful smile.

 

Our Vision

To remain patient-focused, personalized & transparent when providing services for every dental concern our patients have.

Meet our Dentists

Dr. Donna B. Rivera

Dr. Donna B. Rivera is the proud owner and the head dentist of Maya Dental Clinic. She received her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree from the Centro Escolar University in Manila, Philippines.

She passed the National Dental Examining Board of Canada in 2016, and practiced Dentistry in Vancouver before moving back to Edmonton to open her first clinic, Maya Dental Clinic-South in 2018. And in 2023, she opened her 2nd clinic, the Maya Dental Clinic- West.

With more than 20 years experience in Dentistry, she is able to provide high quality and treatment to her patients.  Dr. Donna’s number one priority is to provide her patients an enjoyable and stress-free dental experience. She strongly believes that effective communication between doctor and patient is key to the success of treatment. As such, she takes time to explain to her patients the treatment options  and procedures available to manage their expectations.

Dr. Donna is passionate about learning. She pursues continuing education here and abroad to further update herself with new technologies and innovations in her field.

 Outside of the clinic, Dr. Donna loves to travel with her husband, Malvin, and their two girls, Maya and Yumi.

Dr. Kristina Belina Lapuz
