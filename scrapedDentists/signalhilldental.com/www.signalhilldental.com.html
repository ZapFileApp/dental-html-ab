Commonly Asked Dental Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have availabilities for same-day emergencies?

Yes – we have daily available slots dedicated for emergencies. Please call our team and we will do our best to either see you or guide you on best procedures.

How to fix a chipped tooth?

Most often a chipped tooth can be easily repaired with a composite dental filling procedure. You and your dentist will discuss the best options to restore your teeth. 

How long do dental implants take?

Dental implants are a relatively involved process which require a number of visits over several months. It involves x-rays, impressions, surgical placement, and healing time. The post and anchor for the tooth may be implanted at the same time or over multiple appointments. Care and surgery will depend on the individual case and affected area.

What to do after a tooth extraction?

It is important to follow the post-extraction recovery procedures outlined by your dentist. He or she may prescribe an antibiotic to prevent the extraction area from becoming infected. To reduce the pain and swelling, your dental provider also may recommend pain medication and the application of ice to the affected area in 10-minute increments.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes, our team would love to meet you. You can book your first appointment online by requesting a call-back, or you can call us to schedule. We will gather your onboarding health history in the office at your first visit.
