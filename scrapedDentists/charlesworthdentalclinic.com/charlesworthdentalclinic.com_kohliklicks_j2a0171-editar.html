About Our Clinic

Charlesworth Dental Clinic is a modern, family-focused dental practice located in the heart of Charlesworth, Edmonton, AB. We proudly serve patients from Charlesworth, Alces, Ellerslie, Laurel, and surrounding communities, providing high-quality, compassionate dental care for patients of all ages. Our mission is to provide compassionate, high-quality dental care in a comfortable and welcoming environment where patients of all ages feel informed, valued, and confident about their oral health. With a team dedicated to excellence and ongoing education, we combine advanced technology, gentle techniques, and personalized treatment plans to ensure every visit is efficient, effective, and stress-free.

We proudly offer a full range of dental services, including General Dentistry, Cosmetic Dentistry, Restorations, Oral Surgery, Pediatric Dentistry, Root Canal Treatment, Sedation, CDCP Dentistry, and Emergency Dentistry. Whether you need routine cleanings, teeth whitening, wisdom tooth extraction, crowns, or urgent care for sudden dental pain, we are here to support your smile and long-term oral wellness. At Charlesworth Dental Clinic, your comfort, safety, and satisfaction are our top priorities — because your smile deserves the very best.

Hassle-Free Direct Billing & Insurance Assistance

At Charlesworth Dental Clinic, we strive to make every visit as convenient as possible by offering direct billing to all major dental insurance providers. Our team takes care of claim submissions and paperwork so you can focus on your oral health without worrying about upfront costs or lengthy forms.
We are also pleased to accept the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP), giving more families access to essential dental services. In addition, we welcome students and newcomers, including refugees, with eligible dental coverage. Our goal is to ensure every patient in our community can enjoy affordable, stress-free, and reliable dental care for a brighter, healthier smile.
