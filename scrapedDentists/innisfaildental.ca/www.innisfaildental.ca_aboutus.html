Why Us

New Patient Special

We love to welcome new patients to our practice! To show our appreciation, we offer a special one-time welcome gift for all new patients ages 16 and over who schedule and complete a new patient exam and cleaning.

Choose Your New Patient Gift:

New patients (over the age of 16) can select one of the following complimentary options:

  •  An Electric Toothbrush (Sonicare or Oral-B)
  •  A Custom Take-Home Whitening Kit (Includes a 20-minute appointment for impressions; trays ready in 7–10 days)

Loyalty Rewards for Our Valued Patients

Already a valued patient? Your continued trust in our care means the world to us! As a token of our gratitude, we  offer you the same special gift as our new patients. All you have to do is successfully refer a friend or family member to our practice. Once they complete their first exam and cleaning, you’ll receive your complimentary new patient gift as our way of saying thanks!

Please note: Whitening may not be suitable for all patients. Our dental team will assess eligibility based on oral health factors such as tooth decay, gum disease, enamel erosion, sensitivity, allergies, pregnancy, breastfeeding, age, and existing dental restorations.

Why Choose Innisfail Family Dental?

Because your visit will be comfortable, convenient, and rewarding. Here’s what you can expect:

  •  Exceptional & Compassionate Dental Care – Our experienced team provides high-quality treatment tailored to your needs, ensuring your comfort every step of the way
  •  Fee Guide Prices – Transparent pricing for all dental services
  •  Direct Billing to Insurance – Don't stress about your coverage—we handle most insurance claims to make payments hassle-free
  •  Fun & Encouraging Environment for Kids – Young patients get to pick a gift from our awesome prize box after every visit! 
  •  Monthly "Star Brushers & Flossers" Draw – Kids ages 3-16 who show great oral hygiene can win a gift card to a local business in our monthly giveaway!
  •  Coffee & Tea Bar – Relax and enjoy a complimentary beverage while you wait

Ongoing Care & Maintenance

To help you maintain a healthy and bright smile, we offer the choice of:

  •  A replacement Oral B/Sonicare brush head at your 6-month cleaning visit OR
  •  Two whitening gel refill syringes for your custom trays at your 6-month visit

We look forward to providing you exceptional dental care!
To schedule an appointment or learn more about this offer, please contact us today.

Personal Consultation

We offer a personal consultation for every new patient to our clinic. We'll take this opportunity to get to know you better, learn about your goals, answer any questions you have about the procedures you choose, and offer advice as to what procedures will give you the results you are looking for.

Dr. Champney and the Innisfail Family Dental team can design a customized treatment plan for you based specifically on your goals and objectives.

Our Philosophy

Our team of Dental Health Professionals understand that preventive oral health is integral to your general health. Our focus is proactive maintenance and restoration of your smile while considering your overall health, in a caring and comfortable atmosphere. The mission of our practice is 'A Mindful Approach to Exceptional Dental Health.'

Meet Our Doctors

 

Dr. Mitch Champney

Dr. Mitch Champney has proudly owned the practice since 2012 and has been caring for patients as a dentist since 2005. Originally from Lethbridge, he earned his Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Lethbridge. During his final semester, he volunteered at a public health clinic and hospital in South Africa, where he developed a strong appreciation for healthcare and dentistry. He went on to earn his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Alberta in 2005.

Dr. Champney enjoys all aspects of general dentistry and is committed to providing compassionate, high-quality care in a welcoming environment. He is especially proud of the dedicated team at the clinic and the excellent care they provide together for patients and families.

Outside of the clinic, Dr. Champney enjoys family ski trips, boating at Gull Lake, mountain biking, and spending time with his family. One of his long-term goals is to travel through Europe with his family.

Dr. Rob Kurio

Originally from Lethbridge, Dr. Rob Kurio developed a strong connection to Central Alberta while playing volleyball in Red Deer. He completed his Bachelor of Science in Biology before earning his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Alberta in 2015.

Dr. Kurio enjoys all aspects of general dentistry and has a particular interest in working with children. He also offers nitrous sedation for both children and adults who may feel anxious about dental treatment and is committed to making every visit as comfortable and positive as possible. His approach to patient care is centered on quality, communication, and helping patients feel at ease throughout their treatment.

Outside of dentistry, Dr. Kurio enjoys golfing, swimming, cheering on the Edmonton Oilers, and spending time in the mountains with his family.

Dr. Travis Lynn

Dr. Travis Lynn grew up in Central Alberta and is proud to be serving the community he calls home. He believes dentistry should feel clear, comfortable, and collaborative, and takes time to understand each patient’s goals and concerns in order to provide practical solutions that support both oral health and overall well-being.

Dr. Lynn completed his dental training at the University of Melbourne, graduating with distinction and building a strong foundation in general dentistry. He is committed to providing thoughtful, detail-oriented, evidence-based care and values consistency and quality in every patient interaction.

Outside of the clinic, Dr. Lynn enjoys cooking, playing squash, and spending time outdoors enjoying and photographing nature. A fun fact about Dr. Lynn—he is left-handed, which patients may notice during their visits.
