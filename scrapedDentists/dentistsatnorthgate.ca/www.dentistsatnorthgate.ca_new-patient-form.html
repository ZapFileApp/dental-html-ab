For Our New Dental Patients in Edmonton

As a new patient at The Dentists at Northgate, there are a few things you can do beforehand to help make sure your initial appointment runs smoothly. Fill out and complete these patient forms prior to your appointment at our Edmonton clinic and let our clinic manager know that you have filled them out online.

By having them filled out when you sign in at our front desk, you allow us to get you into the dental chair as quickly possible. If you have any questions about the forms, do not hesitate to call during clinic hours.
