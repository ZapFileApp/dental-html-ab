Evan R PerkinsDDDenturist and Owner

Evan has been providing Denture services in the Calgary area for over 15 years. He is a proud member of The Alberta Denturist Association, The College of Alberta Denturists, The Canadian Denturist Association and The National Denturist Association. He is passionate about his profession and enjoys learning new techniques that allow him to provide his patients with the highest quality treatments. When not at work, he enjoys traveling, coaching youth soccer and spending time with his wife and two daughters.

Jennifer PerkinsRDADental Assistant and Owner

Jennifer has been a Registered dental assistant since graduating from SAIT in 2000. She always loved her visits to the dentist as a child and so she decided to pursue a career in the dental field. Jennifer enjoys meeting new patients and helping them smile. In her spare time Jennifer can be found swimming or baking with her two daughters.
