Welcome to Blue Fern Denture and Implant Clinic. A proud Airdrie business since 2017. We strive to provide the highest level of denture care in a compassionate, family atmosphere. Our hand crafted, custom appliances are made in our on site dental lab. Let us help you smile with confidence.

Accepting New Patients

Our full service denture clinic offers a wide range of denture and oral health care services including Complete and Partial Dentures, Dentures over Implants, Repairs, Relines, and Anti Snoring Devices.

We are accepting new members into our family. Contact us today!
