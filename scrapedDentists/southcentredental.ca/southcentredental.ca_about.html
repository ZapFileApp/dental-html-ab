About Southcentre Dental Clinic

Your trusted dental care partner in Calgary. We offer comprehensive dental care under one roof, making it easy to maintain your oral health at every stage of life.

Who We Are

Your trusted dental care partner in Calgary—focused on comfort, quality, and long-term oral health.

At Southcentre Dental Clinic, we believe dentistry should feel comfortable, transparent, and tailored to you.

Our clinic was built on a simple philosophy: treat every patient like family. Whether you’re coming in for a routine checkup or urgent dental care, our goal is to provide a stress-free experience backed by clinical excellence.

We proudly serve individuals and families across Calgary with a full range of dental services—from preventive care to advanced cosmetic treatments—helping you achieve a healthy, confident smile for life.

Our mission is to provide high-quality dental care in a welcoming environment where patients feel confident, informed, and cared for.

Our Approach to Care

We understand that visiting the dentist isn’t always easy. That’s why we focus on creating an environment where you feel informed, relaxed, and genuinely cared for.

Meet Our Caring Dentist

Dr. Arzy Kafrouny earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from Dalhousie University Faculty of Dentistry in 2005 and holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Microbiology and Immunology from the same institution. She has provided care in various dental clinics across Alberta, gaining valuable experience in all areas of general dentistry. Now the owner of Southcentre Dental, Dr. Arzy is creating a welcoming environment where patients feel informed, comfortable, and well cared for. She also maintains certification in both Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) and Basic Life Support (BLS).
Known for her gentle, calming presence, Dr. Arzy focuses on building trust and getting to know each patient. Clear communication and thoughtful guidance are at the heart of her care, ensuring every visit feels manageable and stress-free. Whether it’s a routine checkup or a more complex treatment, Dr. Arzy personalizes each experience to meet individual needs with compassion and clarity.

Dr. Arzy maintains active membership in several respected professional organizations as part of her commitment to ongoing growth. These memberships include the Canadian Dental Association, Alberta Dental Association, College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta, Calgary and District Dental Society, American Academy of Craniofacial Pain, and the Academy of General Dentistry.

Dr. Shiva is a general dentist who loves helping patients feel comfortable, cared for, and at ease, especially those who may feel anxious about dental visits. She graduated from the University of Saskatchewan in 2020 and completed a hospital-based General Practice Residency, where she gained experience treating medically complex cases and developed a gentle, patient-centered approach. She offers a full range of services, including Invisalign, dental implants, and sedation dentistry, and always takes the time to ensure her patients feel relaxed and fully informed.

Outside of the clinic, Shiva enjoys baking, travelling, and learning new things, most recently picking up skiing. She also loves exploring local cafes and brunch spots, and spending time with her family and her toy poodle, Matcha.
