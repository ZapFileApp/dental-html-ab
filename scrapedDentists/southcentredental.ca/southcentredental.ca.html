Cosmetics Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry encompasses a range of cosmetic dental procedures aimed at enhancing the appearance of teeth.

Teeth Whitening

Learn More

At Southcentre Dental Clinic, we use a laser tooth whitening process that is safe to use. Even if your smile only needs to go a few shades lighter, the effects of our ZOOM whitening process will be noticeable.

Veneers

Learn More

Porcelain veneers, sometimes referred to as dental veneers are thin shells of porcelain, designed to match the colour and texture of the rest of your mouth, therefore making them appear just like your natural teeth.

Invisalign

Learn More

Invisalign is the clear and comfortable alternative to metal braces for adults and teens. Some people even call them invisible braces or clear braces! It’s an effective way to bring overcrowded smiles into proper alignment.

Smile Makeovers

Learn More

Our smile makeovers combine cosmetic and restorative procedures to create a natural, beautiful smile you can feel proud of.

Crowns & Bridges

Learn More

We offer crowns and bridges to restore both strength and beauty to your smile. Crowns act like protective caps for damaged teeth, while bridges fill in the gaps left by missing teeth.
