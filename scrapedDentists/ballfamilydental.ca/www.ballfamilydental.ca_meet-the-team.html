Clinical Team

Dr. Leanne Ball, DDS

I graduated from the University of Alberta in 2016 with my DDS degree. I am very passionate about helping people through dentistry! A forever student, I am continuously educating myself and my team in new techniques to provide improved dental services.

Dr. Kolby Olsen, DDS

Dr. Kolby was born and raised in Lacombe, Alberta. He graduated with his DDS degree from the University of Western Ontario in 2016. He takes a special interest in surgery and sedation dentistry. But what he truly loves most about dentistry is resolving pain for his patients. A small town boy at heart, with a love for hunting, fishing, and hiking, he is an excellent fit to our practice and community.

Dr. Rupinder Brar, DDS

Dr. Brar graduated with honors from the University of the Pacific in 2022 with a DDS, having previously earned her BDS in 2012 from Punjab, India, and an MPH from Saskatchewan, Canada, in 2015. She is passionate about creating smiles and promoting community health, engaging with patients on oral health care and staying updated on dental advancements. Dr. Brar seeks to work in a small town to make a meaningful impact. In her free time, she enjoys badminton, cooking, and traveling with her family.

Colleen Devey, RDA

Colleen is a seasoned dental assistant and mom to two great kids. is passionate about her family (including her family pets), the great outdoors, and daily exercise! We love the energy, friendliness, and efficiency Colleen brings to the office!

Kennedy Labreche, Lead RDA

Kennedy is one of our beautiful and talented dental assistants! She is passionate giving people a great encounter at the dentist, challenging the stigma of the dental office being a “scary” place. She loves to see people leave the office with a confident and happy mindset about their experience.

Emily Stauffer, RDH
