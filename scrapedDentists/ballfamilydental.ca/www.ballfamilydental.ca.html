My name is Dr. Leanne Ball.

It all started in 2016 when I graduated from the University of Alberta with my dental degree, married the love of my life, and moved out to his 5th generation family farm in Flagstaff County. We joined forces and made it our mission to open a dental clinic in Killam, AB.

I am passionate about changing the stereotypes in dentistry! A forever student, I am continuously educating myself and my team in new techniques to provide improved dental services. I care deeply about my patients, my team, and my community.

Our mission is to Redefine Dentistry. I want to give you an amazing experience in our dental chair! I want your visit to be impactful. I want to help you be the best version of yourself possible, starting with your smile. Together, my team and I are in the business of Changing Lives.

- Leanne Ball, DDS

Ball Family Dental is a clinic that is dedicated to providing high quality dental care for our clients.  We offer a wide variety of general dental services and will continue to expand these options!  We are challenging the stereotypes; the dental office should not be a cold, scary place that people are afraid of.  Our love for dentistry has inspired us to create a space where we can foster a love for dentistry in our patients and in our community.
