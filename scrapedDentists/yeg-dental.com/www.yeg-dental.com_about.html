    ABOUT  YEG DENTAL

    At YEG Dental, we provide personalized care using the latest technology to meet Edmonton’s dental needs. From routine checkups to urgent care, our team ensures every visit is comfortable and efficient. We’re committed to enhancing your oral health with exceptional service.

    Who We Are

    Your Trusted Dental Team in Northeast Edmonton

    TRUST AND COMMUNICATION

    We believe that a positive dental experience begins with trust and communication. We strive to create a welcoming environment where your concerns and goals are heard and addressed. Whether you require routine check-ups, advanced dental procedures, or cosmetic enhancements, our skilled professionals are dedicated to providing comprehensive care and guidance. This ensures your dental journey is as smooth and comfortable as possible.

    COMPREHENSIVE SERVICES

    Your smile is our passion, and we are devoted to helping you maintain its brilliance and health for years to come. Our comprehensive range of dental services covers all your oral health needs, including:

    ​

    • General Dentistry: Comprehensive dental care for maintaining overall oral health.

    • Preventive Care: Keep your mouth healthy with regular checkups and cleanings.

    • Cosmetic Dentistry: Improve your smile with teeth whitening and veneers.

    • Restorative Treatments: Fix damaged or missing teeth with fillings, crowns, and bridges.
