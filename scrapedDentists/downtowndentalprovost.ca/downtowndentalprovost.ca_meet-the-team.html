Pragti Bimra, BDS, Owner  

Meet Dr. Pragti Bimra – Owner & Heart Behind Downtown Dental

My journey to becoming a dentist has been one of passion, perseverance, and a deep love for people. I earned my dental degree in 2011 in India, where I also spent two years as a junior lecturer, sharing what I had learned with future dentists. In 2013, I made the life-changing decision to move to Canada. It wasn’t easy—but I embraced every challenge head-on and successfully completed my Canadian dental exams in 2016.

In 2019, after years of hard work, I took a leap of faith and became the proud owner of Downtown Dental Provost. What started as a dream slowly took shape—with consistency, heartfelt care, and an unwavering vision. I designed a brand-new clinic from the ground up, and today, it stands as a trusted home for dental care in Provost and the surrounding communities. I’m incredibly grateful for every patient who walks through our doors, every team member who shares this mission, and every community member who has supported this journey.

Giving back is at the core of who I am. Over the years, I’ve volunteered on dental missions in India and the Philippines. Every Friday, I also help University of Alberta dental students serve the community at Radius Community Health & Healing, offering mentorship and support as they provide care to underserved populations. Outside the clinic, I recharge with quiet joys—nurturing my indoor plants, getting lost in inspiring books, or exploring trails with my loyal pup, Bella.

My vision for Downtown Dental is simple: to be a one-stop destination for all your dental needs, so families in and around Provost don’t have to travel far for quality care. With advanced treatments, a compassionate approach, and a team you can trust, I’m proud to provide dental care that’s personal, professional, and made to serve generations.

Dr. Gagan Dosanjh, DDS

My journey in dentistry began in India, where I first discovered my passion for oral health—and especially for the intricate world of root canals. That interest led me to complete a three-year residency in Endodontics, where I deepened my knowledge and refined my skills.

After moving to Canada, I continued my education and earned my DDS degree from the University of Alberta’s School of Dentistry in Edmonton. I’m proud to be a member of the Alberta Dental Association and the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta. I’m always striving to stay current with the latest advancements in the field because I believe my patients deserve the very best care.

While I have a special love for root canal treatments, I enjoy all aspects of general dentistry. What truly motivates me is the chance to connect with patients, build lasting relationships, and help them feel confident in their smiles. For me, great dentistry starts with trust.

Outside the clinic, I love exploring new places, trying different cuisines, and spending time with my family and friends. Whether it’s discovering a new foodie spot or planning my next travel adventure, I’m always looking for new experiences and meaningful moments.
