Welcome To Edgemont Dental

Feel Great About Your Smile – And Your Edmonton Dentist


Edgemont Dental Clinic in Southwest Edmonton is a family-owned and operated practice. Dr. Scott Kershaw and his wife, Dr. Nicole L’Heureux, seek to provide affordable, quality dental care to the families in the community where they grew up and now raise their families. You can often find them running after their children on bikes, frequenting playgrounds and enjoying the many ponds that our beautiful neighbourhood has to offer.


Your oral health and comfort are the top priorities of the entire team here at Edgemont Dental. As you enter the office, our friendly and accommodating staff will greet you and make you feel at home.

Same-Day Emergency Appointments in Edmonton

We’re here for your dental emergency in Edmonton! At Edgemont Dental Clinic, we are experienced at treating all types of emergencies and will address the issue quickly and with as little stress as possible. Dental emergencies can be scary, but the best thing is to stay calm and contact your Edmonton dentist immediately, especially if it involves a child. Our dentists are available six days a week and offer same-day emergency appointments so that you can receive affordable dental care when you need it.
