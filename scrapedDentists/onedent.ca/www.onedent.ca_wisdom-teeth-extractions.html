    Wisdom teeth extractions

    Wisdom teeth extractions

    Wisdom tooth extraction is a procedure that is performed to remove one or more of the wisdom teeth. Wisdom teeth are the third molars and usually erupt in the late teens or early twenties. Many people get their wisdom teeth extracted because they can be difficult to clean and can cause pain and other oral health problems. Our dentists are experienced with handling a variety of wisdom tooth extractions. Give us a call today if you would like a wisdom tooth consult with one of our experienced dentists!
