A South Edmonton Dentist You Can Count On

At Spearmint Dental South, we’re not just your local dental clinic—we’re your long-term partners in oral health. We believe in treating patients with kindness, honesty, and customized care.

Whether you’re new to the area or simply looking for a dental clinic closer to home, you’ll find our location near Heritage Valley to be convenient, modern, and equipped with the latest dental technology to serve your needs.

Full-Service Dental Care in One Convenient Location

We’re proud to offer a wide range of dental treatments to meet your needs—whether you're visiting for prevention, repair, or cosmetic improvements. Every service is delivered with attention to detail and your comfort in mind.

Preventive and General Dentistry

Keep your teeth and gums healthy for life with:

  • Dental exams and cleanings
  • Digital x-rays and diagnostic imaging
  • Oral cancer screenings
  • Sealants and fluoride treatments
  • Children's dentistry

Our general dental services help prevent problems before they start—and keep your smile strong and pain-free.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Want to improve the look of your smile? We offer cosmetic solutions including:

  • Professional teeth whitening
  • Porcelain veneers & Lumineers
  • Tooth reshaping and bonding
  • Smile design consultations

Our cosmetic dental team helps you achieve a confident, natural-looking smile that reflects the best version of you.

Restorative Dentistry

If you’ve experienced tooth damage or loss, we offer:

  • Crowns and bridges
  • Tooth-colored fillings
  • Dental implants
  • Denture fittings and repairs

We restore comfort, chewing ability, and aesthetics with strong, functional restorations.

Invisalign & Orthodontics

Looking for straighter teeth without metal braces? We offer:

  • Invisalign® clear aligners
  • Orthodontic consultations
  • Retainers and bite correction

Our discreet orthodontic solutions are perfect for both teens and adults in South West Edmonton.

Emergency Dental Care

Dental pain or trauma? Call us immediately for:

  • Broken or knocked-out teeth
  • Severe toothaches or abscesses
  • Lost crowns or fillings
  • Emergency exams and x-rays

We offer same-day emergency appointments when possible to get you out of pain quickly.

Sedation Options

For those with dental anxiety or complex procedures, we offer Sedation solutions:

  • Nitrous oxide (laughing gas)
  • Oral sedation
  • Gentle and supportive care

Your comfort matters. We’ll help you relax every step of the way.
