Wrinkle Treatment in Edmonton

We provide rejuvenation treatment that safely reduces facial wrinkles and fine lines. We also provide treatment for clenching teeth and migraines.

How it Works

The treatment involves us making small injections into the facial muscles being treated. The injections are safe and are administered by one of our dentists in our office.

Facial Rejuvenation

The facial rejuvenation treatment works by relaxing your facial muscles, which then reduces the wrinkles or lines around your eyes and mouth.

Clenching

We treat chronic jaw clenching by making injections into your jaw muscles, causing them to relax so you stop clenching your teeth.

Migraines

The injections can provide some patients with relief from migraine symptoms. To learn more about migraine treatment please speak to one of our dentists.

Lip Augmentation

Our lip augmentation treatments can make your lips appear luscious and full. We can design a treatment plan that’s perfect for your facial shape and ideal lip size. The treatment usually lasts 9-12 months and the procedure is adjustable and reversible if needed.
