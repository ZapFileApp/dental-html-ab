Angel Aligner in St. Albert

If you’re looking for a discreet, effective way to straighten your teeth, Angel Aligner offers a modern solution with expert-guided care. At Nuvo Dental in St. Albert, we help patients of all ages achieve beautifully aligned smiles—without the hassle of metal braces.

With over 20 years of science driven innovation behind it, Angel Aligner is trusted by dentists worldwide to deliver high-quality, customized results using clear, nearly invisible aligners.

Why Choose Angel Aligner?

Efficient Tooth Movement

Designed for speed and precision, even in complex cases.

Comfort-Focused Design

Flexible Wear

Easily removable for eating, brushing, or special occasions.

Science-Driven

Backed by over two decades of clinical expertise and trusted by dentists worldwide.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Angel Aligner cost?

Cost varies depending on the complexity of your treatment. We’ll provide a personalized quote during your consultation.

Is it covered by insurance?

Many dental insurance plans include orthodontic benefits that may apply to Angel Aligner. Check with your provider or let us help you review your coverage.

How long is the treatment?

Treatment length depends on your case. Some patients see results in a few months, while more complex cases may take longer.

When and how often should I wear my aligners?

Aligners should be worn for 20–22 hours per day, and only removed for brushing, eating, or drinking anything other than water.

How are Angel Aligners different from traditional braces?

Unlike metal braces, Angel Aligners are removable and nearly invisible, allowing for more comfort, better hygiene, and no food restrictions.

How are Angel Aligners different from Invisalign?

Both Invisalign and Angel Aligners offer a discreet, comfortable way to straighten teeth without traditional braces—but there are some important differences between the two systems. Click here to learn more.

Who can use Angel Aligners?

Angel Aligners are suitable for both teens and adults. Treatment is personalized for each patient’s needs and lifestyle.

What is the first step in the Angel Aligner treatment process?

It starts with a consultation at Nuvo Dental, including a digital scan, x-rays, and a discussion of your goals.

What happens after I complete my treatment?

You’ll be fitted with a custom retainer and continue to visit us for follow-ups to ensure your smile stays aligned.
