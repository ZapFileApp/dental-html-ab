WELCOME TO MIDTOWN

From routine cleanings to complex surgical procedures, Midtown Dental handles it all.

When you visit Midtown Dental, you can expect high-quality dental care delivered in a welcoming, relaxed and comfortable environment. We are guided by the values of prevention, clinical excellence, integrity and genuine care. We focus on providing personalized treatments that support a healthy, confident smile for life.

Dr. Vikas Khanna, Dr. Aaron Liu and our experienced team respect your time and keep our office running on schedule so your appointment fits smoothly into your day.
