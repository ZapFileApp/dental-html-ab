Gum & Tooth Contouring in Okotoks, AB

Some popular cosmetic dental procedures include teeth whitening and straightening. Although the colour and alignment of your teeth affect your smile’s appearance, the shape of your teeth and gums are just as important. Gum and tooth contouring is one way to achieve an enhanced smile!

Gum & Tooth Contouring Near You

If you're looking to change the shape of your teeth, you may want to consider receiving gum and tooth contouring!

Teeth contouring involves polishing your teeth and then re-shaping the tooth into its desired shape and size. Gums also play a vital role in protecting your oral health. The function of your gums is to protect the tooth and interior from exposure and bacteria. The colour, depth, shape, and consistency of gums differ from person to person.

If you are dissatisfied with the shape of your gums, gum contouring may be right for you. You can enhance the look of your gums while still making sure they are as healthy as possible.

The Gum & Tooth Contouring Process

The contouring of gums and teeth in Okotoks, AB, begins with drawing a line on your gums to show you the new profile of your gum line. Then, your gums will be reshaped – contoured – under local anesthesia using scalpels, radiosurgery, and/or laser surgery.

Takeaways About Gum & Tooth Contouring

  • When your dentist performs gum and tooth contouring near you at a dental office, it can enhance the appearance of your smile.
  • Those who have gummy smiles usually opt for gum contouring.
