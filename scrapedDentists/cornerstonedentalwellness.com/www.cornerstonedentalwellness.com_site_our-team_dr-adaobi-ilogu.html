Dr. Adaobi Ilogu is a General Dentist at Cornerstone Dental Wellness in Okotoks

Dr. Ilogu is a patient-focused and dedicated dentist. She is a graduate of the Dr. Gerald Niznick College of Dentistry, University of Manitoba where she obtained her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree. She also holds a Bachelor of Dental Surgery degree from the College of Dentistry, University of Lagos. Dr. Ilogu is skilled at providing all aspects of General dentistry including Wisdom tooth extraction, Invisalign and Implant restorations. She believes strongly in effective patient communication and interaction in developing comprehensive treatment plans that meet individual patient's needs. With her calm demeanor, she strives to provide a comfortable experience for her patients and also offers Oral and Nitrous Sedation as needed.

Dr. Adaobi Ilogu is constantly updating her skills and knowledge in Dentistry by attending a variety of Continuing Education courses to keep up with the latest trends. She is a member of multiple Professional Study Clubs as well as a member of the Alberta Dental Association, College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta, Calgary District Dental Society and the Canadian Dental Association.

Outside of work, Dr. Ilogu enjoys cooking and trying out new recipes, cycling and spending quality time with her husband and three children.

Dr. Ilogu looks forward to meeting you!
