Our Team

Get to Know Us

We‘re happy to see you here!

Dr. Shabih Syed

Dr. Shabih Syed is the proud owner and Lead General Dentist at Clean Smiles Dental, where she has been providing compassionate, high‑quality dental care. With several years of clinical experience, she has built her practice around a simple philosophy: every patient deserves the same level of care, respect, and attention she would give her own family.

She is skilled in a wide range of dental services, including restorative and surgical procedures, pediatric dentistry, endodontic therapy, and comprehensive smile care. She has a special interest in Invisalign clear aligner therapy, Sleep apnea and snoring management using oral appliance therapy, and custom‑fabricated occlusal guards for patients who clench or grind their teeth.

Whether she’s helping a child feel brave in the dental chair or guiding an adult through a transformative treatment plan, Dr. Syed blends modern techniques with a personalized touch — always focused on long‑term health, comfort, and confidence. 

She is honoured to serve the Edmonton community and looks forward to welcoming you to Clean Smiles Dental — where your smile is always in caring hands..

Dr. Afaque Sheikh

With over 16 years of experience, Dr. Afaque Sheikh is dedicated to helping patients achieve healthy, confident smiles through compassionate, high-quality dental care. Known for his gentle approach and exceptional communication, he takes the time to ensure every patient feels comfortable, informed, and at ease, especially those with dental anxiety. Sedation options, including nitrous oxide and oral sedation, help make every visit as stress-free as possible.

Dr. Sheikh earned his Master’s degree in Maxillofacial Prosthetic Rehabilitation from King’s College, providing advanced expertise in complex treatment planning and comprehensive dental care. His philosophy is simple: treat every patient like family while focusing on long-term oral health and overall well-being.

He offers a full range of services under one roof, including dental implants, root canal therapy, surgical extractions, cosmetic dentistry, crowns and bridges, sedation dentistry, and preventive care. Whether you need routine treatment or advanced dental solutions, Dr. Sheikh is committed to delivering personalized care with precision, honesty, and compassion.

Your comfort, your health, and your smile are always our priority.

Anila Irfan

Anila is an exceptional registered Dental Hygienist whose passion for oral health shines through in every patient interaction. She firmly believes that a healthy mouth is the foundation of overall wellness, and she is dedicated to helping patients prevent dental problems before they start. Her focus on education, prevention, and long‑term oral health empowers patients to maintain strong, healthy smiles for life.

With extensive knowledge and experience in dental hygiene, Anila is known for her gentle touch, thorough care, and genuine compassion. Patients appreciate her ability to make every visit comfortable, informative, and personalized — it’s no surprise she’s loved by everyone who meets her.

Her warmth, expertise, and commitment to patient care make her an invaluable part of the Clean Smiles Dental team.

Tammy(RDH,COM)

She is registered dental hygienist and our Orofacial Myologist. Tammy has been practicing dental hygiene for over 10 years and is also a part time clinical instructor at the UofA. She loves learning and is constantly sharing her passion. With Orofacial myology, she is able apply her knowledge and experience as a dental hygienist to help her clients address their Orofacial myofunctional disorders. She is very passionate in helping patients, so they are able to improve the quality of life by improving and maintaining their dental care.

When Tammy is not working she is spending her time with her family, traveling, or trying new restaurants.”

Carla

Our amazing registered dental hygienist.

Carla has years 7 of experience as a dental hygienist. In all these years her passion for dentistry has only grown. She loves to work with a holistic approach, integrating all aspects of her patient’s concerns and their lifestyles to achieve long-life healthy oral health.

In her free time, she loves to rock climb and walk the trails around the river valley. Whenever possible she loves to busy travelling the world

Compassionate Service

At Clean Smiles Dental Clinic in Griesbach, North Edmonton, we believe every smile deserves personalized care. Our experienced and compassionate team provides family, cosmetic, restorative, and emergency dental services in a welcoming, patient-focused environment.
As a trusted dental clinic in Edmonton, Alberta, we offer dental cleanings, crowns, bridges, extractions, laser dentistry, and customized treatment plans designed around your oral health goals. We focus on comfortable, high-quality dental care for patients of all ages.
Using modern technology and evidence-based techniques, our team is committed to helping individuals and families achieve healthy, confident smiles with affordable dental care in Edmonton. Whether you’re visiting for preventive care or advanced treatment, we strive to make every experience calm, supportive, and stress-free.
