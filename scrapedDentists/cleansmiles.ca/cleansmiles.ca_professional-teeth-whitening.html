What Is Professional Teeth Whitening?

Professional teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that aims to improve the appearance of teeth by lightening their colour and can eliminate surface stains. This treatment is performed by a trained dental professional, such as a dentist or an oral hygienist, specializing in teeth whitening services. The process involves the application of a bleaching agent to the teeth, which helps break down and remove yellow stains caused by common factors like coffee, tea, tobacco, and aging. 

It’s important to note that teeth whitening procedures are generally considered safe when performed by trained professionals using approved products and techniques. While some experience temporary tooth sensitivity or gum irritation immediately following treatment, these side effects usually subside within a few days. 

When considering teeth whitening in Edmonton, consult a trusted dental hygienist or dentist. They’ll assess your oral health, discuss your goals, and outline costs based on discolouration and dental conditions. Professional in office teeth whitening offers a safe way to achieve whiter teeth, a brighter smile, and greater self-confidence.

If you live in Edmonton, finding a reliable option for top-notch teeth whitening in Edmonton, it’s just a simple visit away at Clean Smiles Dental Clinic. With proper consultation from a qualified dentist specializing in cosmetic dentistry services like teeth whitening, you can be on your way to enjoying a radiant smile that leaves a lasting impression.

Why Choose Professional Teeth Whitening?

If you want to whiten teeth safely and effectively, professional care is the best choice. While store-bought teeth whitening products and whitening kits may give short-term results, they often fade quickly and can cause problems for people with sensitive teeth.

At Clean Smiles, our dental team provides in office treatment tailored to your needs. With professional whitening, you’ll achieve a brighter, more natural look that lasts longer and helps you maintain a truly confident smile.

Zoom Teeth Whitening

Zoom Teeth Whitening is one of the most popular teeth whitening treatments available today. It is a highly effective and efficient cosmetic options that offers noticeable results leaving you with a new smile in just one session. 

The Zoom Teeth Whitening process utilizes a special gel and a light-activated technology to break down and remove stains and discolouration on the teeth. The whitening gel contains hydrogen peroxide, which acts as a bleaching agent, penetrating the enamel and removing deep-seated stains. 

This treatment is performed by professional dentists or dental technicians who have received specific training in using the Zoom system. One of the reasons why Zoom Teeth Whitening has gained immense popularity is because of its quick results. 

Advantages of Zoom 

Unlike at-home whitening kits that may take weeks or even months to show any noticeable changes, Zoom offers immediate improvements in the whiteness and brightness of your smile. In just a single visit to your trusted teeth whitening specialist, you can achieve a noticeably whiter smile that will leave you feeling more confident than ever before. 

Another advantage of Zoom Whitening treatment is its ability to tackle even the most stubborn stains. Teeth naturally darken from certain foods like coffee, tea, red wine, or tobacco stains, this treatment can effectively remove them and restore your teeth to their natural white shade. 

This makes it an excellent choice for individuals who have struggled with discolouration for years or have tried various teeth whitening services without success. When considering teeth whitening, Edmonton residents are often concerned about the potential harm that such treatments could cause. 

Fortunately, Zoom Teeth Whitening has been extensively tested for safety and efficacy by dental professionals worldwide. Although some people may experience temporary tooth sensitivity during or after the treatment, this usually subsides within a few days. 

Investing in a professional teeth whitening treatment near you is not just about aesthetics but also about boosting your self-confidence and overall oral hygiene. Zoom Teeth Whitening is a highly effective and safe method for achieving a whiter, brighter smile. 

Is Zoom Teeth Whitening Harmful?

Zoom teeth whitening is one of the most popular options to whiten teeth and achieve a brighter smile, and when performed by a qualified dentist, it’s considered both safe and effective. The most common side effect of whitening effects is temporary tooth sensitivity, which usually fades within a few days. Dentists reduce this risk by protecting the gums, using desensitizing agents, and applying the correct hydrogen peroxide concentration for your teeth.

Safety also comes from proper assessment. Before treatment, a dentist will evaluate your oral health to ensure you’re a good candidate and that the procedure won’t affect sensitive teeth or existing dental work. With the right protocols and professional oversight, Zoom is a trusted form of dentistry that delivers fast, noticeable results while keeping your oral hygiene protected.

How Much Is In Office Whitening?

The cost of teeth whitening in Edmonton can vary depending on the specific treatment chosen. Generally teeth whitening services ranges from $200 to $800 or more.

It’s important to keep in mind that this range is just an estimate and can differ based on various factors. The first factor to consider is the reputation and expertise of the dentist providing the service. 

Experienced dentists who specialize in cosmetic dentistry or teeth whitening may charge higher fees due to their advanced knowledge and experience, which leads to better, brighter results. Another factor impacting the cost is the type of teeth whitening treatment chosen. 

 

Treatment Options

There are various treatment options available, including in office teeth whitening treatments such as Zoom teeth whitening or take-home kits provided by your dentist.

In office whitening treatments generally tend to be more expensive as they offer more immediate results under professional supervision, while take-home kits with customized trays, provide a more gradual improvement but at a lower cost and are superior to store-bought teeth whitening system or whitening toothpaste. 

Remember, the cost of teeth whitening is an important factor to consider, but it should not be the sole determining factor.
