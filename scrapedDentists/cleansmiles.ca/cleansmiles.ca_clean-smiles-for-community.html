Clean Smiles For Community

Giving Back to Our Edmonton Community

If you’ve been searching for “a cheap dentist near me,” or “cheap dentist Edmonton” Our Clean Smiles for Community Program is for you!

We know that oral health and dental treatment is vital to overall health, so we created our unique affordability program to ensure that dental services and preventative dental care are within reach for everyone in our North Edmonton community.

Routine dental screenings and professional hygiene treatments play a vital role in identifying and addressing problems early on, thereby minimizing the likelihood of requiring emergency dental care for oral health issues. You shouldn’t have to forgo dental care due to cost concerns – we’re here to assist you.

Dental treatment should be available for everyone and at Clean Smiles we work hard to make sure that quality dental care from dental professionals is available.  We also want to point you in the direction of the Alberta dental fee guide which you can find HERE and the new Canadian dental care plan (here) which provides tax help for people with dental emergencies and quality oral health care. 

What Affordable Dental Services Are Available In The Program?

The Clean Smiles for Community Program encompasses fundamental dental services tailored to meet our patients’ needs and our clinician’s recommendations, ensuring accessibility to those searching for a “cheap dentist near me.” Our affordable dental care includes:

  • Digital Dental X-Rays (at the clinician’s discretion)

  • Comprehensive Dental Screening – Intraoral and Extraoral examinations

  • Scaling – Professional teeth cleaning (up to 2.5 units of scaling)

  • Teeth Prophy – Polishing for a radiant smile

  • Fluoride Application

We understand the importance of affordable dental care, and our program is designed to cater to those seeking cheap dental cleaning costs. Whether you’re looking for affordable dental implants or a low-cost dental clinic near you, our commitment is to provide accessible and affordable dental care for everyone. We offer low-income dental assistance for adults, further emphasizing our dedication to making dental health accessible to the entire community.

How Do I Know If I Am Eligible?

Wondering if you qualify for our assistance? This initiative caters to individuals with limited financial means, lacking access to dental care, or without dental insurance coverage. It’s as simple as expressing your interest in utilizing this program – and you’re in!

To determine if the Clean Smile Community Program aligns with your needs, reach out to us at (780) 800-6655 or contact us online. We are here to provide affordable dental solutions, including an affordable dentist near you, affordable dental implants, a cheap dental clinic near you, and low-income dental assistance for adults. Your oral health matters, and we’re dedicated to making it accessible to everyone.

Cost for the Program

If you’ve had professional dental care within the last 1–2 years, services under the Clean Smile Community Program are available at a fixed rate of:

$250 (*As per clinician’s discretion.)
Pricing may vary for complex cases or for patients who have not received professional dental care in 2+ years. Some restrictions may apply.
