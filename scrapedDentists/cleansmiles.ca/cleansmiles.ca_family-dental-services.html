Frequently Asked Questions

What services does a family dentist in Edmonton provide?

A family dentist at Clean Smiles Dental Clinic offers comprehensive dental care, including routine checkups, professional cleanings, preventive care, restorative treatments, and emergency dental services for patients of all ages.

Why choose Clean Smiles Dental Clinic for family dental care?

Clean Smiles Dental Clinic is known for its patient-focused approach, modern dental technology, and comfortable environment. The clinic provides personalized care tailored to each family member’s dental needs.

Do you provide dental care for children and families?

Yes, Clean Smiles Dental Clinic specializes in family dentistry, offering gentle and effective care for children, teens, adults, and seniors—all in one convenient location.

How often should I visit a family dentist in Edmonton?

It is recommended to visit a family dentist every six months for regular checkups and cleanings to maintain good oral health and catch potential issues early.

What preventive dental services are available?

Preventive services at Clean Smiles Dental Clinic include dental exams, professional teeth cleaning, fluoride treatments, sealants, and oral health education to help prevent cavities and gum disease.

Do you offer emergency dental services?

Yes, Clean Smiles Dental Clinic provides emergency dental care to address urgent issues such as toothaches, broken teeth, infections, or dental injuries.

Are your dental services affordable?

Clean Smiles Dental Clinic follows standard fee guidelines and aims to provide affordable dental care options for families, making quality treatment accessible to everyone.

What can I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, the team at Clean Smiles Dental Clinic will perform a thorough examination, discuss your dental history, and create a personalized treatment plan based on your needs.

Do you create personalized dental treatment plans?

Yes, every patient at Clean Smiles Dental Clinic receives a customized treatment plan designed to support long-term oral health and address individual concerns.
