Frequently Asked Questions About Clean Smiles Dental Clinic

What Can I Expect as a Patient?

At Clean Smiles, it is our goal to make your dental experience an exceptional one. We have experienced, professional and knowledgeable staff that provides the best services and care that you deserve. We use our skills and the latest technology to ensure the best suited treatment for all of our patients.

  • Please arrive 10-15 minutes early.
  • Fill out our patient information form and get to know us.
  • Bring your dental insurance card and information with you.
  • Expect your first appointment (for adults) to be about 1 ½ hour.
  • We provide direct billing from dental insurance companies. However, it is your responsibility to pay the portion of services that is not covered by your dental insurance coverage. If you are unfamiliar with how your dental insurance works – or the amount of your coverage – our administrative team will be happy to assist you.
  • We follow current Alberta Fee Guide.
  • Feel free to ask any questions you might have.

Do You Have a Dentist On Site?

Yes, we have an amazing Independent General Dentist on site. Our dentist and his team are available to take care of any dental emergencies, Dental Restorations (Fillings), Root Canal Treatment (RCT), Extractions, Crowns, Veneers, and Bridge Work. Our dentist and his team are not only great with adults but they are amazing with kids as well.

Our dentist is accepting new patients, feel free to give us a call to book an appointment.

Do You Take X-Rays?

 Our dentist, and our team of independent hygienists can take X-rays at the clinic. We have a digital x-ray facility to diagnose and interpret dental and periodontal conditions.

How Do I Book an Appointment?

To book your appointment, give us a call at our office or text us at 780-800-6655. You can also fill out a quick online form on the website or feel free to DM us on our Instagram: @cleansmilesdental. We will gladly answer any concerns or interests you have about your dental health.

Are you Open on Weekends?

We are open on Saturdays from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm. Our Clinic is closed on Sundays.

Do you Accept Referrals?

Yes, we highly appreciate it when our patients refer their friends and families. Fun fact, all of our patients are loyalty cardholders and with this program, you can earn a point with every referral and this is our small way of saying thank you. With those points, you can earn cool gifts or even our in-office zoom teeth whitening treatment.

Are my Dentist and Dental Hygiene Services (dental cleanings) Covered by my Dental Insurance?

Dental Services and preventative care services are covered under most dental plans. Contact your dental insurance provider for specific coverage details. Clean Smiles Dental Clinic is an dental clinic and can direct bill to your insurance company for services rendered. We can also pre-authorize your planned treatment, so there are no financial surprises at the end of your appointment. We welcome all of your questions!

Ask us anything regarding your dental coverage and we will try our best to help you.

Do I need my dentist’s permission to have dental hygiene services performed elsewhere?

You can choose to have your dental hygiene services from any registered dental hygienist. At Clean Smiles Dental Clinic, our independent registered dental hygienists are trained to recognize and assess any dental concerns that need to be addressed. We work synergistically with dentists and dental hygienists to achieve optimal oral health. We strive to provide preventative care to avoid any dental problems and work with our patients to maintain oral health.

You can choose to have your dental hygiene services done by one of our experienced and skilled dental hygienists and see your existing Dentist for any dental work that you might need. Or, if you would like to have any dental work done you can consider having and an exam done by the Our dentist. He is our Independent General Dentist available on site, and he will be happy to assist any of your dental needs.

It’s been a long time since I’ve had my teeth checked and cleaned, is it too late now?

At Clean Smiles Dental Clinic, we pride ourselves on being non-judgmental. We believe it is never too late to start working towards a healthier smile. We will assess your dentition and soft tissue health and guide you in the right direction. Our Dentist can take care of any dental work that needs to be done for optimal oral health, and then our registered dental hygienists make a dental hygiene plan (in our office and at your home) for you to maintain a clean and healthy smile. This will make sure you can maintain the healthy teeth throughout your life time.

What is the right age for kids to start oral assessment, preventative care/dental hygiene services?

We recommend that children commence receiving hygiene services at 2-3 years of age. It is always better to start early so that any dental issues can be detected and addressed sooner, rather than later. For young children, the first couple of visits is primarily for them to get to know us and get comfortable with our kid’s friendly environment. We love to work with kids and to assist them in creating healthy oral habits and maintaining healthy and clean smiles.

How often should I get a dental screening / dental cleaning done?

Generally, we recommend getting general dental screening/ dental cleaning done 2 times a year to obtain the best dental health. But in some cases, patients need to visit more frequently. People who have underlying health conditions, have periodontal disease or smoke most likely need to visit more frequently.

What can I do if I have dental anxiety?

At clean smiles, we love to hear our patients. We have compassionate, skilled, and experienced dentist and dental hygienists who can provide the best suited dental treatment by managing your dental anxiety. We are here to provide you with a free consultation session where you can discuss your fear and anxieties, and together, we can figure out the best-suited solution to get you the best dental care you need and deserve.

We love to see you smile!
