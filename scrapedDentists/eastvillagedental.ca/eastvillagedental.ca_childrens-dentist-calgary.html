Let’s Make Your Children’s Visits to the Dentist Fun, Comfortable, and Worthwhile!

Our office offers specialized services with certified pediatric dental specialists and a child-friendly environment, encouraging families to bring their children for their first dental visit.

Want to give your child a fantastic reason to smile? Visit a pediatric dentist!!

You’ve come to the right place!

 

Trusted by thousands of parents, East Village Dental is the haven of children’s dentists in Calgary, where your child’s health and comfort are our priority.

Dr. Navdeep Dhaliwal is dedicated to providing his young patients with optimal care, love, and comfort so that their specialized dental care needs never get lost in the busy inner city of downtown Calgary.

We strive to foster a child-friendly environment that dispels stress, inhibits fears, and encourages beautiful, happy smiles. A child’s dental check-up is crucial for early detection of issues and comprehensive oral health evaluation.

We leverage our unique communication skills and caring approach to oral health to ensure that your children always look forward to their next visit to the dentist. The first dental visit is significant in establishing a positive rapport with the dental team.

Dr. Dhaliwal started his career in pediatric dentistry, and we can safely say that he also possesses excellent care and ability for our children’s teeth.

We offer popular iPad games in the waiting area and Netflix TVs and tablets in the dental rooms. We also offer multiple forms of dental sedation to make your child’s dental procedure painless and seamless. It’s refreshing to know that visiting a dentist or dental home can be easy and fun.

Encouraging a Great Dental Experience with Pediatric Dental Specialists

We offer hundreds of fantastic prizes for children after treatment to make things even more exciting.

Kids love Dr. Dhaliwal, and he loves children’s dentistry!

He interacts with every bit of patient one-on-one to achieve the best results for every child’s teeth and dental procedure.

Our ultimate goal is to offer preventive care services so your child’s precious smile lasts a lifetime!

Preventing dental decay through regular check-ups and proper oral hygiene is crucial for maintaining your child’s dental health.

We don’t compromise your child’s comfort, hygiene, or treatment quality. We are specially trained to deal with children of all ages. We can confidently say that your child’s next dental appointment with East Village will be positive.

Children’s Dental Care

We prioritize children’s comfort so that we can be confident in how well your kids can handle the children’s dentist! We emphasize the importance of oral health care for children with special needs, such as Autism Spectrum Disorder, Down Syndrome, and Cerebral Palsy.

While our children’s dentists are busy treating your teeth and child with love and care, you can relax in our reception area and unwind with a healthy selection of premium coffee or other complimentary refreshments.

From magazines to Netflix TV, we have enough to keep you entertained! You can also explore the beautiful neighborhood of East Village in Downtown Calgary—and we’ll call you back when your child’s session is over!

Your Child’s Best Trip to the Dentist

When your child needs a trip to the dentist, you think about more than just the dental care. Will my child be afraid? Will my child cry? Will the wait be long? At East Village Dental, we’re set up to help children relax before and during their appointments, and we’re fortunate to have Dr. Navdeep Dhaliwal, one of the best pediatric children’s dentists in Calgary.

Dr Dhaliwal’s pediatric dentistry practices focus on the child’s comfort and quality dental care. Our pediatric dental specialists have the expertise and training to treat young children, adolescents, and children with special needs. Dr. Dhaliwal and his assistants speak to pediatric patients with care and understanding. The pediatric dentist and staff find ways to let the child know they are safe and do not need to be afraid. They do this while completing dental exams, filling cavities, and completing dental cleanings.

If your child’s diagnosis requires a procedure that could be painful, East Village Dental offers sedation dentistry. Sedation allows the dentist to work efficiently without causing extra discomfort to the sensitive mouth or child. Sedation dentistry not only helps the child but puts parents at ease as well.

Sometimes, as the saying goes, the waiting is the hardest part. While our office runs very efficiently, 10 minutes can feel much longer for a child than for an adult. We’ve set up our waiting room and dental treatment rooms with the child patient in mind. Your kids will find iPads with games, Netflix on TVs, and other sources of entertainment. Your children may enjoy the dental office experience so much that they may surprise you by looking forward to returning to the dentist.

When looking for a pediatric dentist in Calgary, look no further than Dr. Dhaliwal at East Village Dental. We’re accepting new patients and will be happy to discuss our approach to pediatric dentistry in our Calgary office. Contact us today for more information.
