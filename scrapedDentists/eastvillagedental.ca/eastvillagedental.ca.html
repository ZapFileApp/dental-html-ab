Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the Calgary dentist for a checkup and cleaning?

Most patients should see a dentist every six months for an exam and professional cleaning. Some people, such as those with gum disease or braces, may need more frequent visits. We’ll recommend the best schedule for your needs.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call East Village Dental immediately. If you have a knocked-out tooth, broken tooth, or severe pain, prompt treatment is vital. We offer same-day emergency dental care in Calgary.

Do you offer sedation for nervous patients?

Yes! We provide gentle sedation options, including nitrous oxide (“laughing gas”), to help anxious patients relax during treatment.

Can you help with dental anxiety?

Absolutely. Our team is experienced with nervous patients and will take extra time to ensure you feel comfortable and in control. We also offer sedation and distraction options.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 24 hours’ notice for appointment changes. This allows us to offer your time slot to another patient. Missed appointments may result in a cancellation fee.

Are dental x-rays safe?

Yes. We use modern digital x-rays, which use a very low dose of radiation and are safe for patients, including children and pregnant women.

Do you offer direct billing to insurance?

Yes, we offer direct insurance billing for most major plans—so you only pay what isn’t covered.

Can you help me improve my smile’s appearance?

Definitely! We provide teeth whitening, veneers, Invisalign, bonding, and other cosmetic solutions. Book a consultation to learn about your options.
