Restore and Rebuild

When life takes a toll on your teeth, restorative dentistry brings back both function and confidence. Edson Family Dentistry offers professional, personalized care to repair damage and replace missing teeth. With treatments tailored to your needs, we help you smile and chew with ease again.

Restore your oral health today—book an appointment now!

Contact Us

Fillings

Cavities and chipped teeth don’t have to interrupt your smile. We provide both composite (white) and amalgam (silver) fillings based on your preference and the location of the tooth. Composite fillings are ideal for visible areas, blending seamlessly with your natural teeth.


﻿Discuss your options with Dr. Nordstrom at your appointment for a personalized approach to your dental health.

Contact Us

Bridges

Missing teeth can make eating and smiling a challenge, but dental bridges restore both function and confidence. Using a process similar to crowns, we create a custom bridge in two appointments to replace lost teeth.


﻿At your second visit, we’ll also provide detailed cleaning instructions to ensure your bridge stays healthy and functional.

Contact Us

Crowns

For teeth that are badly broken or have undergone root canals, crowns provide lasting protection and improved aesthetics. Crafted in two appointments, crowns are custom-made to fit perfectly and strengthen weakened teeth.


﻿At your first visit, we’ll prepare your tooth and fit a temporary crown, while the permanent crown is expertly fabricated in a dental lab.

Contact Us

Root Canal Therapy

When trauma or deep decay threatens your tooth, a root canal can save it. During the procedure, we remove the infected tissue and seal the canals to prevent further issues. To ensure long-term strength, we recommend crowning root-canaled teeth.


﻿Keep your smile intact and functional with this effective treatment.

Contact Us

Extractions

In cases where a tooth is beyond repair, extractions are performed safely and efficiently. Dr. Nordstrom has extensive experience removing damaged, decayed, and simple wisdom teeth.


﻿With a focus on patient comfort, we provide thorough care to make the process as smooth as possible.

Contact Us
