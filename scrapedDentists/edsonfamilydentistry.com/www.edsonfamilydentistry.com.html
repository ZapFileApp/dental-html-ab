About Us

For over 20 years, Edson Family Dentistry has proudly served Edson and surrounding areas with personalized care. We focus on gentle treatments, patient education, and lasting solutions for oral health. Our friendly and experienced team is committed to providing a welcoming environment for your family’s dental needs.

Services

General Dentistry

New Patient Exam:  Start your journey to better oral health with a comprehensive exam tailored to your needs.

Emergency Exam:   Address dental pain or concerns quickly with targeted diagnosis and care.

Hygiene Services:  Prevent cavities and gum issues with thorough cleanings and tailored hygiene advice.

Night Guards:  Protect your teeth and ease discomfort with a custom night guard.

Sports Guards:  Stay protected during sports with a custom-fitted mouth guard.

Restorative Dentistry

Fillings:  Restore damaged teeth with durable, natural-looking fillings.

Bridges:  Replace missing teeth seamlessly with custom dental bridges.

Crowns:  Protect and strengthen damaged teeth with a custom-made crown.

Root Canal: Save your tooth with gentle, effective root canal therapy.

Extractions:  Remove damaged teeth safely and comfortably, including wisdom teeth.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Tooth Whitening:  With our take-home whitening kits, you can enhance your look conveniently and effectively, achieving a fresh, polished appearance that reflects your confidence.
