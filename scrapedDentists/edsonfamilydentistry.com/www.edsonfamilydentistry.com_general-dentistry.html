Specific Exam / Emergency Exam

If you’re experiencing dental pain or if you have a specific concern, we’re here to help. Emergency exams include targeted X-rays and a focused assessment of the affected area.

Hygiene Services

Our Registered Dental Hygienists provide professional cleanings to maintain gum and teeth health. Your first session includes X-rays, gum health assessments, and a cleaning tailored to your needs.


﻿Regular hygiene visits are key to preventing dental issues. We’ll guide you on the best schedule for maintaining your smile.

Night Guards

Teeth grinding or clenching during sleep can lead to cracks, sensitivity, and other types of damages. A custom-fitted night guard provides protection, alleviates symptoms, and helps prevent further wear.


﻿Crafted to fit your upper or lower teeth, it ensures comfort and effective protection while you sleep.

Contact Us

Sports Guards

Protect your teeth, tongue, and cheeks during sports with a custom-made sports guard. These mouth guards are also designed to minimize the risk of concussions. Created in-house for a precise fit, our sports guards are typically ready within 24 hours.


﻿Safeguard your smile during every athletic activity!
