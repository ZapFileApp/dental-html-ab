Dr Monica Nguyen is a General Dentist at Seton Dental Wellness in Calgary

Dr. Monica is a compassionate and dedicated general dentist who truly loves connecting with people. She finds joy in all aspects of dentistry, but especially in building meaningful relationships with her patients and helping them achieve their best smiles. Originally from Winnipeg, Manitoba, Dr. Monica now calls rural Alberta home, where she lives with her husband, daughter, and two cats.

Outside of dentistry, she enjoys exploring new cultures, traveling, discovering great restaurants, and spending time outdoors.

Dr. Monica earned her degree from the University of Manitoba and has extensive training in oral sedation, implants, Invisalign, and general dentistry for both children and adults. Her approach is patient-focused, always aiming to make every visit a positive and comfortable experience.
