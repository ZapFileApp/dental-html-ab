Receiving Dental Implants in Calgary

Regardless of how many teeth you are missing or where the spaces are, modern dental implants are made from specialized materials to offer a permanent solution. Dental implants are built of three elements: a titanium post that is placed in your jaw, an abutment that is attached to that post, and a restoration (a tooth or bridge, or even dentures) attached to that abutment.

Titanium is an ideal material for dental implants because it is highly resistant to corrosion and has the best strength-to-density ratio. Most importantly, your jaw bone accepts titanium as a replacement tooth root and bonds powerfully to it through a process called osseointegration. Receiving dental implants in Calgary, AB, can change your life through this restorative procedure.

If you have any questions about receiving dental implants near you, please contact us.

Dental Implant Expectations

The process of osseointegration between your jaw and that titanium post also stimulates new bone growth in your jaw, meaning you end up with a stronger and healthier jaw than before the process began. Dental implants can replace single teeth, multiple adjacent teeth by providing the foundation for a bridge with a titanium post at each end, and can even provide permanent foundations for full sets of dentures. Advances in implant techniques, materials, and technology mean that there are implant options available today for even the smallest of jaws, and even if you have sub-optimal bone density.

When you are looking for a dental clinic that provides dental implants in Calgary, AB, there are a few things to consider. The process begins with an assessment of your teeth and jaws in our clinic, where we’ll take a series of images and discuss the full range of options available to you. Once a treatment plan is in place, we will perform any necessary extractions before inserting your titanium post(s). Once each post has been fully accepted and firmly bonded to your jaw, we will work with you to design your final restoration.

The Key Points About Dental Implants

  • Implants are made from specialized materials ideal for medical prosthetics and dental implants.
  • When dentists help you obtain implants, you’ll be able to eat properly without sacrificing your nutrition due to tooth loss, which may restore your overall health.
  • Titanium implants stimulate new and healthy bone growth in your jaw.
  • Implants are a permanent solution to tooth loss.
Our dentist in Calgary, AB, can explain the procedure to you to help you better understand it and determine if you are a candidate. If you are interested in receiving dental implants near you, contact our dental clinic today to schedule a consultation.
