Removable Clear Retainers

Prevent Teeth Movement after Orthodontics

Get replacement retainers with a digital scan. You don’t need to visit your orthodontist.

Retainers are thin clear plastic dental trays that fit over your teeth (similar to Invisalign Aligners) to maintain the existing or corrected position of teeth. We recommend retainers should be replaced every 2 years to ensure that teeth don’t move.

Lost or Broke Your Retainer?

Get a replacement quickly to avoid teeth from inadvertantly shifting or moving out of place without seeing an orthodontist.

We can take a digital dental scan (no more goopy impressions) and make a new set for you! We can help check your insurance benefits for new retainers and accept payments from your insurance; you only need to pay what is not covered

Most Common Places Retainers are Lost: Left in Hotel Rooms, Camping Trips
Most Common Ways Retainers are Broken: Teeth Clenching and/or Grinding, Not being stored in a case, Dogs and Cats finding them!

Steps to Get Your New Clear Retainers

Step 1. Digital Dental Scan

Within 5-10 minute we take a digital dental scan (a video 3D model of your teeth). No more goopy impression trays! We use this scan to print 3D models which we send to have your retainers made.

Step 2. Retainer Try-in and Delivery

After your retainers are made, we bring you into the office to make sure that these appliances have been made correctly (this helps to avoid future problems from developing)
