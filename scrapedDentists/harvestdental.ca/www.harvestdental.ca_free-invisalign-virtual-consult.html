The Next Steps

These are the steps that we follow to get your Invisalign Treatment started! All dentist planned and directed treatmetn uses Genuine Invisalign Aligners (not all aligners are created equal).

Step 2. In-Person Data Collection

At the Data Collection appointment we take a 3D Dental Photo Scan Dental and Dental Images which are used to develop your Invisalign treatment plan.

NOTE: A $300 fully refundable deposit is requested to book your Data Collection appointment

Step 1. Virtual Video Consultation

Have a Free 15 minute video consultation with an Invisalign Dentist to discuss your dental alignment conerns.

Step 3. Invisalign Fitting

2-3 weeks after your Data Collection appointment, we fit the aligners to your teeth and show you how to use and change the aligners. Treatmnent is monitored using photos taken with your smart phone

  • Full service dentistry refers to a comprehensive approach that encompasses a wide range of dental services under one location. This includes preventive care such as cleanings and check-ups, restorative procedures like fillings and crowns, and more special interest treatments like orthodontics and oral surgery. By offering a complete array of dental services, our full-service approach aims to address all aspects of oral health and provide patients with convenient and holistic dental care.

    Multi-disciplinary dental treatment encompasses a wide range of procedures and services that involve various specialties within the field of dentistry. These specialties may include areas such as orthodontics, periodontics, endodontics, and prosthodontics, each focusing on different aspects of oral health and care.
