What’s included with our Invisalign Treatment?

Initial consult, Photos & Digital Dental Scan, Genuine Invisalign Clear Aligners, Additional Aligners, One upper and lower Removable Final Retainer, All Invisalign treatment visits and Invisalign Virtual Care Program. Save an additional $100 by making payment in full at your Data Collection appointment. Additional records may be required for an orthodontic assessment on teen and child patients.

How do I get started with Invisalign?

We recommend that you have a Free Invisalign Video Consultation with one our dentists. After your consultation, we will book a Data Collection appointment to collect records needed to make your treatment plan. A fully refundable (no questions asked) $300 deposit is required to schedule the Data Collection appointment. If you choose to start treatment at the Data Collection appointment, you'll need to pay an additional $700 to complete the necessary down payment to order your Invisalign Aligners.
