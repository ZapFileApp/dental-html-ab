How It
Works

At Harvest Dental Care with
Invisalign Virtual Care

Step 1. Register or Book a Free Virtual Consultation

When we say free, we mean free. No strings attached. Have a free 15 minute video consultation with as Invisalign Dentist at Harvest Dental Care to discuss your dental concerns.

Step 2. Data Collection

At the Data Collection appointment we take a dental scan (a video 3D model of your teeth) and photos which are used to assess and plan your Invisalign treatment. In under ½ hour, we will have the info we need to make your Invisalign aligners.

NOTE: A $300 fully refundable deposit is requested to book your Data Collection appointment

Step 3. Invisalign Aligner Delivery

2-3 weeks after your Data Collection appointment, the Invisalign Aligner Delivery appointment, we fit the aligners to your teeth and show you how to use the aligners. We will monitor your treatment through photos taken on your smart phone, on a weekly basis. Save your time by avoiding unnecessary dental appointments.
