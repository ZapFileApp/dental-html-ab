Dental Checkups in North Edmonton

Regular dental checkups are an important part of maintaining a healthy smile. Routine visits allow us to detect potential issues early, prevent more serious problems, and keep your teeth and gums in great condition.

At Oxford Dental, we provide thorough, professional dental checkups in North Edmonton, with a focus on prevention, comfort, and long-term oral health.

What Happens During a Dental Checkup?



Comprehensive Oral Exam

During your visit, we carefully examine your teeth, gums, and overall oral health. This includes checking for early signs of cavities, gum disease, and other concerns that may not yet be causing noticeable symptoms. By identifying issues early, we can recommend simple, effective treatments to help prevent more complex problems in the future and keep your smile healthy.

Book appointment


Professional Dental Cleaning

A professional cleaning removes plaque and tartar buildup that regular brushing and flossing can’t always reach. Our hygienists carefully clean around the teeth and along the gumline to help reduce the risk of cavities and gum disease. This process not only supports your oral health but also leaves your teeth feeling smooth, polished, and refreshed.

Book appointment


Digital X-Rays & Early Detection

When needed, digital X-rays allow us to see what’s happening beneath the surface of your teeth and gums. These images help us detect hidden concerns such as decay between teeth, bone loss, or issues developing below the gumline. With early detection, we can recommend the most appropriate treatment and help prevent small problems from becoming more serious.

Book appointment

Why Regular Dental Checkups Matter

Routine dental checkups are one of the best ways to protect your oral health. Visiting your dentist regularly helps prevent problems before they start and ensures any concerns are treated early.

Our North Edmonton dental team focuses on proactive care to help you avoid discomfort, costly treatments, and long-term complications.

Preventive Dental Care for All Ages

At Oxford Dental, we provide dental checkups in North Edmonton for patients of all ages. Preventive care plays an important role in maintaining healthy smiles throughout every stage of life.

Children’s Dental Checkups

Regular dental checkups help children build healthy habits early and keep their developing teeth and gums on track. During each visit, we monitor growth, check for early signs of cavities, and provide gentle cleanings in a comfortable, welcoming environment. Our team focuses on creating positive experiences so children feel confident and relaxed at the dentist.

Senior Dental Care

As you get older, your oral health needs can change, making regular dental checkups even more important. We monitor for concerns such as gum disease, tooth wear, and dry mouth, while ensuring any existing dental work remains in good condition. Our goal is to help you maintain comfort, function, and a healthy smile at every stage of life.

Adult Preventive Care

Dental checkups play an important role in maintaining your oral health as an adult. Routine exams and cleanings help prevent cavities, gum disease, and other common concerns while allowing us to catch any issues early. We also provide personalized recommendations to help you maintain a healthy smile between visits.

Family-Friendly Appointments

We make it easy for families to stay on top of their oral health by offering convenient, family-friendly dental checkups. Our team works with patients of all ages, allowing you to schedule appointments together and receive consistent care in one place. We focus on creating a comfortable, welcoming environment for every member of your family.
