Family Dentistry – Dental Care for Every Generation

We’re your one-stop dental home for the entire family. From your child’s first dental visit to your parents’ denture care, we provide gentle, comprehensive dentistry for all ages.

Teeth Cleaning & Preventive Care

Keep your smile healthy with regular cleanings and checkups.

Professional teeth cleaning removes plaque and tartar that brushing can’t reach, preventing cavities, gum disease, and bad breath. We recommend cleanings every 6 months.

What’s included:

  • Thorough cleaning and polishing

  • Comprehensive oral exam

  • Cavity detection

  • Gum health assessment

  • Oral cancer screening

Learn More: Teeth Cleaning Calgary

Pediatric Dentistry (Kids' Dental Care)

Gentle, fun dental care that makes kids smile.

We make dental visits stress-free for children. Our friendly team knows how to put kids at ease, building positive dental habits that last a lifetime.

Services for kids:

  • First dental visits (age 1-3)

  • Cavity prevention and fluoride treatments

  • Dental sealants

  • Gentle cleanings and exams

  • Early orthodontic assessment

Learn More: Pediatric Dentistry Calgary

Senior Dental Care

Specialized dental care for seniors.

As we age, our dental needs change. We provide compassionate care for seniors, addressing issues like dry mouth, gum disease, tooth loss, and denture maintenance.

Services for seniors:

  • Gentle cleanings and exams

  • Denture care and adjustments

  • Gum disease treatment

  • Tooth replacement options

  • Oral cancer screenings

Learn More: Senior Dental Care Calgary

Root Canal Therapy

Save your tooth and end the pain.

Severe tooth pain? A root canal removes infected pulp, eliminates pain, and saves your natural tooth from extraction.

Signs you need a root canal:

  • Severe, persistent toothache

  • Sensitivity to hot or cold (lingers after stimulus is removed)

  • Swelling or tenderness in gums

  • Darkening of the tooth

  • Pimple-like bump on gums

Learn More: Root Canal Calgary

Tooth Extraction

Safe, gentle tooth removal when necessary.

Sometimes extraction is the best option—whether it’s a severely damaged tooth, impacted wisdom tooth, or overcrowding. We make the process as comfortable as possible.

Common reasons for extraction:

  • Severe decay beyond repair

  • Advanced gum disease

  • Impacted wisdom teeth

  • Overcrowding (for orthodontics)

  • Broken tooth below gum line

Learn More: Tooth Extraction Calgary

Sedation Dentistry

Relax through your dental treatment.

Dental anxiety keeping you from getting care? We offer sedation options to help you feel calm and comfortable during procedures.

Sedation options:

  • Nitrous oxide (laughing gas)

  • Oral sedation

  • IV sedation (for complex procedures)

Learn More: Sedation Dentistry Calgary

Cosmetic Dentistry – Transform Your Smile with Confidence

Your smile is one of the first things people notice. Whether you want whiter teeth, straighter alignment, or a complete smile makeover, we offer advanced cosmetic treatments that deliver natural-looking, beautiful results.

Teeth Whitening

Get a brighter, whiter smile in just one visit.

Professional teeth whitening removes years of stains from coffee, wine, and aging—giving you a smile that’s 3-8 shades whiter.

Why professional whitening?

  • Faster results than drugstore kits

  • Safer for sensitive teeth

  • Longer-lasting brightness

  • Custom-fitted trays for even whitening

Learn More: Teeth Whitening Calgary

Invisalign (Clear Aligners)

Straighten your teeth without metal braces.

Invisalign uses clear, removable aligners to gradually straighten your teeth—no wires, no brackets, no embarrassment.

Benefits:

  • Nearly invisible (no one will notice)

  • Removable (eat and drink normally)

  • Comfortable (no metal irritation)

  • Faster than traditional braces (12-18 months average)

Learn More: Invisalign Calgary

Porcelain Veneers

Cover imperfections and create your dream smile.

Veneers are thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of your teeth, instantly correcting chips, gaps, stains, and misalignment.

What veneers can fix:

  • Chipped or cracked teeth

  • Stubborn stains (that whitening can’t fix)

  • Gaps between teeth

  • Slightly crooked or misshapen teeth

  • Worn-down teeth

Learn More: Porcelain Veneers Calgary

Orthodontic Treatment

Straighten teeth and correct bite problems.

Beyond Invisalign, we offer comprehensive orthodontic solutions including traditional braces for complex cases.

We treat:

  • Crooked or crowded teeth

  • Overbite, underbite, crossbite

  • Gaps between teeth

  • Jaw alignment issues

Learn More: Orthodontic Treatment Calgary

Restorative Dentistry – Repair, Replace & Restore Your Smile

Missing or damaged teeth affect more than your appearance—they impact your ability to eat, speak, and smile with confidence. Our restorative treatments rebuild your smile’s function and beauty.

Dental Implants

The permanent solution for missing teeth.

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed in your jawbone, acting as artificial tooth roots. Topped with natural-looking crowns, they’re the closest thing to real teeth.

Why choose implants?

  • Permanent (lasts 20+ years)

  • Prevents bone loss

  • No damage to adjacent teeth

  • Eat anything (no food restrictions)

  • Looks and feels natural

Cost: Aroud $2,999 per tooth

Learn More: Dental Implants Calgary

Dental Crowns

Restore damaged teeth with natural-looking crowns.

A dental crown is a “cap” that covers a damaged tooth, restoring its shape, strength, and appearance. Think of it as a helmet for your tooth.

When you need a crown:

  • After a root canal

  • Large cavity or filling (tooth is weak)

  • Cracked or broken tooth

  • Severely worn tooth

  • Cosmetic improvement

Learn More: Dental Crowns Calgary

Dental Bridges

Replace missing teeth without surgery.

A dental bridge “bridges” the gap where teeth are missing, using crowns on adjacent teeth to support artificial teeth in between.

Benefits:

  • Permanent (fixed in place)

  • Restores chewing ability

  • Prevents teeth from shifting

  • Natural appearance

  • No surgery required

Learn More: Dental Bridges Calgary

Dentures

Affordable tooth replacement for multiple missing teeth.

Dentures replace multiple missing teeth with removable prosthetics. We offer full dentures (entire arch) and partial dentures (some teeth).

Types of dentures:

  • Full dentures (all upper or lower teeth)

  • Partial dentures (some missing teeth)

  • Implant-supported dentures (more stable)

Learn More: Dentures Calgary
