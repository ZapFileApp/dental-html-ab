What is Invisalign?

Invisalign is a modern orthodontic treatment that straightens teeth without traditional metal braces. It utilizes a series of custom-made, virtually invisible aligners to shift teeth into their ideal positions gradually. This discreet approach makes Invisalign a popular choice for teens and adults in Calgary seeking a straighter smile.

Each set of clear, removable aligners is worn for about two weeks, gently guiding your teeth into alignment. You can easily remove the aligners to eat, drink, brush, and floss, making it a convenient and hygienic option.

The duration of your Invisalign treatment is unique to you, depending on your individual needs and the complexity of your case. On average, most patients in Calgary complete their Invisalign journey within 12 to 18 months, but your journey may differ.

How Does Invisalign Treatment Work?

Invisalign is a revolutionary orthodontic treatment that offers Calgarians a discreet and effective way to achieve a straighter smile without traditional metal braces. Its popularity stems from its ability to transform smiles while preserving the wearer’s confidence and daily routine.

The Invisalign Process: Your Smile Transformation Journey

  1. Personalized Treatment Plan: At The Port Dental Clinic in Calgary, your journey to a new smile begins with a digital scan or impression of your teeth. This creates a 3D model of your mouth, allowing us to visualize your unique smile and design a treatment plan tailored to your needs.

  2. Custom-Made Aligners: Based on your treatment plan, a series of clear, removable aligners are custom-made for you. Each aligner is slightly different, so gentle pressure is applied to specific teeth to gradually move them into their desired positions.

  3. Gradual Alignment: Patience is key. You’ll wear each aligner for approximately two weeks, removing them only to eat, drink, brush, and floss. With each new set, your teeth will shift gradually and comfortably, bringing you closer to your ideal smile.

  4. Progress Monitoring: Regular checkups at The Port Dental Clinic in Calgary allow us to monitor your progress and ensure your teeth move as planned. We’ll make any necessary adjustments to your treatment plan along the way.

  5. Reveal Your New Smile: Once you’ve completed all your aligners, you’ll unveil a beautifully aligned smile you’ll love showing off.

Key to Success: Wear Time

To ensure optimal results, it is crucial to wear your Invisalign aligners for at least 20-22 hours per day. This consistent wear time keeps your treatment on track and allows you to achieve your smile goals efficiently.

Who should opt for Invisalign Treatment in Calgary?

Invisalign stands out as a versatile orthodontic treatment, uniquely capable of addressing a wide range of smile concerns. Its discreet nature, free from the visibility of traditional braces, makes it a popular choice for those seeking a subtle way to straighten their teeth.

Common Concerns Invisalign Can Correct

  • Crooked Teeth: Invisalign gently guides teeth into proper alignment.
  • Overbite: Invisalign can help correct an overbite where your upper teeth overlap your lower teeth too much.
  • Underbite: Invisalign can also address an underbite, where your lower teeth protrude further than your upper teeth.
  • Crossbite: This occurs when some upper teeth fit inside your lower teeth instead of outside, and Invisalign can be an effective solution.
  • Crowding: If your teeth are too close, Invisalign can create space for them to align correctly.
  • Spacing: Invisalign can work magic to close those unwanted gaps between your teeth, giving you a more even and attractive smile.

Why should you opt for Invisalign Treatment in NE Calgary?

If you’re interested in getting the Invisalign, the first step is to contact our clinic to schedule a consultation with our dentist in Calgary.

  • Our experienced dentists will consult and confirm if you are a candidate for Invisalign or not; they will begin creating a customized treatment plan for Invisalign. This process involves creating digital X-rays, impressions, and photographs of your teeth to create a digital road map for experts to follow.
  • Then, our dentist will use Invisalign software to predict how your teeth will move over time, how they will appear during each stage of your treatment, and finally, at the conclusion.
  • You will receive sets of custom-made aligners that will be changed every two weeks as a part of our Invisalign treatment in Calgary. Unlike traditional braces, the custom aligners will not irritate your cheeks and can be removed whenever you eat, drink, or brush your teeth.
  • The best part is that you do not need to come to the clinic every month; however, every month's dental visit is necessary to see any issues or improvements.

The benefits of Invisalign are the following:

  • Gradual alignment and crowding correction of teeth
  • Less pain comparative to traditional braces
  • Removable aligners allow for eating, drinking, brushing, and flossing
  • Aesthetically pleasing and more convenient to wear
What to expect after getting Invisalign?

First, remember that it will align teeth; thus, mild discomfort or pain will remain for a few days after getting the Invisalign treatment.

  • You may feel difficulty in speaking at the start, but that will resolve in a few days.
  • Wear the aligners for at least 20-22 hours per day to get the best results.
  • While brushing, flossing, and eating, take out your aligners. Never wear it while consuming anything hot.
  • Maintain the cleanliness of your aligners. While brushing, be sure to brush your aligners in the same manner.
  • Keep them secure in the box provided by your Orthodontist to avoid contamination.
