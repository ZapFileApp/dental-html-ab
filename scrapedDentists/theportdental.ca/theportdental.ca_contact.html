Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept new patients?

Yes! We’re currently accepting new patients and families. Book your first appointment today.

Do you offer same-day emergency appointments?

Absolutely. If you’re experiencing a dental emergency (severe pain, broken tooth, knocked-out tooth), call us immediately at 403-345-1110. We’ll try our best to get you in the same day.

What insurance do you accept?

We accept most major dental insurance plans and offer direct billing. Call us at 403-345-1110 with your insurance details, and we’ll verify your coverage before your appointment.

Do you treat children?

Yes! We provide gentle, family-friendly dental care for patients of all ages—from toddlers to seniors.

What if I have dental anxiety?

We understand dental anxiety is real. Our team takes extra time to explain every step, answer your questions, and make you comfortable. We also offer sedation dentistry for nervous patients.

How much will my treatment cost?

We provide transparent cost estimates before any treatment begins. We’ll explain what your insurance covers (if applicable) and discuss payment options so there are no surprises.

Where are you located?

We’re in Calgary NE at #3145 4250 109 Ave NE, Calgary, AB T3N 1Z3. Easy to find with plenty of free parking. Get directions →
