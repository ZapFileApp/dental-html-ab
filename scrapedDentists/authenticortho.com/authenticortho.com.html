Welcome to Authentic Orthodontics, the office of
Dr. Michael French, Dr. Michael Olsen & Dr. Landon Leavitt.

We can't wait to meet you and welcome you to our office! At Authentic Orthodontics, we are passionate about what we do and are excited for your orthodontic journey to begin. Whether you are young or simply young at heart, we want you to look at life with the confidence of a stunning smile.

We pride ourselves on the care we deliver to our patients. Dr. Michael French, Dr. Michael Olsen & Dr. Landon Leavitt provide the one on one attention you deserve by creating custom treatment plans that are tailored to your specific goals and needs.

We are proud to offer our orthodontic services in 2 locations in SE Calgary; in the community of Seton and Willow Park. We are also pleased to welcome patients in our Okotoks office from numerous communities around Southern Alberta including but not limited to; High River, Diamond Valley and Foothills. No referrals are necessary so if you are in Auburn Bay, Mahogany, Chaparral, Silverado, Okotoks or High River, we are close by and can’t wait to meet you!
