    FAQ's

    Do you offer sedation dentistry for anxious patients?

    Yes, we perform sedation dentistry to relax and comfort our patients during the procedure. Your safety and comfort are always taken as a priority at our dental clinic in NE Calgary.

    How often should I get a dental check-up and cleaning?

    We suggest that the majority of patients visit a dentist every six months for a cleaning and check-up. Regular visits to a dentist near you keep your teeth and gums healthy.

    Do you provide veneers, crowns, and bridges?

    Yes, our services include veneers, crowns, and bridges to restore your smile’s function and beauty. You can trust our experienced team at the dental clinic near you.

    At what age should my child first visit the dentist?

    Your child needs to visit the dentist for the first time at age one or when their first tooth erupts. Our caring dentist in NE Calgary provides a welcoming experience for children.

    What is the recovery time after wisdom tooth removal?

    Recovery is typically 3 to 7 days, depending on the case complexity and your healing. We will be caring for you every step of the way throughout the recovery.
