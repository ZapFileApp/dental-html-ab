    Meet your NE Calgary Dentist's

    Dr. Ahmed Abou Hassan

    Dr. Ahmed Abou Hassan, born and raised in Calgary, brings international experience and a passion for patient care to Memorial Square Dental. After earning his Bachelor of Oral Health in Dental Science and Master of Dentistry in Gold Coast, Australia, he now focuses on delivering comprehensive care in a safe, welcoming environment. With a special interest in root canals and oral surgery, Dr. Hassan stays current through the Alberta Dental Association and College. Outside the clinic, he enjoys skiing, hockey, camping, and quality time with loved ones. After five years away, he and his wife, Leanne, are excited to reconnect with Calgary and its community.

    Dr. Iju Chuah

    Dr. Iju Chuah earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Alberta in 1992 and completed a General Practice Residency at the University of Alberta Hospital. After a brief time practicing in Edmonton, she joined Memorial Square Dental in 1993, where she continues to provide compassionate, comprehensive care. A dedicated member of the Canadian Dental Association, Alberta Dental Association and College, and the Calgary and District Dental Society, Dr. Chuah is passionate about all aspects of general dentistry. Outside the clinic, she enjoys volunteering, reading, traveling, and spending quality time with family and friends.
