Root Canal Therapy

Root canal therapy is a crucial treatment for saving teeth that are severely decayed or infected. At Smiles At Saddleridge Dental, we perform root canals to remove infected pulp and bacteria from inside the tooth, preventing the need for extraction. This procedure not only alleviates pain but also restores the tooth’s function and integrity.
The process involves removing the infected pulp, cleaning and disinfecting the root canals, and filling them with a biocompatible material. Finally, the tooth is sealed and often capped with a crown to protect it from future damage. Our experienced team ensures that the procedure is as comfortable and stress-free as possible, helping you maintain your natural smile.

Wisdom Tooth Extraction

Wisdom teeth, or third molars, often need to be extracted due to lack of space in the mouth, impaction, or other issues. At Smiles At Saddleridge Dental, we provide safe and efficient wisdom tooth extractions to prevent complications such as pain, infection, and crowding of other teeth. Our skilled dentists will assess your situation and recommend extraction if necessary.
The extraction procedure is performed with great care to ensure your comfort and minimize recovery time. We offer sedation options to help you feel relaxed during the process. After the extraction, we provide detailed aftercare instructions to promote healing and reduce the risk of complications. With our expert care, wisdom tooth extraction can be a straightforward and stress-free experience.
