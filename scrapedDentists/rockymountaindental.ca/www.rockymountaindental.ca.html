All of our dentists at Rocky Mountain Dental are general dentists (unless specified). As general dentists, they provide cosmetic dental procedures such as porcelain veneers, dental crowns, dental braces, and teeth whitening as part of their general dentistry licence. Cosmetic Dentistry and Family Dentistry are not specialties recognized by the Alberta Dental Association & College (ADA&C). As Banff family dentists, our team provides general dental procedures for all ages within the family.
