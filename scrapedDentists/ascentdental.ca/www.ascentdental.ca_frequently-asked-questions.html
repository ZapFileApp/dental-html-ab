FAQs

When are you open?

We are open Monday through Thursday from 8am to 5pm and Friday from 8am to 4pm.

Do you follow the Alberta Dental Fee Guide?

Yes, our office follows the Alberta Dental Association Suggested Fee Guide.

Can you direct bill my insurance?

Yes, our office electronically sends in the insurance claim directly on your behalf.

Are you accepting new patients?

Yes! Call us today at 1-800-663-5041 or 403-627-3290 and we will be happy to assist you.

Can I see photos of the office?

Click here to see a photo gallery of our office.

What is your contact information?

Look here to see our contact info and location.

How often should I get my teeth cleaned?

In general most patients will benefit from a cleaning every six months, although we may recommend a different frequency tailored to your individual situation.

Do you respond to outside of hours emergencies?

Yes, we are willing to respond to emergencies.

Do you provide children's dentistry?

Yes! We are always happy to have children in the office. If necessary, work may also be done using laughing gas (nitrous oxide).
