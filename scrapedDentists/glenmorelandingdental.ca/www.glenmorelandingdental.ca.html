FAQs

How often should I have a checkup at a dental clinic near me?

Every six months, you should visit your dentist for a comprehensive exam and cleaning for optimal oral health. Regular visits to your dentist allow you to catch and correct issues sooner rather than later.

Why is it important to get a dental cleaning on a regular basis?

Professional cleanings help to remove plaque and tartar buildup that often brushing and flossing may leave behind, which aids in cavity and gum disease prevention and a fresh smile!

What should I do if I am in a dental emergency?

If you are experiencing sudden pain, swelling, and/or a broken tooth, contact your dental clinic in SW Calgary as soon as possible. Most dental clinics will see a patient on the same day or on the weekend for dental emergencies.

How can I improve the look of my smile?

From whitening, veneers, and clear aligners, there are many cosmetic options that can help you feel more confident with your smile. Ask your dentist to determine which is best for your needs.
