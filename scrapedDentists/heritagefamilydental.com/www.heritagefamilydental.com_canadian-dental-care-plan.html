The Canadian Dental Care Plan in Red Deer

The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) is a program designed to make dental care inexpensive for Canadians who need it the most. Starting in May 2024, this plan helps cover dental costs for families and individuals earning less than $90,000 annually who don’t have dental insurance.

How Does the CDCP Work?

The CDCP helps Canadians pay for essential dental services based on their family income:

  • Income under $70,000: You only pay the difference between what the CDCP covers and the fees outlined in your BC fee guide.
  • Income between $70,000 and $79,999: You cover 40% of your dental costs.
  • Income between $80,000 and $89,999: You cover 60% of your dental costs.

Not all treatments are covered under the Canadian dental care plan. To avoid unexpected costs, it’s wise to check which procedures are included in the plan before beginning your dental care.

Frequently Asked Questions

What Dental Services Are Covered?

The CDCP helps cover these dental services:

  • Preventive care
  • Diagnostic services
  • Restorative treatments
  • Endodontic procedures
  • Prosthodontic services
  • Periodontal care
  • Oral surgery

Note: Some treatments may require approval before you can begin. Don’t worry—our team will guide you through the process!

Who Can Apply for the CDCP?

To qualify for the CDCP, you must:

  • Be a Canadian resident.
  • Earn less than $90,000 annually
  • Have no dental insurance.

Belong to one of these groups:

  • Children under 18
  • Seniors 65 and older
  • Adults with a valid Disability Tax Credit certificate

How to Apply for the CDCP?

Applying for Canada’s dental care plan is easy! Here’s how:

  • Visit the Health Canada website to submit your application online.
  • Once approved, you’ll receive a package from Sun Life with details about your benefits, start date, and a member card.

Need assistance? Our team is happy to help you with your application and answer any questions.

When Can You Apply?

The CDCP is being introduced in phases. Here’s the current schedule:

  • Seniors 65 and older: Applications are open now.
  • Children under 18: Applications are open now.
  • Adults with a Disability Tax Credit Certificate: Applications are open now.
  • All other eligible individuals: Applications start in 2025.

If you’re unsure about your eligibility or need help applying, feel free to reach out to us.

Can You Use the CDCP with Other Dental Plans?

Yes! If you’re already part of other government programs, the CDCP can work with them to maximize your benefits. However, if you have private dental insurance, the CDCP isn’t a replacement. Be sure to keep your current plan if you have one.

Can I combine the CDCP with other government programs?

Yes, the CDCP works with federal and provincial programs to maximize your benefits.

What do I need to apply?

When applying for the CDCP, make sure you have this information ready:

  • Social Insurance Numbers (SIN) for everyone applying (if available).
  • Birthdates and full names of all applicants.
  • Your home address and mailing address.
  • Details of any other dental coverage you might have.
  • Proof that you and your spouse (if applicable) filed your 2023 tax return.

Having these details handy will make the process faster and smoother.

How can I check my application status?

Want to check how your application is coming along. Here’s how:

  • Online Status Checker: Use your application code or client number along with your SIN to check your status.
  • Automated Phone Service: Available 24/7 for quick updates.

If you have trouble, feel free to call us, and we’ll help you figure it out!
