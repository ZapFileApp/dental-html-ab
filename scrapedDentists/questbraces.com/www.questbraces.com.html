Smiles Made Awesome

Smiling is one of the most basic and most powerful forms of human expression. In moments of happiness and joy, our smile lets the world know how we feel.

At Quest Braces, we’re here to help you share your best smile. To us, that means enjoying life with a smile that truly expresses the feelings and emotions that make you smile. It also means having a healthy mouth, a comfortable bite, and good overall health and wellness.

Meet Dr. Mo

Dr. Mo is a world traveler, a private pilot, a roughrider fan (sorry, Stamps fans), and a George Strait wannabe. Dr. Mo Korayem aka “Dr. Mo” Orthodontist noun (orth.o.DON.tist): A specialized dentist who has completed 3 years of full-time specialized training in the art and science of dental alignment and bite correction to become a certified dental specialist.

Dr. Mo is our resident official tooth nerd. He’s an expert at aligning teeth and creating beautiful smiles. When he’s not doing that, he can be found flying small planes, woodworking, doing home renovations and singing/writing country songs! Dr. Mo and his wife Sherry have two insanely cute kids who keep them busy, inspired, and thoroughly sleep-deprived.
