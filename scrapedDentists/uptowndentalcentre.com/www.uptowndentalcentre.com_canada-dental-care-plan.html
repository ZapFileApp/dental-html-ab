Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) in Edmonton

Home / Canada Dental Care Plan

The CDCP increases access to dental care in downtown Edmonton. Uptown Dental Centre provides clear guidance on eligibility and coverage.

What Is the Canadian Dental Care Plan?

The Canadian Dental Care Plan includes essential dental services such as exams, fillings, and extractions.

This federal plan enhances dental affordability for seniors and families in Edmonton.

Who Qualifies for the CDCP Benefit?

Eligibility for the CDCP depends on your age (children, youth under 18, and adults), whether you have private or employer-based dental insurance, and whether your household income is below the federal income threshold.
  • Children and youth under 18 can qualify if their household income falls within the federal CDCP guidelines and they do not have private or employer-based dental insurance coverage.
  • Adults are eligible if they do not have private or employer-based dental insurance and their household income is below the federal government-established CDCP threshold.

We assist Edmonton seniors and families in confirming their CDCP eligibility.

What Dental Services Are Covered?

The plan covers a range of essential dental services, including:

  • Dental exams & professional cleanings
  • Diagnostic services (X-rays)
  • Fillings and restorations
  • Tooth extractionUrgent or emergency dental treatments

Specialized or cosmetic procedures may not be included. We clearly outline your policy’s coverage limits.

How to Apply for the Canadian Dental Plan in Edmonton

Applying for CDCP benefits involves four steps:
  1. Gather all required documents, including proof of residency, identification, and income statements.
  2. Apply online through the Government of Canada’s official portal.
  3. Schedule your Uptown Dental Centre visit after your benefits are approved.

Our team guides you through the process and helps you maximize your benefits.

How Uptown Dental Centre Supports CDCP Patients

We support Edmonton patients at every stage of the CDCP benefits process.

  • Direct billing assistance: Our team manages all paperwork and billing so you can focus on your oral health.
  • Compassionate care: Our Edmonton dentists prioritize gentle, patient-centered care for seniors, families, and individuals.
  • Central Edmonton location: Our downtown clinic offers convenient access to CDCP benefits for the local community.

FAQs About Canadian Dental Care Plan

Who is eligible for the Canadian Dental Care Plan?

Canadian residents without private dental insurance who filed their tax returns and have a family income under CAD 90,000.

What treatments does the plan cover?

Routine exams, cleanings, fillings, root canals, crowns, dentures, oral surgeries, and some orthodontic treatments.

Does CDCP cover all dental costs?

Coverage depends on income. Full coverage for incomes below CAD 70,000; partial coverage for incomes up to CAD 89,999.

How can I apply?

Apply online through My Service Canada Account, by phone, or by mail with your SIN, birth date, and tax documents.

Where can I use the CDCP benefits?

At dental clinics registered with CDCP, which bill Sun Life directly.
