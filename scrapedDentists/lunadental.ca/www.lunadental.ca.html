Meet the Luna Dental Team

We strive to create a warm, friendly environment where we get to know all of our patients. We aim to build long-lasting relationships with you and your family by providing exceptional dental care in a calm and comfortable environment. Meet the team

Welcoming New Patients

At Luna Dental, Dr. Parekh and the team will conduct a comprehensive initial visit that includes reviewing your medical and dental history, taking necessary x-rays and photographs, and performing a thorough oral health examination. This includes a head and neck exam, TMJ exam, checking for oral cancer, gum health, and cavities. We discuss treatment options, ensuring you’re well-informed to make decisions about your care.
