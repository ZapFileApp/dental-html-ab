Our Team

Our Expert Dental Team

With years of experience and a commitment to excellence, our dentist provides personalized care to help you achieve and maintain a healthy smile.

Dr. Dong-Gi Kim

Owner & Dentist

Dr. Kim earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery (D.D.S.) degree in 1995 from Dankook University in South Korea. He began his career working at a Public Health Center for three years before managing several dental practices alongside his wife, Dr. Lee. In 2004-2005, he attended Dr. Deporter’s “Biomaterials & Implant/Reconstructive Dentistry” course at the University of Toronto. During this time, his family briefly lived in Toronto, and after returning to South Korea, they realized their deep connection to Canada and decided to immigrate. Dr. Kim obtained his Canadian dental qualification in 2011 and became a licensed general dentist in Alberta in 2012.

With a passion for all aspects of dentistry, Dr. Kim specializes in prosthodontics (implants, bridges, dentures) and periodontics (bone and soft tissue grafts). He has completed numerous advanced training courses to refine his expertise in these areas, ensuring top-quality care for his patients. Dr. Kim is an active member of the Alberta Dental Association and College (ADAC), the Calgary and District Dental Society (CDDS), the Canadian Dental Association (CDA), and BITES Institute Dental Implant Training. In his free time, he enjoys golfing, skiing, and learning to play the guitar. He cherishes spending time and traveling with his daughters, Isabelle and Kate.

Dr. So-Young Lee

Dentist

Dr. Lee earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery (D.D.S.) degree from Dankook University in South Korea in 1995. She further honed her expertise by completing a one-year internship and a two-year residency in the Conservative Department (Endodontics and Operative Dentistry) at Dankook University Dental Hospital. She also obtained her Master’s Degree and contributed to dental research with her published article, “A Study on the Apical Microleakage of Glass Root Canal Sealer” (Lee and Hong, 1998). After immigrating to Canada, Dr. Lee earned her qualification as a Canadian dentist in 2011 and became a licensed general dentist in 2012.

Passionate about patient care, Dr. Lee stays up to date with the latest advancements in dentistry to provide the best possible treatments. She believes that beyond technical skills, the most important aspect of dentistry is treating patients with kindness and care, just as she would her own friends and family. She is an active member of the Alberta Dental Association and College (ADAC), the Calgary and District Dental Society (CDDS), and the Canadian Dental Association (CDA). In her spare time, she enjoys playing golf, cooking, and spending quality time with her family.

Brittney Aldrich

Registered Dental Hygienist

Born and raised in Sundre, Brittney moved to Edmonton to pursue her passion for dental hygiene, earning a Bachelor of Science in Dental Hygiene at the University of Alberta, specializing in Periodontology and Implantology. Brittney has since made it her mission to provide top-notch preventative care for every patient she serves.

Brittney prioritizes patient comfort and health, offering personalized care with a focus on thorough dental hygiene therapy. She is certified in local anesthesia, diode laser therapy for periodontal treatments, and cold sore management, ensuring her patients receive the best possible care.

As a member of the Canadian Dental Hygienists Association (CDHA) and the Alberta College of Dental Hygienists (ACDHA), Brittney stays at the forefront of her profession, continually enhancing her skills and knowledge.

Outside the office, Brittney is a proud mother of three sons and enjoys spending quality time with her family. She brings positivity and creativity to all areas of her life, from work to home.
