Our Services

Best Quality Services

Sundre Dental offers a wide range of procedures. Experience exceptional dental care with precision, comfort, and lasting results.

 

Implants and Bone Graft

Dental implants are the best natural tooth alternative, made from biocompatible materials like orthopedic implants.

Dentures

Dentures are removable replacements for missing teeth, made of acrylic resin or metal, and can be secured with implants for better retention.

Root Canals

A root canal treats infected or damaged teeth by removing infected tissue, filling the space, and sealing the tooth.

Professional Hygiene and Whitening

Cleanings prevent decay and gum disease by removing deposits, recommended every six months. We also offer night guard whitening for a brighter smile.

Crowns and Bridges

Crowns protect weakened teeth, often after root canals or large fillings, while bridges replace missing teeth by attaching a false crown to adjacent teeth.

Fillings and Child Dental Treatment

Fillings treat tooth decay or chips, often detected by exams or X-rays, and we provide care for children as young as 2.

Our Technology

Advanced Dental Care with Cutting-Edge Technology

We use the latest innovations to provide precise, comfortable, and effective treatments for your oral health.

CBCT

A CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) machine in dentistry creates 3D X-ray images of the teeth, jaws, and surrounding structures, offering more detailed views than traditional 2D X-rays. It aids in precise planning for procedures like implants, root canals, and surgeries

Laser

Laser technology in dentistry uses focused light to treat various oral conditions with precision and minimal discomfort. It is commonly used for procedures like soft tissue removal, gum reshaping, and laser periodontal therapy offering faster healing times and reduced risk of infection compared to traditional methods.

Dental Microscope

A dental microscope provides enhanced magnification and illumination, allowing dentists to see intricate details of teeth, gums, and surrounding structures. This improved visibility aids in performing precise procedures, such as root canals and surgeries, ensuring better accuracy and patient outcomes.

Intraoral Scanner

An intraoral scanner is a digital device used in dentistry to capture detailed, 3D images of a patient's teeth and gums. It eliminates the need for traditional molds, offering a quicker, more comfortable experience while providing highly accurate data for treatments like crowns, implants, and nightguards/ flippers.
