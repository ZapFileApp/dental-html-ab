The Best Dental Clinic That You Can Trust

FAQ

Frequently Asked Question

Find answers to common questions about our services.

Do you offer direct billing?

We offer direct billing to applicable plans, and can submit pre-authorizations at your request so that you can always stay informed of procedure costs.

Our office currently follows the 2025 Abbreviated Alberta Dental Fee Guide for General Dentists

Can you provide emergency Dental care?

Sundre Dental is always available to help. If you have a dental emergency and require immediate dental care, call our office or leave a message, we will try to help you as soon as possible.

Do you accept new patients?

Absolutely, we are happy to welcome you to the Sundre Dental Clinic.
