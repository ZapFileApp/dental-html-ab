More About Us

Our Family Careing for Yours

Most of us realize that diet and exercise play an important part in keeping ourselves healthy. But did you know that a healthy dental condition also plays an essential role in this? With great care and the use of the latest technology, Sundre Dental Clinic will ensure that every patient will leave with a smile and satisfaction of their oral health care.

Our philosophy is to treat patients with the utmost care and treat them like family. We truly care about your dental health, and we want to emphasize the importance of the maintenance of your oral health more than the actual treatment itself. Our focus is to approach patients with the least-invasive treatment possible as we value the conservation of our patient’s natural teeth.

Our friendly staff is dedicated to the highest quality of patient care. Our entire team has extensive experience, training and skill with sensitivity and kindness.
