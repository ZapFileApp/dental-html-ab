Schedule Your Next Dental Appointment

Bonnie Doon Dental Clinic would love to hear from you! To schedule your next appointment for our dental services or to request more information about our clinic and availability, please contact us today via phone, email, or by filling out the form on this page at your convenience. A member of our friendly staff will answer your inquiry as soon as possible.

We Accept Walk-ins and Dental Emergencies

Walk-ins and urgent-care patients are always welcome at our Edmonton clinic, which you will find in the Bonnie Doon Shopping Centre on the main level at the north end of the mall close to MaKami College and Dollarama. We’re open daily with extended hours available to accommodate dental emergencies, although hours may vary during the year. We’re even open on holidays, except for Christmas and New Year’s Day.
