WELCOME TO CANYON DENTAL CENTRE

Your Reason to SMILE!

Since 1992, our privately owned and family oriented general practice has been providing dental care to thousands of patients in Calgary and surrounding areas. Our mission is to make dental care accessible, affordable, and convenient to all patients, no matter what age or stage of life. We are always welcoming new patients and are pleased to offer onsite parking, as well as early morning and weekend appointments.

We are conveniently located in Southcentre Mall at the intersection of Anderson Road and Macleod Trail. Inside the mall, we are on the lower level across from SportChek.

LEARN MORE ABOUT CANYON DENTAL

Preventative Focus. Evidence Based

Canyon Dental Centre is proud to offer a wide range of dental services to help you achieve optimal oral health and the beautiful smile you have always wanted! We are committed to helping you incorporate regular dental care seamlessly into your life. At every appointment, you will experience our team’s dedication to you and your family’s dental and overall, well-being. From dental cleanings and fillings to more complicated procedures like implants, veneers or Invisalign, we offer a personalized experience to every patient with your comfort and health always top of mind.

The dentists at Canyon Dental are all General Dentists. As general dentists, they provide cosmetic dental procedures such as porcelain veneers, dental crowns, dental braces, and teeth whitening as part of their General Dentistry license. Cosmetic Dentistry & Family Dentistry are not specialties recognized by the Alberta Dental Association & College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta (CDSA). As South Calgary Family Dentists, they provide General Dental procedures for all ages within the family.
