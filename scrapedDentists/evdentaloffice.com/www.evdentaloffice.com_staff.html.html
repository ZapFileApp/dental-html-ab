General Dentist D.M.D.

Dr. Parikh is a graduate of TUFTS University of Dental Medicine, Boston, MA.  She is a member of The American Dental Society, The California Dental Society and The Santa Clara Dental Society.
 
Dr. Parikh has dedicated her professional career to providing you and your family with the best that dentistry has to offer, by keeping up with the latest technology with continuous education in cosmetics and endodontics.
 
She is continually upgrading herself and her practice with the newest and latest techniques and approaches to state of the art, comfortable, personalized and antiseptic dental care.
 
She is gentle, caring and health oriented, able to care for all ages. She is quality minded.  She has an outstanding clinical experience while serving dentistry for the last 25 years. She lives in Evergreen, San Jose with her husband, son and daughter.
