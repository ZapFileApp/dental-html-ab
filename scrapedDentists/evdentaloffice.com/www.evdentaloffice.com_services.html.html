Services

Evergreen Valley Dental Office is a Family Dental Practice! 


 Here are some of services provided:

  • Children dental examination from age 3
  • Digital Radiography/ Intraoral 3-D Sirios Tooth Scanner
  • Gentle Ultrasonic Scaler based Cleaning.
  • Lumineers / E-MAX Impress Porcelain Veneers  
  • Crown / Bridges
  • Composite(tooth-colored) Fillings
  • Inlays/Onlays 
  • Dentures / Partial Dentures
  • Extractions 
  • Root Canals
  • Zoom2! Teeth Whitening
