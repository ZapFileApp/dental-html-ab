Meet Dr. Manny Pandher
General Dentist

Dr. Manny Pandher is a general dentist born and raised in Edmonton, Alberta. He attended Concordia University of Edmonton where he completed his undergraduate degree in Chemistry. He then moved to Boston, Massachusetts to complete his dental degree at Boston University, graduating summa cum laude.

Dr. Manny was taught using the most up-to-date knowledge and technology. He strives to integrate his comprehensive care with these teachings in every patient visit. He is committed to ensure that all of his patients experience dental appointments that are very positive and tailored to their unique needs. In order to do so, he focuses to ensure every patient has a comfortable and anxiety free visit, with each question and concern answered.

Dr. Manny recently got married and enjoys spending majority of his off-time with his wife. They enjoy a very active lifestyle and love to enjoy the outdoors any opportunity they get. He also enjoys traveling, trying local restaurants, and spending time with his friends and family.

Meet Dr. Andrew Cojulun
General Dentist

Dr. Andrew Cojulun is a general dentist, who grew up in Abbotsford, BC, before relocating to Leduc, AB in 2007. He pursued his dental education at the University of Alberta, earning his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree in 2018.

Dr. Andrew is committed to providing personalized, high-quality dental care with a focus on prevention and long-term oral health. He believes in taking a holistic approach, taking the time to understand each patient’s unique needs, lifestyle, and overall health. Dr. Andrew ensures his patients receive the most effective and up-to-date care. Whether it's routine check-ups or more advanced procedures, his goal is to help patients maintain healthy, confident smiles for life.

Dr. Andrew enjoys staying active at the gym, gaming, and spending quality time with his wife, daughter and their three cats. During the winter months, he frequently heads to the mountains for skiing adventures with his wife. A dedicated traveler, Dr. Andrew has explored a variety of countries, including Japan, Malaysia, Cambodia, Vietnam, South Korea, Guatemala, Mexico, and Italy.
