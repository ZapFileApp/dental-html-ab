South Calgary Dental & Orthodontics Community Involvement

Dr. Angela Sharma and her team at South Calgary Dental & Orthodontists are dedicated to comprehensive Family Dental Care, Orthodontic Dentistry, and Cosmetic (Restorative) Dentistry.

At South Calgary Dental & Orthodontics, our exceptional dental team believes in community involvement and giving back to the local Legacy community. That is why it is an honour to positively influence our extended community by giving back. Here are some of the events, charities, teams, and foundations we support or have contributed to.
