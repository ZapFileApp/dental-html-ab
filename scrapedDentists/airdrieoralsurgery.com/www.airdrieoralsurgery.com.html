Our Commitment

Exceptional Service, Expert Care

At Airdrie Oral Surgery, your comfort, safety, and health are our top priorities. Whether you’re looking for a skilled Airdrie oral surgeon or specialized surgical care for complex dental concerns, our experienced team is here to help.

We proudly serve the community of Airdrie and surrounding Calgary Deep North neighborhoods with a full range of oral and maxillofacial procedures — from in-office surgeries to hospital-based care.

Our surgical facility is fully accredited by the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Alberta, Alberta Health Services, and the Alberta Dental Association and College. Anesthesia services are provided by certified physician anesthesiologists with expertise in outpatient care.

We are committed to delivering the highest quality care with professionalism, compassion, and clarity—so you can make informed decisions about your health with confidence.

Meet The Surgeons

Airdrie Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery is committed to ensuring our patients
have the information and resources to make informed decisions about their health.

Dr. Brett Habijanac

DMD, FRCDC

Dr. Habijanac was born and raised in southern Alberta, Dr. Habijanac earned his Biochemistry degree from the University of Alberta before completing his Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD) at the University of British Columbia in 1999. He then pursued specialty training in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at McGill University, where he honed his expertise in facial trauma and reconstructive surgery.

Learn More

Dr. Graham Cobb

DDS, MD, FRCDC

Dr. Graham Cobb was born and raised in North Bay, Ontario. He completed his Dental School, Medical School, and Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery residency training at Dalhousie University in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Dr. Cobb then completed a Head and Neck Oncology and Reconstructive Surgery fellowship in Portland, Oregon, at Legacy Emanuel Hospital and the Providence Portland Cancer Center.

Learn More

Dr. Miller Smith

DDS, MD, FRCDC

Born and raised in Calgary, Dr. Miller Smith earned his Dental degree from the University of Alberta. He pursued additional Hospital Dentistry training in San Antonio, Texas, before undertaking postgraduate specialty training. Dr. Smith further enhanced his expertise with a fellowship in Head & Neck Oncology, Microvascular Surgery, and Craniofacial Reconstruction in Glasgow, United Kingdom.

Learn More

Dr. Simon Touchan

DMD, MD, FRCDC

Dr. Simon Touchan obtained his dental degree from McGill University and his medical degree from Wayne State University. After completing a certificate in General Surgery, he pursued specialty training in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at the University of Michigan.

Learn More

Dr. Brad Fisher

DDS, MD, FRCDC

Dr. Brad Fisher was born and raised in Redcliff, Alberta. He obtained his Bachelor of Medical Science and Doctor of Dental Surgery degrees at the University of Alberta. After practicing as a general dentist in Calgary,  Dr. Fisher pursued specialty training, completing his residency in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery, as well as earning his Doctor of Medicine and Master of Science degrees at Dalhousie University. During his residency, he served as President of the Canadian Resident’s Association for Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery.

Dr. Tuan G. Bui

Raised in Montreal, Quebec, Dr. Tuan G. Bui completed his undergraduate education in Human Physiology at McGill University. He then obtained his medical degree from the University of Western Ontario, followed by a dental degree from McGill University.

Learn More

Dr. Chad Sampson

DMD, MD, FRCDC

Dr. Sampson was born in Calgary and raised just east of the city in Chestermere, Alberta. He completed his Bachelor of Science in Physiology at the University of Saskatchewan in Saskatoon. Following this, he completed a Doctor of Dental Medicine degree at McGill University in Montréal. He then returned to Alberta to complete a General Practice Residency at the University of Alberta, in Edmonton. Dr. Sampson worked as a general dentist in Calgary for the next four years. Following this, he completed both a Doctor of Medicine degree, a Master of Science degree and his residency training in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at Dalhousie University, in Halifax. Dr. Sampson then went on to complete a Head & Neck Oncology and Microvascular Reconstructive Surgery Fellowship, primarily based at the Institute of Neurological Sciences in the Queen Elizabeth University Hospital in Glasgow, Scotland.

Dr. Sampson has returned to Calgary to practice. His primary professional interests involve management of head & neck cancer, benign pathology, craniomaxillofacial trauma, orthognathic surgery and temporomadibular joint surgery.

Dr. Sampson is a Fellow of the Royal College of Dentists of Canada.

Outside of work, Dr. Sampson enjoys spending time with his family snowboarding, playing basketball and golf.

Dr. Yahya Fiteih

DDS, MSc, Cert. OMOP

Dr. Fiteih was born in Cairo, Egypt. He has been practising dentistry since 2003. Dr. Fiteih is an Oral Medicine specialist who joined the TMD/Oral Medicine Clinic in 2017. Dr. Fiteih completed his Oral Medicine specialty training in 2020. His scope of practice includes management of oral mucosal diseases and disorders (including biopsies), temporomandibular joint disorders, orofacial pain, and obstructive sleep apnea.

I enjoy working with patients with chronic oral conditions and chronic pain. The most rewarding thing is when I can diagnose and treat patients who have been in pain and suffering for years.

  • Masters in Medical Sciences, University of Alberta (2014)
  • Joined Oral Medicine Residency Program, University of Alberta (2017)
  • Masters in Medicine, University of Alberta (2022)
  • Certified Oral Medicine and Pathology Specialist, Canada (2022)
