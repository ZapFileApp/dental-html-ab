Dental Services For Lethbridge Residents 

It is obvious that Dr. Conner Hendry and Dr. Mike McKee at Peak Dental have a passion for dentistry whether you visit them for long-term comprehensive care, preventive care or a dental emergency. Our dental services include teeth whitening, teeth cleaning, crowns, fluoride application, root canal therapy, bridges, implants, porcelain veneers and more!  We are committed to improving the oral health of our patients and we do so with a focus on their quality of life. Visit our dental office in Lethbridge and choose from a broad range of dental treatments and a variety of services!

Teeth whitening

Teeth Whitening is a great way to restore the natural colour of your teeth and give you that perfect smile. The doctor will remove stains in one visit. It's a one-time procedure, performed by a dentist in our dental clinic! Visit our dental clinic for complete tooth restoration and good-looking teeth.

Veneers

Peak Dental offers veneers for those looking to improve the appearance of their teeth. Our expert dentists help determine if dental veneers are right for you and provide insight into what the veneers can do for your smile.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a great option for people who have lost teeth or want better chewing ability. Dental implants allow you the option of restoring your healthy smile through stainless steel fixtures that fit securely into place beneath the gum tissue.

Fillings

If you have a cavity, our dentists will remove the decayed portion of your tooth and then fill it in its place with fresh material. Fillings are also used to repair cracked or broken teeth; these treatments help prevent further damage while ensuring that no one can tell there was ever anything wrong!
