About Us

Our Practice Philosophy

At Sixth Avenue Dental, the patient is the most important person in our dental clinic. Our commitment to excellence not only must emphasize the technical skills of dentistry but also reflect strong components of humanism, kindness, and compassion. Only after gaining an insight into the patient’s concerns (his or her dental needs) together with a comprehensive dental examination, proper diagnosis, and appropriate prognosis that an individual treatment plan is prepared and presented to the patient. Consultation with other dental and medical specialists and timely referrals when indicated are part of our continuing commitment to our patient’s oral health. We are very enthusiastic about serving our patient’s every dental need and what is ultimately the most satisfying occupation of all services–the art of care and healing for those in need.

Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Preet's professional career started with completing her dental education and training in 2010. Her practice philosophy is to provide gentle, stress-free dental care and highest quality treatment for her patients in a comprehensive, conservative and comfortable manner. She believes that skill and compassion go hand in hand when providing exceptional patient care. She prides herself on being a lifetime learner and enjoys courses, study clubs and seminars to keep her knowledge up to date. She believes in creating long lasting relationships with her patients through compassion, caring and trust. When not in Scrubs, she enjoys spending quality time with her family, watching movies and reading. She adores the beauty of Canada and enjoys exploring new places and going on easy hikes. She enjoys trying out new foods and restaurants. Dr. Preet, look forward to meeting you soon and helping you achieve optimum oral health.
