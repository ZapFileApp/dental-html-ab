Check-ups and Preventative Dentistry

As family dentists in Chestermere, we specialize in preventative dentistry services and treatments, including regular dental check-ups, hygiene, fluoride treatments, and other basic dental work.

We also care for your children’s baby teeth as well as their permanent teeth. Regular appointments are key in catching any further issues before they become more serious. During these visits, our dentists will conduct a thorough examination and take modern X-rays to look for cavities.

At the same time, our trained dental team can provide advice on brushing, flossing, and rinsing to maintain your oral health between appointments. Together, we can keep your smile bright and healthy!

At Lifepath Dental & Wellness, we are dedicated to providing fast and efficient relief from pain during dental emergencies. If you or someone in your family is suffering from any dental problems, do not hesitate to contact us immediately.
