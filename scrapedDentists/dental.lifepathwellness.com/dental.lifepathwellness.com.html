Welcome to Lifepath
Dental & Wellness

At Lifepath & Wellness, the dental team of Dr. Jed Snatic, Dr. Griffin Myers, Dr. Jean-Marc (Jimmy) Arsenault, Dr. Cocoa Huang, Dr. Kayla Lavallee,  Dr. Erin Wilderman, and Dr. Saurabh Srivastava are dedicated to comprehensive family dental care and cosmetic (restorative) dentistry.

We strive to create a warm, friendly, and welcoming environment to make you feel comfortable during every dental visit.

As part of the Lifepath Dental & Wellness team, our Chestermere dentists and wellness providers offer our clients access to over 300 unique health, wellness, and dental specialty services, all in one location. We are proud to serve the communities of Chestermere, AB.

Book an Appointment with Our Chestermere Dentist Today!

Our Lifepath Dental & Wellness team is pleased to offer all the services of a general dentist, including general family dentistry, emergency dentistry, children’s dentistry, dental implants, orthodontic dentistry, invisalign braces, dental hygiene checkups, and periodontal dentistry. Click here for a list of our available general & family dental services.

When you visit Lifepath Dental & Wellness, we focus on your comfort. From televisions in our reception area and every operatory, as well as a video game station for younger guests to a gourmet hot beverage and water centre for your enjoyment, our team offers you the best possible care in the most comfortable surroundings.

Lifepath Dental & Wellness has been serving the Chestermere community since 2006 and we invite you to come and experience the Lifepath Dental & Wellness difference. Lifepath Dental & Wellness offers healthcare you can trust. We care! Please be sure to read the latest Lifepath Dental & Wellness reviews on Google and Facebook.
