Dr. Preeti Bhardwaj

Dr. Preeti Bhardwaj

Dentist

Dr. Preeti Bhardwaj, known to her patients simply as Dr. Preeti, brings over three decades of experience and compassion to the field of general dentistry. She began her dental journey in India, graduating in 1994, and later earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine degree from Boston University’s Goldman School of Dental Medicine in 2006.

After nearly a decade of practice in Ontario, Dr. Preeti fulfilled her dream of owning a dental practice in Drumheller, Alberta, where she served the community from 2015 until selling the practice in 2023. Though no longer a practice owner, she continues to call Drumheller home.

Dr. Preeti enjoys all aspects of general dentistry, with a particular passion for restorative procedures, fixed prosthodontics, endodontics and pediatric care.  She is also experienced in providing nitrous oxide sedation to ensure patient comfort.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Preeti finds joy in the company of her four beloved dogs, as well as indulging in her hobbies of reading and painting.
