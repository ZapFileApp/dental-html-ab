Our Dental Hygienists

Meet Our Dental Hygiene Team

At Lifepath Dental & Wellness, our dental hygienists are licensed healthcare professionals who are credentialed and registered with the College of Registered Dental Hygienists of Alberta. Each dental hygienist was required to obtain years of formal training and education before being registered as a practitioner. Most jurisdictions require at least a two-year degree in dental hygiene as well as science-related courses as part of the program. A dental hygienist will have taken courses that provide background in oral anatomy, pharmacology, nutrition, and periodontology.

Dental hygienists work closely with our dentists as part of the Lifepath Dental & Wellness team, performing tasks to help with preventive dental care. Our dental hygiene team follows a client-centered philosophy, promoting health and wellness with an unwavering commitment to excellence. Our hygienists offer personalized hygiene treatments while fostering friendly patient relationships that are based on trust and open communication. Their treatment modalities and collaborative approach to dental wellness are centered on a foundation of evidence-based knowledge and years of experience. Their mission is to assist you in achieving and maintaining positive oral health outcomes through prevention, education, and comprehensive care.
