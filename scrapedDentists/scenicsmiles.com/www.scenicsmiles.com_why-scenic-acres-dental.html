Are you looking for a NW Calgary Dentist that is Family Friendly? Then look no further as the Scenic Acres Dental Team is committed to providing you with quality dental care and exceptional customer service that you can count on!

Our friendly and knowledgeable team are here to help you and your entire family work toward optimal dental health and wellness. We are here to treat your dental needs and to ensure every patient has the opportunity for a lifetime of healthy smiles.

10 Great Reasons to choose Scenic Acres Dental Centre!

  1. We welcome ALL new patients to our practice
  2. We have convenient, family-friendly hours
  3. No referral is required to visit our practice as a patient
  4. We electronically Direct Bill to most insurances
  5. We offer comprehensive dental services for both kids & adults
  6. We love to educate our patients about what is happening in their mouths
  7. We have modern, up-to-date equipment
  8. We offer sedation options for anxious or nervous patients
  9. We offer emergency dental care for your family
  10. We just love meeting new patients!

Do You Need More Reasons to Choose Us?

Here are some ways that we add those extra special touches for your comfort and safety!

  1. Our cozy waiting room has a wide variety of the latest magazines and newspapers for our patients’ enjoyment.
  2. Our kid’s play zone is fully equipped with entertainment for kids of all ages, including X-box, toys & coloring books.
  3. Coffee, Tea, Hot Chocolate, and Water are available at our coffee station.
  4. We have blankets and neck pillows for your comfort during your visit.
  5. We use a Digital X-Ray system that reduces your exposure to radiation and provides us with more thorough imaging.
  6. Each Dental Station has an iPad with Netflix and headphones so that you can relax during your procedure.
  7. We follow the Alberta Dental Association and Canadian Dental Association protocols for the sterilization of instruments and handpieces for infection control.
  8. Every sterilization cycle is carefully monitored and logged for your safety.  We participate in the sterilization program with the Provincial Laboratory of Alberta.
  9. Each operatory is equipped with modern Fiberoptic handpieces for a thorough examination.
  10. We have a fun treasure box filled with prizes for the kids after a successful visit.
  11. Emergency care is available on evenings, weekends, and holidays.
  12. Phone, text, or email appointment reminders.
  13. Free Wi-Fi.

Dental Services for Your Entire Family

Scenic Acres Dental Centre offers all the services of a general dentist, including Invisalign Clear Aligners, Family Dentistry, Hygiene/Preventative Dentistry, Children’s Dentistry, Root Canal Therapy, Sedation Dentistry, Wisdom Tooth Extractions, and Restorative (Cosmetic) Dentistry services.

Convenience

Scenic Acres Dental Centre is conveniently located at 8541 Scurfield Dr. NW in Calgary, Alberta. Our office is easily accessible with plenty of free dedicated parking. We are pleased to provide our patients with quality dentistry in a relaxed, friendly, and comfortable environment. We work together as a team, always treating ALL our patients with kindness, integrity, and respect. We also reserve time in our daily schedule for Emergency Dental Appointments.
