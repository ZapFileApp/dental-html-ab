About Luxor Dental

Exquisite Care, Exceptional Smiles

Welcome to Luxor Dental, where we are dedicated to providing top-quality dental care in a friendly and welcoming environment. Located at 4-6791 50th Avenue, Red Deer, Alberta, our family-run, locally owned practice that just opened with Dr’s that have been in practice for over 25 years.

Our Mission

At Luxor Dental, our mission is to deliver exceptional dental care that exceeds your expectations. We strive to create a comfortable and positive experience for every patient, ensuring you feel valued and cared for at every visit. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in everything we do, from our state-of-the-art technology to our personalized approach to patient care.

Our Story

Luxor Dental recently opened with a vision to offer comprehensive dental services in a warm and inviting atmosphere. As a family-owned business, we understand the importance of trust and relationships. Our team is dedicated to building long-lasting relationships with our patients, treating everyone like a member of our own family.

Our Team

Dr. Ahmed Mohamed

Dr. Ahmed is a general dentist, graduated in 1997 and dedicated to advanced post-doctoral study. For more than 25 years, he enjoys practicing dentistry so much and brings a wealth of experience in general dentistry.

Dr. Ahmed makes it a priority to educate patients about their oral health and ensure that they understand their treatment options. Before any procedure, he takes the time to explain the exact issues in detail and how they can be treated. Dr. Ahmed makes patients feel comfortable and relaxed during their visits.

He is a strong believer in continuing education to ensure that his patients receive the highest quality dental care. He constantly strives to keep up to date with the latest advancements in dentistry through continuing education. He focuses on comprehensive and conservative dentistry by using the most up to date technology.

Ahmed currently resides in Red Deer city with his wife and four children. He lives an active lifestyle and enjoys playing table tennis, swimming, fishing and traveling in his spare time.

Dr. Moanes Soliman

Dr. Moanes Soliman completed his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree at Cairo University in 1997. What Dr. Soliman enjoys most in dentistry is working to make every patient happy and helping them maintain a beautiful smile. While he enjoys all aspects of dentistry, he loves teeth restoration and dental surgeries the most. He is a member of the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta (CDSA). A charity Dr. Moanes supports is the Red Deer Food Bank Society. When Dr. Moanes Soliman is not in the office, he enjoys hiking, going to the gym, watching TV shows, and playing chess. Dr. Soliman strongly believes in the power of sharing knowledge with others. He is the father of four loving daughters. Dr. Soliman graduated over 27 years ago and feels that now is the most meaningful time of his professional life
