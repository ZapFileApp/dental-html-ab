About Us

Our Mission

We believe in making dental care something you actually look forward to. At our practice, you’ll find expert care, modern comfort, and transparent pricing that makes sense, because your smile deserves more than just the basics.

Our Vision

To be your trusted dental destination – a place where quality meets personalized care, and every visit feels like a retreat. We’re leaders in redefining what it means to go to the dentist, one confident smile at a time.

Our Story

Poplar Dental was built on a vision to create a fresh, modern, and welcoming space that does not feel like a typical dental office. After working in a variety of clinics, Dr. Perry set out to design a practice centered on kindness, empathy, exceptional care, and most of all, patient comfort.

Poplar first opened its doors in 2020 with our Chinook location. Thanks to incredible support and five-star patient feedback, we quickly grew and opened our second clinic in the growing community of Greenwich in 2023. As our team continues to expand, we remain committed to innovation, empathy, and delivering the kind of dental care you actually look forward to.

Giving Back

At Poplar, community matters. We believe everyone deserves access to quality dental care and feel-good moments, whether they’re in our chair or out in the world.

From sponsoring local events to supporting underserved communities through outreach and donations, we’re proud to give back wherever we can. Our team regularly takes part in volunteer initiatives and charitable partnerships that reflect our commitment to creating healthy, confident smiles.

We're always looking for new ways to make a positive impact. If you know of a local cause or event that could use our support, we would love to hear from you.
