General Dentistry

TMJ Treatment

If you are experiencing jaw pain, tension, headaches, or clicking in the jaw, you may be dealing with TMJ (temporomandibular joint) dysfunction.

We offer a range of non-surgical treatments to help manage symptoms and improve jaw function, including custom-made oral appliances, therapeutic injections, and targeted facial therapy using advanced heat-based technology. Our approach is tailored to your individual needs, with a focus on relieving discomfort and restoring balance to your bite and jaw.
