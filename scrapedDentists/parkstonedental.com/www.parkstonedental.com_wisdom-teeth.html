Meet The Doctors

At Parkstone Dental, we believe you are in control of your oral health and should have a say in your dental care. Our friendly and caring team is by your side to answer all your questions, address your concerns, and help you preserve your beautiful smile through patient education and regular checkups. As your dentist in Sherwood Park, we want you and your family to feel great about every visit you have with us! If you live in Tofield, we have a second location to serve you.
