General Family Dentistry

What is General Family Dentistry?

General family dentistry involves routine preventative care as well as corrective and restorative treatment. We offer a full range of general dentistry procedures that are ideal for every member of the family. At Langdon Dental, our goal is to provide you with a comfortable and relaxing experience while receiving the care and treatment that you need. Dr. Jess Chhokar is fully trained and experienced offering general dentistry procedures of all kinds.

Why do you need routine General Dentistry?

By receiving regular dental care, you will find that your teeth and mouth are healthier. General dental procedures involve both preventative as well as restorative care. Preventative care procedures like cleanings and exams can keep your teeth looking their best. Restorative care is important in correcting dental problems before they are allowed to get worse.

What are Some Common General and Family Dentistry Treatments?

Each patient will need different treatment, so we can customize the care that you receive. The first step is coming into our office for an exam so that we can create a personalized treatment plan that is right for you. Some of the most common procedures done concerning general dentistry include:

• Preventative care
• Fillings
• Crowns and bridge work
• Surgeries and extractions
• Implants
• Dentures and Partials
• Periodontal care

By receiving routine dental care, you will find that your teeth and mouth are healthier. General dental procedures involve both preventative as well as restorative care.

What to Expect at Your Appointment

You will first come in and establish yourself as a patient with an exam performed by Dr. Jess Chhokar. The exam is designed to check for problems relating to the teeth and mouth. We will check your gums for signs of gingivitis and gum disease, allowing us to create a personalized treatment plan as needed. You can feel confident coming to us for all of your dental care. Our goal is to provide personalized care for every member of your family in a calm, comfortable and professional environment. We provide many different treatment options to improve and maintain your oral health.

If you’re in need of care for yourself or your whole family, call our office so that our office staff members can better assist you.

All services are performed by a general dentist.
