Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic Dentistry

When you meet someone, your smile is one of the first things they see. A vibrant smile can not only help make a positive impression, but it makes you feel better about yourself and your appearance. At Highland Dental Clinic, we offer several options for enhancing your smile.

Porcelain Veneers

If you’re unhappy with your smile because of imperfections like discoloration, gaps, cracks, misalignment or wear, porcelain veneers offer an easy, lasting way to create the bright, vibrant smile you’ve always wanted.

Porcelain veneers are a thin layer of porcelain or resin placed over the front of the teeth. They can change the shape, colour and alignment of teeth, and hide flaws like chips, cracks and gaps. And because veneers are very thin, and require little or no removal of tooth structure, they’re one of the least intrusive ways to dramatically improve your smile and your confidence.

Get the smile you deserve. Contact Highland Dental Clinic, and we’ll be happy to discuss the benefits and advantages of porcelain veneers with you.

Full Mouth Reconstruction

Full mouth reconstruction reverses the effects of aging, neglect and damage. Combining all the cosmetic dentistry procedures at our disposal, full mouth reconstruction involves repairing and restoring teeth, gums, bite and jaw muscles to a natural looking, youthful state.

With a full mouth reconstruction, you can undo years of damage to your teeth and mouth and bring back the beautiful smile you’ve been missing. We’ll repair or replace missing teeth with implants, crowns or bridges, place porcelain veneers to protect your teeth and correct any flaws, and treat any muscle or joint problems you may be experiencing because of damaged teeth. Think of it like a renovation for your mouth.

If you’re ready to rebuild a dazzling smile, contact us at Highland Dental Clinic, and we can discuss the best options for putting your smile on the road to recovery.

Single / Multiple Dental Implants

If your smile is marred by missing or damaged teeth, Highland Dental Clinic can help. Dental implants involve replacing missing or damaged teeth by placing an artificially constructed tooth over a post implanted in the jaw. Dental implants are a comfortable, natural-looking way to restore your smile to its full glory.

To discuss how dental implants can make your smile whole again, contact us at Highland Dental Clinic. 

Teeth Whitening

There are a number of things that cause stained teeth, and it’s difficult, if not impossible, to avoid them all. Aside from the aging process, there’s coffee, tea, soda, red wine, smoking, and certain foods – all of which can stain your teeth. This is why many people choose to whiten their teeth.

In-office tooth whitening is a simple and short procedure performed by your dentist. Not only is it safe and comfortable, but the results are immediate and long-lasting – up to eight shades lighter. During the procedure, a whitening gel will be applied to your teeth for three to four 15-minute sessions.

In addition to in-office whitening, we offer take-home whitening kits with custom made trays as well.

Contact Highland Dental Clinic to make an appointment for your teeth whitening.
