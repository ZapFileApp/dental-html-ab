FREE Consultations and Dental Emergencies

Have you chipped a tooth or had some other incident that has damaged your teeth? We provide IMMEDIATE appointments for patients with dental emergencies and dental urgencies. If you want it done NOW, we are your clinic. We also offer FREE dental consultations, allowing you to speak to a member of our dental team about your oral health needs or concerns. We will bill your insurance for you.

 

Ample FREE parking is available at the shopping centre, and our clinic is wheelchair accessible. If you're looking for a dentist in Fort McMurray or if you would like to schedule your next dentist appointment, give us a call today.
