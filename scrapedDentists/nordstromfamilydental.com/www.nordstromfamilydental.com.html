    ABOUT US

    ABOUT US

    Nordstrom Family Dental is a full-service dental practice located in Stettler, Alberta. The practice has been owned and operated by Dr. Patrick Nordstrom since August 2011.

    ​

    With a dedicated team of diligent, friendly staff members, Nordstrom Family Dental is proud to be able to offer comprehensive, compassionate dental care to existing and new patients from Stettler and the surrounding area.

    ​

    Patrick was born and raised in Viking, Alberta, and graduated from the University of Alberta’s dental program in 2008.  He has spent his career practicing dentistry in rural Alberta.
