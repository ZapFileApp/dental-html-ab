Dental Care Under the NIHB Program

Proud NIHB Provider in Airdrie

At Sierra Springs Dental, we are proud to provide dental services for the NIHB Program to all eligible First Nation and Inuit community members. We understand the importance of accessible, culturally respectful dental care and are committed to ensuring every patient feels welcome and valued.

We Handle the Paperwork

Navigating insurance and benefits programs can be overwhelming. That is why our team handles direct billing and assists with all paperwork related to your NIHB claims. We will guide you through the process so you can focus on what matters most, your oral health and well-being.

Comprehensive and Compassionate Care

From routine check-ups and cleanings to fillings, extractions, and more specialized treatments, we provide the full range of dental services covered under NIHB. Our experienced team delivers care with compassion, respect, and a deep commitment to your comfort and health.
