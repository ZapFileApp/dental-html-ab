About Us

Learn more about our practice.

Our dentists personally invite patients to explore what makes the practice unique. At Diamond Valley Dental you'll experience our wonderful dental team and receive the benefits of state-of-the-art technology that helps create and maintain the smile you deserve.

We know many people feel anxious about dental visits. Our mission is to make every step, from arrival through treatment, feel comfortable. We aim to make Diamond Valley Dental your new dental home.

Meet the Doctors

Care from a team you can trust.

Dr. Brock Hall

Owner & Dentist

Dr. Hall grew up in Sundance, South Calgary, and attended Lord Beaverbrook High School. He completed his dental degree at Midwestern University in Glendale, Arizona. He owns and operates both Fish Creek Dental in South Calgary and Diamond Valley Dental.

After working in Texas, Dr. Hall relocated to Calgary in 2020 to raise his family in his childhood community. He has clinical expertise across general dentistry, wisdom teeth extraction, orthodontics (traditional braces and Invisalign), dental implant placement, and IV sedation dentistry.

His philosophy emphasizes a comfortable, welcoming environment built on trust and long-term relationships. He regularly pursues continuing education to remain current with modern techniques.

Outside work, he enjoys mountain biking and outdoor activities with his wife Karli and their two children, Quinn and Cooper.

Dr. Marnus Grobbelaar

Dentist

Dr. Marnus Grobbelaar was born in South Africa, then grew up in both the Middle East and Calgary before moving to Cairns, Australia to complete his Bachelor of Dental Surgery with Distinction at James Cook University. Not long after moving back to Calgary to be closer to his family, Dr. Marnus fell in love with Diamond Valley and the surrounding area. After meeting the dedicated, local team at Diamond Valley Dental he decided this was where he wanted to put down some roots for the long term.

Dr. Marnus is fluent in English and Afrikaans and enjoys working with his patients to build long-lasting relationships to ensure they feel comfortable and well cared for, along all aspects of their dental journey. He enjoys being able to provide patients with a wide range of services to address their specific needs and concerns, with special interests in extractions, root canal therapy and restorative work.

Outside of dentistry, he values time with family and loves any outdoor activities such as golfing, hiking, hunting, fishing and cycling.
