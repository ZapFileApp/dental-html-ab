How it works

Free Consultation

During your FREE orthodontic consultation, Dr. Bullock will discuss your treatment goals, as well as take a 3D scan of your teeth to show you a digital simulation of what your smile can truly look like if you proceed with our treatment plan!

He will also determine which type of orthodontic appliance is best applicable to your specific case. 

The Treatment Process

When you are ready to move forward with your new smile, our dentists will make sure that we make your treatment as comfortable as possible!

Whether it’s braces or Invisalign, Dr. Bullock is dedicated to making sure that you are well informed and educated about all the steps that your teeth are about to go through. 

Periodically, we are going to bring you into Parkway Smiles to make sure that your treatment is going according to plan as well as offer you new trays if applicable. 

Your New Smile

Once your treatment is over and your teeth have reach the desired position, not only will you’ll receive clear retainers to maintain your new smile but we will also whiten your teeth for FREE to give your smile a completely new look!

Our goal is to make sure that your smile receives the make over that it deserves!

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the FREE consultation?

We want to make sure you have a good idea of what your orthodontic condition is and exactly what treatment options may be available for you.

During our hour long orthodontic consultation, you will get an opportunity to tour the office, as well as meet Dr. Bullock and his team. He will then look at your mouth and assess the complexity of your treatment and review what treatment options are available to you. Together with you, he will make sure to discuss any concerns you have when it comes to your personalized treatment plan. 

How early can my child start?

Your child can start their treatment as early as 3 years old! 

Should I wait for my child's adult teeth to come in before considering Orthodontics?

The Canadian Dental Association recommends every child have an orthodontic assessment no later than age 7. That’s because not all orthodontic problems can be treated if we were to wait for all adult teeth to be present. Waiting on treatment can lead to a less than ideal result. Certain orthodontic problems are best treated when children have a combination of adult and baby teeth.

Do you provide financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible financing options such as 0% financing and extended payment plans. We will work with you to create the perfect payment plan.
