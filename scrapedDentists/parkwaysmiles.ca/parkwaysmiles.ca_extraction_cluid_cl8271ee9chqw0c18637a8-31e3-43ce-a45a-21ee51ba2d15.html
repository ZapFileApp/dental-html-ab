Why Tooth Extraction May Be Necessary

Tooth extraction may be recommended for various reasons:

  • Extensive tooth decay
  • Impacted wisdom teeth
  • Overcrowding
  • Tooth abscess and infection
  • Severe tooth trauma
  • Advanced gum disease
  • Cracked or split tooth
  • Extensively damaged tooth

Our skilled dentists assess each case to determine if teeth removal Calgary is the best solution for your oral health. We prioritize minimally invasive techniques to ensure a smooth procedure and quick recovery.
