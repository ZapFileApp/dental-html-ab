Our Locations

Stony Plain, AB

Parkland Aesthetic Denture Clinic is located in the heart of Oldtown Stony Plain. We are a full service Denture Clinic specializing in removable & Implant oral prosthetics. We take pride in providing the best care for our patients by offering the highest quality of products as well as staying on top of all the newest advances in technology. We look forward to meeting you and more importantly getting to restore your smile.

Learn more

Edmonton, AB

Central Aesthetic is a full-service Denture Clinic in the heart of downtown Edmonton. We are conveniently located on 107st with plenty of parking at the rear of our building. We take pride in providing the highest quality of care for our patients. We specialize in highly aesthetic dentures. What sets our clinic apart from the rest is that we spend time on making your smile unique to you by finding out what your true needs are and catering to those needs to give you the smile you have always wanted.
