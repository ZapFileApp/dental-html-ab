OUR CLINIC

Parkland Aesthetic Denture Clinic is located in the heart of Oldtown Stony Plain. We are a full service Denture Clinic specializing in removable & Implant oral prosthetics. We take pride in providing the best care for our patients by offering the highest quality of products as well as staying on top of all the newest advances in technology. We look forward to meeting you and more importantly getting to restore your smile.

OUR MISSION

 At Parkland Aesthetics Denture Clinic, we are committed to improving our patients overall quality of life. We take pride in working closely with our patients to provide convenient quality denture treatment. We believe strongly in offering our customers fair and honest treatment and we go above and beyond to meet the needs of each patient. 

OUR VISION

our vision is to provide our patients with the best patient care. We believe strongly in keeping up to date with the latest technology's in the industry and implementing them in our daily practice. We spend countless hours improving our quality to meet the needs of our patients. 

CORE VALUES

  • Honesty
  • Quality of care
  • Innovation
  • Education 

ONSITE DENTAL LAB

Our denture clinic offers chairside treatments and also has an on-premise fully equipped denture lab. This lab allows us to fabricate complete, partial, and implant-supported dentures for our patients quickly, and effectively as well as any and all other lab procedures.
