Dr. Lisa Loeffelmann, Family Dentist

Proud to Live and Work in Sherwood Park

Dr. Lisa Loeffelmann, B.Sc. D.D.S.

Born and raised in Grande Prairie, Alberta, Dr. Lisa Loeffelmann obtained her Bachelor of Science degree from the University of Alberta, followed by a Doctor of Dental Surgery degree in 2000.

She has worked as a dentist in Sherwood Park since 2001. In 2005 she, and her husband Dr. Seiji Ohuchi, opened Cascade Dental Centre. She enoys practicing many aspects of dentistry, including using neuromodulators(eg. Botox) to treat nighttime teeth grinding and clenching, as well as for cosmetic procedures. She enjoys attending lectures and continuing education programs to learn about the newest advances in dentistry.

Dr. Loeffelmann enjoys playing piano and various sports in her free time. She enjoys doing a variety of sports with her two kids, including indoor climbing, snowboarding, and cross country skiing.

Botox Treatment

Dr. Loeffelmann is a certified Botox practitioner.
