Family Dentistry

Cascade Dental Centre is a calm and relaxing environment, complete with intraoral cameras which allow patients to see up close inside their own mouths and digital x-rays that can reduce radiation exposure when compared to traditional x-rays.

General Dental Care

With a focus on general dental care and a gentle, understanding approach, the staff at Cascade Dental Centre enjoy putting patients at ease.

Maintaining a High Standard of Dentistry

Our office is committed to maintaining a high standard of dentistry and we attend regular continuing education courses to keep up to date on new techniques and materials.

Open Communication

We believe in an open atmosphere where patients feel comfortable discussing their treatment needs. We encourage questions and comments to assist patients in making good choices for themselves. By doing so, we ensure expectations can be met for both patient and the dentist, since it requires effort from both parties to create long lasting healthy smiles.

New Patients Welcome

New patients and emergencies welcome. We accept all approved dental plans.
