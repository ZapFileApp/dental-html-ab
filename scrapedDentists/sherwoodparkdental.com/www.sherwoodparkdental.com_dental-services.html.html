Comprehensive Dental Services

Cascade Dental prides itself on its ability to provide almost any dental procedure. If you require a dental service not listed below please feel free to contact us at 780-467-2030 for direct assistance.

Invisalign

Invisalign invisibly straightens your teeth with a series of custom-molded aligners. Forget everything you've heard about teeth straightening.

  • Learn More

Neuromuscular Dentistry | TMJ

"TMJ", although it has become almost a household word, is a non-specific, catch-all term for a variety of pain/dysfunction conditions of the head and neck. It was originally derived from "Temporomandibular Joint", your jaw joints - once thought to be the source of most of these ailments.

  • Learn More

Cosmetic Dentistry

Create a positive impact on your overall health in addition to improving esthetics. Services include:

  • Porcelain Veneers
  • Cristal Porcelain Veneers
  • Teeth Whitening

Emergency Dentistry

From broken teeth to oral injuries, Cascade Dental Centre has you covered. Remember: "If it hurts, it's an emergency!"

Hygiene/Periodontal Dentistry

Services include:

  • x-rays to check bone levels
  • measuring the depth of periodontal pockets
  • checking for loose teeth or bleeding gums
  • removing plaque or tartar above and below the gum line
  • reviewing your home care techniques and their resulting effectiveness
  • oral cancer screening and checking the tissues of the head and neck

Restorative Dentistry

Reconstructive dentistry procedures that involve replacing or repairing broken or missing teeth, bones, or tissue. These procedures include:

  • mouth reconstruction
  • dental implant restorations
  • TMJ treatment
  • dental bridge placement
  • dental crown placement
  • inlays and onlays
  • replacing old metal fillings

Root Canals

When the pulp of a tooth becomes infected or dies, root canal therapy is required. Cascade Dental can provide a comfortable treatment that can save your tooth.

Dentures

Well-made dentures have both functional and aesthetic benefits:

  • enjoy all kinds of food
  • support for the lips and cheeks, improving one's appearance

Tooth Extractions

Extraction is the ideal treatment option when there is:

  • significant decay rendering it unrestorable
  • gum disease resulting in compromised support for the tooth
  • a fracture extending below the gum line which cannot be repaired
  • an adverse affect on other teeth or a risk of infection (wisdom teeth)
