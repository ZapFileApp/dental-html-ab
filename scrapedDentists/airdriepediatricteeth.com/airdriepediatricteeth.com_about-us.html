About Us

Meet Dr. Ethan, Dr. Adam, and Dr. Jane

Dr. Ethan Zuker

Dr. Ethan Zuker was born and raised in Southern California. He earned his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas School of Dental Medicine, graduating Magna Cum Laude.

He completed his Specialty Certificate in Pediatric Dentistry from Riley Hospital for Children at Indiana University, where he also earned a master’s degree in Dental Science and a Minor in Child Psychology. He had the privilege to serve as Chief Resident and was involved in the specialized care for infants, children, adolescents and patients with special health care needs. His experience also includes the correction of lip and tongue tie, working with lactation consultants and speech therapists.  

Dr. Ethan Zuker is a Fellow of the Royal College of Dentists of Canada and a Diplomate of the American Board of Pediatric Dentistry. 

Dr. Ethan Zuker and his wife, Dr. Genevieve Zuker, are the proud parents of two happy and brilliant girls. When he isn’t in the office, he enjoys spending time with his family, hiking, camping, playing and watching sports, travelling, and especially being a dad. 

Dr. Adam Siray

Dr. Adam Siray was born and raised in the foothills of Millarville, Alberta. He attended the Dentistry program at the University of Alberta, where he received degrees in Doctor of Dental Surgery and a Bachelor of Medical Sciences.  After several years of practicing general dentistry in Calgary, Dr. Siray’s enthusiasm and passion for the care of children brought him to the University of Manitoba where he received his Masters of Dentistry in Pediatric Dentistry and fellowship in Pediatric Dentistry. 

His favorite part of the day is taking the time to chat and getting to know all the kids visiting the clinic, especially finding out their favorite movies!  He especially loves anything Star Wars, and thanks to his daughters, he has a thorough knowledge of all the famous Disney princesses.  He believes communication is essential to creating a calm and trusting atmosphere.

Outside of work, Dr. Adam loves to spend time with his wife Stephanie and his 3 beautiful children Ava, Ciara, and Luke. You can often find them exploring the mountains in the summertime camping, hiking, and biking, and in the winter hitting the slopes on their snowboards.  

Dr. Jane Hendrickson-Rebizant

Dr. Jane Hendrickson-Rebizant was born and raised in Manitoba. After graduating from dental school at the University of Manitoba in 2015, she completed a General Practice Residency at Western University/London Health Sciences Centre, an internship in Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery at McGill University, and a Masters in Dental Sciences at McGill. Between completing her Masters degree and entering a pediatric dentistry residency, Dr. Jane worked as a general dentist in private practice, in nursing stations in Northern Ontario, and in the operating room at Sioux Lookout Meno Ya Win Health Centre. Dr. Jane completed her pediatric specialty at Boston University.

In her spare time, Dr. Jane enjoys reading mysteries, watching F1 and reality tv, keeping her plants alive, getting lost on hiking trails, shopping consignment, and not cooking.
