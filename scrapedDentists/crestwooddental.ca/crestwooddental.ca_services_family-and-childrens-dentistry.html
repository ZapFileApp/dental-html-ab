Family and Children’s Dentistry

Our dental clinic in Medicine Hat creates a friendly atmosphere for your family. We seek to provide a fear-free experience for your children and help develop positive responses to dental care that will serve them well as adults.

Your child and family are in great hands

We know how important your children and family’s comfort and confidence is to you. Rest assured, our friendly team will do everything we can to ensure your child and family feel safe and at ease in our home.

In our experience with pediatric dentistry, we’ve found that children respond well when exposed to a compassionate and positive introduction to dentistry. They are more likely to grow up maintaining good oral health habits—it’s a win-win!

Our approach to family and children’s dentistry is to create a warm and welcoming space. We want to provide you with exceptional dental care in a pleasant setting each and every visit. With our pediatric dentistry clientele, we are dedicated to the complete oral health of children—from toddlers to teens.

Essential pediatric dentistry care

Proper dental care is essential for children. Without it, they risk the chance of possible oral decay and disease that can lead to major complications. Examining and treating children in a calm and relaxing environment will ease them into the process of receiving various treatments.

Crestwood Dental Clinic welcomes the whole family. From a child’s first teeth cleaning to fitting a teen with braces to denture adjustments for seniors—we’re here to serve you. When children are accommodating their experience with dental procedures will become more favourable. During a pediatric dental exam, there shouldn’t be a feeling of stress or intimidation. We work with parents and children to ensure their patience and understanding of dental care is not as terrifying as it seems!

Parents can feel at ease knowing their children are in knowledgeable hands and excellent care with our doctors at Crestwood Dental Clinic.

Have questions about proper care for your family’s teeth? Call us to book an appointment today – 403-526-0777.
