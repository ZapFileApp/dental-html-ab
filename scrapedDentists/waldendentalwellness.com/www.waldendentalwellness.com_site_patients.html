Patient Information

Read on to learn more about our clinic and what you can expect once you've been referred by your dentist, along with payment and financing options.

Please note that a referral from your general dentist is required to schedule an appointment at our clinic.

Contact Us

Your First Visit

Please arrive about 10 minutes early so that you have enough time to fill out a New Patient form. 

Initial Examination

We will begin by reviewing the medical and dental history provided by your referring dentist, along with any relevant notes or imaging. We’ll then conduct a comprehensive specialist examination of your teeth, gums, bite, and jaw, followed by any additional imaging or tests needed to complete our assessment.

Treatment Plan

Based on your history and specialist examination, we will create a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs. This plan will be coordinated with your referring dentist to ensure seamless, long-term care. We’ll also review our recommendations with you and answer any questions about your oral health or treatment options.

Insurance & Financing

At Walden Dental Wellness, we'll help you navigate our options for payment, insurance, and financing so you can focus on achieving optimal oral health.

Insurance Claims

We can direct bill your insurance company and our administrative team is happy to assist you in understanding your insurance plan better.

Payment Methods

The full treatment cost must be paid at the time of your appointment. We accept payment by cash, debit or most major credit cards.

Financing

We offer financing options at our dental clinic. Contact us for more details.

Support for Anxious Patients

Anxiety or fear around dental appointments is very common and it can prevent people from getting the dental care they need. If this sounds like you, don't fret.

The team at Walden Dental Wellness has a variety of practices in place, including dental sedation options, to help you relax during your procedure.

Office FAQS

Read the FAQs to learn more about what to expect at Walden Dental Wellness. 

  • What features does your clinic offer?

    Our clinic offers a number of features, including:

      • Convenient Location & Hours
      • Free On-Site Parking
      • Accessible by Public Transit
      • Wheelchair Accessible
      • Comfortable Seating & Entertainment
      • Same-Day Appointments
      • Support for Anxious Patients
      • Multiple Appointment Bookings for Families
      • Direct Billing to Insurance Companies
      • Automated or Phone Appointment Reminders
  • How do I cancel or reschedule an appointment?

    To cancel or reschedule an appointment, please call us. Please let us know about any change of plans as soon as you can, so we can offer the space in our schedule to another patient.

  • Do you offer appointment reminders?

    In the days before your appointment, you will receive automated phone, text or email appointment reminders from us. If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please call us as soon as possible to let us know.

  • Does your clinic have on-site parking?

    Yes, we have free on-site parking available for all our patients. We are also easily accessible by public transit. 

  • Do you provide services in any languages other than English?

    Yes, our office is able to provide dental services in several languages including Spanish, Hindi, Punjabi and Urdu.

    If you have a language preference, please advise us when booking your appointment.

Dental Specialists in Calgary

If you have severe periodontal disease or need oral surgery or another specialized dental procedure, Walden Dental Wellness is here to help. With a dentist referral, we provide comprehensive dental care to meet your needs.
