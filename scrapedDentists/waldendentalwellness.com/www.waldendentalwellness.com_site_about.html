About Walden Dental Wellness

Walden Dental Wellness is a specialty dental clinic that accepts patients by dentist referral. We provide a broad range of complex dental services for patients in Calgary. Welcome to our clinic.

Contact Us

Meet the Walden Dental Wellness Team

Our dentists and specialists are here to welcome you to Walden Dental Wellness – a referral-only specialty practice. Our friendly, relaxed, and professional dental practice is passionate about building strong relationships with our patients and their oral health.

We focus on providing comprehensive specialty dental care. We provide a range of dental services that are designed to enhance, repair, and help preserve your oral health and the beauty of your smile.

Treatments include porcelain veneers, crowns, and implants, and root canal therapy. We also provide sedation dentistry to patients who deal with dental anxiety, sensitivity, or are about to undergo a lengthy procedure; talk to our team to learn more. Finally, if you’re in need of immediate care, don’t hesitate to come into our clinic.

Our dentists will do their best to see you as quickly as they can and get your smile back on the right track.
