Medical Lake Dental Implants

Restore your smile and confidence with dental implants, the modern solution for replacing missing teeth and preserving jawbone health.

Restore your smile with dental implants in Medical Lake

For years, dentures and bridges have been the go-to solutions for replacing missing teeth. However, if you’ve lived with dentures, you know how challenging it can be to speak, eat, and smile comfortably. Fortunately, dental implants offer a game-changing solution. This page is designed to help you better understand dental implants and the transformative benefits they provide.

In the past, replacing one or two missing teeth often required a bridge, which involved grinding down healthy tooth structure. Implants eliminate the need for this. The illustration here shows the key differences between a bridge and an implant, making it clear why implants are the superior choice.

A dental implant consists of a titanium screw placed in the jawbone to securely hold a crown or denture. This technology has transformed the lives of many patients at Chandler Dental. With implants, there’s no need to rely on adhesives or suction to keep dentures in place, and there’s no need to grind healthy teeth into stubs to anchor a replacement tooth.

The implant process is straightforward and involves minimal discomfort and downtime. Most patients experience little to no discomfort during or after the procedure. Implants are composed of three parts: the titanium screw that is placed into the jawbone, the abutment that connects to the implant, and the crown that replicates the natural tooth. The accompanying illustration demonstrates how closely an implant mimics the structure and function of a real tooth.

At Chandler Dental, we understand the frustration dentures can cause. While upper dentures often benefit from suction to improve comfort, many patients find both upper and lower dentures difficult to tolerate. Dr. Chase Davis specializes in placing implants and designing dentures that stay securely in place. We offer a variety of implant-retained denture options, including hybrid dentures, All-on-4 dentures, and locator-supported dentures. Each option has unique benefits, costs, and considerations. Schedule a complimentary exam to evaluate your specific case and get answers to all your questions from Dr. Davis.

Implants are also a great option for preventing bone loss in the jaw. When teeth are removed, the supporting bone deteriorates, leading to two significant issues:

  1. Loss of supporting bone for neighboring teeth, which can cause sensitivity, looseness, and aesthetic concerns.

  2. A sunken appearance in the cheeks, contributing to premature aging, as shown in the accompanying photo.

Dental implants revolutionize tooth replacement by preserving bone structure. Once an implant is placed and stabilized, bone loss is minimal. If you’re considering dental implants, call us today to begin your journey to a healthier, more confident smile.
