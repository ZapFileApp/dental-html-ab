Flexible payment options for every smile

We believe high-quality care should be accessible and stress-free, which is why we offer a variety of payment options. We accept all insurance policies, including Delta Dental, Blue Cross, Regence, Blue Shield, MetLife, Cigna, and more. If you have questions about your coverage or payment options, give us a call—we’re happy to help.

For guests without insurance, we offer flexible payment options to ensure you can receive the care you need. We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express. We also offer an in-house dental plan through Dental Menu, which provides instant coverage, includes preventive care like cleanings, exams, and X-rays, and offers up to 25% off additional treatments.

To help make treatment more affordable, we partner with trusted healthcare financing providers:

• Cherry – A flexible, patient‑friendly payment plan option that offers fixed monthly payments and no hidden fees. Cherry provides personalized repayment plans so you can get the treatment you need now and pay over time with predictable payments. Applying is fast and simple, and you can complete the process online or with assistance from our team.

• CareCredit – A healthcare payment plan option that allows you to pay for treatment over time with convenient monthly payments. You can apply for CareCredit online or in our office, and our team is happy to help you through the process.
