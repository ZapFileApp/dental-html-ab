Meet our Dental Office Team

Our dedicated team of doctors and staff is committed to providing exceptional care with a personal touch. From the front desk to the treatment room, every member of our team works together to create a welcoming, comfortable experience for you and your family.

Meet Dr. Bushman

Lead Dentist

Dr. Ryan Bushman combines skill, service, and a spirit of adventure in everything he does. A Quincy native, he earned his undergraduate degree at Eastern Washington University before completing his dental degree at the Arizona School of Dentistry and Oral Health. Committed to both family and community, Dr. Bushman and his wife chose to settle in the greater Spokane area, where they raise their five children surrounded by extended family and the outdoor lifestyle they love.

Beyond the office, Dr. Bushman is always on the move—whether skiing, boating, or exploring the beauty of the Inland Northwest with his family. His dedication extends beyond dentistry, as he proudly serves in the Army National Guard, Dental Corps, providing care to fellow service members. Whether treating patients in the office or serving those in uniform, Dr. Bushman is passionate about helping people smile with confidence.

Meet Dr. McDonald

Partner Dentist

Dr. Rachelle McDonald was born and raised in Ephrata, WA, and always knew she wanted to give back to communities like the one she grew up in. She earned dual degrees in Biology and Chemistry from Eastern Washington University, then went on to the University of Washington School of Dentistry as part of the RIDE program—an education track designed to train dentists to care for rural and underserved communities across the Pacific Northwest. Through her clinical rotations, Dr. McDonald developed a deep appreciation for building trust and relationships with those she serves.

After graduation, she and her husband Josh returned to Spokane, where they now call home. Today, she feels incredibly grateful to serve the Medical Lake community alongside a team that shares her passion for patient-first care. Dr. McDonald is known for her gentle approach, thoughtful explanations, and genuine investment in every guest she sees.

We are thrilled to share that Dr. Rachelle McDonald and her husband, Josh, are expecting their first baby this month! We couldn’t be happier for them as they step into this wonderful new chapter of life. Please join us in wishing them all the love, joy, and sweet moments that come with parenthood. No stress or worries, Dr. McDonald will be returning to the office in Spring 2026, and we can’t wait to welcome her back then! 

Meet Dr. Buck

Partner Dentist

Dr. Garrett Buck is a Northwest native with a heart for people and a passion for both artistry and precision. Raised in Bellevue, Washington, he grew up exploring the Seattle scene, skateboarding with friends, playing soccer, and jamming in his high school rock band. That creative spirit continues today—when he’s not in the office, you’ll often find him producing original music with professional vocalists as his favorite outlet.

Dr. Buck discovered his calling in dentistry early on, recognizing it as the perfect blend of science, skill, and artistry. He earned his bachelor’s degree in Biochemistry from the University of Washington before earning his dental degree from Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. He returned to Washington in 2018, where he planted roots, met his wife Kamila, and welcomed their daughter, Ellie. Today, he feels grateful to serve the Spokane Valley community with compassionate, detail-driven care that's always personalized.

Meet Dr. Hermes

Partner Dentist

We are excited to introduce the newest member of our dental team, Dr. Jake Hermes! Born and raised right here in Spokane, Dr. Jake is thrilled to return home and care for the community that means so much to him. After earning his undergraduate degree in Biology from Carroll College in Helena, MT, Dr. Jake went on to complete his Master’s degree at Midwestern University in Arizona, then his Doctor of Dental Medicine at Midwestern University in Illinois. Along the way, he met his wife, Hannah, while in Helena, and the two are happy to be back in their hometowns to build their life together.

Outside of the office, Dr.Jake and Hannah enjoy all that the Northwest has to offer. Camping, hiking, golfing, and snowboarding keep them busy year-round. His warm personality and commitment to patient care make him a perfect fit for our practice, and we’re confident our patients will feel at ease under his care.

Our doctors at Medical Lake Family Dental are skilled, compassionate professionals dedicated to providing top-tier care with a personal touch. With advanced training and a passion for patient-centered dentistry, they create customized treatment plans that prioritize both health and confidence. Backed by a warm and knowledgeable team, we ensure every visit is comfortable, efficient, and tailored to your needs.
