Welcome to Our South Calgary Dental Practice

Welcome to Legacy Dental Care, located in South Calgary. We are happy you found us and that you are considering our dental team to look after your family’s oral health and smiles.

A Family-Friendly Experience in South Calgary

A boutique dental practice, Legacy Dental Care is owned and operated by three dentists who are also brothers – Dr. Sandon Wiedemann, Dr. Laiton Wiedemann, and Dr. Jayde Wiedemann. We strive to make families feel welcome and enjoy treating the multigenerational patients in our practice.

We welcome patients from their very first dental visit at age one and customize dentistry to meet every life stage. Whether you are a new family just starting out, or you are caring for an elder’s health, we invite you to become a patient in our practice.

Our Boutique Legacy Dental Practice

By implementing some of the latest technology and making dentistry comfortable for patients, we hope that your dental appointments are something you will look forward to.

We enjoy getting to know our patients and developing long-term relationships based on trust. We always tailor treatment to meet your needs and goals.

Our dental office was featured in Lonny Magazine in recognition of its unique atmosphere and design. Take a look and see why Legacy Dental Care stands out from your typical dental office!

Here When You Need Us Most

If you experience a dental emergency, you will likely want prompt care to relieve tooth pain. We reserve time in our daily schedules to look after broken teeth, toothaches, and damaged dentistry so you can minimize missed time at work or school.

Our dentists also offer advanced procedures such as extractions and root canals right here in our Calgary dental office.

Educating and Partnering with Patients

Rather than selling dentistry, we see ourselves as educators and partners in your oral health. We use intraoral cameras and photographs to give you an accurate picture of your oral health. By presenting every treatment option and the expected outcomes, we will help you make an educated choice regarding the future of your oral health.

Our dentists and hygienists will demonstrate proper oral care techniques to children and adults alike so that you get the most out of your home care.

Alberta Fee Guide Participants

To eliminate confusion when planning finances, we comply with the Alberta Fee Guide – a suggested fee list developed by the Alberta Dental Associate & College in 2017. You can read about suggested fees on their website.

Direct billing improves the convenience of budgeting for your dental treatment.

We proudly serve communities in the Calgary area, including Legacy, Silverado, Chaparral, Walden and many others in south Calgary. To book an appointment, you can reach us at (587) 625-9222. To contact us via email, visit our Contact page.

On behalf of our dentists and the Legacy Dental Care team, we look forward to welcoming your family.
