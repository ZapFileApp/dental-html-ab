Dr. Mary Karolin, DDS

Dr. Mary Karolin is proud to serve the Leduc community as the principal dentist at Trinity Family Dental. She earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) degree from the prestigious University of Alberta in 2017 and is a proud member of the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta, the Alberta Dental Association, and the Canadian Dental Association. She gained vast experience in the Industry as a practicing Dentist abroad for extensive years.

Dr. Karolin practices all aspects of general dentistry, providing comprehensive care that ranges from restorative treatments to oral surgery, endodontics, and pediatric dentistry. She is well-versed in modern dental technologies and continually advances her skills through ongoing continuing education, ensuring her patients benefit from the latest techniques and evidence-based care.

Known for her gentle approach and genuine compassion, Dr. Karolin strives to make every patient feel comfortable, respected, and fully supported throughout their dental experience. She is committed to educating her patients, empowering them to make confident decisions about their oral health at every stage of life.

Outside the clinic, Dr. Karolin enjoys spending quality time with her husband, two children, and their beloved fur baby. She loves cooking, reading, and gardening. However, her greatest passion is traveling the world and immersing herself in different cultures.

Dr. Mary Karolin looks forward to welcoming you and your family to Trinity Family Dental and helping you achieve a healthy, confident smile for years to come!
