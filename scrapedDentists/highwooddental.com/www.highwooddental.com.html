Your High River General & Family Dentist!

Welcome to Highwood Dental, we are your High River holistic dental care provider proudly serving the High River community for over three decades! Conveniently located at 19A 3 Ave SE, we offer a full range of family, general and cosmetic dental services, including restorative and preventative dentistry, to patients of ALL ages. We are pleased to offer complimentary on-site parking, as well as early morning appointments for added convenience. We look forward to seeing you soon and hope we can work together on your road to optimal oral health and wellness.

Comprehensive General Dental Care Designed to Fit All Your Family's Needs!

At Highwood Dental, our ultimate goal is to promote optimum oral health and wellness, in a warm, welcoming and comfortable environment. The entire Highwood Dental team is dedicated to delivering effective and fully responsive services that are tailored to the unique wants and needs of each patient. Whether you have a planned procedure or require urgent support for a dental emergency, our team is here to provide you with the best care possible.

From routine cleanings, checkups and restorative dentistry like crowns, bridges and fillings, to emergency dentistry, extractions, orthodontics and cosmetic dental services like teeth whitening, veneers and root canal therapy, we offer a comprehensive list of services to meet ALL your dental needs in one convenient location.

Our mission is to keep your family’s oral health at peak level. Whatever your needs, our team of dental professionals is committed to providing affordable, quality services with the latest technologies and procedures at our comfortable clinic.

The Dental Health of you and your family is our top priority!

Family Dentistry

Choosing a family dentist is serious business; after all, your dentist will be part of your family for years to come! From infants and toddlers, through adolescence, adulthood and beyond, Highwood Dental treats patients of ALL ages and stages of life

Wisdom Teeth Extractions

Although adult teeth are meant to last a lifetime, there are some situations where their extraction is the best course of action.

Porcelain Veneers

Do you want a Hollywood ready, red-carpet smile? A red-carpet ready smile is often the work of a dentist and veneers! If you want to take stained, chipped, cracked or crooked teeth and turn them into perfect pearly whites, porcelain veneers may be the ideal choice.

Dental Crowns

If you are looking for a way to restore the overall aesthetics and function of damaged or discoloured teeth, dental crowns might be right for you.

Dental Bridges

If you are feeling self-conscious because of missing teeth, or becoming frustrated because of trouble chewing, speaking and even biting, a dental bridge might be right for you.

Tooth Colour Match Fillings

If you are worried about tooth damage or decay, a tooth colour match filling may be the answer. Damage or decay is most often seen in the form of a cavity, which are very common in patients young and old.

Dental Hygiene & Teeth Cleanings

Healthy teeth and gums are the bedrock of a healthy mouth and a beautiful smile! That is why Highwood Dental is pleased to offer dental hygiene and teeth cleaning appointments to help you achieve optimal oral health and wellness.

Dental Implants

If missing teeth are causing you to feel self-conscious, or making eating and chewing difficult, it is time to fix the problem and consider dental implants.
