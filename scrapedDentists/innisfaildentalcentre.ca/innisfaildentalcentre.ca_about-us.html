About Us

Patient Focus and Excellent Care at Innisfail Dental Centre

At Innisfail Dental Centre, patient comfort comes first. We have created a relaxing environment where every member of the family will feel welcome. We are also strong advocates of good patient communication and believe it to a fundamental component to any good dental practice.

Dr. Rit Donga and his team are happy to answer any questions you might have about your dental health. We invite you to contact our office at anytime to discuss your oral health concerns.

For your convenience, we can speak the following languages at the office: English, Ukrainian, Tagalog, Gujarati and Hindi
