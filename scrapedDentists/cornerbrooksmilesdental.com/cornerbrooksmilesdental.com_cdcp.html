About the plan

What is the Canadian Dental Care Plan?

The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) is a federal government program that provides dental coverage to eligible Canadians who do not have access to private dental insurance. Administered through Sun Life Financial on behalf of the Government of Canada, the plan covers a wide range of dental services.

Eligible Canadians can access preventive, restorative, and emergency dental care at no cost or reduced cost depending on household income.

Am I eligible? What's covered?

Most uninsured Canadians qualify. Here's a quick overview — our team will confirm your exact coverage at your visit.

Eligibility requirements

  • Canadian residents with a valid SIN
  • No private dental insurance coverage
  • Adjusted family net income under $90,000
  • You or a family member filed taxes last year

What's covered

  • Preventive care (cleanings, exams, X-rays)
  • Restorative care (fillings, extractions)
  • Dentures and prosthodontics
  • Endodontic services (root canals)
  • Periodontal treatments
  • Oral surgery procedures

Frequently asked questions

Does Cornerbrook Smiles accept CDCP?

Yes — we are enrolled with the Canadian Dental Care Plan and can bill Sun Life directly on your behalf.

What do I need to bring?

Bring your CDCP member card or confirmation letter, a valid government-issued ID, and your SIN. Our front desk will handle the rest.

Is there a co-pay?

Co-pays depend on your household income. Families earning under $70,000 pay no co-pay; those between $70,000–$90,000 pay 40%. We'll confirm your coverage before treatment.

How do I sign up for CDCP?

Apply through Service Canada online or by calling 1-833-537-4342. Once approved, you'll receive a member card in the mail.
