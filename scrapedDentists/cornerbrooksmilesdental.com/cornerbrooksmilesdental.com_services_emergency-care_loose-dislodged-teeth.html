Emergency Care

Loose or dislodged teeth

Gentle stabilization to give the tooth its best chance of being saved.

Book Appointment(403) 300-4444

Part of Emergency Care

7 treatments offered

Overview

Trauma — a fall, a sports injury, a hard bite — can loosen a tooth without knocking it out. Gentle, prompt stabilization gives it the best chance of healing back into place.

What's included

Repositioning if the tooth has shifted
Soft-wire splinting to keep it stable while it heals
Imaging to rule out root or jaw fracture
Vitality monitoring over follow-up visits

Why patients choose us for this

  • 1

    Higher chance of saving the natural tooth

  • 2

    Prevents misalignment of neighbouring teeth

  • 3

    Comfortable splint that doesn't interfere with daily life

What to expect at your visit

  1. 1

    We listen first.

    Tell us your concerns, history, and goals. We'll ask questions and take any necessary x-rays or scans.

  2. 2

    We explain your options.

    You'll get an honest assessment, all viable treatment options, and a transparent estimate before any work starts.

  3. 3

    We treat you with care.

    Gentle hands, modern tools, and sedation if you want it. You stay in control the whole time.
