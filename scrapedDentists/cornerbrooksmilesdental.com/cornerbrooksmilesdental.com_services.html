Emergency Care

Same-day appointments. Walk-ins welcome.

Dental emergencies don't wait — and neither do we. Our team is available 7 days a week, 8 am to 8 pm, to relieve pain and protect your smile when minutes matter. We bill insurance directly and offer flexible payment options to keep urgent care accessible.

  • Severe toothache from decay or infection
  • Chipped or broken teeth
  • Knocked-out teeth
  • Loose or dislodged teeth

Gum Disease & Bad Breath

Healthier gums. Fresher breath. Confidence restored.

Gum disease is the most common cause of tooth loss in adults — and a frequent culprit behind chronic bad breath. We treat the cause, not just the symptoms, with care plans tailored to the stage of disease.
