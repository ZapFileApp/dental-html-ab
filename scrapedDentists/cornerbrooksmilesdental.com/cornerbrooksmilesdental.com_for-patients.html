Your First Visit

The beginning of your optimal-oral-health journey.

Your first appointment at Cornerbrook Smiles Dental is about listening, learning your goals, and giving you a clear picture of your oral health today. We'll do an oral assessment and take digital x-rays where helpful, then build a treatment plan that fits your needs and your schedule.

Our team prioritizes comfort and comprehension — we'll explain everything clearly, answer your questions, and never recommend treatment we wouldn't choose for our own family.

What to bring

  • Photo ID and your insurance card (if applicable)
  • List of current medications and any allergies
  • Recent dental records or x-rays, if you have them
  • Your completed online forms (or arrive 10 minutes early)
