About Us

Dr. Kara Bagdan, Dr. Natali Schindler, and the rest of the dedicated staff provide comprehensive dental treatment. Our Banff general dental office is committed to providing our best care to all of our patients. We love what we do, and are proud to serve this beautiful community.

We welcome all patients to our family-focused practice. At Bite Family Dentistry in Banff, AB, we focus on sustaining the health and function of our patient’s smiles; from those with their first teeth to those looking to replace teeth with prosthetics and restorations such as crowns and bridges. As members of an active community, we provide custom mouth guards and emergency dental services whenever necessary. 

Dental care should begin at a young age. Our practice encourages parents to bring their children in for treatment. We take the time to ensure they are at ease, working at their pace to ensure they have a positive first dental visit.

We take the time to build lasting relationships with our patients. Each individual receives an in-depth consultation of their oral health concerns and aesthetic needs, and develop a treatment plan if needed. At Bite Family Dentistry, we are focused on your smile’s continued health and beauty by providing necessary preventive services as well as cosmetic dental treatments. 

Whether you’re just visiting Banff, AB, and find yourself with a dental emergency, or a member of the community and want care for your smile, we have the treatments you need. Contact the Banff dental practice of Dr. Kara Bagdan to schedule an appointment today!

We cater to individual smiles and work with trusted specialists so our patient’s smiles can be healthier for longer. Dr. Kara Bagdan understands the importance of finding the right family dentist, and works to address everyone’s unique concerns.
