Our story

A healthy smile is the real definition of a perfect smile.

We believe oral health plays an important role in helping people feel better, function better, and protect their overall wellbeing.

Why Kynd

Reasons patients actually stay.

01
Unhurried, considered care.
Real conversations, no rushed chair time, ever. Every appointment respects the person in the chair, not the schedule.
02
Full spectrum, in-house.
Hygiene to implants to cosmetic injectables, all under one roof. No specialist waitlists, no piecing care together across three buildings.
03
Modern technology, quietly applied.
Digital scans replace clay molds. Same-day appliance printing skips the two-week temporary. Comprehensive imaging catches issues early.
04
A routine to look forward to.
Soothing scents, tropical plants, under-eye patches during cleanings. The space respects your senses as much as your teeth.
05
Open communication.
Multiple paths forward for every treatment. Transparent pricing in writing before anything begins. We help with insurance and explain every option.
06
Same-day emergencies.
Pain doesn’t wait. Same-day emergency appointments are available across both Kynd locations during business hours.

Oral Wellbeing · Shaping Your Future

Your mouth is the gateway to your body.

Most people think about longevity through diet, sleep and exercise. Oral wellbeing sits right there alongside them, directly connected to your sleep quality, nutrition and healthspan.

Research continues to show connections between oral health and inflammation, diabetes management, sleep quality, and cardiovascular health. The full spectrum of care at Kynd exists for that reason. We believe oral health plays an important role in helping people feel better, function better, and protect their overall wellbeing.
