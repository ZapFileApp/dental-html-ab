Meet Erna

ErnaDental Assistant

Hi there, my name is Erna. I graduated in the Dental Assisting Program in Victoria, B.C., with additional Assisting certifications in orthodontics, prosthodontics, Botox, CPR and OH+S. I bring 30+ years of dental experience to the team.

Originally from Prince George, B.C. I have called St Albert home for the past 27 years. I am proud to serve the community of St. Albert since joining Dentus Family Dental in 2022.
Whether I am preparing for a procedure or chatting with you about your day, my priority is to ease dental anxiety by making you comfortable, informed, and heard.

Outside of the office, I enjoy exploring the local trails, Pilates, sewing/crocheting items to donate to Craft for YEG, watching murder mysteries (not creepy) and spending time with family.
