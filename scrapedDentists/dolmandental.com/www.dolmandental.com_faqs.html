Are families and children able to be treated at Dolman Dental?

Of course! Dolman Dental is a family dental clinic, and we are happy to see every member of your family. As a family practice, we treat children, adults and seniors. 

Why are regular dental checkups important?

Regular dental appointments can identify oral health issues before they become serious. With our comprehensive dental examination and x-rays, we can diagnose cavities and any underlying concerns. Our dentists are also trained to provide comprehensive oral cancer screenings.

Our dentists and dental hygienists also give you the knowledge to properly brush, floss and take care of your dental and oral health.

Do you accept emergency appointments?

We are an appointment-based practice, but we understand that emergencies occur, so we are able to accommodate emergency patients.

Do you direct bill to insurance companies?

Our office is happy to work with your insurance company. We offer direct billing for most insurance companies, so you don't have to worry about the entire cost of the appointment.

What should I bring to my first appointment at Dolman Dental?

Bring your insurance and a list of any medications you are taking. We may need to acquire your dental history from your previous dentist's office. Also, bring any questions or concerns you have, and we will be able to address them during your first dental appointment.
