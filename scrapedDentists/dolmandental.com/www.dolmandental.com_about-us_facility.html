Our Dental Clinic in Lacombe

Home - About Us - Office & Facility

 

The Lacombe dentists at Dolman Dental want to help make your dentist appointment as comfortable as possible. Our fully equipped clinic has advanced dental equipment allowing us to utilize the dental techniques that will maintain your oral and overall health. Some of our sedation therapy options include the Wand. It is a computer-controlled anesthetic delivery system and is much less intimidating than a traditional syringe needle. Most importantly, it can be very effective in reducing dental anxiety.

Our office is conveniently located just north of the Lacombe Memorial Centre in downtown Lacombe. As a result, we are able to provide dental services for patients across Central Alberta.

There is free parking in front of and behind our building and across the street. Our office is wheelchair accessible, as there is an elevator.
