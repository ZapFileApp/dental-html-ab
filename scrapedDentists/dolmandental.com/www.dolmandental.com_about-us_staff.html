Our Dentists

Dr. Lauren McLeod

Lauren has a big heart and wants to help whenever she can. She deeply cares about every patient and strives to provide the best care for everyone. Lauren enjoys all aspects of dentistry. She took her dental training at the University of Alberta and did an extra year of training in a General Practice Residency program at the U of A hospital. Lauren grew up in central Alberta and is proud to still call it home.              

Dr. Lisa Barr

Lisa was born and raised in central Alberta and has been surrounded by dentistry her whole life – with both her dad and older brother being dentists and sharing their passion for the profession. Lisa enjoys all aspects of dentistry, particularly working with our pediatric patients, and is always striving to provide the best care for our community. She loves living and working in Lacombe with her husband, and being part of the team at Dolman Dental. Outside of the dental office, Lisa enjoys spending time with her friends and family, playing sports, and travelling

Dr. Judy Kim

Judy grew up in Cold Lake, AB. From a very young age, she knew she wanted to pursue a career in dentistry. She completed her post-secondary education in Saskatoon at the University of Saskatchewan. During this time, she obtained her Bachelors of Science in Physiology and Pharmacology with Great Distinction, and her Doctor of Dental Medicine. Judy enjoys many different aspects of dentistry, but has a particular interest in pediatrics and restorative dentistry. Outside of work, you will find Judy travelling with her fiancé, Miles and spending time with family and friends (and her cat, Nylah). 

Dr. Adam McCourt

Adam grew up in the nearby town, Drayton Valley. He always loved visiting Lacombe to play sports as a kid and is excited to be a part of the community with his wife. Adam performed his dental training at the University of Alberta which was followed by a one-year General Practice Residency at Dalhousie University in Halifax. Adam strives to provide the highest level of care he can to his patients. In his free time, Adam enjoys golfing, playing tennis, reading, and spending time with friends and family.
