Children's Dentistry in Red Deer, AB

Children's Dentist Near You

At Delta Dental, we love taking care of kids’ smiles! We realize trips to the dentist may be stressful for kids, but we make each appointment fun and calming. All of our dental team is trained to treat kids of all ages. Through children’s dentistry in Red Deer, AB, we help your child learn good brushing and flossing habits that will keep their teeth strong for life.
Healthy baby teeth are very important. They assist in helping your child to eat, talk clearly, and leave space for adult teeth to grow properly. If children start visiting the dentist early, they realize that it is a friendly place. This makes them feel confident in dental care as they grow older.

What is Children's Dentistry?

Children’s dentistry is specialized dental care designed for kids. This type of dentistry focuses on the health of your kid’s teeth and gums when their first tooth comes in and up to their teenage years. Our dentists are able and skilled in helping kids feel safe and comfortable when visiting.
Our dentist in Red Deer, AB, uses gentle methods and explains everything in a fun, easy way so children are not scared.

Children's Dentistry Services We Offer

  • Dental Exams & Cleanings : Regular checkups allow us to find small problems before they become big ones. During an exam, we gently check each tooth and the gums. Our children’s dentist near you also helps remove any plaque and tartar buildup that brushing might not be able to clean. To keep your child’s smile bright and healthy, they most likely need a cleaning every six months.
  • Dental Fillings : Kids sometimes get cavities from eating sweet food or not brushing enough. A cavity is a small hole in the tooth. We fix cavities with dental fillings. We will remove the decay first and fill it with a safe material. This prevents further decay in cavities and strengthens the tooth once again.
  • Kids’ Night Guard : Some children grind their teeth when they sleep. This is called bruxism. Grinding can cause jaw and tooth pain. To protect their teeth at night, children wear a soft rubber cover called a night guard. It prevents the teeth from grinding and fits them comfortably.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can veneers stain like regular teeth?
Veneers resist stains much better than natural teeth. However, you should still avoid too much intake of coffee, tea, or smoking to keep them looking their best.
Are veneers permanent?
Yes, dental veneers near you are considered permanent because we remove some enamel from your teeth. You’ll always need to have veneers or another restoration on those teeth.
Will veneers affect my bite?
No, veneers are custom-made to fit your bite comfortably.
How much do veneers cost at Delta Dental?
The cost depends on how many teeth need veneers and the material we use. Call our clinic or a consultation, and we’ll give you an exact price for your specific needs.
