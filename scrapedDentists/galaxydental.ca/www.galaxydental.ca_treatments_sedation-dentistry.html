Sedation Dentistry in Calgary – IV, Oral & Nitrous Oxide Sedation at Galaxy Dental

Many patients feel anxious or nervous about dental treatment—and that’s completely normal. At Galaxy Dental in Calgary, we offer safe and comfortable sedation dentistry options to help you relax during your visit. Whether you need a simple procedure or a longer dental surgery, our sedation techniques are designed to keep you calm, pain-free, and stress-free.
We offer three types of dental sedation:

  • IV Sedation (Intravenous)

  • Oral Sedation (Pill Sedation)

  • Nitrous Oxide Sedation (Laughing Gas)


Below is a clear explanation of what each option involves so you can make the choice that feels best for you.

  • IV Sedation Dentistry in Calgar - IV sedation is one of the most controlled and predictable forms of dental sedation. It is administered through a small IV line in your hand or arm.


What patients experience:

  • You feel very relaxed and sleepy

  • Most patients do not remember the procedure

  • You are still breathing on your own

  • You may fall into a light sleep, but you are not unconscious


Best for:

  • Wisdom teeth removal

  • Dental implants

  • Long or multiple procedures

  • Patients with high dental anxiety or strong gag reflex

  • Patients who have avoided dental work due to fear


Safety:

A trained sedation provider continually monitors your breathing, heart rate, and oxygen levels from start to finish. You will need someone to drive you home after IV sedation.

Oral Sedation (Pill Sedation)

Oral sedation uses a prescription medication taken before your appointment. It helps you feel calm, relaxed, and less aware of your surroundings.

What Patients Experience

  • A feeling of deep relaxation

  • Drowsiness, but you remain awake

  • Some patients nap during the procedure

  • You may remember little about the appointment afterward


Best For

  • Fillings, crowns, root canals

  • Patients who are nervous but do not want IV sedation

  • Mild to moderate dental anxiety

  • You will need someone to pick you up after your appointment because you will not be able to drive


Nitrous Oxide Sedation (Laughing Gas)

Nitrous oxide, commonly known as laughing gas, is a fast-acting and gentle sedation option. You breathe it in through a soft nose mask while the treatment is performed.

What Patients Experience

  • Light relaxation and calmness

  • Reduced anxiety and discomfort

  • You remain fully awake and aware

  • The effects wear off within minutes


Best For

  • Children or teens

  • Quick or mild procedures

  • Patients who only need light sedation

  • Patients who want to drive themselves home afterward

Nitrous oxide is one of the safest and most widely used forms of sedation in dentistry.


Is Sedation Dentistry Safe?

Yes. Your health and safety are always our top priority. Our team has advanced training in dental sedation and monitors you continuously throughout your visit. Sedation is customized based on age, weight, health history, and anxiety level.

Many patients say sedation dentistry has changed the way they feel about dental treatment. It allows you to receive the care you need in total comfort—without fear.

Why People Choose Sedation Dentistry at Galaxy Dental

  • Calm, relaxed dental experience

  • Little to no memory of the procedure

  • Easier treatment for anxious patients

  • Helpful for long or complex dental procedures

  • Safe for kids and adults

  • Performed by trained sedation professionals


Still Have Questions or Concerns?

If you feel nervous, scared, or overwhelmed about dental treatment, you are not alone—and you have options.

Contact Galaxy Dental today to schedule a consultation and talk about which sedation dentistry option is right for you.
