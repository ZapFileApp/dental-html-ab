Our Story

Affordable Dental Clinic in Calgary

Your confident smile is a reflection of your overall good health and well-being.

That is why getting regular dental checkups is an investment in your total health.

At Galaxy Dental, we are a team of passionate and experienced dental professionals committed to providing oral healthcare to people of all ages.

We provide a range of services like dental fillings, Invisalign treatments, teeth whitening, nightguards, and dental exams at an affordable price in Calgary.

      Dr. Patel graduated as a dentist in 2012 and began his career serving underserved communities at a university dental hospital, strengthening his clinical skills and compassion for patient care.       Throughout his career, Dr. Patel has pursued ongoing education to stay current with the latest advancements in dentistry.
