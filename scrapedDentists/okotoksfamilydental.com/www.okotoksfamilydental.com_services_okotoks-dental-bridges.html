Okotoks Dental Bridges

Your Okotoks choice for Dental Bridges!

If you have one or more missing teeth and are seeking an effective solution, a dental bridge might be the right dental treatment for you! My Family Dental is pleased to offer this natural-looking restoration option designed to replace missing teeth and help you achieve the smile you deserve!

What is a Dental Bridge?

A dental bridge is an effective restoration option carefully designed to replace one or more missing teeth within the same area. Attaching to abutment teeth, which are used as a secure anchor, dental bridges essentially act as a seamless “bridge,” closing the gap left by your missing tooth or teeth. A carefully crafted false tooth or teeth are placed in the vacant space, providing a natural look. My Family Dental takes great care to shape and colour your bridge to seamlessly blend in with the natural appearance of your existing teeth, ensuring a look that feels both authentic and functional within the overall aesthetics of your mouth. To create stability and preserve your overall bite, the adjacent teeth are also crowned. This approach results in a resilient, long-lasting and aesthetically pleasing solution for missing teeth, effectively revitalizing your smile and your confidence!

If the appearance of your smile has been affected by the loss of one or more teeth, a dental bridge may be the right restoration solution for you! If you are interested in learning more about dental bridges or you would like to know whether a dental bridge may be right for you, please call the dedicated My Family Dental team at (403) 995-9544 and we will be happy to help!
