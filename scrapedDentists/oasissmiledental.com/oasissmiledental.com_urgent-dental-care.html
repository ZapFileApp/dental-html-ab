Welcome

Same-day emergency care when you need it most. Call us right away if you’re experiencing pain, swelling, or a dental injury.

Dental emergencies don’t wait, and neither do we. Our team is always ready to see walk-ins and urgent cases to relieve pain quickly and protect your long-term oral health.

If you’re unsure whether what you’re experiencing counts as an emergency, call us — we’ll help you decide the right next step.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What constitutes a dental emergency?
Any issue causing severe pain, bleeding, swelling, or a knocked-out tooth.
What to do for a knocked-out tooth?
Handle by the crown, rinse gently, try to reinsert or keep moist in milk/saliva, and call immediately.
How much will my urgent care visit cost?
The cost depends on the treatment required. We’ll provide a detailed estimate before beginning any procedures.
