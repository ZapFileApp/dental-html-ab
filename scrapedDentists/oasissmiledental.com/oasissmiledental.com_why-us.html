Same-Day Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies can arise at any moment–which is why our team will take full action when someone needs immediate dental care. Our clinic is open 7 days a week so that you can get treated as soon as an emergency arises. We offer same-day appointments and emergency walk-ins to make the dental care you need accessible when you need it.

Signs You’re Having A Dental Emergency

  • Severe Toothache
  • Bleeding Gums
  • Chipped or Broken Teeth/Jaws
  • Knocked-Out Tooth
  • Abscessed Tooth
  • Lost or Broken Dentures
  • Pain that prevents you from eating or sleeping
  • Excessive bleeding
  • Broken dentures or veneers
  • Wisdom teeth pain
  • Knocked-out teeth
  • Broken or loose braces
  • Chipped, cracked, or broken teeth
  • Lost filling or crown
  • Abscess or "pimple" on the gumline
