Orthodontic Specialty Practice

Services We Offer

Early Orthodontic Treatment

Early interceptive orthodontic treatment makes room for permanent teeth by correcting the growth of the jaw and certain bite problems.

Read More
Two-Phase Treatment

Two-phase orthodontics is a type of orthodontic treatment that involves one course of treatment in childhood and another during adolescence.

Read More
Orthodontics For All Ages

We offer orthodontic treatment for all ages. We acknowledge that each patient have different needs, and require a different level of attention.

Read More
Clear Aligners

An alternative way to straighten teeth, like braces can, with the use of a series of clear, removable acrylic trays.

Read More
Clear Braces

Clear braces are a less noticeable alternative to traditional metal braces and provide patients with an equally effective treatment.

Read More
Metal Braces

Metal braces use metal brackets and archwires to straighten your teeth and are more comfortable today more than ever.

Read More
Invisalign

Invisalign is the clear alternative to braces. By wearing a clear plastic aligner you can receive orthodontic treatment without anyone knowing!

Read More
Surgical Orthodontics

Surgical orthodontics is used to correct severe cases that include bad bites, jaw bone abnormalities, and malocclusion.

Read More
Orthodontic Retainers

Using orthodontic retainers after your treatment is important to hold your new smile and to prevent orthodontic relapse.

Read More
