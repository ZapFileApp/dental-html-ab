About Orthodontics

What Is Orthodontics?

Orthodontics is a specialized branch of dentistry that focuses on the diagnosis, prevention, and correction of misaligned teeth and jaws. These misalignments — often called malocclusions — can affect not only your smile's appearance but also your oral health and overall well-being. Common issues we treat include overbite, underbite, crossbite, crowding, and spacing.

Treatment Options

Orthodontic Treatment Options

Invisalign®

Embrace the discreet and comfortable path to a straighter smile with Invisalign®. This innovative system uses a series of clear, removable aligners to gently shift your teeth into their desired positions.

02

Comprehensive Evaluations

Detailed orthodontic assessment to identify the right treatment for your bite, jaw alignment, and aesthetic goals.

03

Bite Correction

Address overbite, underbite, crossbite, and other malocclusions for healthier, more functional teeth.

04

Treatment for Crowding & Spacing

Correct crowded teeth and noticeable gaps to improve both your smile's look and how you chew, speak, and clean your teeth.
