Non-Insured Health Benefits

We Accept The Non-Insured Health Benefits For First Nations And Inuits

Our team direct-bills NIHB so eligible First Nations and Inuit patients receive seamless coverage for cleanings, fillings, and restorative care — with no upfront paperwork or out-of-pocket surprises. Whether it’s a routine check-up or a complex treatment, you’re welcome here.

  • Direct billing — no out-of-pocket payments
  • Coverage for preventive, restorative & emergency care
  • Welcoming, judgment-free team
