At Home

Post-Treatment Care

Ongoing care is essential to maintaining the long-term success of your cosmetic dentistry treatment.

  • Dietary guidelines to avoid staining teeth after whitening or damaging restorations.
  • Oral hygiene tips to maintain optimal oral health and prevent complications.
  • Regular check-up appointments to monitor progress and ensure long-term success of treatment.
