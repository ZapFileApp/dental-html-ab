Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bow River Dental Centre and where are you located?

Bow River Dental Centre is a family dental clinic centrally located at #104, 30 Bow Street Common in downtown Cochrane, Alberta — right at the corner of Bow Street and Centre Avenue, next door to Good Earth Coffeehouse. The clinic has served Cochrane and surrounding communities for over two decades, and our motto is simple: We Treat Everyone Like Family.

Is Bow River Dental Centre accepting new patients?

Yes — we are always welcoming new patients and their families. No referral is required. You can book your first appointment by calling us at 403-932-9889 or through our website. Our team will walk you through everything before your first visit, and we even have a dedicated First Visit page and a short YouTube video to make the process easy and stress-free.

What are Bow River Dental Centre Cochrane hours?

We offer extended hours to fit busy schedules. Our current hours are: Monday 8am–7pm, Tuesday 8am–8pm, Wednesday 8am–8pm, Thursday 8am–4:30pm, Friday 8am–4:30pm, and Saturday 9am–3pm (by appointment only). Evening and Saturday availability make us one of the most accessible dental clinics in Cochrane.

Is parking available at Bow River Dental and is the clinic accessible?

Yes. Bow River Dental Centre has convenient on-site parking and is fully wheelchair accessible. The clinic has its own standalone entrance, accessible washrooms, and a modern, spacious facility designed with every patient's comfort in mind.

Why do patients from Calgary and surrounding areas come to Bow River Dental?

While we are proudly based in Cochrane, patients regularly travel from Calgary, Springbank, Bragg Creek, Crossfield, and other nearby communities to see us. Families and individuals choose Bow River Dental for our experienced team, extended hours, advanced technology, spa-like comfort, and a patient-first philosophy.

What makes the team at Bow River Dental different?

Our team is genuinely embedded in the Cochrane community — many of our staff live here, raise their families here, and are your neighbours. Our clinic has earned a 5.0-star rating on Google with over 1,000 reviews, and the number one thing patients mention is the warmth, honesty, and expertise of our people. We don't just treat teeth — we build long-term relationships and treat every patient the way we'd want our own family treated.

How long has Bow River Dental Centre been serving Cochrane, and why do so many families choose it?

Bow River Dental Centre has been part of the Cochrane community since 1998, making it one of the oldest and most established dental clinics in the city. Over more than 25 years, we've grown into a practice that Cochrane families return to generation after generation — and one that consistently earns the trust of patients moving to the area from Calgary and beyond. We are proud to hold a 5.0-star rating on Google based on over 1,037 patient reviews, making us one of the highest-rated and most-reviewed dental clinics in Cochrane. Patients tell us they choose Bow River Dental for our experienced team, honest care, advanced technology, and an atmosphere that genuinely feels welcoming.

What dental services does Bow River Dental Centre offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of dental services under one roof in Cochrane, including: general dentistry (fillings, crowns, bridges, dentures, inlays, onlays, oral appliances, sports guards, night guards); cosmetic dentistry (composite and porcelain veneers, teeth whitening); cleaning and prevention (exams, digital x-rays, panoramic imaging, oral cancer screening); oral surgery (extractions, laser dentistry, sedation dentistry); endodontics and root canal therapy; pediatric dentistry; and emergency dental care.

Do you offer cosmetic dentistry in Cochrane?

Yes. Bow River Dental Centre offers a full range of cosmetic dental treatments including composite veneers, porcelain veneers, and professional teeth whitening. Our cosmetic services are designed to give you a healthy, natural-looking smile at a price that follows the Alberta Dental Association Fee Guide. Book a consultation to explore your options.

Do you use modern dental technology?

Yes — we invest heavily in the latest dental technology to improve accuracy, comfort, and outcomes. Our clinic features digital x-rays, a panoramic scanner that captures a complete view of your jaw and teeth in a single image, 3D intraoral scanners that eliminate the need for uncomfortable dental mold impressions, laser dentistry tools, microscopes for precision work, and a wide range of in-chair comfort technology. We believe better tools lead to better care.

I'm nervous about going to the dentist. How does Bow River Dental help anxious patients?

Dental anxiety is completely normal, and we've built our entire patient experience around making people feel at ease. We offer sedation dentistry for patients who need extra help relaxing. Our operatories are designed like a spa — you can enjoy noise-cancelling headphones, a TV above the chair with access to all major streaming platforms, warm blankets, pillows, hot towels, and even massage chairs. Our team is also trained to communicate with patience, honesty, and compassion. Many of our most anxious new patients become our most loyal ones.

What is sedation dentistry and do you offer it?

Sedation dentistry uses safe, professionally administered medication to help patients relax during dental procedures. It's ideal for patients with dental anxiety, a strong gag reflex, or those undergoing longer or more complex treatments. Yes, we offer sedation dentistry at Bow River Dental Centre in Cochrane. Contact us to discuss which sedation option is right for you.

Do you accept the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)?

Yes.Bow River Dental Centre is a participating provider for the Canadian DentalCare Plan (CDCP). We welcome CDCP patients and our team is highly experiencedwith the plan — we are one of Cochrane's most knowledgeable practices when itcomes to helping patients understand and use their CDCP coverage. If you havequestions about eligibility or what's covered, our team is happy to help.

What insurance plans do you accept and do you direct bill?

We work with most major Canadian dental insurance providers and offer direct billing, so you don't have to pay out of pocket and wait for reimbursement. Our fees follow the Alberta Dental Association Fee Guide, which means our pricing is transparent, standardized, and fair. We accept all payment methods including cash, debit, and all major credit cards.

What if I don't have dental insurance?

No insurance? No problem. Our fees follow the Alberta Dental Association Fee Guide, making our pricing fair and predictable. We accept all payment methods, and our team will always explain treatment costs clearly before any work begins so you can make informed decisions. We believe everyone deserves excellent dental care.

What happens at my first appointment at Bow River Dental Centre Cochrane?

Your first visit is designed to be straightforward and welcoming. It typically includes a professional dental cleaning, digital x-rays (including a full panoramic scan if needed), and a comprehensive exam with one of our dentists. We'll take the time to review your dental history, understand your goals, and answer any questions you have — no rushing, no pressure. We have a dedicated First Visit page and a short video on our YouTube channel that walks you through exactly what to expect.

Do I need a referral to become a patient at Bow River Dental Centre Cochrane?

No referral is needed. Simply call us at 403-932-9889 or book online through our website. We're always accepting new patients and new families.
