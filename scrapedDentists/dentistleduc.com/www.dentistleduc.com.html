Your Responsibility & What to Bring:

  • Current Insurance Information: Bring your insurance card or policy details, including policy number and insurance provider information.
  • Identification: Bring a valid ID for verification purposes.
  • Know Your Coverage: Understand your insurance plan – what’s covered, the extent of the coverage, co-pays, and deductibles. Some treatments may not be fully covered.
  • Out-of-Pocket Costs: Be prepared for any possible out-of-pocket expenses for services not covered or partially covered by your insurance.
