St. Albert Dental Implants

Dental implants replace missing teeth and are an excellent alternative to bridges and dentures.

Dental videos by Optio Publishing © 2003-2026 Terms of use

How do dental implants work? A dental implant is an artificial tooth that is anchored into your jawbone by a titanium metal fixture.

If you are in good general and oral health and do not have periodontal disease, you may be a candidate for dental implants.
