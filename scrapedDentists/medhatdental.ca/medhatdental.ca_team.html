Our Team

Knowledgeable Dental professionals at your service.

Meet our team.

Dr. Riley Miller
Dentist

Dr. Miller completed his dental training at the University of Sydney in Australia. After earning his Doctor of Dental Medicine degree, he returned to his hometown of Nanaimo, BC, where he practiced for several years before relocating to Medicine Hat, Alberta. Dr. Miller and his family are thrilled to be part of this wonderful community.

He is a member of the Canadian Dental Association, the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta, the Alberta Dental Association, and the BC College of Oral Health Professionals. Dr. Miller enjoys all aspects of general dentistry and is committed to ongoing continuing education to stay current with the latest techniques and advancements in the field.
Outside the office, he enjoys spending time with his wife and daughter, golfing, playing recreational league hockey, and exploring local fishing spots.

Abby
Office Co-ordinator

I’m thrilled to be part of the amazing team at Medicine Hat Dental, where making you feel welcome is always our top priority. I’ve spent the past six years working in patient care, including time as a Certified Medical Laboratory Assistant at Medicine Hat Hospital after graduating from SAIT, a Recreation Aide at a local assisted living center, and most recently, the last two years in the dental field.

I truly thrive in patient care and love building meaningful relationships with everyone who walks through our doors. I’ve lived in Medicine Hat for the past 14 years and originally come from Rocky Mountain House. I’m getting married in 2026, and outside of work you’ll find me hunting, golfing, travelling, watching sports, or spending time with my red fox Labrador retriever, Stoeger.

I come from a big, blended family—one of seven kids—and am a proud aunt to two nephews and one niece. I’m so excited to meet both new and familiar faces at Medicine Hat Dental and to be part of your journey to a healthy, beautiful smile!
