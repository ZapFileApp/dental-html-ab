Perfect Solution For Your Dentistry

Teeth Whitening

Teeth Whitening

This procedure is performed by your dentist or hygienist. The in-office treatment can be done at the same time as a teeth cleaning or during a separate appointment. It is typically completed within an hour and produces immediately white teeth.

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation Dentistry

For adult patients who battle a higher level of dental anxiety or for more involved procedures such as tooth extraction or implant placement, you may be seeking a sedation modality that offers a deeper level of relaxation.

Root Canal Therapy

Root Canal Therapy

At Chinook Smiles, root canal therapy near you involves a detailed process tailored to remove infection and protect your tooth from further damage. The procedure starts with numbing the affected area to ensure your comfort.
