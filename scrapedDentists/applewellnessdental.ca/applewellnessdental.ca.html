The Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP)

The Canadian Dental Care Plan is a federal government program that helps eligible Canadian residents cover a portion of their dental care costs. The program is designed to reduce financial barriers so more people can access the treatment they need.

Apple Wellness Dental accepts patients enrolled in the CDCP. If you are 100% covered under the plan, we do not collect any remaining balance from you. For patients with partial CDCP coverage, the plan will cover its portion and we can work with you on the rest through our payment plan options.

To qualify for the CDCP, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • No existing dental insurance coverage
  • Adjusted family net income below $90,000
  • Canadian resident for tax purposes
  • Filed a tax return for the previous year

The CDCP is one of several payment options we support. We also offer direct billing to most private insurance providers and follow the Alberta Fee Guide. Whether you are covered through the CDCP, private insurance, or paying out of pocket, our administrative team can walk you through your options before treatment begins.

If you have questions about your eligibility or want to understand what the CDCP covers for your situation, call us at (587) 332-6767.

Dental Services

Apple Wellness Dental. Your one-stop shop for all your dental needs! Whether you’re in need of a routine cleaning or require a root canal, we ensure that you feel comfortable and well taken care of throughout the entire process.

Hygiene Therapy

Hygiene therapy emphasizes the importance of maintaining proper cleanliness and personal hygiene practices to promote overall health and well-being.

Children’s Dentistry

Children’s dentistry focuses on providing specialized dental care tailored to the unique needs of young patients, promoting oral health habits from an early age.

Dental Emergencies

Emergency dental services address urgent oral health issues, providing immediate care for conditions such as severe toothaches, broken teeth, or other dental emergencies to alleviate pain and prevent further complications.

Custom Mouthguards

Custom mouthguards are personalized dental appliances designed to fit an individual’s teeth precisely, providing effective protection during sports or preventing teeth grinding, offering a comfortable and tailored solution for oral safety.

Dental Extractions

Dental extractions involve the removal of a tooth from its socket, typically performed to address severe damage, decay, or crowding, with the goal of maintaining or improving overall oral health.

Dental Implants

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to serve as sturdy artificial roots for prosthetic teeth, offering a durable and natural-looking solution for replacing missing teeth and restoring oral function.

Root Canal Therapy

Root canal therapy is a dental procedure designed to treat and save a severely infected or damaged tooth by removing the infected pulp, cleaning the canal, and sealing it to prevent further infection.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a cosmetic dental procedure that utilizes bleaching agents or other techniques to lighten and brighten the color of teeth, enhancing their appearance and creating a whiter, more radiant smile.

Crowns and Bridges

Crowns and bridges are dental prosthetics used to restore damaged or missing teeth. With crowns covering individual teeth for protection and bridges replacing one or more.

Restorative Dentistry

Restorative dentistry involves repairing and restoring damaged or missing teeth, aiming to enhance both oral function and aesthetic appearance through various dental procedures and treatments.

Partial & Full Dentures

Dentures are removable prosthetic devices designed to replace missing teeth, offering individuals a functional and aesthetic solution for restoring their smile and improving chewing abilities.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry concentrates on enhancing the visual appeal of the teeth and smile, offering procedures like teeth whitening, veneers, and dental implants to achieve aesthetic improvements.
