Common Questions About Our Dental Services in Prairie Springs, Airdrie

Q: Does your clinic accept new patients from the Prairie Springs neighborhood?

A: Yes, we welcome new patients from all parts of Prairie Springs and surrounding Airdrie neighborhoods.

Q: Which insurance plans do you accept?

A: We work with most major insurance providers, including Alberta Blue Cross, Sun Life, Manulife, Great-West Life, and many others. Our team can help verify your coverage and process direct billing.

Q: How often should I visit the dental clinic for check-ups?

A: For most patients, we recommend professional cleanings and check-ups every six months. However, some conditions may require more frequent visits. We’ll create a personalized care schedule based on your specific oral health needs.

Q: What should I do if I have a dental emergency?

A: Contact our office immediately for guidance. We set aside time for same-day emergency appointments whenever possible.

Q: How much does teeth whitening cost?

A: Professional teeth whitening costs vary based on the method chosen. In-office treatments typically range from $350-500, while take-home custom whitening kits range from $200-300.

Q: Do you provide sedation dentistry for anxious patients?

A: Yes, we offer various sedation options, including nitrous oxide (laughing gas) and oral conscious sedation for patients with dental anxiety.

Q: Is your dental clinic accessible for patients with disabilities?

A: Yes, our facility is wheelchair accessible with appropriate accommodations for patients with various mobility needs.

Q: How long does the dental implant process take?

A: The dental implant process typically takes 3-6 months from initial consultation to final restoration. The exact timeline depends on your specific case, including whether bone grafting is required.
