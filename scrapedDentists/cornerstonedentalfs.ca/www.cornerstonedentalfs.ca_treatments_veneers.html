WHAT ARE VENEERS?

Veneers are a cosmetic dentistry solution designed to improve the appearance of cracked or yellowed teeth. Made from porcelain or composite resin, veneers are thin shells that cover the front of your teeth. They can be customized to match the colour, shape, and size ideal for your smile. With proper care, veneers can last up to 15 years before needing replacement.

PORCELAIN VENEERS IN FORT SASKATCHEWAN, AB

Porcelain veneers are wafer-thin ceramic shells that bond directly to the surfaces of teeth. They are popular for their ability to disguise a variety of dental issues and are known for their versatility and relatively simple application.

PROBLEMS THAT PORCELAIN VENEERS CAN ADDRESS

Crooked teeth
Discoloured teeth
Crowded teeth
Gapped teeth
Rotated teeth
Small teeth
Poorly shaped teeth
Worn or chipped teeth
These issues can be resolved in less than a month, resulting in a stunning new smile.

PORCELAIN VENEERS TREATMENT PROCESS

Initial Consultation: Schedule an appointment at Cornerstone Dental Centre to discuss your goals and determine if veneers are right for you.
Preparation: During your next visit, we reshape the surface of your teeth to make space for the veneers. We then take an impression of your teeth, and send them to the lab where they are custom-made. This process usually takes two weeks.
Placement: At your final visit, we try in the veneers to ensure they meet your expectations. We then place and bond the veneers onto your teeth using a curing light and special bonding agent. Any final adjustments are made to perfect your new smile.

BENEFITS OF PORCELAIN VENEERS

Customized Range of Change: Decide how much you want to alter your smile, whether it's a dramatic change or a subtle tweak.
Long Lasting: Made from advanced dental ceramics, porcelain veneers are strong and typically last 10 or more years without chipping or cracking. They are also highly stain-resistant.
Natural Appearance: Porcelain veneers look like natural teeth, giving you a straighter, brighter smile that blends seamlessly with your existing teeth.
Brighter Than Whitening: Veneers can make your smile as white as you want, often achieving a level of whiteness beyond traditional whitening methods.
