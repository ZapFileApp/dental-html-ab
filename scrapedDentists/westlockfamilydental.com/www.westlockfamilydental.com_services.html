Dental Services

General Dental Care for Patients of All Ages in Westlock, Alberta

Westlock Family Dental offers a range of general dental services for children, adults, and seniors. Treatment recommendations are based on individual clinical assessment, and we take time to walk patients through findings, options, and expected timelines before care begins.

Select a service below to learn more about what may be involved and what you can typically expect.

Our Services

Exams & Cleanings

Regular dental exams and professional cleanings help support oral health and allow your dentist to monitor changes over time. The frequency and type of care recommended may vary depending on individual needs.

Restorative Dentistry

Restorative dental treatment may be recommended when a tooth has been affected by decay, damage, or wear. Treatment options are discussed based on clinical findings and individual circumstances.

Extractions

A tooth extraction may be recommended when a tooth cannot be restored or when removal is indicated for other clinical reasons. Your dentist will discuss the process and available options beforehand.

Root Canal Therapy

Root canal therapy may be recommended when the inner pulp of a tooth becomes infected or inflamed. Treatment is aimed at preserving the tooth where clinically appropriate.

Crowns & Bridges

Dental crowns and bridges are fixed restorations used to protect damaged teeth or replace missing ones. Suitability and treatment approach are determined based on individual clinical assessment.

Dental Implants

Dental implants may be used to replace missing teeth by supporting a crown, bridge, or denture. Treatment involves multiple stages and a thorough assessment of bone and overall oral health.

Clear Aligners

Clear aligner therapy such as Invisalign may be an option for patients looking to address certain alignment concerns. Suitability is determined following a clinical assessment and consultation.

Teeth Whitening

Professional teeth whitening options may include in-office treatment or a take-home kit provided by your dentist. Suitability varies depending on individual oral health and clinical assessment.

Veneers

Dental veneers are thin restorations applied to the front surface of teeth to address certain cosmetic or structural concerns. Treatment suitability is determined on an individual basis.

Children's Dental Care

We provide age-appropriate dental care for children in Westlock, Alberta, with a focus on prevention, education, and making visits comfortable. Treatment is tailored to each child's developmental stage and individual needs.

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies may include tooth pain, swelling, infection, or sudden damage to a tooth or restoration. Contact our Westlock clinic to discuss your concerns and available appointment options.

The availability and suitability of any service varies depending on individual needs and clinical assessment.

Not Sure Where to Start?

If you are unsure about which service applies to your situation, we encourage you to book a general exam. Your dentist will assess your oral health, discuss any findings, and recommend next steps based on your individual needs.
