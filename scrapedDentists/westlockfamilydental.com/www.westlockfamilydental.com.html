Dental Services

Westlock Family Dental offers a range of general dental services, which may include:

View All Services

Exams & Cleanings

Regular dental exams and professional cleanings help support oral health and allow your dentist to monitor changes over time. The frequency and type of care recommended may vary depending on individual needs.

Dental Implants

Dental implants may be used to replace missing teeth by supporting a crown, bridge, or denture. Treatment involves multiple stages and a thorough assessment of bone and overall oral health. Your dentist will discuss suitability and alternatives based on your clinical situation.

Emergency Dental Care

Dental emergencies may include tooth pain, swelling, infection, or sudden damage to a tooth or restoration. Contact our Westlock clinic to discuss your concerns and available appointment options.

Note: If you are experiencing severe swelling, difficulty breathing, or significant trauma, seek immediate emergency care.

Clear Aligners

Clear aligner therapy such as Invisalign may be an option for patients looking to address certain alignment concerns. Suitability is determined following a clinical assessment and consultation.

Children's Dental Care

We provide age-appropriate dental care for children, with a focus on prevention, education, and making visits comfortable. Treatment is tailored to each child's developmental stage and individual needs.

The availability and suitability of any service vary depending on individual needs and clinical assessment.

About Our Clinic

Westlock Family Dental offers general dental services for patients of all ages. Our team is committed to thoughtful treatment planning and care tailored to each patient’s individual needs.

We welcome children, adults, and seniors. Before any treatment begins, we take time to walk patients through clinical findings, available options, associated costs, and expected timelines — so you can make decisions that feel right for you.

Our Approach to Care

Patient-Centred Communication

We take time to explain clinical findings, recommendations, and available alternatives so patients have the information they need to make informed decisions about their dental health.

Comfort-Focused Visits

We aim to provide care in a calm, respectful environment. Treatment pacing may be adjusted based on individual comfort and clinical needs.

Modern Diagnostic Tools

Our clinic uses digital X-rays and intraoral imaging to support accurate assessment and treatment planning, based on each patient's clinical presentation.

Insurance Providers We Work With

We follow the Alberta Provincial Fee Guide and accept the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP). Direct billing is available to most major insurance providers.
