Finding Us

Westlock Family Dental is located at 10411 100 Ave in Westlock, Alberta. The clinic is accessible by a paved sidewalk with no stairs. If you have specific accessibility requirements, please contact our team ahead of your visit.

Booking an Appointment

To book an appointment at Westlock Family Dental, please call us directly during clinic hours. New patients are welcome without a referral. If you are unsure which type of appointment is right for your situation, our team is happy to help guide you.

For information on what to bring and what to expect at your first visit, please visit our Patient Information page.

780-349-4431

Urgent Dental Concerns

If you are experiencing a dental emergency during clinic hours, please call us as soon as possible so our team can assess your situation and discuss available options.

If you are experiencing a dental emergency outside of clinic hours, please follow the instructions on the answer machine.If you are experiencing severe swelling, difficulty breathing, significant bleeding, or major trauma, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room immediately.

Cancellation and Rescheduling

If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please contact our team as early as possible and no later than 48 business hours before your scheduled visit. For full details on our cancellation policy, visit our Insurance & Payments page.
