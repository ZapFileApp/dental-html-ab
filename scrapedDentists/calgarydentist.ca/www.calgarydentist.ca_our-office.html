YOU HAVE QUESTIONS

We Have Answers
Find out more about some of our most frequently asked questions about our Calgary dentist office.
What are your infection control procedures?

Our Calgary dentist office follows all infection prevention and control protocols mandated by the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta. For more information about our infection prevention and control protocols, please see our infection prevention and control page.

Does your Calgary dentist office direct bill insurance?

Yes, let us do the paperwork! Our Calgary dentist office will direct bill most dental insurance companies so you don’t have to fuss with insurance submissions.

What type of dentist is our Calgary dentist, Dr. Dirk Thompson?

Dr. Dirk Thompson, D.D.S., is a general Calgary dentist. Dr. Dirk Thompson has been licensed to practice general dentistry in Alberta by the College of Dental Surgeons of Alberta since 1994.

Does your Calgary dentist office accept emergency dental patients?

Our Calgary dentist office accepts emergency dental patients even if our Calgary dentist is not your usual family dentist. We also always strive to fit in same day dental emergencies so you can get the dental work you need, now.

Does your Calgary dentist office have early morning, evening and weekend dental appointments? cover?

Our Calgary dentist is pleased to offer our patients early morning, late evening and weekend appointments by special request. It’s just another thing we do to ensure you can get the dental work you need at a time that’s convenient for you.

How can I find the best Calgary dentist for me?

One of the best ways to find the best Calgary dentist for you and your family is to ask for a recommendation from your family and friends. If your mom and sister like their dentist, why not give them a try?

We also recommend that patients attend for a meet and greet with a new dentist to ensure that the dental clinic is a good fit for you and your family. Our Calgary dentist is pleased to offer complimentary meet and greet visits so we can both get to know each other a little better, give you the opportunity to see our dental office, meet our fantastic dental team, become familiar with our office policies and ask any questions you may have.

Our Calgary dentist is always happy to request a pre-determination of your dental benefits on your behalf. If you are considering dental treatment but are wondering what your insurance benefits will cover, please call our Calgary dentist office or submit your request below for our office to request a pre-determination of benefits on your behalf.
