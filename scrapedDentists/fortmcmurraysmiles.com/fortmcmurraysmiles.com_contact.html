Meet Our Dentists

Dr. Navneet Singh Sandhu

Dr. Navneet Singh Sandhu is a dedicated and experienced dentist with a distinguished academic background and a passion for improving oral health. With over 13 years of experience in the field, Dr. Sandhu offers a comprehensive range of dental services, from routine checkups and fillings to complex procedures like implant placement and root canals.

Dr. Sameet Gill

Dr. Sam Gill is a registered Dentist and influential health care provider in Alberta. She graduated from Boston University’s Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine. Before completing her Doctorate, she received her Bachelor of Physical Science from the University of Alberta. As one of the co-founders of Peak Dental Group, Dr. Gill dedicates herself to her practice and the pursuit of the highest quality dental care in the industry.

Dr. Gurshant Grewal

Dr. Gurshant Grewal is a registered dentist who is dedicated to his practice. In 2013, he graduated from Boston University’s Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine. Prior to completing his Doctorate, he obtained a Bachelor of Science with a major in Microbiology at the University of California in Berkeley. As a successful entrepreneur, he is the co-founder of Peak Dental Group, a corporation committed to building new practices and partnering with other medical professionals through Alberta and Ontario.

Do you have an emergency dental concern? You may have chipped a tooth or have intense pain. We understand you need help right now. We are available for same day emergency appointments and walk-ins. Give us a call today!
