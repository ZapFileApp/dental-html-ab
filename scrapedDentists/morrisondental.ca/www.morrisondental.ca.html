Frequently Asked Dental Questions

Do I really have to go to the dentist every six months?

How often you visit for dental check-ups varies with your oral health requirements. The aim is to identify minor issues early. For many people, this means a dental exam from a dentist every six months. Our dentist near you will recommend you come in more or less often based on the health of your teeth and gums, any issues you have that must be examined or treated, the rate at which tartar accumulates on your teeth, and so on. If you’re looking for a dental clinic near you, we’re here to help with all your oral health needs.

Does my dentist need to wear gloves and a mask, and how do I know he or she is using clean tools?
Your health is very important to your dentist. One of the most important things that your dentist near you does to keep you healthy is to prevent germs from spreading. One of the finest methods of doing this is to wear barrier protection, including gloves and a mask.
When should I take my child to the dentist for the first time?
It’s important to get an early start on dental care so that your child will learn that visiting the dentist at a dental clinic near you is a regular part of health care. The first step is to choose a dentist for your child.
