About Morrison Dental

Providing our patients with the highest quality dental care.

Our Story

For over 40 years, Morrison Dental has been a trusted dental clinic in Fort McMurray. We are one of the oldest clinics in the area! Our success can be attributed to our ability to always put the patient first, no matter their age or dental needs. We provide excellent oral care for your entire family.

Our Mission

Our dentist mission is to provide our patients with the highest quality dental care in a timely manner. Offering restorative dentistry without the wait is how we put our patients first. We can fabricate crowns, veneers, and inlays right in our clinic, which minimizes your wait time. Allowing you to walk out with healthier, more attractive teeth after just one visit.

Dr. Joshua Tan, Dentist

Dr. Joshua Tan was born and raised in Malaysia before pursuing his education in Canada, where he earned his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) from Dalhousie University in Nova Scotia. After graduating, he chose to settle in Fort McMurray, where he has been committed to making a positive and lasting impact on the community. Known for his friendly and enthusiastic demeanour, Dr. Josh creates a welcoming atmosphere that puts patients at ease. He believes that trust and comfort are essential to a great dental experience. His primary goal is to help every patient achieve optimal oral health and a confident smile, offering personalized care tailored to individual needs. Outside the clinic, Dr. Josh leads an active and balanced lifestyle. He enjoys outdoor adventures and has a strong passion for sports, particularly soccer and badminton. Additionally, his interest in powerlifting reflects his discipline and dedication to fitness, reinforcing his commitment to overall well-being. Dr. Joshua Tan is proud to serve as a trusted dentist to Fort McMurray residents.
