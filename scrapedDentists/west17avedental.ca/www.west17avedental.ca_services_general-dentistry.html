Prevention First

Why Regular Dental Care Matters

Preventive dentistry is the cornerstone of a healthy, confident smile. Regular checkups and cleanings don't just keep your teeth clean — they catch small problems before they become expensive emergencies, screen for oral cancer, protect your gum health, and give our team the chance to know you as an individual rather than a chart number.

At West 17th Avenue Dental Care, our general dentistry approach is thorough, compassionate, and personalized. Whether you're overdue for a cleaning after years away, managing dental anxiety, or simply looking for a new dental home in Calgary SW, we'll meet you exactly where you are.

Our General Dentistry Services

Everything you need to maintain and protect your family's oral health, all under one roof.

Teeth Cleanings & Checkups

Professional cleanings remove tartar that brushing can't reach, while comprehensive exams catch issues early — before they become costly. We recommend visits every 6 months for most patients.

Root Canal Therapy

Modern root canal therapy is nothing to fear. Using precision techniques and effective anaesthesia, we eliminate tooth pain and save your natural tooth — usually in a single comfortable appointment.

Tooth Extractions

When a tooth can't be saved, we perform gentle extractions with your comfort as the priority. We'll also discuss replacement options like implants to restore your smile.

Gum Disease Treatment

Periodontal (gum) disease affects nearly half of adults. We offer scaling, root planing, and personalized maintenance programs to halt progression and restore gum health.

Fluoride Treatments

Professional fluoride application strengthens enamel and provides long-lasting protection against cavities — especially important for children and patients with higher decay risk.

Dental Sealants

A thin protective coating applied to the chewing surfaces of molars, sealants create a physical barrier against bacteria and food — an excellent preventive measure for children and teens.

Sports & Night Guards

Custom-fitted mouthguards protect teeth during contact sports, while night guards prevent damage from grinding (bruxism) while you sleep. Both are made from impressions of your exact bite.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

Most adults benefit from checkups and cleanings every 6 months. Patients with gum disease, high cavity risk, or other concerns may need more frequent visits — we'll recommend a schedule tailored to you.

Does a root canal hurt?

Modern root canal therapy is no more uncomfortable than getting a filling. We use effective local anaesthetic and gentle technique. Most patients are surprised by how comfortable the procedure is — and relieved to finally be free of the tooth pain that brought them in.

What's included in a routine checkup?

A full exam includes a professional cleaning, digital X-rays (as needed), oral cancer screening, gum health assessment, and a full evaluation of all teeth and existing restorations. You'll also receive personalized oral hygiene recommendations.

Do you accept new patients?

Absolutely — we warmly welcome patients of all ages! Call us or book online to schedule your first appointment. New patients receive a complimentary whitening kit or electric toothbrush.
