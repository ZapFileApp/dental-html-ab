Our Dentists

Expert Care, Compassionate Hands

Dentist

Dr. Tiffany Chen

With her dental degree from the University of Witwatersrand and 12 years of practice in Taiwan, Dr. Chen brings exceptional global expertise to Calgary. She specializes in root canal treatments, extractions, and orthodontics.

Languages: English, Mandarin, Taiwanese
Outside the clinic: Amateur triathlete, travel, Netflix

Dentist

Dr. Harit Talwar

Dr. Talwar holds a BDS (2011) and MDS (2015) and brings over a decade of clinical experience to West 17th Avenue. Before moving to Canada he practiced dentistry and served as an Associate Professor at Christian Dental College, teaching and mentoring both undergraduate and postgraduate students. He relocated to Canada in 2021 and became licensed here in 2023 after completing the NDEB Equivalency Process. Known for his calm, approachable manner, Dr. Talwar takes the time to listen, explain treatment options clearly, and ensure every patient feels comfortable and informed throughout their visit.

Languages: English
Outside the clinic: Family time, exploring Alberta's landscapes, travelling, staying active

Dentist

Dr. Suprabha Pabbi-Doad

Known warmly as "Dr. Supra," she holds a Master's in Global Health and is passionate about patient education and accessible, compassionate care. Fluent in four languages.

Languages: English, Punjabi, Hindi, Urdu
Outside the clinic: Rocky Mountain hiking, camping

Dentist

Dr. Jasmine Rana

Dr. Jasmine Rana is a skilled and compassionate dentist practicing in Calgary, Alberta. She completed her dental school in India and successfully completed the national dental equivalency process in Canada in 2024. Dr. Rana prioritizes patient education and creating a comfortable, reassuring environment — helping patients overcome anxiety and feel at ease during every visit.

Languages: English
Outside the clinic: Hiking, running, spending time with her dog
