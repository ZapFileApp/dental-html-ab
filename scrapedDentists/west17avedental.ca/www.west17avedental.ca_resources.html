New Patients

Your First Visit at
West 17th Avenue

We want your first appointment to be easy and stress-free. Here's exactly what to bring and what to expect.

What to Bring

  • Valid government-issued ID
  • Insurance card (if applicable)
  • List of current medications
  • Previous dental records or X-rays (if available)
  • CDCP or NIHB benefit card (if eligible)

What to Expect

  • Comprehensive oral examination
  • Digital X-rays (when needed)
  • Professional cleaning
  • Oral cancer screening
  • Personalized treatment plan discussion

Helpful Tips

  • Arrive 10 minutes early to complete forms
  • Mention any dental anxiety — we have solutions
  • Write down questions you want answered
  • Register your parking at the front desk
  • Bring a support person if it helps you relax

Insurance

We Direct-Bill
Most Major Plans

We're proud to offer direct billing to most major dental insurance providers — which means we submit the claim to your insurer directly, and you only pay your out-of-pocket portion at the end of your appointment. No waiting for reimbursement, no confusing paperwork.

Simply bring your insurance card to your appointment and our administrative team will look after everything. We'll verify your benefits ahead of time when possible and let you know what to expect before treatment begins.

Indigenous Health Benefits

Non-Insured Health Benefits (NIHB)

We proudly serve First Nations and Inuit patients covered under the Non-Insured Health Benefits program. NIHB covers a range of dental services including exams, cleanings, fillings, and extractions. Please bring your Status Card or NIHB benefit card to your appointment.
