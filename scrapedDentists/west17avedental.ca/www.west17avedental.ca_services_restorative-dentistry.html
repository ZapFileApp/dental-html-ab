Your Smile, Fully Restored

Missing, damaged, or heavily decayed teeth affect far more than aesthetics — they impact your ability to eat comfortably, speak clearly, maintain bone structure, and feel confident. Restorative dentistry addresses all of these concerns with long-lasting, natural-looking solutions.

At West 17th Avenue Dental Care, our restorative treatments are designed to integrate seamlessly with your natural smile. We use tooth-coloured materials, digital impressions, and precision craftsmanship to ensure every restoration is not only functional, but beautiful.

Restorative Treatments

98%Success Rate

Dental Implants

The gold standard for tooth replacement. A titanium post integrates with your jawbone to support a lifelike crown — functionally and aesthetically indistinguishable from your natural teeth.

1–2Visits to Complete

Crowns & Bridges

Dental crowns cap and protect a damaged, cracked, or heavily restored tooth. Bridges use adjacent teeth as anchors to replace one or more missing teeth. Both are crafted from tooth-coloured porcelain for a seamless, natural appearance.

100%Custom-Crafted

Dentures

Custom-fitted full or partial dentures that restore function and appearance. We offer traditional dentures as well as implant-supported options that stay firmly in place — so you can eat, speak, and laugh with complete confidence.

SameDay Treatment

Tooth-Coloured Fillings

Composite resin fillings match the natural colour of your tooth for virtually invisible restoration of cavities. They bond directly to the tooth structure, require less removal of healthy enamel, and provide a beautiful, durable result.

360°Complete Care

Full Mouth Rehabilitation

For patients with extensive decay, worn teeth, multiple missing teeth, or complex bite issues, full mouth rehabilitation combines multiple restorative treatments into a comprehensive plan. We rebuild function, health, and aesthetics.

10+Year Lifespan

Inlays & Onlays

An alternative to full crowns for moderately damaged teeth. Custom-made from porcelain or composite, inlays and onlays provide a more conservative, long-lasting restoration than a large filling.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are dental implants a good option for me?

Implants are suitable for most adults with good general health and adequate bone volume. We'll evaluate your bone density, gum health, and overall health at your consultation to determine if implants are right for you.

How long does a dental crown last?

With good oral hygiene and regular checkups, porcelain or ceramic crowns typically last 10–15 years or more. Avoiding grinding (or wearing a night guard) and regular dental visits will maximize their longevity.

What's the difference between partial and full dentures?

Partial dentures replace some missing teeth while retaining your remaining natural teeth. Full dentures replace an entire arch when all teeth are missing. Both are custom-made for a comfortable, secure fit — and we also offer implant-supported versions.

Do you offer financing for restorative work?

Yes — we offer 0% interest payment plans, direct insurance billing, and will work with you to create a phased treatment schedule if needed. No one should delay necessary dental care for financial reasons.
