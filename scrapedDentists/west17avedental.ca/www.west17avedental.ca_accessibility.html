Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

West 17th Avenue Dental Care is committed to ensuring our clinic, services, and website are accessible to all patients, including those with disabilities. We believe everyone deserves equitable access to quality dental care.

Physical Accessibility

  • Wheelchair-accessible entrance on 17th Avenue SW
  • Accessible parking in our free lot north of the building
  • Ground-floor clinic — no stairs required
  • Please let our team know in advance if you require any special accommodations

Communication Accessibility

  • Multilingual team fluent in English, Mandarin, Taiwanese, Punjabi, Hindi, and Urdu
  • Written communication available in multiple formats upon request
  • We welcome support persons and communication assistants
  • Extended appointment times available for patients who need additional support

Website Accessibility

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Sufficient colour contrast throughout the site
  • Keyboard-navigable interface
  • Descriptive alt text on all meaningful images
  • Responsive design for all screen sizes and devices

Need Assistance?

If you experience any accessibility barriers or require accommodation, please contact us directly. We are committed to resolving concerns promptly and improving our accessibility continuously.
