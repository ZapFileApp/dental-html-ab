Welcome To

Family Dental Clinic located in Fort Saskatchewan

Now accepting new patients

At Riverpointe Dental in Fort Saskatchewan, our team is committed to creating a welcoming and comfortable experience for every patient. We provide gentle, comprehensive, and attentive dental care from the moment you arrive. From the receptionist who greets you, to the dental assistant who helps you get settled, to the hygienist and dentist who provide your treatment, every member of our team works to help you feel at ease, informed, and confident in your care. Our goal is to keep you comfortable, educate you about your oral health, and support a healthy smile at every visit.

We bill the insurances directly to ensure that your dental care is affordable and our extended hours make it possible for you to have your dental appointments outside of your work schedule.

Our Services

ALL services provided by
general dentists

Riverpointe Dental in Fort Saskatchewan is proud to provide comprehensive family dental care for patients of all ages. From routine oral exams and professional cleanings to restorative treatments, our team offers a full range of dental services designed to protect, maintain, and improve your family’s oral health. We focus on personalized care and long term dental wellness so every member of your family can enjoy a healthy, confident smile.

Dental Check Ups

Check ups and cleanings help keep teeth and gums healthy, prevent dental problems, and support long term oral health for every patient.

Teeth Cleaning

Teeth cleanings remove plaque and tartar buildup to support healthy gums, fresher breath, and a cleaner smile.

Teeth Replacement

Restores oral function, and appearance by replacing missing teeth through various methods.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening safely removes stains, helping your smile look brighter, cleaner, and more confident every day.

Dental Emergencies

Dental emergencies need fast treatment to relieve pain, protect your smile, and prevent more serious oral health issues.

Tooth Extractions

Tooth extractions remove damaged or problematic teeth to protect your oral health and support lasting comfort.
